curdle ['kə:dl]

  • vt.,vi. (使)成凝乳；(使)凝结；(使)变质；(使)腐坏：

    • The milk has curdled.

      牛奶凝结了。

  • 短语:
    • curdle the (或one's)blood 吓呆；令人胆战心惊
    • 变形：
    • curdled , curdling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

curdle [ 'kə:dl ]

  • v.
    • turn into curds

      "curdled milk"

      同义词：clabber | clot

    • go bad or sour

      "The milk curdled"

    • turn from a liquid to a solid mass

      "his blood curdled"
