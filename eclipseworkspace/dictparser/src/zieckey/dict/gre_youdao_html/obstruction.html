obstruction [əb'strʌkʃən; ɔb-]

  • n.
    • 障碍物，阻塞物
    • 阻塞，堵塞，阻止，阻碍
    • 被妨碍(或阻碍)
    • 【体育】(足、篮球等中的)阻挡犯规
    • (在立法机关等中故意拖延时间)阻挠议事；阻挠
  • 近义词: obstacle
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

obstruction [ əb'strʌkʃən, ɔb- ]

  • n.
    • any structure that makes progress difficult

      同义词：obstructor | obstructer | impediment | impedimenta

    • the state or condition of being obstructed

      同义词：blockage

    • something immaterial that stands in the way and must be circumvented or surmounted

      "the filibuster was a major obstruction to the success of their plan"

      同义词：obstacle

    • the act of obstructing

      "obstruction of justice"

    • getting in someone's way

以上来源于: WordNet
