irritate1 ['iriteit]

  • vt.
    • 激怒，使恼怒；使烦躁，使不耐烦：

      • His remarks irritated me a little.

        他的话让我有点儿恼火。

    • 使过敏；使发炎；使疼痛；使难受：

      • The gas irritates my nose.

        这种气体让我的鼻子感到难受。

    • (在生理上)刺激，使兴奋
  • vi.
    • 引起不快；引起恼怒
    • (在生理上)引起兴奋；引起发炎
    • 变形：
    • irritated , irritating

irritate2 ['iriteit]

  • vt. 【法律】使无效
    • 变形：
    • irritated , irritating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

irritate [ 'iriteit ]

  • v.
    • cause annoyance in; disturb, especially by minor irritations

      "It irritates me that she never closes the door after she leaves"

      同义词：annoy | rag | get to | bother | get at | rile | nark | nettle | gravel | vex | chafe | devil

    • excite to an abnormal condition, of chafe or inflame

      "Aspirin irritates my stomach"

    • excite to some characteristic action or condition, such as motion, contraction, or nervous impulse, by the application of a stimulus

      "irritate the glands of a leaf"
