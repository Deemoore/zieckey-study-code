onerous ['ɔnərəs; 'əu-]

  • adj.
    • 繁重的，艰巨的；麻烦的
    • 【法律】承担(过重)义务(或责任)的；有偿的
  • 近义词: burdensome , oppressive , exacting
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

onerous [ 'ɔnərəs, 'əu- ]

  • adj. not easily borne; wearing

    "my duties weren't onerous"; " I only had to greet the guests"

    同义词：burdensome | taxing
