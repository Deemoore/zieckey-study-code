jeopardize,jeopardise ['dʒepədaiz]

  • vt. 使处于危险境地；危及，危害：

    • His foolish behaviour may jeopardize his whole future.

      他的愚蠢行为可能会危及他的整个前途。

    • He jeopardized his life to save the drowning child.

      他冒着生命危险挽救那个快要淹死的孩子。

    • 变形：
    • jeopardize,jeopardized , jeopardize,jeopardizing;-ised , jeopardize,jeopardising
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》
