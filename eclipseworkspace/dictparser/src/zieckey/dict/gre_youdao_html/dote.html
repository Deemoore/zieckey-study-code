dote [dəut]

  • vi.
    • 溺爱的，宠爱，过分喜爱(或偏爱)(on， upon):

      • She dotes on her youngest daughter.

        她溺爱她最小的女儿。

    • 脑子糊涂，尤指年老昏聩，(因年老)智力衰退，呆笨：

      • Associated with old age, he doted.

        他因年老而昏聩。

    • [方言](树木)开始腐朽[亦作doat]
  • n.
    • 树木腐朽
    • 变形：
    • doted , doting
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dote [ dəut ]

  • v.
    • be foolish or senile due to old age
    • shower with love; show excessive affection for

      "Grandmother dotes on her the twins"
