devotion [di'vəuʃən]

  • n.
    • 献身；奉献；供奉
    • (对事业、目的等的)专心，用心，专注，热心
    • (对事业、人等的)忠诚，忠心，忠实，诚恳；钟爱，热爱，挚爱
    • 【神学】虔诚，信仰，崇拜
    • [常用复数]【教会】祈祷(式)(尤指用于私人祈祷)，礼拜
    • 献身行为
  • 短语:
    • devotion to duty 忠于职守(常用于正式嘉奖大会上)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

devotion [ di'vəuʃən ]

  • n.
    • feelings of ardent love

      "their devotion to each other was beautiful"

      同义词：devotedness

    • commitment to some purpose

      "the devotion of his time and wealth to science"

    • religious zeal; the willingness to serve God

      同义词：idolatry | veneration | cultism

    • (usually plural) religious observance or prayers (usually spoken silently)

      "he returned to his devotions"
