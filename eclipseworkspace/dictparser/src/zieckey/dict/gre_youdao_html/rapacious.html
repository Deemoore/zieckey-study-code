rapacious [rə'peiʃəs]

  • adj.
    • 强取豪夺的；劫掠的
    • 贪得无厌的；贪婪的
    • (猛禽等)捕食生物的；食肉的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

rapacious [ rə'peiʃəs ]

  • adj.
    • living by preying on other animals especially by catching living prey

      "the rapacious wolf"

      同义词：predatory | raptorial | ravening | vulturine | vulturous

    • excessively greedy and grasping

      "a rapacious divorcee on the prowl"

      同义词：ravening | voracious

    • devouring or craving food in great quantities

      "a rapacious appetite"

      同义词：edacious | esurient | ravening | ravenous | voracious | wolfish

以上来源于: WordNet
