evade [i'veid]

  • vt.
    • (巧妙地)逃脱，逃避，躲避，躲开：

      • to evade one's pursuer

        逃避追捕者

      • to evade military service

        逃避兵役

    • 回避：

      • to evade rules

        回避规则

      • to evade the law

        规避法律

    • 避免做，避免完成：

      • to evade obligation

        逃避责任

      • to evade duty

        推卸职责

    • 规避，避免直接回答：

      • to evade an embarrassing question

        回避令人难堪的问题

    • 逃(税)：

      • to evade payment of a tax

        逃税

      • They evade paying taxes by living abroad.

        他们用移居国外的方法逃避纳税。

    • 使为难，把…难倒，使困惑；没有…注意到：

      • The solution evaded her.

        她想不出解决办法。

  • vi.
    • 逃避掉
    • (巧妙地)逃避，躲避，溜掉
    • [罕用语]逃跑，逃走；逃脱，逃离
  • 近义词: escape
    • 变形：
    • evaded , evading
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

evade [ i'veid ]

  • v.
    • avoid or try to avoid fulfilling, answering, or performing (duties, questions, or issues)

      "They tend to evade their responsibilities"; " he evaded the questions skillfully"

      同义词：hedge | fudge | put off | circumvent | parry | elude | skirt | dodge | duck | sidestep

    • escape, either physically or mentally

      "This difficult idea seems to evade her"; " The event evades explanation"

      同义词：elude | bilk

    • practice evasion

      "This man always hesitates and evades"

    • use cunning or deceit to escape or avoid

      "The con man always evades"
