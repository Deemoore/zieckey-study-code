  • vt.
    • inkle1的变形
  • n.
    • 细微的迹象；暗示
    • 模糊的想法，略知

inkle1 [iŋkl]

  • vt.
    • [英国方言]略知；对…有模糊的概念：

      • He now began to inkle what was going on.

        他现在开始有点明白究竟在发生什么事了。

    • 低声说出；暗示
    • 变形：
    • inkled , inkling

inkling [ 'iŋkliŋ ]

  • n. a slight suggestion or vague understanding

    "he had no inkling what was about to happen"

    同义词：intimation | glimmering | glimmer
