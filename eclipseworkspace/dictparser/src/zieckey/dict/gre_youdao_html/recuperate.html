recuperate [ri'kju:pəreit]

  • vt.
    • 使恢复；使复原：

      • He has recuperated his health.

        他已恢复健康。

    • 弥补(损失等)；挽回；回收：

      • They tried to recuperated waste heat to produce electric current.

        他们试图回收余热发电。

  • vi.
    • 复原；恢复健康；休养：

      • The patient did not recuperate as rapidly as the doctor had expected.

        这位病人并没有像医生所期望的那样很快就复原。

      • She went to the country to recuperate.

        她去乡村休养。

    • 得到补偿；挽回损失；恢复元气：

      • Our firm has recuperated after financial losses.

        我们的公司在财务亏损之后现已恢复元气。

    • 变形：
    • recuperated , recuperating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

recuperate [ ri'kju:pəreit ]

  • v.
    • regain or make up for

      "recuperate one's losses"

      同义词：recover | recoup

    • regain a former condition after a financial loss

      "The company managed to recuperate"

      同义词：recover | go back

    • restore to good health or strength
    • get over an illness or shock

      同义词：recover | convalesce
