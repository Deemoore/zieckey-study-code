baste1 [beist]

  • vt. 【缝纫】(为固定衣片或试样)粗缝，绗缝，用长针脚疏缝：

    • to baste pieces of a garment

      把衣片绗缝起来

    • 变形：
    • basted , basting

baste2 [beist]

  • vt.
    • (烤炙时为防止烤焦)在(烤肉、烤鸡上)涂(或抹、浇、滴)油(或汁)：

      • to baste the meat while cooking

        烤炙时往烤肉上滴油

  • n.
    • (涂在烤肉、烤鸡上的)调味品，调味汁；涂抹液
    • 变形：
    • basted , basting

baste3 [beist]

  • vt.
    • 用棒打，痛打，毒打，狠揍：

      • The father basted the bad child with a stick.

        父亲用棒狠揍那淘气孩子。

    • 大骂，痛斥：

      • an article basting the spokesman for irresponsible statements

        斥责该发言人信口开河的文章

    • 变形：
    • basted , basting
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

baste [ beist ]

  • n. loose temporary stitches

    同义词：basting | tacking

  • v.
    • cover with liquid before cooking

      "baste a roast"

    • strike violently and repeatedly

      同义词：clobber | batter

    • sew together loosely, with large stitches

      "baste a hem"

      同义词：tack
