infringement [in'frindʒmənt]

  • n.
    • (对法律、协议等的)违反，违背
    • (对他人权利的)侵犯，侵害
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

infringement [ in'frindʒmənt ]

  • n.
    • an act that disregards an agreement or a right

      同义词：violation

    • a crime less serious than a felony

      同义词：misdemeanor | misdemeanour | infraction | offence | offense | violation

以上来源于: WordNet
