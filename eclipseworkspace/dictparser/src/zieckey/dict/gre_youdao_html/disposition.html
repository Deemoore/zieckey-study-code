disposition [,dispə'ziʃən]

  • n.
    • 性格；懒惰，脾气，气质
      • 倾向(性)，意向，癖性
      • 物质自然的倾向
    • (房屋的)布置；排列；配置；安排；整理
    • [常用复数]【军事】(部队的)部署
    • (事务的)处理，处置，料理；(事情的)最后解决
    • 清除，除掉，去掉
    • (财产等的)转让；赠与，馈赠；(财产的)处分权
    • (财产等的)出售
    • 处置权，处理权；支配(权)，控制；管理；调整，规定
    • (神的)安排；天命
    • [废语]体质;体格[亦作disposure]
  • 近义词: temperament , temper , character , personality
  • 短语:
    • at(或in)someone's disposition 随某人支配
    • make (one's)disposition 着手准备；从事部署
    • show (或have) a disposition to (do) 有…的倾向，露出…的意思
    • well-oiled disposition 讨人喜欢的性格
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

disposition [ ,dispə'ziʃən ]

  • n.
    • your usual mood

      "he has a happy disposition"

      同义词：temperament

    • the act or means of getting rid of something

      同义词：disposal

    • an attitude of mind especially one that favors one alternative over others

      同义词：inclination | tendency

    • a natural or acquired habit or characteristic tendency in a person or thing

      "a swelling with a disposition to rupture"
