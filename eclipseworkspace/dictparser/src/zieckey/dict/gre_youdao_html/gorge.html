gorge [ɡɔ:dʒ]

  • n.
    • 峡，山峡；小峡谷
    • 【堡垒】(后部的)出入口
    • 咽，咽喉；食道
    • (鹰的)嗉囊
    • 咽下物；胃里的东西
    • [美国英语](阻塞通道的)障碍物，阻塞物
    • 怨恨；憎恶；愤怒；厌恶；作呕
    • (滑轮等的)凹槽
    • [亦作 gorge hook]原始的鱼钩
  • vt.
    • [常用被动式][gorge oneself]使吃饱;使塞饱：

      • The boy gorged himself.

        男孩胡乱地把自己塞饱。

    • 吞下；咽下；狼吞虎咽地吃
    • [通常用被动式]阻塞；使扩张：

      • veins gorged with blood

        淤血的静脉

  • vi.
    • 狼吞虎咽，拚命吃
  • 短语:
    • cast the gorge at 对…感到厌恶；憎恨
    • heave the (或 one's)gorge 作呕
    • make one's gorge rise 引起某人强烈厌恶；使某人作呕
    • rouse(或 stir)the gorge 使勃然大怒，激怒
    • 变形：
    • gorged , gorging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

gorge [ ɡɔ:dʒ ]

  • n.
    • a deep ravine (usually with a river running through it)
    • a narrow pass (especially one between mountains)

      同义词：defile

    • the passage between the pharynx and the stomach

      同义词：esophagus | oesophagus | gullet

  • v. overeat or eat immodestly; make a pig of oneself

    同义词：ingurgitate | overindulge | glut | englut | stuff | engorge | overgorge | overeat | gormandize | gormandise | gourmandize | binge | pig out | satiate | scarf out
