adorn [ə'dɔ:n]

  • vt.
    • 装饰；装潢，装帧：

      • garlands of flowers adorning her hair

        她头上戴着的花环

      • The young girl adorned herself with jewels.

        那少女佩带珠宝首饰。

    • 使生色；使增加美观；使更富美感；给…提供装饰品：

      • A diamond necklace adorned her neck.

        一串钻石项链使她更富美感。

  • 近义词: decorate , ornament , embellish , beautify , bedeck
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

adorn [ ə'dɔ:n ]

  • v.
    • make more attractive by adding ornament, colour, etc.

      同义词：decorate | grace | ornament | embellish | beautify

    • be beautiful to look at

      "Flowers adorned the tables everywhere"

      同义词：deck | decorate | grace | embellish | beautify

    • furnish with power or authority; of kings or emperors

      同义词：invest | clothe

以上来源于: WordNet
