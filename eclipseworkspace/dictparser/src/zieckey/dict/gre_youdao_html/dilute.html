dilute [dai'lju:t; di-]

  • vt.
    • 冲淡，稀释：

      • Dilute the ammonia with water before you use it.

        使用氨之前用水稀释它。

      • Dilute the juice (with water) before you drink it.

        喝果汁之前，用水把它冲淡。

    • (用掺杂方法)降低或改变(效力、力量、效率或效果等)，使减低(效果)，减轻，削弱：

      • The professor's proposals were diluted by the lack of support from his department.

        该教授的建议因缺乏系里的支持而不被重视。

    • 使褪色，褪(色)，使(颜色)变淡，(通过混合)使变弱：

      • to dilute the blue cloth

        使蓝布褪色

    • [主英国英语]使(劳动力)稀释化，用一定数量的非熟练工人取代(熟练工人)
  • vi.
    • 淡化，变稀释：

      • The barsh colours of the painting diluted in the strong light.

        那幅画刺眼的颜色在强光下变淡了。

  • adj.
    • 淡的，稀的，经稀释的
    • (颜色)褪掉了的
    • (在趣味性、主动性等方面)淡化了的，削弱了的，降低了的
    • 变形：
    • diluted , diluting
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dilute [ dai'lju:t, di- ]

  • v.
    • lessen the strength or flavor of a solution or mixture

      同义词：thin | thin out | reduce | cut

    • corrupt, debase, or make impure by adding a foreign or inferior substance; often by replacing valuable ingredients with inferior ones

      同义词：adulterate | stretch | debase

  • adj. reduced in strength or concentration or quality or purity

    "diluted alcohol"; " a dilute solution"; " dilute acetic acid"

    同义词：diluted
