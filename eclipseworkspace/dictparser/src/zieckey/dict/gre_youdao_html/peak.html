peak2 [pi:k]

  • n.
    • (物体的)尖头；尖端
    • 顶部，顶端；伞顶
    • 山顶；峰顶；山峰；孤山
    • (任何物体的)最高点；顶点；最高峰
    • (额前的)V形发尖；须尖
    • (衣着等的)尖形突出部；(帽子等的)帽舌；遮檐
    • 船头(或船尾)尖舱；斜桁尖头；锚爪
    • 【电学】峰值；峰荷最大值
    • [方言]岬；海角
  • adj.
    • 最高的；最大量的；高峰的
  • vt.
    • 使竖起；使成峰状；使高耸：

      • She pursed her pretty lips and peaked her eyebrows.

        她紧闭美丽的双唇，竖起眉毛。

    • 使达到最高峰；使达到最大量：

      • Sales have now peaked, and we expect them to decrease soon.

        现在销售额已经达到顶点，我们估计很快就会下降。

    • (划船休息时)直竖(桨条)；(鲸鱼)竖起(尾巴)：

      • They peaked the oars and had a rest.

        他们把桨竖起，休息起来。

  • vi.
    • 达到最大值；达到高峰；竖起；尖起：

      • Primary school enrolment will peak this year.

        今年小学入学人数将达到最高峰。

  • 近义词: summit
  • 短语:
    • widow's peak 脑门上面长成V形的头发(从前民间认为是早寡的预兆)[亦作 widow's lock]

peak [ pi:k ]

  • n.
    • the most extreme possible amount or value

      "voltage peak"

      同义词：extremum

    • the period of greatest prosperity or productivity

      同义词：flower | prime | heyday | bloom | blossom | efflorescence | flush

    • the highest level or degree attainable; the highest stage of development

      "the peak of perfection"; " summer was at its peak"

      同义词：acme | height | elevation | pinnacle | summit | superlative | meridian | tiptop | top

    • the top point of a mountain or hill

      "the view from the peak was magnificent"

      同义词：crown | crest | top | tip | summit

    • a V shape

      同义词：point | tip

    • the highest point (of something)

      "at the peak of the pyramid"

      同义词：vertex | apex | acme

    • a brim that projects to the front to shade the eyes

      同义词：bill | eyeshade | visor | vizor

  • v. to reach the highest point; attain maximum intensity, activity

    "That wild, speculative spirit peaked in 1929"

  • adj. of a period of maximal use or demand or activity

    "at peak hours the streets traffic is unbelievable"

百科

山顶

指山或山岳的最高的部分。山顶呈长条状延伸的叫山脊。山脊最高点的连线称山脊线，常构成河流的分水岭。山顶或山脊的形态很复杂，一般可分为尖山顶（尖山脊），圆山顶（圆山脊）和平山顶（平山脊）。造成山顶各种形态的主要因素是岩性，构造，外力地质作用的性质和强度，以及山岳地区发展的历史。
