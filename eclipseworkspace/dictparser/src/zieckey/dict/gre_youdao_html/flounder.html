flounder1 ['flaundə]

  • vi.
    • (在水、泥泞或深雪中)挣扎，肢体乱动；踉跄地走(常与about， along， on， through等连用)：

      • The child was floundering about in the water.

        那孩子在水中挣扎。

    • (因糊涂而)犯错误；笨拙、错乱地说(或做)：

      • The girl was frightened by the audience and floundered through her song.

        这个女孩子被听众吓坏了，因而她唱的歌错误百出。

  • n.
    • 挣扎，踉跄行走；笨拙而错乱的行动(或言语)

flounder2 ['flaundə]

  • n.
    • 川鲽(欧洲川鲽属比目鱼,Platichthys flesus)
    • 除鳎以外的比目鱼总称
    • 任何相似的非欧洲产的比目鱼的统称
  • 短语:
    • (as) flat as a flounder 扁而平的；很平很平的
    • 变形：
    • flounders , flounder
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flounder [ 'flaundə ]

  • n.
    • flesh of any of various American and European flatfish
    • any of various European and non-European marine flatfish
  • v.
    • walk with great difficulty

      同义词：stagger

    • behave awkwardly; have difficulties

      "She is floundering in college"
