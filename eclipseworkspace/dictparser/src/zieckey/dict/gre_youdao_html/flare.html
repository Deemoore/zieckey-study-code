flare [flεə]

  • vi.
    • 突然燃起来，旺起来(常与up连用)：

      • The fire flared up as the paper caught.

        纸着火时突然烧了起来。

    • (如火在风中)闪耀；闪光；闪亮：

      • A gust of wind made the torches flare.

        一阵风吹得火把闪闪发光。

    • 突然发怒，突然激动；(脾气、激情等)突然爆发(常与 up 或 out 连用)：

      • Mary's temper flared up.

        玛丽大发脾气。

      • Trouble may flare up in the big cities.

        在大城市可能爆发骚乱。

    • 闪光，发光，发亮：

      • Her golden hair flared like the sun.

        她那金色的头发像阳光般熠熠发亮。

    • (如衣裙的下摆、喇叭的一端等)呈喇叭形展开；逐渐向外展开：

      • The skirt flares at the knees.

        船两侧外倾，这样上面几层甲板就可以有更多的空间。

    • (飞机着陆前的)滑行平飞，拉平
  • vt.
    • 使(火、灯等)闪耀；使闪光，使闪亮：

      • The wind flared the candle.

        风吹得烛光闪动。

    • 用闪光信号发出，用闪光作信号：

      • The rockets flared a warning.

        火箭发出闪光信号。

    • 炫耀，夸示，展示
    • 使(衣裙等)呈喇叭形展开；使…向外逐渐展开
    • 使在着陆前滑行平飞，拉平(飞机)
    • 【戏剧】安装调试(闪光、音响设备)
  • n.
    • 闪光，闪耀，闪烁；摇曳的火焰
      • 照明装置；闪光装置
      • 照明弹；曳光管
    • (用作信号、指路或照明等目的的)火光，闪光
    • (突然)烧起
    • (感情、情绪等的)突然爆发，突然发作
      • (衣裙等的)呈喇叭形张开，逐渐展开，向外弯曲
      • (衣裙等的)张开(或展开)部分
    • 【天文学】(太阳的)耀斑
    • 【摄影】(底片的)翳雾斑；光斑；眩光
    • 【光学】(物镜)反射光斑
    • 【医学】(皮肤)发红，(因充血所致的)皮肤红肿块
    • 【数学】锥度；【船舶学】外倾
    • 【橄榄球】(传给插向边线后卫的)短传球
    • (飞机着陆前的)拉起机头，拉平，滑行平飞
  • 近义词: flame , flash
    • 变形：
    • flared , flaring

flare [ flεə ]

  • n.
    • a shape that spreads outward

      "the skirt had a wide flare"

      同义词：flair

    • a sudden burst of flame
    • a burst of light used to communicate or illuminate

      同义词：flash

    • reddening of the skin spreading outward from a focus of infection or irritation
    • a sudden recurrence or worsening of symptoms

      "a colitis flare"; " infection can cause a lupus flare"

    • a sudden eruption of intense high-energy radiation from the sun's surface; associated with sunspots and radio interference

      同义词：solar flare

    • am unwanted reflection in an optical system (or the fogging of an image that is caused by such a reflection)
    • a sudden outburst of emotion

      "she felt a flare of delight"; " she could not control her flare of rage"

    • a device that produces a bright light for warning or illumination or identification
    • a short forward pass to a back who is running toward the sidelines

      "he threw a flare to the fullback who was tackled for a loss"

      同义词：flare pass

    • (baseball) a fly ball hit a short distance into the outfield
  • v.
    • burn brightly

      "Every star seemed to flare with new intensity"

      同义词：flame up | blaze up | burn up

    • become flared and widen, usually at one end

      "The bellbottom pants flare out"

      同义词：flare out

    • shine with a sudden light

      "The night sky flared with the massive bombardment"

      同义词：flame

    • erupt or intensify suddenly

      "Tempers flared at the meeting"

      同义词：erupt | irrupt | flare up | break open | burst out
