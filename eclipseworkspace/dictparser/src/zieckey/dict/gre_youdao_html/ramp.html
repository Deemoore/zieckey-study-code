ramp1 [ræmp]

  • n.
    • 斜面；斜坡；(轮椅等使用的)坡道
    • [美国英语](上下飞机的)舷梯[亦称作boarding ramp]
    • 弯子(如楼梯扶手的拐弯部分)
    • (船只的)滑行台
    • [亦称作parking ramp](机库前的)停机坪

ramp2 [ræmp]

  • vi.
    • (狮子等)用后脚立起；跃立
    • 作恫吓的姿势
    • 猛撞，横冲直撞；暴跳：

      • They ramped and raged like a pack of trapped beasts.

        他们像一群被围困的野兽，张牙舞爪，暴跳如雷。

    • (草木等的)蔓生，蔓延
  • vt.
    • 使(墙)成斜面；把…做成斜面
  • n.
    • 暴跳；冲撞
    • (狮子等的)跃立姿势
    • (植物的)蔓生
  • 短语:
    • ramp and rage 大怒，暴跳如雷： He ramped and raged when he heard the news. 他听到这个消息时大发雷霆。

ramp3 [ræmp]

  • n. [常用复数]【植物】 北美野韭(Allium tricoccum) [亦作wild leek]

  • n.

  • vi.

  • vt.
    • 向…敲诈

ramp [ ræmp ]

  • n.
    • an inclined surface connecting two levels

      同义词：incline

    • North American perennial having a slender bulb and whitish flowers

      同义词：wild leek | Allium tricoccum

    • a movable staircase that passengers use to board or leave an aircraft
  • v.
    • behave violently, as if in state of a great anger

      同义词：rage | storm

    • furnish with a ramp

      "The ramped auditorium"

    • be rampant

      "the lion is rampant in this heraldic depiction"

    • creep up -- used especially of plants

      "The roses ramped over the wall"

    • stand with arms or forelegs raised, as if menacing
