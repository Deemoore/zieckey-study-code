mantle ['mæntl]

  • vt.
    • (用斗篷)覆盖，罩住：

      • Snow mantled the hill.

        雪覆盖了山丘。

      • A small stream was mantled over with green mosses.

        小河被青苔所覆盖。

    • 掩起，掩藏，遮掩：

      • Clouds mantled the moon.

        云将月亮遮住。

      • The mountains are mantled in silver.

        群山披上了银装。

  • vi.
    • 罩住；覆盖：

      • The whole district mantled in loveliest white.

        整个地区都披上了可爱的银白色外衣。

    • (脸)发红，脸涨红：

      • Her face mantled with shame.

        她的脸羞愧得涨红了。

      • Her cheeks mantled.

        她两颊绯红。

    • (液体)表面上结皮，被盖上泡沫：

      • The goblet was mantling with foam.

        这个高脚杯盖满了泡沫。

    • 猎鹰伸足展翅
  • n.
    • 无袖外套；斗篷；披风；(解)外表
    • (作为权力标志的)衣钵
    • 覆盖物；罩；幕
    • (煤气灯等的)燃罩，白炽罩
    • 【冶金学】(高炉的)环梁壳
    • =mantel
    • 【解剖学】 (大脑)皮质；外表
    • 【地质学】 地幔
    • 【动物学】 (软体动物的)套膜；软膜
    • 【鸟类学】翕
  • 短语:
    • one's mantle falls on (或descends) someone 衣钵传给某人 [源自《圣经》]
    • take over the mantle of someone 继承某人的衣钵 [亦作 inherit the mantle of someone]
    • 变形：
    • mantled , mantling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

mantle [ 'mæntl ]

  • n.
    • the cloak as a symbol of authority

      "place the mantle of authority on younger shoulders"

    • United States baseball player (1931-1997)

      同义词：Mickey Mantle | Mickey Charles Mantle

    • the layer of the earth between the crust and the core
    • anything that covers

      同义词：blanket

    • (zoology) a protective layer of epidermis in mollusks or brachiopods that secretes a substance forming the shell

      同义词：pallium

    • shelf that projects from wall above fireplace

      同义词：mantel | mantelpiece | mantlepiece | chimneypiece

    • hanging cloth used as a blind (especially for a window)

      同义词：curtain | drape | drapery | pall

    • a sleeveless garment like a cloak but shorter

      同义词：cape

  • v.
    • spread over a surface, like a mantle
    • cover like a mantle

      "The ivy mantles the building"
