chunk [tʃʌŋk]

  • n.
    • 厚块，大块；短而厚的木块
    • (数量)相当大，可观，很多
    • [美国英语]粗壮的动物(尤指马)；农用重挽马
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

chunk [ tʃʌŋk ]

  • n.
    • a compact mass

      同义词：ball | clod | glob | lump | clump

    • a substantial amount

      "we won a chunk of money"

  • v.
    • put together indiscriminately

      同义词：lump

    • group or chunk together in a certain order or place side by side

      同义词：collocate | lump

以上来源于: WordNet
