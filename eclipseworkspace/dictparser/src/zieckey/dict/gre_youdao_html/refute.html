refute [ri'fju:t; ri:-]

  • vt.
    • 反驳；驳斥(某人)：

      • I refuted his claim that he was innocent.

        他说他清白无辜，我对此进行了驳斥。

    • 驳倒；推翻(论据等)：

      • We can easily refute his argument.

        我们可以很容易地驳倒他的论点。

  • 近义词: disprove
    • 变形：
    • refuted , refuting
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

refute [ ri'fju:t, ri:- ]

  • v.
    • overthrow by argument, evidence, or proof

      "The speaker refuted his opponent's arguments"

      同义词：rebut

    • prove to be false or incorrect

      同义词：rebut | controvert

以上来源于: WordNet
