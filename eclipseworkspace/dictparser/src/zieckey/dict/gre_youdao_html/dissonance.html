dissonance ['disənəns]

  • n.
    • 刺耳(或不和谐)的声音，嘈杂声
      • 【音乐】不谐和音；不谐和(和弦)
      • 【语言音】不谐和音[参较consonance]
    • (信仰与行为间的)不一致，不协调，不和谐，不调和
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dissonance [ 'disənəns ]

  • n.
    • a conflict of people's opinions or actions or characters

      同义词：disagreement | dissension

    • the auditory experience of sound that lacks musical quality; sound that is a disagreeable auditory experience

      同义词：noise | racket

    • disagreeable sounds

以上来源于: WordNet
