  • n.
    • extreme gluttony

      同义词：edacity | esurience | rapaciousness | voracity | voraciousness

    • reprehensible acquisitiveness; insatiable desire for wealth (personified as one of the deadly sins)

      同义词：avarice | greed | covetousness | avaritia
