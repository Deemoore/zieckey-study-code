flexible ['fleksibl]

  • adj.
    • 易弯(曲)的，柔韧的，可弯曲的
    • 易被说服的，易受影响的；柔顺的，温顺的
    • 易适应的，可变通的；有弹性的；灵活性的
  • 近义词: pliable , elastic , supple , tractable , compliant
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flexible [ 'fleksibl ]

  • adj.
    • capable of being changed

      "flexible schedules"

    • able to flex; able to bend easily

      "slim flexible birches"

      同义词：flexile

    • able to adjust readily to different conditions

      "a flexible personality"

      同义词：elastic | pliable | pliant

    • bending and snapping back readily without breaking

      同义词：whippy

    • making or willing to make concessions

      同义词：compromising | conciliatory
