closure ['kləuʒə]

  • n.
    • 闭合，封闭
    • 结束；结尾；终止；结局
    • 关闭物；围墙；罩子；隔板；堵口
    • 【地质学】闭合度
    • 【语言学】(句法结构理论上的)结束点
    • 【数学】闭合，闭包(性质)，闭包特性；(包含一个集及其所有极限点的)闭合集
    • 【语音学】封闭(指暂时把气流封闭在声道的某一部位上)
    • 闭(合)集
    • 解脱感，了结
  • vt., vi.
    • 【议会程序】 = cloture
    • 变形：
    • closured , closuring

closure [ 'kləuʒə ]

  • n.
    • approaching a particular destination; a coming closer; a narrowing of a gap

      同义词：closing

    • a rule for limiting or ending debate in a deliberative body

      同义词：cloture | gag rule | gag law

    • a Gestalt principle of organization holding that there is an innate tendency to perceive incomplete objects as complete and to close or fill gaps and to perceive asymmetric stimuli as symmetric

      同义词：law of closure

    • something settled or resolved; the outcome of decision making

      "he needed to grieve before he could achieve a sense of closure"

      同义词：settlement | resolution

    • an obstruction in a pipe or tube

      同义词：blockage | block | occlusion | stop | stoppage

    • the act of blocking

      同义词：blockage | occlusion

    • termination of operations

      "they regretted the closure of the day care center"

      同义词：closedown | closing | shutdown

  • v. terminate debate by calling for a vote

    "debate was closured"

    同义词：cloture
