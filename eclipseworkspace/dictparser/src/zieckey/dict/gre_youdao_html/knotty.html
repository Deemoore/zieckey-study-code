knotty ['nɔti]

  • adj.
    • 有结(或节的)；多结(或节的)
    • 难解决的，棘手的；使人困惑的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

knotty [ 'nɔti ]

  • adj.
    • making great mental demands; hard to comprehend or solve or believe

      "I faced the knotty problem of what to have for breakfast"

      同义词：baffling | problematic | problematical | tough

    • used of old persons or old trees; covered with knobs or knots

      同义词：gnarled | gnarly | knotted | knobbed

    • highly complex or intricate

      "a knotty problem"

      同义词：Byzantine | convoluted | involved | tangled | tortuous

    • tangled in knots or snarls

      同义词：knotted | snarled | snarly

以上来源于: WordNet
