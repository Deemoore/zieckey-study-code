quiver1 ['kwivə]

  • vi.
    • 颤抖；战栗：

      • He quivered all over with rage.

        他气得浑身发抖。

      • Her voice quivered as she spoke.

        她说话时声音发颤。

  • vt.
    • 使颤抖；抖动：

      • The white crane quivered its wings and flied away.

        白鹤抖了抖翅膀飞走了。

  • n.
    • 颤抖；颤声
    • 一闪
  • 近义词: shake
  • 短语:
    • all of a quiver 心情非常紧张；浑身哆嗦
    • quiver like an aspen leaf 见 leaf

quiver2 ['kwivə]

  • n.
    • 箭筒；箭囊
    • 箭囊中的箭
  • 短语:
    • a quiver full (或quiverful) of children [戏谑语]大家庭，子女众多的家庭
    • have an arrow (或 a shaft) left in one's quiver 见 arrow

quiver3 ['kwivə]

  • adj. [方言]迅速的，敏捷的

quiver [ 'kwivə ]

  • n.
    • an almost pleasurable sensation of fright

      同义词：frisson | shiver | chill | shudder | thrill | tingle

    • a shaky motion

      同义词：shaking | shakiness | trembling | quivering | vibration | palpitation

    • case for holding arrows
    • the act of vibrating

      同义词：vibration | quivering

  • v.
    • shake with fast, tremulous movements

      同义词：quake | palpitate

    • move back and forth very rapidly

      同义词：flicker | waver | flitter | flutter

    • move with or as if with a regular alternating motion

      同义词：pulsate | beat

以上来源于: WordNet
