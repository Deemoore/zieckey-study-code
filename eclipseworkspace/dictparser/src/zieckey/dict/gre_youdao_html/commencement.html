commencement [kə'mensmənt]

  • n.
    • 开始(时间)；发端
    • (学校等)毕业典礼；学位授予典礼
    • 典礼日；毕业日
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

commencement [ kə'mensmənt ]

  • n.
    • the time at which something is supposed to begin

      同义词：beginning | first | outset | get-go | start | kickoff | starting time | showtime | offset

    • an academic exercise in which diplomas are conferred

      同义词：commencement exercise | commencement ceremony | graduation | graduation exercise

    • the act of starting something

      同义词：beginning | start
