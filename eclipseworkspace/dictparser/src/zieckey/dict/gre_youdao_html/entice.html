entice [in'tais]

  • vt. 利诱，引诱，诱惑，诱使，诱骗；怂恿；唆使；吸引：

    • They were enticed westward by dreams of gold.

      他们被黄金梦引诱到西部。

    • She enticed me away from working.

      她引诱我离开工作。

    • 变形：
    • enticed , enticing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

entice [ in'tais ]

  • v. provoke someone to do something through (often false or exaggerated) promises or persuasion

    同义词：lure | tempt

以上来源于: WordNet
