promiscuous [prəu'miskjuəs]

  • adj.
    • 混杂的；乱七八糟的；杂乱的
    • 不加区别的；(尤指)不分男女的；男女乱交的
    • 偶然的；漫无目的的
  • 短语:
    • at a promiscuous manner 胡乱，不分青红皂白地
    • take a promiscuous stroll 漫步，没有目的地散步
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

promiscuous [ prəu'miskjuəs ]

  • adj.
    • not selective of a single class or person

      "Clinton was criticized for his promiscuous solicitation of campaign money"

    • casual and unrestrained in sexual behavior

      同义词：easy | light | loose | sluttish | wanton
