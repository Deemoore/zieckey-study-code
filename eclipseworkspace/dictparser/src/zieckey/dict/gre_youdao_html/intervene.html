intervene [,intə'vi:n]

  • vi.
    • 插进；介入；介于中间；发生于其间：

      • to intervene between two objects

        插入两物体之间

      • the period which intervenes between March 5 and June 5

        介于3月5日和6月5日之间的时期

    • 干涉；干预：

      • to intervene in the internal affairs of other countries

        干涉别国内政

    • 调停；斡旋：

      • to intervene in a dispute

        调停争端

    • 阻挠；阻碍；干扰；打扰：

      • I shall come if nothing intervenes.

        如果没什么别的事我会来的。

      • We enjoyed the picnic until a thunderstorm intervened.

        一场暴风雨影响了我们野餐时的兴致。

    • 【法律】(与案件有关的第三者为保护个人权利)参加诉讼
    • 变形：
    • intervened , intervening
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

intervene [ ,intə'vi:n ]

  • v.
    • get involved, so as to alter or hinder an action, or through force or threat of force

      "Why did the U.S. not intervene earlier in WW II?"

      同义词：step in | interfere | interpose

    • be placed or located between other things or extend between spaces and events

      "This interludes intervenes between the two movements"; " Eight days intervened"

    • occur between other event or between certain points of time

      "the war intervened between the birth of her two children"
