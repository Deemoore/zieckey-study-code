beatitude [bi（:）'ætitju:d]

  • n.
    • 至福，极乐，全福，天福
    • 赐福，祈福，祝福
    • 【东正教】法座(最高级主教的称号)
    • [the Beatitudes] 【基督教】八福词(耶稣登山训众论福所讲的真福八端)[《马太福音》5∶3～12]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

beatitude [ bi（:）'ætitju:d ]

  • n.
    • a state of supreme happiness

      同义词：blessedness | beatification

    • one of the eight sayings of Jesus at the beginning of the Sermon on the Mount; in Latin each saying begins with `beatus' (blessed)

以上来源于: WordNet
