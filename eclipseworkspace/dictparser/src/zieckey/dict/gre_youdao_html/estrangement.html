estrangement

  • n. estrange的变形

estrange [i'streindʒ]

  • vt.
    • 使疏远；离间；使失和(from)：

      • Their quarrel estranged the two friends.

        他们的争吵使两个朋友失和。

    • 使隔离，使远离，使脱离，使离开(熟悉的环境、亲友或相关物)(from):

      • The necessity for traveling on business has estranged her from her family.

        她因必须出差而一直离开亲人。

    • 使转手；使作别用，转作他用
    • 变形：
    • estranged , estranging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

estrangement [ is'treindʒmənt ]

  • n.
    • separation resulting from hostility

      同义词：alienation

    • the feeling of being alienated from other people

      同义词：alienation | disaffection
