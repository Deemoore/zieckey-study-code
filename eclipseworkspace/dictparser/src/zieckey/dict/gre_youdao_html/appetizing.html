appetizing ['æpitaiziŋ]

  • adj.
    • 促进(或刺激)食欲的，开胃的
    • 美味可口的，鲜美(可口)的；色香味俱佳的

appetizing [ 'æpitaiziŋ ]

  • adj. appealing to or stimulating the appetite especially in appearance or aroma
