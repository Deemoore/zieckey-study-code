propensity [ prəu'pensiti ]

  • n.
    • an inclination to do something

      同义词：leaning | tendency

    • a natural inclination

      同义词：proclivity | leaning

    • a disposition to behave in a certain way

      "the propensity of disease to spread"

      同义词：aptness
