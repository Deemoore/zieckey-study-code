flamboyant ['flæm'bɔiənt]

  • adj.
    • 灿烂的，醒目的，惹眼的
    • 火焰似的；火红色的；(色彩)鲜明的
    • 夸张的，浮夸的，浮华的，虚饰的，奢华的
    • 【建筑学】火焰式的
  • n.
    • 【植物】凤凰木
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flamboyant [ 'flæm'bɔiənt ]

  • n. showy tropical tree or shrub native to Madagascar; widely planted in tropical regions for its immense racemes of scarlet and orange flowers; sometimes placed in genus Poinciana

    同义词：royal poinciana | flame tree | peacock flower | Delonix regia | Poinciana regia

  • adj.
    • elaborately or excessively ornamented

      "flamboyant handwriting"

      同义词：aureate | florid

    • richly and brilliantly colorful

      同义词：unrestrained

凤凰木
