inebriate [i'ni:briət]

  • adj.
    • 酒醉的，醉醺醺的；陶醉的[亦作 inebriated]
  • n.
    • 酒鬼：醉汉；酗酒之徒
  • vt.
    • 使醉；灌醉
    • 使陶醉，使兴奋：

      • to be inebriated by success

        陶醉于成功的喜悦中

    • 变形：
    • inebriated , inebriating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

inebriate [ i'ni:briət ]

  • n. a chronic drinker

    同义词：drunkard | drunk | rummy | sot | wino

  • v.
    • fill with sublime emotion; tickle pink

      "He was inebriated by his phenomenal success"

      同义词：exhilarate | thrill | exalt | beatify

    • make drunk (with alcoholic drinks)

      同义词：intoxicate | soak

    • become drunk or drink excessively

      同义词：souse | soak | hit it up
