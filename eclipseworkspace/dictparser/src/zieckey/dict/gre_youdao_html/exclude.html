exclude [iks'klu:d]

  • vt.
    • 拒绝接纳；不让…入内，阻止…进入，把…关在外面，把…排除(或排斥)在外；不包括：

      • They excluded people under 20 from (joining) the club.

        他们拒绝20岁以下的人加入俱乐部。

      • to exclude immigrants from a country

        不准移民入境

    • 对…不予考虑；对…不加注意；不容许…的发生(或存在、出现、被使用等)，不准；排挤，排斥：

      • Employees and their relatives were excluded from participation in the contest.

        雇员及其亲属不准参赛。

    • 驱逐，逐出，赶出；开除(行为不端的学生等)，除(名)：

      • She was excluded from the club for infractions of the rules.

        她因违反规则被开除出俱乐部。

    • 孵出
  • 近义词: debar , eliminate
  • 反义词: admit , include
    • 变形：
    • excluded , excluding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

exclude [ iks'klu:d ]

  • v.
    • prevent from being included or considered or accepted

      "The bad results were excluded from the report"

      同义词：except | leave out | leave off | omit | take out

    • prevent from entering; shut out

      "This policy excludes people who have a criminal record from entering the country"

      同义词：keep out | shut out | shut

    • lack or fail to include

      "The cost for the trip excludes food and beverages"

    • prevent from entering; keep out

      同义词：bar | debar

    • put out or expel from a place

      同义词：expel | eject | chuck out | throw out | kick out | turf out | boot out | turn out
