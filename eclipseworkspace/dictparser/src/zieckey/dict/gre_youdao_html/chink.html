chink1 [tʃiŋk]

  • n.
    • 裂缝，裂口，缝隙
    • 漏洞，空子
    • (从裂缝中射出的)一道光线
  • vt.
    • 堵塞；堵塞…的裂缝：

      • They chinked the holes in the walls with plaster.

        他们用灰泥堵塞住墙上的洞。

    • [废语]使成裂缝

chink2 [tʃiŋk]

  • n.
    • (金属或玻璃器皿等互相撞击所发出的)丁当声
    • [俚语]钱，硬币；现金
  • vt.
    • 使发丁当声
  • vi.
    • 发丁当声，丁当响

Chink [tʃiŋk]

  • n. [有时作 c-][美国俚语、贬义]中国佬

chink [ tʃiŋk ]

  • n.
    • offensive terms for a person of Chinese descent

      同义词：Chinaman

    • a narrow opening as e.g. between planks in a wall
    • a short light metallic sound

      同义词：click | clink

  • v.
    • make or emit a high sound

      同义词：tinkle | tink | clink

    • fill the chinks of, as with caulking
    • make cracks or chinks in

      同义词：check

以上来源于: WordNet
