deluge ['delju:dʒ]

  • n.
    • 洪水
    • 暴雨，倾盆大雨，豪雨
    • 洪水般的汹涌，洪水般涌来的东西；大批(或大量、大肆)泛滥
  • vt.
    • 淹没，使泛滥：

      • The spring thaw caused the river to deluge the region.

        春天冰雪融化，使河水泛滥淹没了那地区。

    • 使充满；压倒，涌集(with):

      • We were deluged with offers.

        报价单纷至沓来。

      • They were deluged with applications.

        申请书使他们应接不暇。

    • 变形：
    • deluged , deluging

Deluge ['delju:dʒ]

  • n. [the Deluge]【圣经】洪水灭世,大流激 (=the Flood) [《创世记》7]
  • 短语:
    • After me the Deluge ! 我死后哪怕洪水灭世!(或：身后之事与我何干!)[源出法王路易十五情妇蓬巴杜女侯爵及时行乐的名言]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

deluge [ 'delju:dʒ ]

  • n.
    • an overwhelming number or amount

      同义词：flood | inundation | torrent

    • a heavy rain

      同义词：downpour | cloudburst | waterspout | torrent | pelter | soaker

    • the rising of a body of water and its overflowing onto normally dry land

      同义词：flood | inundation | alluvion

  • v.
    • fill quickly beyond capacity; as with a liquid

      同义词：flood | inundate | swamp

    • charge someone with too many tasks

      同义词：overwhelm | flood out

    • fill or cover completely, usually with water

      同义词：inundate | submerge
