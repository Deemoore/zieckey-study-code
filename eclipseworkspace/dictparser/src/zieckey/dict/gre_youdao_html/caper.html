caper1 ['keipə]

  • vi.
    • 欢跃，雀跃，蹦蹦跳跳：

      • There are many lambs capering in the meadow.

        很多小羊在草地上蹦蹦跳跳。

  • n.
    • 雀跃，欢跃
    • 嬉戏；戏弄，恶作剧
    • [美国俚语](图谋)犯罪，(尤指抢劫等)犯罪活动，持械抢劫；勾当
    • [俚语]饮酒闹事，发酒疯；大吵大闹
    • 惊险喜剧片
  • 短语:
    • cut a caper (或 capers)
      • 蹦蹦跳跳，雀跃
      • 嬉戏；开玩笑；胡闹；出洋相，作怪样
    • Cut your capers! 滚开!
    • I'll make him cut his capers. 我会叫他后悔的。

caper2 ['keipə]

  • n.
    • 马槟榔属或槌果藤属(Capparis)植物,(尤指)刺山柑,老鼠瓜(Capparis spinosa)
    • [常作复数]刺山柑等的嫩芽
  • adj.
    • 白花菜科(Capparidaceae)植物的[参较 caper family]

caper3 ['keipə]

  • n. [古语] = privateer
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

caper [ 'keipə ]

  • n.
    • any of numerous plants of the genus Capparis
    • pickled flower buds used as a pungent relish in various dishes and sauces
    • a crime (especially a robbery)

      同义词：job

    • a playful leap or hop

      同义词：capriole

    • gay or light-hearted recreational activity for diversion or amusement

      同义词：play | frolic | romp | gambol

    • a ludicrous or grotesque act done for fun and amusement

      同义词：antic | joke | prank | trick | put-on

  • v. jump about playfully
