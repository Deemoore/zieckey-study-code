appreciative [ə'pri:ʃiətiv]

  • adj.
    • 欣赏的；有欣赏力的，有赏识力的，有眼力的，有鉴赏力的，(能)确认价值的
    • (表示)感激的，感谢的，表示赞赏的[亦作appreciatory]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

appreciative [ ə'pri:ʃiətiv ]

  • adj.
    • feeling or expressive of gratitude

      "was appreciative of his efforts"; " an appreciative word"

    • having or showing appreciation or a favorable critical judgment or opinion

      "appreciative of a beautiful landscape"; " an appreciative laugh from the audience"

以上来源于: WordNet
