cloister ['klɔistə]

  • n.
    • 修道院，寺院
    • (安静的)隐居地
    • 修道院生活
    • 【建筑工程】(修道院、教堂等地的)回廊；四周有回廊的庭院
  • vt.
    • 使隐居于修道院(或寺院)中；使与尘世隔绝，使隐居：

      • He has cloistered himself in a monastery for more than thirty years.

        他隐居于修道院中已三十余年。

      • He cloistered himself away with his books.

        他只顾埋头读书。

    • 在…设回廊，围以回廊：

      • The courtyard is cloistered.

        庭院四周围以回廊。

  • 近义词: convent , nunnery , monastery , abbey , priory
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cloister [ 'klɔistə ]

  • n.
    • residence that is a place of religious seclusion (such as a monastery)

      同义词：religious residence

    • a courtyard with covered walks (as in religious institutions)
  • v.
    • surround with a cloister, as of a garden
    • seclude from the world in or as if in a cloister

      "She cloistered herself in the office"
