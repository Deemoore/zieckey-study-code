corroborate [kə'rɔbə,reit]

  • vt. 确证，证实：

    • The new theory has been partially corroborated.

      这个新的理论已部分地被证实。

    • 变形：
    • corroborated , corroborating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

corroborate [ kə'rɔbə,reit ]

  • v.
    • establish or strengthen as with new evidence or facts

      同义词：confirm | sustain | substantiate | support | affirm

    • give evidence for

      同义词：validate

    • support with evidence or authority or make more certain or confirm

      同义词：underpin | bear out | support

以上来源于: WordNet
