plaster ['plɑ:stə; 'plæs-]

  • n.
    • (涂墙等用的)灰泥，灰浆
    • =plaster of Paris
    • 硬膏；膏药；橡皮膏
    • 安慰(或治疗、缓解)用品(或措施)
    • [美国俚语](尤指1美元的)钞票
  • vt.
    • 涂以灰泥；厚厚地涂上灰泥：

      • His coat was plastered with thick yellow mud.

        他的上衣上沾着厚厚的黄泥。

    • 粘贴；使紧贴：

      • to plaster posters on walls

        往墙上贴标语

    • 使平；梳理：

      • to plaster one's hair down

        (用发蜡等)把头发弄平

    • 用熟石膏处理：

      • the plastered wheat

        用熟石膏处理过的小麦

    • 敷膏药于；贴橡皮膏于：

      • to plaster a sprain

        在扭伤处贴膏药

    • 掩饰，掩盖：

      • The government thinks the cracks in its policies can be plastered over with fine-sounding promises.

        政府以为可以用动听的许诺掩饰其政策上的缺陷。

    • 安慰；减轻
    • [俚语]击败；猛轰；杀伤目标：

      • I'm afraid our football team was really plastered last week.

        恐怕我们的足球队上星期真的被打得落花流水。

    • [戏谑语]赔偿医药费
  • vi.
    • 抹水泥：

      • to plaster over holes

        在洞上抹水泥

  • 短语:
    • in plaster 上着筒形石膏夹
    • plaster with praise 滥夸奖
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

plaster [ 'plɑ:stə, 'plæs- ]

  • n.
    • a mixture of lime or gypsum with sand and water; hardens into a smooth solid; used to cover walls and ceilings
    • any of several gypsum cements; a white powder (a form of calcium sulphate) that forms a paste when mixed with water and hardens into a solid; used in making molds and sculptures and casts for broken limbs

      同义词：plaster of Paris

    • a medical dressing consisting of a soft heated mass of meal or clay that is spread on a cloth and applied to the skin to treat inflamed areas or improve circulation etc.

      同义词：poultice | cataplasm

    • a surface of hardened plaster (as on a wall or ceiling)

      "there were cracks in the plaster"

      同义词：plasterwork

    • adhesive tape used in dressing wounds

      同义词：adhesive plaster | sticking plaster

  • v.
    • cover conspicuously or thickly, as by pasting something on

      "The demonstrators plastered the hallways with posters"; " She let the walls of the apartment be beplastered with stucco"

      同义词：beplaster

    • affix conspicuously

      "She plastered warnings all over the wall"

    • apply a plaster cast to

      "plaster the broken arm"

    • apply a heavy coat to

      同义词：plaster over | stick on

    • coat with plaster

      同义词：daub

    • dress by covering with a therapeutic substance

      同义词：poultice
