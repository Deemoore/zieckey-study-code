evoke [i'vəuk]

  • vt.
    • 唤起，召唤；召(魂)，唤(神)，召来(亡灵等)：

      • to evoke a spirit from the dead

        从死者身上招魂

    • 引起，博得，惹出，使发出：

      • Her comment evoked protests from the shocked listeners.

        她的评论引起震惊的听众的抗议。

    • 唤起，使人想起，使人产生(联想、回忆、感情等)：

      • to evoke a memory

        使人产生回忆

    • 描绘出；使再现：

      • a short passage that manages to evoke the smells, colours, sounds, and shapes of that metropolis

        一篇力图描绘出那个大都会的味、色、音、形的短文

    • 【法律】(下级法院)移送(案卷)至上级法院
    • 变形：
    • evoked , evoking
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

evoke [ i'vəuk ]

  • v.
    • call forth (emotions, feelings, and responses)

      "evoke sympathy"

      同义词：arouse | elicit | enkindle | kindle | fire | raise | provoke

    • evoke or provoke to appear or occur

      同义词：provoke | call forth | kick up

    • deduce (a principle) or construe (a meaning)

      同义词：educe | elicit | extract | draw out

    • summon into action or bring into existence, often as if by magic

      同义词：raise | conjure | conjure up | invoke | stir | call down | arouse | bring up | put forward | call forth

    • call to mind

      "this remark evoked sadness"

      同义词：suggest | paint a picture

以上来源于: WordNet
