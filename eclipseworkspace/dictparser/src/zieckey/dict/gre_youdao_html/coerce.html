coerce [kəu'ə:s]

  • vt.
    • 强制，胁迫；迫使：

      • He was coerced into signing the contract.

        他被迫签了合同。

    • 支配，抑制，压制
  • 近义词: force
    • 变形：
    • coerced , coercing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

  • v. to cause to do through pressure or necessity, by physical, moral or intellectual means :
