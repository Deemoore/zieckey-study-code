lure [ljuə]

  • n.
    • 诱惑力；吸引力；魅力
    • 诱惑物
    • 诱回猎鹰的彩色羽毛
    • (狩猎或钓鱼用的)诱饵；鱼饵；囮子
  • vt.
    • 吸引；引诱；诱惑：

      • The price lured many buyers.

        这个价格吸引了许多买家。

      • He was lured to destruction.

        他受诱惑而走向毁灭。

    • 以诱饵吸引：

      • The animal was lured out of its hole.

        野兽被诱出了洞。

    • (以诱惑物)诱回(猎鹰)
  • 近义词: entice , inveigle
  • 反义词: repel
  • 短语:
    • alight on the lure 上当；上钩[亦作 come (或 stoop) to one's (或the) lure]
    • bring someone to his (或 the) lure 引某人上钩
    • 变形：
    • lured , luring
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

lure [ ljuə ]

  • n.
    • qualities that attract by seeming to promise some kind of reward

      同义词：enticement | come-on

    • anything that serves as an enticement

      同义词：bait | come-on | hook | sweetener

    • something used to lure victims into danger

      同义词：bait | decoy

  • v. provoke someone to do something through (often false or exaggerated) promises or persuasion

    "He lured me into temptation"

    同义词：entice | tempt
