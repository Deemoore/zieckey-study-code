decorous ['dekərəs]

  • adj. (外表、仪态、行为等)端庄稳重的，正派得体的；谦恭的，有礼貌的，有教养的；高雅的，大方的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

decorous [ 'dekərəs ]

  • adj.
    • characterized by propriety and dignity and good taste in manners and conduct

      "the tete-a-tete was decorous in the extreme"

    • according with custom or propriety

      同义词：becoming | comely | comme il faut | decent | seemly

以上来源于: WordNet
