parody ['pærədi]

  • n.
    • (为嘲弄某作者或某作品而作成的)模仿滑稽作品
    • 拙劣的模仿
    • 模仿诗文
  • vt.
    • 通过模仿嘲弄：

      • All these peculiarities of his style have been parodied by his assailants.

        他的所有这些风格特征都受到攻击者模仿嘲弄。

  • 近义词: caricature
    • 变形：
    • parodies
    • parodied , parodying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

parody [ 'pærədi ]

  • n.
    • a composition that imitates somebody's style in a humorous way

      同义词：lampoon | spoof | sendup | mockery | takeoff | burlesque | travesty | charade | pasquinade | put-on

    • humorous or satirical mimicry

      同义词：mockery | takeoff

  • v.
    • make a spoof of or make fun of
    • make a parody of

      同义词：spoof | burlesque

戏仿
