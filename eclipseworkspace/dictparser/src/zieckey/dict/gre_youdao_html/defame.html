defame [di'feim]

  • vt.
    • 破坏(或毁坏)…的名誉(或荣誉、声誉)：诽谤，诬蔑，诋毁，中伤：

      • The newspaper editorial defamed the politician.

        该报社论诬蔑这名政治家。

      • We expect politicians to defame each other in an election year.

        我们预料政客们在选举年会互相诽谤。

    • [古语]使丢脸，使失面子，使出丑，丑化，使不名誉
    • [古语]控告，指控，对…起诉；指责
    • 变形：
    • defamed , defaming
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

defame [ di'feim ]

  • v. charge falsely or with malicious intent; attack the good name and reputation of someone

    "The journalists have defamed me!"

    同义词：slander | smirch | asperse | denigrate | calumniate | smear | sully | besmirch

以上来源于: WordNet
