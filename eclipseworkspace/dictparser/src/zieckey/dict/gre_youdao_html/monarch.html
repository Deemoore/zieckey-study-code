monarch ['mɔnək; -ɑ:k]

  • n.
    • 君主；国王；皇帝；女皇；最高统治者
    • 王，大王
    • 帝王般的人(或物)
    • 【昆虫】大斑蝶(Danaus plexippus)[亦作 monarch butterfly]
  • vi.
    • [常与 it连用]称王
  • 短语:
    • be monarch of all one surveys 处于绝对优势的主宰地位，做当然的主宰
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

monarch [ 'mɔnək, -ɑ:k ]

  • n.
    • a nation's ruler or head of state usually by hereditary right

      同义词：sovereign | crowned head

    • large migratory American butterfly having deep orange wings with black and white markings; the larvae feed on milkweed

      同义词：monarch butterfly | milkweed butterfly | Danaus plexippus
