rage [reidʒ]

  • n.
    • 盛怒，狂怒
    • (风、浪等的)狂暴，凶猛
    • 激情；狂热；狂欢，作乐；聚会
    • 风行一时的事物
    • [废语]精神错乱
    • 洪水
  • vi.
    • 发怒，恼火：

      • She raged against her husband for some household affairs.

        她为一些家庭琐事对丈夫大发雷霆。

    • (风浪等的)狂暴，激烈，凶猛：

      • The forest fire has raged for several days.

        森林大火已经燃烧了好几天。

      • The wind is raging wildly,and we'd better go home at once.

        风刮得太大了，我们最好赶快回家。

    • (灾、病等的)猖獗；流行：

      • Flu raged throughout the city.

        流行性感冒蔓延全市。

    • 狂欢，作乐
  • 近义词: anger , fashion
  • 短语:
    • all the rage [口语]流行起来；(成为)时尚
    • boil with rage 怒火中烧
    • fly into a rage 勃然大怒
    • have a rage for 对…非常爱好；对…有特殊嗜好
    • rage itself out (暴风雨等)平息下来：
    • turn purple with rage 气得脸发紫
    • vent one's rage on… 泄怒于…
    • 变形：
    • raged , raging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

rage [ reidʒ ]

  • n.
    • a feeling of intense anger

      "his face turned red with rage"

      同义词：fury | madness

    • a state of extreme anger

      "she fell into a rage and refused to answer"

    • something that is desired intensely

      "his rage for fame destroyed him"

      同义词：passion

    • violent state of the elements

      "the sea hurled itself in thundering rage against the rocks"

    • an interest followed with exaggerated zeal

      "it was all the rage that season"

      同义词：fad | craze | furor | furore | cult

  • v.
    • behave violently, as if in state of a great anger

      同义词：ramp | storm

    • be violent; as of fires and storms
    • feel intense anger
