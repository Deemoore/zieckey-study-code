limp1 [limp]

  • vi.
    • 一瘸一拐地走，跛行，蹒跚：

      • The old man limped away on the stick.

        老人拄着拐杖一瘸一拐地走开了。

    • 慢腾腾地进行；缓慢费力地进行：

      • The damaged ship limped into the harbour.

        受损的船艰难地驶进港口。

    • 进展缓慢；几乎无进展；停滞不前
    • (诗歌)韵律紊乱
  • n.
    • 跛行
    • 慢腾腾的前进

    • 柔软的；易曲的；松沓的
    • 疲倦的；疲乏的；劳顿的；无生气的；软绵绵的
    • (性格)软弱的
    • (封面装订)软面的；非硬纸板的
    • 变形：
    • limper , limpest

limp [ limp ]

  • n. the uneven manner of walking that results from an injured leg

    同义词：hitch | hobble

  • v.
    • walk impeded by some physical limitation or injury

      同义词：gimp | hobble | hitch

    • proceed slowly or with difficulty

      "the boat limped into the harbor"

  • adj.
    • lacking in strength or firmness or resilience

      "gave a limp handshake"; " a limp gesture as if waving away all desire to know"

      同义词：flaccid | lax | slack

    • not firm

      同义词：wilted

以上来源于: WordNet
