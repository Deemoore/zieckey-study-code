contingent [kən'tindʒənt]

  • adj.
    • 因情况而异的，视条件而定的
    • 偶然发生的，意外的，原因不明的
    • 可能发生的，可能的
    • 【逻辑学】(命题)既非逻辑上必然的，也不是逻辑上不可能的
    • 【哲学】不受宿命论左右的；并非被迫的
  • n.
    • 偶然事件，意外事件
    • 小分队，分遣队；(派遣的)代表团
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

contingent [ kən'tindʒənt ]

  • n.
    • a gathering of persons representative of some larger group

      "each nation sent a contingent of athletes to the Olympics"

    • a temporary military unit

      "the peacekeeping force includes one British contingent"

      同义词：detail

  • adj.
    • possible but not certain to occur

      "they had to plan for contingent expenses"

    • determined by conditions or circumstances not yet established

      "arms sales contingent on the approval of congress"

      同义词：contingent on(p) | dependent on(p) | dependant on(p) | depending on(p)

    • uncertain because of uncontrollable circumstances

      "the results of confession were not contingent, they were certain"

以上来源于: WordNet
