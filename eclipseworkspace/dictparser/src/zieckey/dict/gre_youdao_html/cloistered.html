cloistered ['klɔistəd]

基本释义 同近义词

  • adj. 隐居的，与世隔绝的
  • v. 使与尘世隔绝；在…设回廊（cloister的过去分词）
  • adj. 隐居的，与世隔绝的

    • reclusive , enclosed

cloistered [ 'klɔistəd ]

  • adj.
    • of communal life sequestered from the world under religious vows

      同义词：cloistral | conventual | monastic | monastical

    • providing privacy or seclusion

      "the cloistered academic world of books"

      同义词：reclusive | secluded | sequestered
