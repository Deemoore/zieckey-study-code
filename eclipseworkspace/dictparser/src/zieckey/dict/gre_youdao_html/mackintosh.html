mackintosh ['mækintɔʃ]

  • n.
    • 胶布雨衣
    • 防水胶布

mackintosh [ 'mækintɔʃ ]

  • n.
    • a lightweight waterproof (usually rubberized) fabric

      同义词：macintosh

    • a waterproof raincoat made of rubberized fabric

      同义词：macintosh | mac | mack
