menace ['menəs]

  • n.
    • 威胁；恐吓；恐吓行为
    • 威胁性言行；具有危害的人；危险物品
    • [口语]讨厌的人(或事物)；引起烦扰者
  • vt.,vi.
    • 威胁；恐吓
    • (使)遭受危险
    • 变形：
    • menaced-acing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

menace [ 'menəs ]

  • n.
    • something that is a source of danger

      同义词：threat

    • a threat or the act of threatening

      "he spoke with desperate menace"

  • v.
    • pose a threat to; present a danger to

      同义词：endanger | jeopardize | jeopardise | threaten | imperil | peril

    • express a threat either by an utterance or a gesture

      "he menaced the bank manager with a stick"

    • act in a threatening manner

以上来源于: WordNet
