detritus [di'traitəs]

  • n.
    • 【地质学】岩屑，碎石，碎岩，碎屑
    • 瓦砾(堆)；碎片，屑粒
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

detritus [ di'traitəs ]

  • n.
    • the remains of something that has been destroyed or broken up

      同义词：debris | dust | junk | rubble

    • loose material (stone fragments and silt etc) that is worn away from rocks

以上来源于: WordNet
