ballast ['bæləst]

  • n.
    • 【造船业、航空学】(船、飞机或车辆的)压载物，压舱物；镇重物；镇重沙袋
    • (性格、品格、人与人的关系等的)稳定力量，稳定因素；镇定物
    • 【交通】(铁路)道砟
    • 【建筑学】(用以搅拌混凝土的)石渣，碎石
    • 【电学】镇流器 [亦作 ballast resistor]
  • vt.
    • 供以压舱物(或镇重物)；给(飞机)镇重；给(船只)压载；用压舱物(或镇重物)稳定：

      • They ballasted the ship.

        他们给船只装压舱物。

    • 为(铁路)铺道砟：

      • to ballast the railroad

        给铁路铺道砟

    • 使沉着，使稳定；使坚定：

      • parental responsibilities that ballast someone

        令某人稳重的父母职责

  • 短语:
    • in ballast
      • (船只)只装压舱物(或压载物)，空载
      • (为人)可靠；(性格)稳重，沉着，镇定
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ballast [ 'bæləst ]

  • n.
    • any heavy material used to stabilize a ship or airship
    • coarse gravel laid to form a bed for streets and railroads
    • an attribute that tends to give stability in character and morals; something that steadies the mind or feelings
    • a resistor inserted into a circuit to compensate for changes (as those arising from temperature fluctuations)

      同义词：ballast resistor | barretter

    • an electrical device for starting and regulating fluorescent and discharge lamps

      同义词：light ballast

  • v. make steady with a ballast
