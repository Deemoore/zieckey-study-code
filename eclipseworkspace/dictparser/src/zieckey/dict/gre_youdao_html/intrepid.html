intrepid [in'trepid]

  • adj. 刚毅的；勇猛的；无敌的；无畏的；坚忍不拔的
  • 近义词: brave
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

intrepid [ in'trepid ]

  • adj. invulnerable to fear or intimidation

    "intrepid pioneers"

    同义词：audacious | brave | dauntless | fearless | unfearing
