grate1 [ɡreit]

  • n.
    • (壁炉、炉子等装燃料的)炉栅，炉格，炉箅，炉床，炉排
    • 壁炉，火炉；烤炉
    • (窗、门等的)铁格栅；栅板；滤栅
    • 【采矿】护排(箅)；箅条筛，固定筛
    • [废语]牢笼
  • vt.
    • 为…装炉栅(或炉格、炉箅)；装栅栏于：

      • to grate a window

        为窗户装铁栅栏

    • 变形：
    • grated , grating

grate2 [ɡreit]

  • vt.
    • 磨碎，碾碎，擦碎：

      • to grate cheese

        磨碎干酪

      • to grate a carrot

        把胡萝卜擦成细丝

    • 擦响，磨响；使发出摩擦声；用刺耳的声音说；磨(牙)：

      • to grate the teeth

        磨牙发出嘎嘎声

    • 激怒，使气恼：使生气；使焦急
    • [古语]擦伤，擦破；磨损，磨去
  • vi.
    • 使人烦躁；令人气恼：

      • Their rude manners grate on us.

        他们的粗鲁无礼激怒了我们。

    • 擦响，摩擦发声，摩擦得嘎嘎响，发嘎嘎声：

      • The door grated on its rusty hinges.

        这扇门因铰链生锈开关发出嘎嘎的声音。

    • 发刺耳声；使人产生不愉快的感觉：

      • The singer's high notes grated on the ears of her listeners.

        这位女歌唱家的高音调使听众感到刺耳。

    • 变形：
    • grated , grating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

grate [ ɡreit ]

  • n.
    • a frame of iron bars to hold a fire

      同义词：grating

    • a harsh rasping sound made by scraping something
    • a barrier that has parallel or crossed bars blocking a passage but admitting air

      同义词：grating

  • v.
    • furnish with a grate

      "a grated fireplace"

    • gnaw into; make resentful or angry

      同义词：eat into | fret | rankle

    • reduce to small shreds or pulverize by rubbing against a rough or sharp perforated surface

      "grate carrots and onions"; " grate nutmeg"

    • make a grating or grinding sound by rubbing together

      "grate one's teeth in anger"

      同义词：grind

    • scratch repeatedly

      同义词：scrape

以上来源于: WordNet
