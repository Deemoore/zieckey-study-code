manacle [ 'mænəkl ]

  • n. shackle that consists of a metal loop that can be locked around the wrist; usually used in pairs

    同义词：handcuff | cuff | handlock

  • v. confine or restrain with or as if with manacles or handcuffs

    同义词：cuff | handcuff
