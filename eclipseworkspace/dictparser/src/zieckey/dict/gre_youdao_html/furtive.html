  • adj.
    • 鬼鬼祟祟的，偷偷摸摸的；秘密的；私下的；暗中的
    • 狡猾的；偷来的
  • 近义词: se
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

furtive [ 'fə:tiv ]

  • adj.
    • marked by quiet and caution and secrecy; taking pains to avoid being observed

      "a furtive manner"

      同义词：sneak(a) | sneaky | stealthy | surreptitious

    • secret and sly or sordid

      "furtive behavior"

      同义词：backstair | backstairs
