cagey ['keidʒi]

  • adj.
    • 机警的；狡诈的
    • 小心谨慎的；谨防受骗的[亦作 cagy]
    • 变形：
    • cagier , cagiest
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cagey [ 'keidʒi ]

  • adj.
    • showing self-interest and shrewdness in dealing with others

      "a cagey lawyer"

      同义词：cagy | canny | clever

    • characterized by great cautious and wariness

      "a cagey avoidance of a definite answer"

      同义词：cagy | chary

以上来源于: WordNet
