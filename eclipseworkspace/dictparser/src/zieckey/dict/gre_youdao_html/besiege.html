besiege [bi'si:dʒ]

  • vt.
    • 围攻(城堡等)，包围，围困：

      • The troops besieged the fort.

        部队围攻城堡。

    • 团团围住，拥(或挤)在…的周围，群集：

      • Vacationers besieged the travel office.

        度假者把旅行社围了个水泄不通。

    • 被扰乱，被困扰，使烦恼：

      • a kind of loneliness that besieges the old woman

        困扰着这位老太婆的一种孤独感

    • 缠磨；扰得…应接不暇；用大量问题、请求或要求等缠着(某人)(常与with连用):

      • be besieged with queries

        被询问弄得应接不暇

      • He was besieged with requests for money.

        他被缠着要钱。

    • 变形：
    • besieged , besieging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

besiege [ bi'si:dʒ ]

  • v.
    • surround so as to force to give up

      "The Turks besieged Vienna"

      同义词：beleaguer | surround | hem in | circumvent

    • cause to feel distressed or worried

      "She was besieged by so many problems that she got discouraged"

    • harass, as with questions or requests

      "The press photographers besieged the movie star"

以上来源于: WordNet
