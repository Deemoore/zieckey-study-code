linger ['liŋɡə]

  • vi.
    • 徘徊；继续逗留；留恋；流连；闲荡：

      • The girl lingered by the lake until it was dark.

        女孩在湖畔一直徘徊到天黑。

    • 历久犹存；缓慢消失：

      • The beautiful music is still lingering in my ears.

        美妙的音乐依然在我耳际萦绕。

    • 苟延残喘：

      • He lingered several days after the car accident.

        他在车祸后拖了几天才死去。

    • 沉思；沉湎于
    • 延误；拖延；迟缓；磨蹭
    • 漫步；闲逛
  • vt.
    • 混日子，消磨，挨过(时间等)(常与 away 或 out 连用)：

      • The apprentice lingered his work.

        这个徒弟磨洋工。

      • The family linger away the whole summer vacation at a pleasure resort.

        这家人在一处游乐胜地消磨了整个暑假。

    • [口语]拖长，拖延
  • 近义词: stay
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

linger [ 'liŋɡə ]

  • v.
    • remain present although waning or gradually dying

      "Her perfume lingered on"

    • be about

      同义词：loiter | lounge | footle | lollygag | loaf | lallygag | hang around | mess about | tarry | lurk | mill about | mill around

    • leave slowly and hesitantly

      同义词：tarry

    • take one's time; proceed slowly

      同义词：dawdle

    • move to and fro

      "The shy student lingered in the corner"

      同义词：hover

以上来源于: WordNet
