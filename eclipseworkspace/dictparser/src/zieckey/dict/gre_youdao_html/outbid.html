outbid [,aut'bid]

  • vt.
    • (在拍卖等中)出价高于(某人)；(在投标时)开价低于(他人)
    • 超过
    • 【纸牌】争叫压过(对手)；低叫(手中牌)
    • 变形：
    • outbid , 或bade , outbidden或outbid , outbidding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

outbid [ ,aut'bid ]

  • v.
    • bid over an opponent's bid when one's partner has not bid or doubled
    • bid higher than others

以上来源于: WordNet
