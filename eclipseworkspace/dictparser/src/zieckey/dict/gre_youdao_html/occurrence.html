occurrence [ə'kə:rəns; -'kʌ-]

  • n.
    • 发生；出现；存在
    • [常用复数][英国英语]突发事件(包括伤害警察、自杀、突然死亡等)；发生的事物；(尤指)偶发事件，事变；事故
    • 【采矿】(矿床等的)存像，埋藏
  • 近义词: event , incident , episode , circumstance
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

occurrence [ ə'kə:rəns, -'kʌ- ]

  • n.
    • an event that happens

      同义词：happening | occurrent | natural event

    • an instance of something occurring

      "a disease of frequent occurrence"; " the occurrence (or presence) of life on other planets"
