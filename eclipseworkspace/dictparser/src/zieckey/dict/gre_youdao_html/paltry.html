paltry ['pɔ:ltri]

  • adj.
    • 无价值的；微不足道的；次要的
    • 可鄙的，轻蔑的
  • 近义词: petty
    • 变形：
    • paltrier , paltriest
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

paltry [ 'pɔ:ltri ]

  • adj.
    • not worth considering

      "he considered the prize too paltry for the lives it must cost"

      同义词：negligible | trifling

    • contemptibly small in amount

      "a paltry wage"

      同义词：measly | miserable
