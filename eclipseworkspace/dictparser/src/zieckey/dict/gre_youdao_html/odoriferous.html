odoriferous [,əudə'rifərəs]

  • adj.
    • 散发气味(尤指香味)的；香的
    • 发臭味的，臭的
    • (道德上)不良的，令人反感的

odoriferous [ ,əudə'rifərəs ]

  • adj.
    • morally offensive

      "odoriferous legislation"

    • giving off an odor

      "the odoriferous elements in perfume"

    • emitting an odor

      同义词：odorous

    • having a natural fragrance

      "odoriferous spices"

      同义词：odorous | perfumed | scented | sweet | sweet-scented | sweet-smelling
