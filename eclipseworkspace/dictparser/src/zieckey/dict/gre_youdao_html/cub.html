cub [kʌb]

  • n.
    • (狼、狐、熊、虎、鲸等的)幼仔；幼鲨
    • 没经验的年轻人；不懂事的小伙子
    • 新手，初学者；学徒
    • [C-] =Cub Scout
    • =sub reporter
  • vi.
    • 当年轻的记者；(狼、狐、熊、虎、鲸等)生幼仔
    • 猎幼狐
    • 变形：
    • cubbed , cubbing

cub.

cub [ kʌb ]

  • n.
    • an awkward and inexperienced youth

      同义词：greenhorn | rookie

    • a male child (a familiar term of address to a boy)

      同义词：lad | laddie | sonny | sonny boy

    • the young of certain carnivorous mammals such as the bear or wolf or lion

      同义词：young carnivore

  • v. give birth to cubs

    "bears cub every year"
