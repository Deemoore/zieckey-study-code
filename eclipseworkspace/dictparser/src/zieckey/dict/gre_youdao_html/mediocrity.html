  • n.
    • 平庸；平凡
    • 平庸的才能；平凡的成就
    • 庸人；平凡的人

mediocrity [ ,mi:di'ɔkrəti, ,med- ]

  • n.
    • ordinariness as a consequence of being average and not outstanding

      同义词：averageness

    • a person of second-rate ability or value

      同义词：second-rater
