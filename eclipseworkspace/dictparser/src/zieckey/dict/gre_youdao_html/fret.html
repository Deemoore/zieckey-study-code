fret1 [fret]

  • vt.
    • 使烦躁，使苦恼，使焦急，折磨：

      • Doubts began to fret his mind.

        疑惑使他烦躁不安。

      • You are fretting yourself needlessly.

        你在不必要地折磨自己。

    • 侵蚀，腐蚀，使耗损，磨损，擦损，摩擦：

      • Rust frets metal.

        锈腐蚀金属。

      • Constant use had fretted the sandal strap to the breaking point.

        凉鞋由于经常穿，鞋带快磨断了。

    • 磨损成，(经侵蚀而)形成(通道)
    • 使(水面)起波浪(或起涟漪)：

      • wind fretting the water

        使水面起涟漪的风

  • vi.
    • 烦躁，不安；苦恼，发愁：

      • What are you fretting about?

        你为何烦恼?

      • Don't fret, all will be well.

        不用愁，一切都会好的。

    • 被腐蚀；被侵蚀：

      • acids that fret at the strongest metals

        能腐蚀最强金属的酸

    • 摩擦，耗损，磨损(常与 away 连用)：

      • to fret away under constant wear

        由于经常穿戴逐渐被磨损掉了

    • 侵蚀成通道；(通过腐蚀、侵蚀等)打通，凿穿：

      • The river frets at its banks until a new channel is formed.

        那条河侵蚀河岸直到一条新的河道形成。

    • (水面)起波浪
  • n.
    • 恼怒，烦闷，焦急，焦躁，担忧
    • 磨损；腐蚀；侵蚀
    • 被侵蚀之处；磨损处
  • 短语:
    • fret and fume 恼火，焦急
    • fret one's gizzard 自寻烦恼，发愁
    • 变形：
    • fretted , fretting

  • vt.

fret3 [fret]

    • 【音乐】
  • n.
    • (斑卓琴、吉他、曼陀林琴等弦乐器指板上定音的)档子，品，柱，桥，马
  • vt.
    • 给(弦乐器)装品、档子、柱、马、桥，把(乐器弦线)压在定音品上
  • vi.
    • (弦乐器)装品定音
    • 变形：
    • fretted , fretting

fret [ fret ]

  • n.
    • agitation resulting from active worry

      同义词：stew | sweat | lather | swither

    • a spot that has been worn away by abrasion or erosion

      同义词：worn spot

    • an ornamental pattern consisting of repeated vertical and horizontal lines (often in relief)

      "there was a simple fret at the top of the walls"

      同义词：Greek fret | Greek key | key pattern

    • a small bar of metal across the fingerboard of a musical instrument; when the string is stopped by a finger at the metal bar it will produce a note of the desired pitch
  • v.
    • worry unnecessarily or excessively

      同义词：fuss | niggle

    • be agitated or irritated

      "don't fret over these small details"

    • provide (a musical instrument) with frets

      "fret a guitar"

    • become or make sore by or as if by rubbing

      同义词：chafe | gall

    • cause annoyance in
    • gnaw into; make resentful or angry

      同义词：eat into | rankle | grate

    • carve a pattern into
    • decorate with an interlaced design
    • be too tight; rub or press

      同义词：choke | gag

    • cause friction

      同义词：rub | fray | chafe | scratch

    • remove soil or rock

      同义词：erode | eat away

    • wear away or erode

      同义词：eat away
