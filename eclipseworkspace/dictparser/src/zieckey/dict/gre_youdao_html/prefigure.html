prefigure [pri:'fiɡə; -ɡjuə]

  • vt.
    • 预示；预兆：

      • This visit may prefigure an improvement in relations between the two countries.

        这次访问可能预示两国关系的改进。

    • 预想；预测：

      • Few writers care to prefigure the future.

        很少有作家对预测将来感兴趣。

    • 变形：
    • prefigured , prefiguring
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prefigure [ pri:'fiɡə, -ɡjuə ]

  • v.
    • imagine or consider beforehand

      "It wasn't as bad as I had prefigured"

    • indicate by signs

      同义词：bode | portend | auspicate | prognosticate | omen | presage | betoken | foreshadow | augur | foretell | forecast | predict
