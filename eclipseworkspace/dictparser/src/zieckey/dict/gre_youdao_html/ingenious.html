ingenious [in'dʒi:njəs]

  • adj.
    • 机灵的，机智的；有独创性的；足智多谋的
    • 制作精巧的；(方法等)巧妙的
    • [废语]有天才的，有才华的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ingenious [ in'dʒi:njəs ]

  • adj.
    • (used of persons or artifacts) marked by independence and creativity in thought or action

      "the invention of the knitting frame by another ingenious English clergyman"; " an ingenious device"

      同义词：imaginative | inventive

    • skillful (or showing skill) in adapting means to ends

      "an ingenious press agent"; " an ingenious scheme"

      同义词：adroit | clever

    • showing inventiveness and skill

      "an ingenious solution to the problem"

      同义词：clever | cunning

以上来源于: WordNet
