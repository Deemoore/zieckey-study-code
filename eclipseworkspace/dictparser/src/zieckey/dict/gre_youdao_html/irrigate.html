irrigate ['iriɡeit]

  • vt.
    • 灌溉：

      • to irrigate a farmland

        灌溉农田

    • 【医学】冲洗(伤口等)：

      • to irrigate one's throat with hot water

        用热水冲洗喉咙

    • 滋润，使清晰：

      • He irrigates his mind by reading widely.

        他通过广泛阅读来丰富自己的思想。

  • vi.
    • 灌溉：

      • The farmer is irrigating in his field.

        那个农夫正在田里灌溉。

    • [美国俚语]饮酒；酗酒
    • 变形：
    • irrigated , irrigating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

irrigate [ 'iriɡeit ]

  • v.
    • supply with water, as with channels or ditches or streams

      同义词：water

    • supply with a constant flow or sprinkling of some liquid, for the purpose of cooling, cleansing, or disinfecting

      "irrigate the wound"
