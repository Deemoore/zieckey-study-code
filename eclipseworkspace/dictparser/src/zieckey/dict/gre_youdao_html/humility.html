humility [ hju:'miləti ]

  • n.
    • a disposition to be humble; a lack of false pride

      "not everyone regards humility as a virtue"

      同义词：humbleness

    • a humble feeling

      "he was filled with humility at the sight of the Pope"

      同义词：humbleness
