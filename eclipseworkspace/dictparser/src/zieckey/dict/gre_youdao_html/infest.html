infest [in'fest]

  • vt.
    • (害虫、盗贼等)大批出没于，成群活动于；侵扰；蔓延：

      • places infested by snakes

        蛇大批出没的地方

      • to be infested with pirates

        海盗横行

    • 寄生于：

      • fleas infesting dogs

        寄生于狗身上的跳蚤

    • 遍布于：

      • The landscape is drab and infested with brick ovens.

        景色单调，惟见砖窑遍布。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

infest [ in'fest ]

  • v.
    • invade in great numbers

      "the roaches infested our kitchen"

      同义词：overrun

    • occupy in large numbers or live on a host

      "the Kudzu plant infests much of the South and is spreading to the North"

      同义词：invade | overrun

    • live on or in a host, as of parasites

以上来源于: WordNet
