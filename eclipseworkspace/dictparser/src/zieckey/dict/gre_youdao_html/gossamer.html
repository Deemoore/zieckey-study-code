gossamer ['ɡɔsəmə]

  • n.
    • 蛛丝，游丝
    • 一网蛛丝；一根游丝
    • [美国英语]轻而薄的雨衣
    • 薄纱，轻而薄的织物
    • 轻飘似蛛丝的东西；脆弱似蛛丝的东西
  • adj.
    • 如游丝(般)的，蛛丝(似)的；极轻而薄的；纤细的，虚无飘渺的
    • 脆弱的,薄弱的[亦作 gossamered, gossamery]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

gossamer [ 'ɡɔsəmə ]

  • n.
    • a gauze fabric with an extremely fine texture
    • filaments from a web that was spun by a spider

      同义词：cobweb

  • adj.
    • characterized by unusual lightness and delicacy

      "gossamer shading through his playing"

      同义词：ethereal

    • so thin as to transmit light

      "gossamer cobwebs"

      同义词：diaphanous | filmy | gauzy | gauze-like | see-through | sheer | transparent | vaporous | vapourous | cobwebby

以上来源于: WordNet
