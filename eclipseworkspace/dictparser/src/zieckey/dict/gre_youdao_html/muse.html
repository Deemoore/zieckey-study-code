muse [mju:z]

  • vi.
    • 沉思,冥想(与about, on, upon, over连用)：

      • She mused on what she has heard.

        她仔细考虑自己听到的事。

      • He mused over memories of his youth.

        他重温青年时期的往事。

      • He mused about whether his proposal would be accepted.

        他思忖着他的建议是否会被接纳。

    • 若有所思地凝视；凝望；仔细端详 (与on， upon连用)：

      • to muse upon a distant scene

        凝望远处的风景

    • [古语]感到惊讶；感到惊异(与at连用)
  • vt.
    • 沉思；冥想；反复思考：

      • She has mused that question once more.

        她已经又一次仔细思考了那个问题。

    • 沉思地说：

      • I could do it, he mused.

        我能做这件事，他沉思着。

    • [废语]使…为难
  • n.
    • 沉思；冥想；默想
    • [废语]惊讶；惊异
  • 近义词: ponder
    • 变形：
    • mused , musing

Muse [mju:z]

  • n.
    • [the Muses] 缪斯(九位文艺和科学女神的通称)
    • [(the) muse] 给予诗人灵感的女神；诗人(或其他艺术家)的灵感
    • [亦作m-] [罕用语]诗人
  • 短语:
    • meditate the muse 把精神放在作诗上
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

muse [ mju:z ]

  • n.
    • in ancient Greek mythology any of 9 daughters of Zeus and Mnemosyne; protector of an art or science
    • the source of an artist's inspiration

      "Euterpe was his muse"

  • v. reflect deeply on a subject

    同义词：chew over | think over | meditate | ponder | excogitate | contemplate | reflect | mull | mull over | ruminate | speculate
