niche [ni:ʃ; nitʃ]

  • n.
    • 壁龛；类似壁龛的一隅
    • 合适的职务(或地位等)
    • [亦称作 niche market]【商业】有利可图的市场(或形势等)
    • 【生态学】生态龛
  • adj.
    • 【商业】有利可图市场的
  • vt.
    • [常用被动语态]把…放置在壁龛中：

      • to niche a small vase

        把一只小巧的花瓶放在壁龛中

    • [niche oneself]把(自己)安置在适当的地位
  • 短语:
    • have a niche in the temple of fame 流芳千古
    • 变形：
    • niched , niching
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

niche [ ni:ʃ, nitʃ ]

  • n.
    • a position particularly well suited to the person who occupies it

      "he found his niche in the academic world"

    • a small concavity

      同义词：recess | recession | corner

    • an enclosure that is set back or indented

      同义词：recess

    • (ecology) the status of an organism within its environment and community (affecting its survival as a species)

      同义词：ecological niche
