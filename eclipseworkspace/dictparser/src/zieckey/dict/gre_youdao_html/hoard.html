hoard1 [hɔ:d]

  • n.
    • 隐藏，贮藏
    • 聚藏的钱财；贮藏物；秘藏物
    • (知识等的)宝库；(古代的)宝窟
  • vt.
    • 聚藏，贮藏；隐藏：

      • How many curios do you hoard in your study?

        你在你书房里聚藏了多少古玩?

      • to hoard one's real goal

        隐藏真实目的

  • vi.
    • 积聚钱财；贮藏货物：

      • The police found much heroin hoarded in his store.

        警方找到了他藏在他店中的大量的海洛因。

hoard2 [hɔ:d]

  • n. 【建筑学】临时围篱；板围
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

hoard [ hɔ:d ]

  • n. a secret store of valuables or money

    同义词：cache | stash

  • v.
    • save up as for future use

      同义词：stash | cache | lay away | hive up | squirrel away

    • get or gather together

      同义词：roll up | collect | accumulate | pile up | amass | compile
