  • n.
    • (文章、论点等的)要旨，要领，本质，实质
    • 【法律】(诉讼的)依据，主因
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

gist [ dʒist ]

  • n.
    • the central meaning or theme of a speech or literary work

      同义词：effect | essence | burden | core

    • the choicest or most essential or most vital part of some idea or experience

      "the gist of the prosecutor's argument"

      同义词：kernel | substance | core | center | essence | heart | heart and soul | inwardness | marrow | meat | nub | pith | sum | nitty-gritty
