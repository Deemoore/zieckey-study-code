preclude [pri'klu:d]

  • vt.
    • 使不可能；(尤指)预防；排除；消除：

      • Their move does not preclude others from investing.

        他们这一行动并不影响其他人进行投资。

    • 阻止，妨碍：

      • The temporary cease-fire agreement does not preclude possible retaliatory attacks later.

        临时停火协议不能防止以后发生报复性攻击的可能性。

    • 消灭；杜绝；消除：

      • so as to preclude all doubts

        为了消除疑点

  • 近义词: prevent
    • 变形：
    • precluded , precluding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

preclude [ pri'klu:d ]

  • v.
    • keep from happening or arising; make impossible

      "Your role in the projects precludes your involvement in the competitive project"

      同义词：prevent | forestall | foreclose | forbid

    • make impossible, especially beforehand

      同义词：rule out | close out
