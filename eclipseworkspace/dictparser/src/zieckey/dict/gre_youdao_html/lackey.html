lackey ['læki]

  • n.
    • (穿制服的)男仆，仆人；跟班
    • 走狗；马屁精；谄媚者；卑躬屈膝者
  • vt.,vi.
    • [罕用语]伺候；服侍；巴结[亦作lacquey]
    • 变形：
    • lackeys
    • lackeyed , lackeying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

lackey [ 'læki ]

  • n.
    • a male servant (especially a footman)

      同义词：flunky | flunkey

    • a person who tries to please someone in order to gain a personal advantage

      同义词：sycophant | toady | crawler
