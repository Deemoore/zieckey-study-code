impound [im'paund]

  • vt.
    • 把(牲畜等)关在圈中：

      • to impound stray horses

        把走失的马关在圈中

    • 把…围起
    • 没收；扣留：

      • ships that are impounded

        被扣留的船只

    • 蓄(水等)：

      • to build a dam to impound water

        建造水坝蓄水

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

impound [ im'paund ]

  • v.
    • take temporary possession of as a security, by legal authority

      "The customs agents impounded the illegal shipment"

      同义词：attach | sequester | confiscate | seize

    • place or shut up in a pound

      同义词：pound

以上来源于: WordNet
