plot1 [plɔt]

  • n.
    • 小块土地，小块地皮；一小块地上的作物
    • 基址，地基
    • 小区，小地段
    • (建筑物等的)平面图；标绘图；基址图；(曲线等)图表；【军事】(地图上炮击目标的)标定点；
    • (戏剧、小说等的)情节
    • 阴谋(事件)；策划，秘密计划
    • 【军事】(炮兵)测算表
  • vt.
    • 绘制…的地图；标绘出…的位置：

      • The prisoners plotted an escape route.

        囚犯画出了逃跑路线。

    • 把(土地)分成小块，划分；规划：

      • New factory's district is all plotted out.

        新厂区的范围都已划定。

    • 密谋；策划：

      • They are plotting his murder.

        他们正密谋杀害他。

    • 为(文学作品)设计情节，构思：

      • He plotted his play carefully.

        他仔细地为他的剧本构思。

    • 测定(点线的)位置；作图表示方程式；画曲线：

      • The technician plotted the thermal conductivity versus mean temperature.

        技术人员勾绘出导热率对应平均温度的曲线。

  • vi.
    • 计划；密谋：

      • They are plotting against him.

        他们正密谋反对他。

    • 设计作品情节：

      • He plots better than most novelists.

        他设计作品的情节比许多小说作家要好。

    • 标绘；作图；用坐标确定位置，坐标显示：

      • The data plotted at a single point.

        数据在单独一点上标出来了。

  • 近义词: intrigue , machination , conspiracy , cable
  • 短语:
    • The plot thickens. [口语]情节(或事情)愈来愈复杂了。
    • 变形：
    • plotted , plotting

plot [ plɔt ]

  • n.
    • a secret scheme to do something (especially something underhand or illegal)

      "they concocted a plot to discredit the governor"

      同义词：secret plan | game

    • a small area of ground covered by specific vegetation

      "a bean plot"

      同义词：plot of ground | patch

    • the story that is told in a novel or play or movie etc.

      "the characters were well drawn but the plot was banal"

    • a chart or map showing the movements or progress of an object
  • v.
    • plan secretly, usually something illegal

      "They plotted the overthrow of the government"

    • make a schematic or technical drawing of that shows how things work or how they are constructed

      同义词：diagram

    • make a plat of

      同义词：plat

以上来源于: WordNet
