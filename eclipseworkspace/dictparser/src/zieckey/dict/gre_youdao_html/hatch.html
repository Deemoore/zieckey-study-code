hatch1 [hætʃ]

  • vt.
    • 孵，孵化:

      • to hatch eggs

        孵蛋，抱窝

    • 孵出:

      • A goose was hatched among ducks.

        在一群小鸭中孵出了一只小鹅。

    • 图谋，策划；筹划；使产生，创造:

      • to hatch a scheme

        策划阴谋

      • They are hatching a plot.

        他们在搞阴谋。

  • vi.
    • (雌禽)抱窝；(卵)孵化；(小鸡等)孵出:

      • The baby birds have hatched.

        幼鸟已出壳了。

  • n.
    • 孵；孵化
    • (小鸡等的)一窝，一孵
    • (昆虫的)羽化
    • 结果，结局
  • 短语:
    • hatched, matched, and despatched (完全)结束，终了
    • the hatches, (catches) matches and despatches (报纸上的)出生、(订婚)、结婚、死亡通告栏

hatch2 [hætʃ]

  • n.
    • 【航海学】
      • (甲板的)升降口，舱口，入口[亦作 hatchway]
      • 舱口盖，升降口盖
    • 天窗，小窗口；(地板上的)开口
    • 地窖盖；格子门，小门，活板门
    • (上下开关门的)下半扇
    • 【航空学】(飞机或飞船等的)舱口，出口，开口
    • (食物贮藏等的)小室，小间，储藏室
    • 闸门，水闸
    • 鱼栏
    • (食堂的)卖饭口
    • [口语](人的)嘴
  • vt.
    • 用舱盖(或活板门等)盖住，盖上
  • 短语:
    • Down the hatch! [口语]干杯!
    • under hatches
      • 在甲板下
      • 被埋没
      • 被监禁；在困苦中
      • 受压制
      • 埋葬，死

hatch [ hætʃ ]

  • n.
    • the production of young from an egg

      同义词：hatching

    • shading consisting of multiple crossing lines

      同义词：hatching | crosshatch | hachure

    • a movable barrier covering a hatchway
  • v.
    • emerge from the eggs

      "young birds, fish, and reptiles hatch"

    • devise or invent

      同义词：think up | think of | dream up | concoct

    • inlay with narrow strips or lines of a different substance such as gold or silver, for the purpose of decorating
    • draw, cut, or engrave lines, usually parallel, on metal, wood, or paper

      "hatch the sheet"

    • sit on (eggs)

      同义词：brood | cover | incubate
