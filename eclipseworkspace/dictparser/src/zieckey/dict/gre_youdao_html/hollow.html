hollow ['hɔləu]

  • adj.
    • 空的，空心的，中空的；不坚实的
    • 坑洼的；有洞的
    • (两眼)凹陷的；凹(面)的，凹形的
    • (声音)空洞的，沉闷的
    • 空腹的，饥饿的
    • 无内容的，无价值的；无意义的
    • 虚伪的，不诚实的
    • [美国口语]全的
  • n.
    • 中空，空心
    • 破洞，穿孔
    • 凹陷处；凹地，洼地；坑，洞，穴
    • 山谷，溪谷；盆地
  • adv.
    • 空洞无物地，乏味地；无用地
    • [美国口语]完全，彻底
  • vt.
    • 挖空，使中空；使成凹形(常与 out 连用)：

      • He hollowed out a cave in the snowfield for night.

        他在雪原上挖了个洞以过夜。

  • vi.
    • 变空；凹陷：

      • This tree has hollowed.

        这棵树的芯已经空了。

  • 近义词: hole , vain
  • 短语:
    • beat all hollow [口语]把(某人)完全打败，大大超过(某人)；使…大为失色
    • in the hollow of someone's hand 在某人的手掌心里
    • ring hollow (话语)听着空洞(或不诚恳)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

hollow [ 'hɔləu ]

  • n.
    • a cavity or space in something

      "hunger had caused the hollows in their cheeks"

    • a small valley between mountains

      "he built himself a cabin in a hollow high up in the Appalachians"

      同义词：holler

    • a depression hollowed out of solid matter

      同义词：hole

  • v.
    • remove the inner part or the core of

      同义词：excavate | dig

    • remove the interior of

      "hollow out a tree trunk"

      同义词：hollow out | core out

  • adj.
    • not solid; having a space or gap or cavity

      "a hollow wall"; " a hollow tree"; " hollow cheeks"; " his face became gaunter and more hollow with each year"

    • deliberately deceptive

      "hollow (or false) promises"

      同义词：false

    • as if echoing in a hollow space

      "the hollow sound of footsteps in the empty ballroom"

    • devoid of significance or point

      "a hollow victory"

      同义词：empty | vacuous
