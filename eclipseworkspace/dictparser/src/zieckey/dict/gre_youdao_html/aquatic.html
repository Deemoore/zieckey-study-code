aquatic [ə'kwætik]

  • adj.
    • 水的，水产的，水生的；水栖的，栖于水中(或水面)的
    • 【体育】水上的，水中的；在水中(或水面)进行的
  • n.
    • 水生动物；水生植物
    • [复数，动词常用单数，也可用复数]水上运动
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

aquatic [ ə'kwætik ]

  • n. a plant that lives in or on water
  • adj.
    • relating to or consisting of or being in water

      "an aquatic environment"

    • operating or living or growing in water

      "boats are aquatic vehicles"; " water lilies are aquatic plants"; " fish are aquatic animals"
