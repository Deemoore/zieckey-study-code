disintegrate [dis'intiɡreit]

  • vi.
    • 粉碎，碎裂，分裂，分化，分解：

      • The old book is gradually disintegrating with age.

        这本旧书因长年翻阅正在逐渐破损。

      • At that speed the plane began to disintegrate.

        飞机以那种速度飞行时开始解体。

    • 瓦解，解体，崩溃：

      • Their marriage rapidly disintegrated.

        他们的婚姻迅速破裂了。

    • 【核物理学】蜕变；衰变，裂变：

      • U²³⁶disintegrates spontaneously into strontium and xenon.

        U²³⁶自发地裂变成锶和氙。

    • 【地质学】(岩石)崩解，剥蚀：

      • The older strata gradually disintegrated.

        年代较久的地层逐渐崩解了。

  • vt.
    • 使碎裂，使分裂，使粉碎；使分化，使分解：

      • The bomb disintegrated our building.

        炸弹把我们的大楼炸得粉碎。

    • 使瓦解，使解体，使崩溃：

      • They disintegrated the labour movement.

        他们瓦解了工人运动。

    • 【核物理学】使蜕变，使衰变，使裂变：

      • to disintegrate an atom

        使原子蜕变

    • 【地质学】使(岩石)崩解，使剥蚀：

      • Rocks are disintegrated by rain and frost.

        岩石被雨和霜剥蚀了。

  • 近义词: decay
    • 变形：
    • disintegrated , disintegrating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

disintegrate [ dis'intiɡreit ]

  • v.
    • break into parts or components or lose cohesion or unity

      "The material disintegrated"; " the group disintegrated after the leader died"

    • cause to undergo fission or lose particles
    • lose a stored charge, magnetic flux, or current

      "the particles disintegrated during the nuclear fission process"

      同义词：decay | decompose
