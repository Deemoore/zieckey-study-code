natty ['næti]

  • adj.
    • (衣着、外貌)整洁的，漂亮的
    • 利落的，灵巧的，灵活敏捷的
    • 变形：
    • nattier , nattiest

natty [ 'næti ]

  • adj. marked by up-to-dateness in dress and manners

    同义词：dapper | dashing | jaunty | raffish | rakish | spiffy | snappy | spruce
