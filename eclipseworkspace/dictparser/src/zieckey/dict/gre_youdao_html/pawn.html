  典当

典当（Pawn） 典当业是向抵押私人物品（不动产除外）的人提供资金周转的行业。一般按抵押品的实际价值打折扣借钱，并约期赎回。

pawn1 [pɔ:n]

  • n.
    • 当出物，抵押品
    • 典当，押当
    • 扣押物；人质
    • 典当的状态
    • 保证，担保
  • vt.
    • 当，典，把…抵押出去：

      • The unemployed reporter pawned his typewriter to pay the rent.

        失了业的记者典当了打字机用以交租。

    • 用…作担保(或抵押)：

      • pawn one's honour

        用名誉担保

      • I will pawn my life for her.

        我愿为她用生命作担保。

  • 近义词: pledge
  • 短语:
    • be at (或in, to) pawn 典当着；抵押出去
    • give (或put) something in pawn 典当掉，抵押掉
    • pawn one's word 许诺；答应；保证
    • pawn something off as 把某物冒充为…押(典)出去

pawn2 [pɔ:n]

  • n.
    • 兵；卒
    • (供人驱使的)爪牙；走狗
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

pawn [ pɔ:n ]

  • n.
    • an article deposited as security
    • a person used by another to gain an end

      同义词：instrument | cat's-paw

    • (chess) the least powerful piece; moves only forward and captures only to the side; it can be promoted to a more powerful piece if it reaches the 8th rank
    • borrowing and leaving an article as security for repayment of the loan
  • v. leave as a guarantee in return for money

    "pawn your grandfather's gold watch"

    同义词：soak | hock

以上来源于: WordNet
