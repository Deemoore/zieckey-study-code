rehash1 [,ri:'hæʃ]

  • vt.
    • 改作；再处理；改头换面地重复：

      • The spokesman has just rehashed the speech of the president.

        这位发言人只是稍加改动地又重复了一遍总统的演说词。

  • n.
    • ／'ri:hæʃ／ (旧材料等的)改作；重复；改头换面的作品；改写本

rehash2 ['ri:'hæʃ]

  • vt. 【计算机】改写，改作；再散列，重杂凑
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

rehash [ ,ri:'hæʃ ]

  • n. old material that is slightly reworked and used again

    "merely a dull rehash of his first novel"

  • v.
    • present or use over, with no or few changes
    • go back over

      同义词：retrograde | hash over

以上来源于: WordNet
