chorus ['kɔ:rəs]

  • n.
    • 合唱
    • 合唱队
    • 合唱曲(歌曲中的合唱部分)
    • 歌舞团
    • 异口同声，齐声(歌唱、说话、叫喊等)
    • (古希腊戏剧中的)颂歌；朗诵；歌曲；合唱队
    • (戏剧中的)解说者；朗诵开场白和收场白的角色
    • (歌曲或赞美诗中的)迭句；副歌；(爵士乐)序曲后的主调
  • vt.,vi.
    • 合唱；齐声朗诵；异口同声地说：

      • The birds were chorusing around us.

        鸟儿在我们周围齐声歌唱。

      • The papers all chorused the praise of the president.

        各报异口同声地对总统进行颂扬。

      • The students chorused their approval of the suggestion.

        同学们对该建议齐声表示赞成。

  • 短语:
    • in chorus 异口同声地，齐声地，大伙一齐，一致
    • swell the chorus 增加合唱的音量；参加合唱；附和别人意见
    • 变形：
    • choruses
    • chorused , chorusing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

chorus [ 'kɔ:rəs ]

  • n.
    • any utterance produced simultaneously by a group

      "a chorus of boos"

    • a group of people assembled to sing together
    • the part of a song where a soloist is joined by a group of singers

      同义词：refrain

    • a body of dancers or singers who perform together

      同义词：chorus line

    • a company of actors who comment (by speaking or singing in unison) on the action in a classical Greek play

      同义词：Greek chorus

  • v.
    • utter in unison

      "`yes,' the children chorused"

    • sing in a choir

      同义词：choir

以上来源于: WordNet
