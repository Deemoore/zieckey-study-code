plumb [plʌm]

  • n.
    • 铅锤;测锤;垂球[参较 plumb line]
    • 垂直(位置)，铅直
    • 钟锤
    • 铅弹
  • adj.
    • 垂直的
    • 完全的
    • 【板球】(三柱门)平直的
    • [口语]实在是，简直是
  • adv.
    • 垂直地
    • [口语]完全，全然；正
    • 立即，马上
  • vt.
    • 用铅锤测量：

      • The bathyscaphe has plumbed the depth of the Indian Ocean.

        深海潜水器探测印度洋的深度。

    • 探究，探索；查明；了解：

      • to plumb the mysteries of the universe

        探索宇宙的奥秘

    • 达到…的最低点：

      • This new play really plumbs the depths of unpleasantness.

        这出新戏实在令人讨厌极了。

    • 使垂直：

      • to plumb a wall

        用铅垂线检查墙的垂直度

    • 用铅封；灌铅以增加…的重量：

      • The luggage was plumbed by the customs inspector.

        行李由海关检查员加上了铅封。

    • [口语]当煤气、水管道的修理工人，当铅管工人：

      • I had a friend plumb his sink.

        我让一位朋友安装他的下水管道。

  • vi.
    • 垂直悬挂(或落下)：

      • The chimney plumbs perfectly.

        烟囱笔直地矗立着。

    • [口语]做铅管工
  • 近义词: vertical
  • 短语:
    • out of (或 off) plumb
      • 不垂直，倾斜
      • 不准确
      • 与…不一致(不符合)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

plumb [ plʌm ]

  • n. the metal bob of a plumb line

    同义词：plumb bob | plummet

  • v.
    • measure the depth of something
    • weight with lead
    • examine thoroughly and in great depth
    • adjust with a plumb line so as to make vertical
  • adj. exactly vertical

    "the tower of Pisa is far out of plumb"

  • adv.
    • completely; used as intensifiers

      "I'm plumb (or plum) tuckered out"

      同义词：clean | plum

    • conforming to the direction of a plumb line
    • exactly

      "fell plumb in the middle of the puddle"

      同义词：plum
