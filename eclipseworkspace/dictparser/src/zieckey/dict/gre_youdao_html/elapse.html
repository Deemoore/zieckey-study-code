elapse [i'læps]

  • vi.
    • (时间)过去，推移，(光阴)流逝，消逝，逝去：

      • Fifteen minutes elapsed before the performance began.

        过了15分钟演出才开始。

  • n.
    • (时间的)过去， (光阴的)流逝，消逝
    • 变形：
    • elapsed , elapsing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

elapse [ i'læps ]

  • v. pass by

    "three years elapsed"

    同义词：lapse | pass | slip by | glide by | slip away | go by | slide by | go along
