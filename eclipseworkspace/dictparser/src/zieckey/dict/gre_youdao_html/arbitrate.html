arbitrate ['ɑ:bitreit]

  • vt.
    • 把(分歧、争端等)交付裁决(或仲裁)，把…交付仲裁人决定；由公断解决，使…听任公断：

      • to arbitrate a dispute

        把争端交付裁决

    • 仲裁，裁决，公断：

      • to arbitrate a dispute regarding wages

        仲裁工资的争执

      • to arbitrate the quarrels between the countries

        裁决国家间的争执

    • [古语]决定
  • vi.
    • 以仲裁人(或公断人)行事(in a dispute,between persons):

      • to arbitrate between two parties in a dispute

        调停双方间的纠纷

    • (对争端)进行仲裁，做出公断：

      • to arbitrate between two persons in a quarrel

        为两个争吵的人进行仲裁

    • 提交裁决
    • 变形：
    • arbitrated , arbitrating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

arbitrate [ 'ɑ:bitreit ]

  • v. act between parties with a view to reconciling differences

    同义词：intercede | mediate | intermediate | liaise
