cozy ['kəuzi]

  • adj.
    • 温暖舒适的；安逸的
    • 相互勾结的；默契的
  • n.
    • (茶壶等的)保暖罩，保温棉垫
  • vi.
    • [口语]结交；奉承
  • vt.
    • 以假话使安心，蒙骗
    • 抚慰：

      • Grandma cozied Alice tenderly.

        奶奶亲切地抚慰爱丽丝。

  • 近义词: comfortable
  • 短语:
    • play it cozy [美国俚语]小心行事
    • 变形：
    • cozier , coziest
    • cozies
    • cozied , cozying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cozy [ 'kəuzi ]

  • n. a padded cloth covering to keep a teapot warm

    同义词：cosy | tea cosy | cosey | tea cosey | tea cozy | cozey | tea cozey | cozie | tea cozie

  • adj.
    • enjoying or affording comforting warmth and shelter especially in a small space

      "a cozy nook near the fire"

      同义词：cosy | snug

    • having or fostering a warm or friendly and informal atmosphere

      "had a cozy chat"; " the small room was cozy and intimate"

      同义词：intimate | informal

    • suggesting connivance

      "a cozy arrangement with the police"
