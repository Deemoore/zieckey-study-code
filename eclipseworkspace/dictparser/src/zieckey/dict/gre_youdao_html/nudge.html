nudge1 [nʌdʒ]

  • vt.
    • (用肘)轻推；轻撞：

      • He nudged me to tell me someone passing.

        他用肘轻轻碰了我一下，告诉我有人过来了。

    • 推进；刺激：

      • His plan nudged the information times coming early.

        他的规划促进了信息时代的早日到来。

      • to nudge one's fighting will

        激发斗志

    • 接近：

      • to nudge millions of people pouring into the square

        大约有上千万的人涌进了广场

  • vi.
    • 轻推；推进：

      • to nudge at someone in his rib

        用肘轻碰某人的腰

  • n.
    • 用肘轻推
    • 推动
  • 短语:
    • nudge one's way 挤进；推进
    • 变形：
    • nudged , nudging

nudge2 [nudʒ]

    • [美国俚语]
  • vt.
    • 向…不停地唠叨：

      • to nudge someone about something

        向某人絮叨某事

  • vi.
    • 唠叨，絮叨
  • n.
    • 没完没了抱怨的人；讨厌家伙
    • 变形：
    • nudged , nudging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

nudge [ nʌdʒ ]

  • n. a slight push or shake

    同义词：jog

  • v.
    • to push against gently

      "She nudged my elbow when she saw her friend enter the restaurant"

      同义词：poke at | prod

    • push into action by pestering or annoying gently

以上来源于: WordNet
