    • 太平间，停尸室
    • ＝funeral home
    • 遗产布施
  • adj.
    • 丧葬的；殡葬的
    • 悲哀的；阴郁的；死一般的
    • 变形：
    • mortuaries

mortuary [ 'mɔ:tjuəri, -tʃuə- ]

  • n. a building (or room) where dead bodies are kept before burial or cremation

    同义词：morgue | dead room

  • adj.
    • of or relating to or characteristic of death
    • of or relating to a funeral
