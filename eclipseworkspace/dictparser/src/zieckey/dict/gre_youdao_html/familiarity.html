familiarity [fə,mili'æriti]

  • n.
    • 亲密，亲近，亲热
    • 无拘无束，亲密无间
    • 随便，放肆，冒昧
    • [常用复数]放肆的言行
    • 放任(或不正当)的两性行为
    • 熟悉，通晓
  • 短语:
    • Familiarity breeds contempt. [谚语]亲不敬，熟生蔑。
    • 变形：
    • familiarities
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

familiarity [ fə,mili'æriti ]

  • n.
    • personal knowledge or information about someone or something

      同义词：acquaintance | conversance | conversancy

    • usualness by virtue of being familiar or well known
    • close or warm friendship

      同义词：intimacy | closeness

    • a casual manner

      同义词：casualness

    • an act of undue intimacy

      同义词：impropriety | indecorum | liberty

以上来源于: WordNet
