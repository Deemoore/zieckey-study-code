dimple [dimpl]

  • n.
    • 酒窝，笑窝，颊窝，靥；甜笑
    • (任何类似的)浅凹，小凹；(搪瓷、玻璃等上的)小坑
    • (水中的)微凹，波纹，涟漪
    • [澳大利亚俚语](鱼雷在船舷击出的)小洞
  • vt.
    • 使现酒窝(或笑窝，笑靥)：

      • A smile dimpled her face.

        一个微笑使她脸上露出笑靥。

    • 使起涟漪；使微凹：

      • The surface of the spring pond was dimpled by the breeze.

        一池春水被微风吹皱了。

    • 【金属加工】(在金属板上)作埋头孔口(以便使用埋头螺栓或埋头铆钉)
  • vi.
    • 现出(或形成)笑靥：

      • Her face dimpled as she smiled.

        她一笑，脸上露出了笑靥。

    • 起涟漪；形成浅凹(或微凹)
    • 变形：
    • dimpled , dimpling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dimple [ dimpl ]

  • n.
    • a chad that has been punched or dimpled but all four corners are still attached

      同义词：dimpled chad | pregnant chad

    • any slight depression in a surface

      "there are approximately 336 dimples on a golf ball"

    • a small natural hollow in the cheek or chin

      "His dimple appeared whenever he smiled"

  • v.
    • mark with, or as if with, dimples

      "drops dimpled the smooth stream"

    • produce dimples while smiling

      "The child dimpled up to the adults"
