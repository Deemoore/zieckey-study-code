empirical [em'pirikəl]

  • adj.
    • 以实验(或观察)为根据的；单凭经验的；经验主义的
    • 来自经验(或观察)的，经验(上)的
    • 江湖医生的，庸医的；单凭实际经验行医的，以实际经验为根据的[亦作 empiristic]
  • n.
    • 【统计学】事后概率;后验概率[参较 mathematical probability]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

empirical

  • adj.
    • derived from experiment and observation rather than theory

      "an empirical basis for an ethical theory"; " empirical laws"; " empirical data"; " an empirical treatment of a disease about which little is known"

      同义词：empiric

    • relying on medical quackery

      同义词：empiric
