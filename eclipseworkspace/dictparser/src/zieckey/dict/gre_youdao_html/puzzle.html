puzzle ['pʌzl]

  • vt.
    • 使迷惑，使为难，使困惑：

      • I am puzzled by his failure to reply.

        他不给我回信使我感到困惑。

    • 想出，试着解决，使苦思：

      • I've been puzzling over all the figures, trying to find what happened to the missing money.

        我一直在反复琢磨这些数字，力图找出少了钱的原因。

    • [古语]使复杂；使纠缠；使混乱
  • vi.
    • 感到迷惑(of)
    • 冥思苦想：

      • We are puzzling about the easiest way to cross the mountains.

        我们在苦思越过这座山的最佳路径。

  • 近义词: perplex , confuse
    • 变形：
    • puzzled , puzzling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

puzzle [ 'pʌzl ]

  • n.
    • a particularly baffling problem that is said to have a correct solution

      "he loved to solve chessmate puzzles"; " that's a real puzzler"

      同义词：puzzler | mystifier | teaser

    • a toy that tests your ingenuity
  • v.
    • be a mystery or bewildering to

      同义词：perplex | vex | stick | get | mystify | baffle | beat | pose | bewilder | flummox | stupefy | nonplus | gravel | amaze | dumbfound

    • be uncertain about; think about without fully understanding or being able to decide

      "We puzzled over her sudden departure"
