ascetic [ə'setik]

  • n.
    • 禁欲(主义)者
    • 苦行者；苦修者；修道者
    • (早期基督教的)遁世修道者
  • adj.
    • 禁欲(主义)的，制欲的，禁止享受的
    • 苦行(主义)的
    • 外貌似苦行者的
    • (态度、外貌方面)极其严肃的，认真的[亦作 ascetical]
  • 近义词: severe
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ascetic [ ə'setik ]

  • n. someone who practices self denial as a spiritual discipline

    同义词：abstainer

  • adj.
    • pertaining to or characteristic of an ascetic or the practice of rigorous self-discipline

      "ascetic practices"

      同义词：ascetical

    • practicing great self-denial

      "Be systematically ascetic...do...something for no other reason than that you would rather not do it"

      同义词：ascetical | austere | spartan

以上来源于: WordNet
