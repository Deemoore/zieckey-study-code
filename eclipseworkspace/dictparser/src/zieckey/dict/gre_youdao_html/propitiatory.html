  • vt.
    • 劝解；和解；抚慰；使息怒：

      • They offered sacrifices to propitiate the god.

        他们供奉祭品以求神息怒。

  • 近义词: pacify
    • 变形：

propitiatory [ prəu'piʃiətəri ]

  • adj.
    • having power to atone for or offered by way of expiation or propitiation

      "expiatory (or propitiatory) sacrifice"

      同义词：expiatory | expiative

    • intended to reconcile or appease

      "sent flowers as a propitiatory gesture"

      同义词：propitiative
