frolic ['frɔlik]

  • adj.
    • [古语]嬉戏的，欢乐的，欢闹的，兴高采烈的
  • n.
    • 嬉戏，闹着玩；作乐
    • 欢乐的聚会(或游戏)
  • vi.
    • 嬉戏，尽情欢乐；作乐，闹着玩；欢跳
  • 近义词: play
    • 变形：
    • frolicked , frolicking
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

frolic [ 'frɔlik ]

  • n. gay or light-hearted recreational activity for diversion or amusement

    "their frolic in the surf threatened to become ugly"

    同义词：play | romp | gambol | caper

  • v. play boisterously

    "The children frolicked in the garden"

    同义词：lark | rollick | skylark | disport | sport | cavort | gambol | frisk | romp | run around | lark about
