incrustation [,inkrʌs'teiʃən]

  • n.
    • 包外壳；包外皮；结硬壳
    • 硬壳；硬外皮
    • 沉积物；水垢，积垢
    • 【医学】(冻)痂；(皮肤的)鳞屑
    • 镶嵌；镶嵌物；(建筑物的)表面装饰，镶嵌物
    • (风俗习惯的)逐渐形成；沉淀形成[亦作 encrustation]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incrustation [ ,inkrʌs'teiʃən ]

  • n.
    • the formation of a crust

      同义词：encrustation

    • a hard outer layer that covers something

      同义词：crust | encrustation

    • a decorative coating of contrasting material that is applied to a surface as an inlay or overlay

      同义词：encrustation
