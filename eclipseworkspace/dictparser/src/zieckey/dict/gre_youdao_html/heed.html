heed [hi:d]

  • vt.
    • 注意，留心；听从:

      • He didn't heed my advice.

        他没有听从我的忠告。

  • vi.
    • 注意，留心:

      • I had told him not to go, but he didn't heed.

        我早就让他不要去，可他就是不听。

  • n.
    • 注意，留心，留意
  • 短语:
    • give (或pay) heed to 注意，留心
    • take no heed (of) (对…)没注意，未提防

heed [ hi:d ]

  • n. paying particular notice (as to children or helpless people)

    "he spends without heed to the consequences"

    同义词：attentiveness | regard | paying attention

  • v. pay close attention to; give heed to

    同义词：mind | listen
