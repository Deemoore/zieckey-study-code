noncommittal [,nɔnkə'mitəl]

  • adj.
    • (态度等)不明朗的；不表态的；不承担义务的
    • 无明确性质的，无明确意义的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

noncommittal [ ,nɔnkə'mitəl ]

  • adj. refusing to bind oneself to a particular course of action or view or the like

    "her boyfriend was noncommittal about their future together"
