cleave1 [kli:v]

  • vt.
    • 劈开，砍开，割开：

      • He cleaved a block of wood in two.

        他把一块木头劈成两半。

    • 砍下；割下：

      • She cleaved a branch from the tree.

        她从树上砍下一根树枝。

    • 开辟，打通：

      • They cleaved a path through the jungle.

        他们在丛林中开辟出一条路来。

    • 穿过；穿入；钻进：

      • Our ship is cleaving her way through the strait.

        我们的船正乘风破浪通过海峡。

  • vi.
    • (木材等顺纹理)被劈开；裂开；摔裂：

      • This wood cleaves easily.

        这种木材容易劈开。

    • 穿过；开路前进；破浪前进(常与through连用)：

      • The two policemen cleaved through the crowd and came to the scene of the accident.

        这两名警察穿过人群来到发生事故的现场。

      • The luxurious liner is cleaving through the waves.

        这艘豪华的邮轮正在破浪前进。

    • 变形：
    • cleaved 或cleft 或clove , cleaved 或cleft或cloven , cleaving

cleave2 [kli:v]

  • vi.
    • 紧紧附着于，粘住(常与to连用)：

      • This material cleaves to the skin.

        这种料子会粘在皮肤上。

    • 坚持；忠于：

      • He always cleaves to his principle in spite of persecution.

        他一贯不顾迫害坚持原则。

  • 近义词: stick
    • 变形：
    • cleaved或clave , cleaved , cleaving
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cleave [ kli:v ]

  • v.
    • separate or cut with a tool, such as a sharp instrument

      "cleave the bone"

      同义词：split | rive

    • make by cutting into

      "The water is going to cleave a channel into the rock"

    • come or be in close contact with; stick or hold together and resist separation

      同义词：cling | adhere | stick | cohere
