handle ['hændl]

  • n.
    • 柄，把，(桶等的)提手
    • 柄状物；可抓握的东西
    • 可乘之机，把柄，口实
    • [俚语]名称，头衔；别号，别名
    • (织物、毛皮等的)手感
    • 举动，行为
    • (赛马等的)赌注总额
    • [俚语]毛利，总收入
    • [美国俚语]横财，意外之财
    • [新西兰英语]汉斗(啤酒的计量单位，约合一品脱)
  • vt.
    • 触、摸、抚；抓，弄；拿，搬：

      • He was handling his toy all the morning.

        整个上午他一直在玩着他的玩具。

      • Don't handle my piano, please.

        请不要搬走我的钢琴。

    • 负责，管理；处理，处置：

      • The travelling accounts are handled by me.

        这次的外出旅游账目由我负责管理。

      • to handle everyday work

        处理日常工作

    • 对待；讨论(问题等)：

      • I have heard that some men handle the culprits roughly, is the problem that serious?

        我听说有人粗暴地对待犯人，有这么严重吗?

      • Now let's handle the questions about our duties and rights.

        下面咱们讨论一下责任和权利的问题。

    • (灵巧地)使用，运用；对付，应付：

      • to handle very skilfully

        运用自如

      • He handles people with tact.

        他待人很老练。

    • 指挥；统治；训练；

      • to handle troops

        指挥军队

      • Under his handling, many boys became excellent swimming players.

        在他的训练指导下，许多小伙子成了优秀的游泳运动员。

    • 驯养(马等)
    • 【商业】经营，经销：

      • This store handles the sort of liquor you need.

        这家商店出售你要的那种酒。

  • vi.
    • 操作，操纵：

      • The car handles well.

        这辆车开起来很灵便。

    • 行动，活动，举动：

      • The troops handled well.

        该部队风纪良好。

  • 近义词: manipulate , wield , ply
  • 短语:
    • fly(或go,slip)off the handle
      • [美国口语]
      • 勃然大怒，大发雷霆；冒火
      • 十分激动；失去控制
      • 死
    • get a handle on [口语]控制，掌握，驾驭，左右
    • give a handle to(或for) 给…以攻击的口实，给…以可乘之机
    • have a handle on [美国英语]控制，掌握，驾驭，左右
    • up to the handle [美国口语]彻底地，极度地
    • 变形：
    • handled , handling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

handle [ 'hændl ]

  • n. the appendage to an object that is designed to be held in order to use or move it

    "he grabbed the hammer by the handle"

    同义词：grip | handgrip | hold

  • v.
    • be in charge of, act on, or dispose of

      "This blender can't handle nuts"

      同义词：manage | deal | care

    • interact in a certain way

      同义词：treat | do by

    • deal with verbally or in some form of artistic expression

      同义词：cover | treat | plow | deal | address

    • touch, lift, or hold with the hands

      "Don't handle the merchandise"

      同义词：palm

    • handle effectively

      同义词：wield

    • show and train

      "The prize-winning poodle was handled by Mrs. Priscilla Prescott"
