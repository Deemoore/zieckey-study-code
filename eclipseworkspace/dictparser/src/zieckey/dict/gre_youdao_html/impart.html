impart [im'pɑ:t]

  • vt.
    • 给予；把…分给；传授：

      • The carpet imparted an air of elegance to the room.

        地毯使房间有了一种优雅的气氛。

      • to impart knowledge to one's pupils

        向某人的学生传授知识

    • 告知；通知；告诉；透露：

      • to impart news to somebody

        将消息通知某人

      • to impart a secret to a friend

        向朋友透露秘密

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

impart [ im'pɑ:t ]

  • v.
    • tell or deposit (information) knowledge

      同义词：leave | give | pass on

    • bestow a quality on

      同义词：lend | bestow | contribute | add | bring

    • make known to the public information that was previously known only to a few people or that was meant to be kept a secret

      同义词：unwrap | disclose | let on | bring out | reveal | discover | expose | divulge | break | give away | let out

    • make known; pass on, of information

      同义词：convey

以上来源于: WordNet
