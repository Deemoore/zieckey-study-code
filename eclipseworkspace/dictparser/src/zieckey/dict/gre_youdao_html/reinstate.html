reinstate [,ri:in'steit]

  • vt.
    • 使复工；使复职；使恢复原职：

      • The company reinstated him as chief engineer.

        该公司恢复了他的总工程师职务。

    • 使恢复原状；修复：

      • The broken mirror was reinstated in the twinkling of an eye.

        转瞬之间破镜子又恢复了原状。

    • 使恢复健康：

      • The trip reinstated my health.

        这次旅行恢复了我的健康。

    • 变形：
    • reinstated , reinstating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

reinstate [ ,ri:in'steit ]

  • v.
    • restore to the previous state or rank
    • bring back into original existence, use, function, or position

      同义词：restore | reestablish
