capacity [kə'pæsəti]

  • n.
    • 容纳能力；吸收力，接受力
    • 容量，容积
    • 最大容量；最大限度
    • 智力，智能；本领
    • (做某事的)能力，才能，才智
    • 生产能力；生产量；最大生产能力；最高生产量
    • 潜在能力，潜力
    • 职位；地位；身份；资格
    • 性能，功能
    • 【法律】(法律上享有的)权力，权限
    • 【电工学】 = capacitance
  • adj.
    • 充其量的；最大限度的
  • 近义词: function
  • 短语:
    • at capacity 以最高程度；以最高的生产量
    • be filled (或 packed) to capacity 客满，满座；挤得满满的
    • in one's capacity as 以…的资格(身份或职位)[亦作 in the capacity of]
    • 变形：
    • capacities
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

capacity [ kə'pæsəti ]

  • n.
    • ability to perform or produce
    • the susceptibility of something to a particular treatment

      同义词：capability

    • the amount that can be contained

      "the gas tank has a capacity of 12 gallons"

      同义词：content

    • the maximum production possible

      "the plant is working at 80 per cent capacity"

    • a specified function

      "he was employed in the capacity of director"; " he should be retained in his present capacity at a higher salary"

    • (computer science) the amount of information (in bytes) that can be stored on a disk drive

      "the capacity of a hard disk drive is usually expressed in megabytes"

    • an electrical phenomenon whereby an electric charge is stored

      同义词：capacitance | electrical capacity

    • the power to learn or retain knowledge; in law, the ability to understand the facts and significance of your behavior

      同义词：mental ability

    • tolerance for alcohol

      "he had drunk beyond his capacity"

以上来源于: WordNet
