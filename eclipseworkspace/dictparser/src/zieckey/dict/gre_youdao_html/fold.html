fold1 [fəuld]

  • vt.
    • 折叠(纸、布等)；把…对折：

      • to fold a sheet

        将纸折起来

    • 折叠好，合拢(使更紧密)：

      • Fold your napkin.

        把你的餐巾叠好。

    • 交叠，交(臂)，抱(臂)：

      • to fold the arms

        交臂

    • (鸟)收起(翅膀)：

      • The bird folded its wings.

        鸟将翅膀收起。

    • 拥抱，搂抱，抱紧：

      • She folded the crying child to her breast.

        她把哭闹的小孩搂抱在怀中。

    • 包，裹；包围；笼罩：

      • Fold a piece of paper round the flowers so that they'll be easier to carry.

        用一张纸把花包起来，这样它们易于携带。

    • 【烹饪】拌(食品配料)，使融合(常与in连用)：

      • Gently fold in the eggs.

        缓缓地拌和鸡蛋。

    • [诗歌用语]笼罩
    • [口语]使结束；使停止：

      • They folded the business after only two month and with great loss.

        他们开业才两个月，因巨大亏损而把生意给停了。

    • 【牌戏】把牌(面)朝下放(以示退出)
  • vi.
    • 折叠起来，对折起来：

      • Paper folds more easily than cardboard.

        纸较纸板易于折叠。

    • 结束，停止；失败，被迫歇业(或停止演出等)：

      • The show will fold after Sunday night's performance.

        星期日晚间演出后该剧就要停止演出了。

      • That company folded in 1999.

        那家公司于1999年倒闭。

    • 垮台；(健康、精神等方面)衰退，垮下
    • 缠绕
    • 【牌戏】(牌面向下)放在桌子上(表示退出玩牌)
  • n.
    • 折叠部分，褶，褶层，裥
    • 褶痕；折缝
    • 折叠，对折；折叠的动作
    • [英国英语]凹地；小谷，山谷
    • 【印刷】折页
    • 折叠器；能被折叠的东西
    • 【解剖学】(膜等的)皱襞，褶
    • 【地质学】褶被

fold2 [fəuld]

  • n.
    • 羊栏
    • 羊栏圈中的羊群或别的牧畜
    • 羊群
    • 一群具有共同利益、目标、信仰等的人们
    • 教会；(同一教会的)信徒
  • vt.
    • 把羊(或别的家畜)关在栏中：

      • The sheep are folded for the night.

        羊被关在栏中过夜。

    • 围栏放牧给(土地)施肥
  • 短语:
    • receive someone back into the fold 重新接纳某人；恢复某人的会籍
    • return (或 come back) to the fold 重返教会，恢复信仰；重新回到原组织

fold [ fəuld ]

  • n.
    • an angular or rounded shape made by folding

      "a fold in the napkin"

      同义词：crease | plication | flexure | crimp | bend

    • a group of people who adhere to a common faith and habitually attend a given church

      同义词：congregation | faithful

    • a geological process that causes a bend in a stratum of rock

      同义词：folding

    • a group of sheep or goats

      同义词：flock

    • a folded part (as in skin or muscle)

      同义词：plica

    • a pen for sheep

      同义词：sheepfold | sheep pen | sheepcote

    • the act of folding

      "he gave the napkins a double fold"

      同义词：folding

  • v.
    • bend or lay so that one part covers the other

      "fold up the newspaper"

      同义词：fold up | turn up

    • intertwine

      "fold one's hands, arms, or legs"

    • incorporate a food ingredient into a mixture by repeatedly turning it over without stirring or beating
    • cease to operate or cause to cease operating

      同义词：close up | close | shut down | close down

    • confine in a fold, like sheep

      同义词：pen up

    • become folded or folded up

      "The bed folds in a jiffy"

      同义词：fold up
