contort [kən'tɔ:t]

  • vt. 扭曲，扭弯，弄歪；歪曲，曲解：

    • His face was contorted with anger.

      他的脸都气歪了。

    • He contorted the document out of its proper meaning.

      他曲解了文件的正确含义。

  • 近义词: deform
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

contort [ kən'tɔ:t ]

  • v. twist and press out of shape

    同义词：deform | distort | wring
