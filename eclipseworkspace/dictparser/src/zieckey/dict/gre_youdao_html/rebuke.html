rebuke [ri'bju:k]

  • vt.
    • 指责；训斥；非难：

      • The manager rebuked the salesgirl sharply for being rude to customers.

        经理严厉斥责女售货员的对顾客无礼。

    • 使相形见绌；成为对…的鞭策：

      • Her carefulness rebukes me.

        她的仔细认真成为对我的鞭策。

    • [废语]阻碍；制止
  • n.
    • 斥责，训斥
  • 短语:
    • without rebuke 无可指摘的；无可非议的
    • 变形：
    • rebuked , rebuking
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

rebuke [ ri'bju:k ]

  • n. an act or expression of criticism and censure

    "he had to take the rebuke with a smile on his face"

    同义词：reproof | reproval | reprehension | reprimand

  • v. censure severely or angrily

    同义词：call on the carpet | take to task | rag | trounce | reproof | lecture | reprimand | jaw | dress down | call down | scold | chide | berate | bawl out | remonstrate | chew out | chew up | have words | lambaste | lambast
