microscopic [,maikrə'skɔpik]

  • adj.
    • 用显微镜可见的；微小的；细小的；微观的；极小的
    • 显微镜的；用显微镜的
    • 显微镜似的；任何细节都不放过的
    • 与显微镜使用(或检查)有关的 [亦作 microscopical, 参较 macroscopic]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

microscopic [ ,maikrə'skɔpik ]

  • adj.
    • of or relating to or used in microscopy

      "microscopic analysis"; " microscopical examination"

      同义词：microscopical

    • too small to be seen except under a microscope

      同义词：microscopical

    • extremely precise with great attention to details

      "examined it with microscopic care"

    • infinitely or immeasurably small

      "reduced to a microscopic scale"

      同义词：infinitesimal | minute

以上来源于: WordNet
