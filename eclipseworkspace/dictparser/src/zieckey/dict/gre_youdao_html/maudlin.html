maudlin ['mɔ:dlin]

  • adj.
    • 易伤感的；感情脆弱的；爱哭的
    • 酒后伤感而落泪的
  • n.
    • 伤感；脆弱的感情
  • 近义词: sentimental

maudlin [ 'mɔ:dlin ]

  • adj. effusively or insincerely emotional

    "maudlin expressions of sympathy"

    同义词：bathetic | drippy | hokey | mawkish | mushy | schmaltzy | schmalzy | sentimental | soppy | soupy | slushy
