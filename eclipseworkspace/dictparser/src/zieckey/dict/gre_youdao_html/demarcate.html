demarcate ['di:mɑ:keit]

  • vt.
    • 勘定…的界线(或边界)，给…划界，定(或划、标)界(或分界线)：

      • He demarcated a piece of property.

        他给一块地产划界。

    • 区分，分开；区别：

      • They demarcated the lots with fences.

        他们以栅栏划分场地。

    • 变形：
    • demarcated , demarcating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

demarcate [ 'di:mɑ:keit ]

  • v.
    • separate clearly and as if by boundaries
    • set, mark, or draw the boundaries of something

      同义词：delimit | delimitate
