reclaim [ri'kleim]

  • vt.
    • 改造；感化；挽救(犯错误者等)：

      • New China has reclaimed many men from a life of crime.

        新中国把许多人从犯罪的道路上挽救了回来。

    • 开垦土地；开拓土地；(填海)造地：

      • to reclaim the land from the sea

        填海造地

      • In Hong Kong many square miles of land have been reclaimed from the sea.

        在香港，许多平方英里的陆地都是填海开拓出来的。

    • (从废料中)回收利用；再制；再生：

      • Their daily work is to reclaim rubber from old tyres.

        他们的日常工作是用旧轮胎再生橡胶。

    • [废语]驯服(野兽等)：

      • to reclaim a hawk

        驯服鹰鹫

  • vi.
    • [古语]喊叫，抗议
  • n.
    • 改造；感化
    • 废物回收利用；再生物品
  • 短语:
    • beyond(或past)reclaim 无可救药地；不可挽救地
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

reclaim [ ri'kleim ]

  • v.
    • claim back

      同义词：repossess

    • reuse (materials from waste products)

      同义词：recover

    • bring, lead, or force to abandon a wrong or evil course of life, conduct, and adopt a right one

      同义词：reform | regenerate | rectify

    • make useful again; transform from a useless or uncultivated state

      "The people reclaimed the marshes"

    • overcome the wildness of; make docile and tractable

      "reclaim falcons"

      同义词：domesticate | domesticize | domesticise | tame
