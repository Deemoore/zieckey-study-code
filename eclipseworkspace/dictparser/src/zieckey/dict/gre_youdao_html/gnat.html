gnat [næt]

  • n.
    • 【昆虫】(咬、叮、螯人的)有翅小昆虫；蚋，蠓
    • [英国英语]蚊子
    • 小烦恼，琐事
  • 短语:
    • strain at a gnat 拘泥细节，迟疑于琐事
    • strain at a gnat and swallow a camel 小事拘谨大事糊涂
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

gnat [ næt ]

  • n.
    • any of various small biting flies: midges; biting midges; black flies; sand flies
    • (British usage) mosquito
