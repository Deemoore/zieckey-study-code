numb [nʌm]

  • adj.
    • (精神、肉体上)麻木的，失去感觉的
    • 麻痹的，迟钝的
    • 冷淡的，无感情的
  • vt.
    • 使麻木；使麻痹，使失去知觉：

      • Another two women were numbed when hearing the voice of Mikle Jackson.

        刚刚听到迈克尔杰克逊的声音，就又有两位妇女昏了过去。

  • vi.
    • 变得麻木：

      • My hands have numbed after 2 hours' biking.

        骑了两个小时的自行车后，我的双手都麻木了。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

numb [ nʌm ]

  • v. make numb or insensitive

    "The shock numbed her senses"

    同义词：benumb | blunt | dull

  • adj.
    • lacking sensation

      "numb with cold"

      同义词：asleep(p) | benumbed

    • (followed by `to') not showing human feeling or sensitivity; unresponsive

      "numb to the cries for mercy"

      同义词：dead(p)

    • so frightened as to be unable to move; stunned or paralyzed with terror

      "too numb with fear to move"

      同义词：petrified
