liable ['laiəbl]

  • adj.
    • 对…应负(法律)责任的；有(法律)义务的
    • 会…的；易于…的；有…倾向的；很可能的
    • 易遭受…的；易患…(病)的
  • 近义词: likely
  • 短语:
    • be liable for 对…有义务；对…应负责任
    • be liable to do (something)
      • 有义务(做某事)
      • 易于(得病等)
      • 常常会；有(做某事)的习惯
    • be liable to (something)
      • 易犯(疾病等)
      • 应受(处罚等)；可能会受(惩罚等)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

liable [ 'laiəbl ]

  • adj.
    • at risk of or subject to experiencing something usually unpleasant

      "she is liable to forget"

      同义词：apt(p)

    • subject to legal action

      "liable to criminal charges"

    • (often followed by `to') likely to be affected with

      "liable to diabetes"

      同义词：nonimmune | nonresistant | unresistant

    • held legally responsible

      "men between the ages of 18 and 35 were liable for military service"
