mendicant ['mendikənt]

  • adj.
    • 行乞的；乞丐的
    • 托钵修道会的
    • 到处化缘的
  • n.
    • 乞丐
    • [常作 M-]托钵僧
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

mendicant [ 'mendikənt ]

  • n.
    • a male member of a religious order that originally relied solely on alms

      同义词：friar

    • a pauper who lives by begging

      同义词：beggar

  • adj. practicing beggary

    "mendicant friars"
