invoke [in'vəuk]

  • vt.
    • 祈求(神灵)保佑；乞灵于：

      • to invoke God

        祈求上帝保佑

      • to invoke evil spirits

        召唤恶魔

    • 乞求；恳求；请求：

      • to invoke somebody's forgiveness

        恳求某人原谅

      • to invoke aid

        请求援助

    • 行使(法权等)；实行；求助于(法律等)：

      • to invoke a veto

        行使否决权

      • to invoke economic sanctions

        实行经济制裁

    • 援引(法规、条文等)；(作为根据)提出：

      • to invoke an article of the U.N. Charter

        援引《联合国宪章》条文

    • 引起，产生；唤起；造成：

      • to invoke new problems

        引发新问题

    • 变形：
    • invoked , invoking
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

invoke [ in'vəuk ]

  • v.
    • summon into action or bring into existence, often as if by magic

      同义词：raise | conjure | conjure up | evoke | stir | call down | arouse | bring up | put forward | call forth

    • cite as an authority; resort to

      "He invoked the law that would save him"; " She invoked an ancient law"

      同义词：appeal

    • request earnestly (something from somebody); ask for aid or protection

      同义词：appeal

以上来源于: WordNet
