callous ['kæləs]

  • adj.
    • 胼胝状的
    • 硬结的，变硬的；起老茧的
    • 无情的，无同情心的；无感觉的；冷淡的；硬心肠的
  • vt.,vi.
    • (使)硬结，(使)变硬；(使)无感觉，(使)麻木
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

callous [ 'kæləs ]

  • v. make insensitive or callous; deaden feelings or morals

    同义词：cauterize | cauterise

  • adj.
    • emotionally hardened

      "a callous indifference to suffering"

      同义词：indurate | pachydermatous

    • having calluses; having skin made tough and thick through wear

      "calloused skin"; " with a workman's callous hands"

      同义词：calloused | thickened
