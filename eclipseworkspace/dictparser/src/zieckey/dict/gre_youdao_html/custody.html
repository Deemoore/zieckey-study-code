custody ['kʌstədi]

  • n.
    • 监护；看守；保护；保管，照管
    • 监禁；拘留，羁押
    • [亦称作 child custody]【法律】(对子女的)扶养权[参较 joint custody,sole custody]
  • 短语:
    • in custody 被拘留(或监禁)中
    • take someone into custody 拘留某人；逮捕某人
    • 变形：
    • custodies
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

custody [ 'kʌstədi ]

  • n.
    • a state of being confined (usually for a short time)

      "he is in the custody of police"

      同义词：detention | detainment | hold

    • holding by the police

      "the suspect is in custody"

    • (with `in') guardianship over; in divorce cases it is the right to house and care for and discipline a child

      "your guests are now in my custody"; " the mother was awarded custody of the children"

      同义词：hands
