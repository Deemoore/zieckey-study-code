apathetic [,æpə'θetik]

  • adj.
    • 无情的，无感情的，情感淡薄的，缺乏感情的；无动于衷的，冷漠的
    • 缺乏兴趣的，漠不关心的，漠然的，冷淡的[亦作 apathetical]
  • 近义词: impassive
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

apathetic [ ,æpə'θetik ]

  • adj.
    • showing little or no emotion or animation

      "a woman who became active rather than apathetic as she grew older"

    • marked by a lack of interest

      "an apathetic audience"

      同义词：indifferent

以上来源于: WordNet
