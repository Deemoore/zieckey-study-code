cryptic ['kriptik]

  • adj.
    • 隐蔽的；秘密的；玄妙的
    • 神秘的；含义模糊的；令人困惑的
    • 【动物学】隐蔽的，(适于)隐藏的；有保护色的
    • 不连贯的；简短的
    • 密码的,代码的[亦作 cryptical]
  • 近义词: obscure
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cryptic [ 'kriptik ]

  • adj.
    • of an obscure nature

      "the new insurance policy is written without cryptic or mysterious terms"

      同义词：cryptical | deep | inscrutable | mysterious | mystifying

    • having a secret or hidden meaning

      "cryptic writings"

      同义词：cabalistic | kabbalistic | qabalistic | cryptical | sibylline

    • having a puzzling terseness

      "a cryptic note"
