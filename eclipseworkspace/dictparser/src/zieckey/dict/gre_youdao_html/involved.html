involved [in'vɔlvd]

  • vt.
    • involve的变形
  • adj.
    • 卷入的；陷入的；有牵连的
    • 参与的，参加的
    • 有关的，受影响的
    • 纠缠的；混乱的；难处理的；复杂的
    • 难理解的，难懂的；晦涩的
    • (财政上)困难的
  • 近义词: complex

involve [in'vɔlv]

  • vt.
    • 牵涉，牵连，使卷入；使陷入(麻烦、困境等)：

      • to be involved in a scandal

        卷入丑闻

      • to be involved in the trouble

        陷入麻烦

      • The case involved many innocent people.

        这一案件牵涉了许多无辜的人。

    • 使参与；使参加：

      • to involve employees in the management

        使职员参与管理

    • 包含，含有，包括：

      • This project involves much difficulty.

        这一项目有许多困难。

    • 需要；必需：

      • a project which involves years of work

        一项需要若干年工作的工程

    • 与…有关联；影响：

      • His honour is involved.

        他的名誉受到影响。

    • 包围，围住；笼罩：

      • Clouds involve the mountain top.

        云雾笼罩着山顶。

    • 使专心于；使忙于；使从事于：

      • to be involved in research

        忙于研究

    • [古语]卷缠；盘绕
    • [废语]【数学】把(某数学)乘方
  • 近义词: include
    • 变形：
    • involved , involving
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

involved [ in'vɔlvd ]

  • adj.
    • connected by participation or association or use

      "we accomplished nothing, simply because of the large number of people involved"; " the problems involved"; " the involved muscles"; " I don't want to get involved"; " everyone involved in the bribery case has been identified"

    • entangled or hindered as if e.g. in mire

      "the difficulties in which the question is involved"

      同义词：mired

    • emotionally involved
    • highly complex or intricate

      "the plot was too involved"

      同义词：Byzantine | convoluted | knotty | tangled | tortuous

    • enveloped

      "a castle involved in mist"; " the difficulties in which the question is involved"
