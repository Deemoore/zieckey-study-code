affiliate [ə'filieit; ə'filiit; -eit]

  • vt.
    • 接纳(或吸收)…为成员(或分支机构)；使成为成员(英国用 to，美国用 with)：

      • to affiliate oneself to (或 with) a party

        加入某政党

      • to affiliate with the church

        参加教会

    • 使隶属，使附属于 (to， with)：

      • The college is affiliated to (或 with) the university.

        这所学院附属于该大学。

      • The research centre is affiliated with the university.

        该研究中心隶属于该大学。

    • [affiliate oneself]使发生联系,使紧密联系,使联合;结交 (with)：

      • Jack is affiliated with good fellows.

        杰克与好人结交。

    • 追求根源，溯源， 追溯…的来源 (或由来、联系)；把…归于：

      • to affiliate a language

        追溯一种语言的来源

    • 【法律】把(私生子、非婚生子女)的父亲确认出来，判定(非婚生子女)的父亲：

      • She affiliated her child to (或 on, upon)him.

        她认定他是她的孩子的生父。

    • 收养，收为养子；使发生关联；使具有亲属关系：

      • to affiliate an orphan

        领养一个孤儿

  • vi.
    • 发生联系；参加，加入；同…一起：

      • to affiliate to (或 with) a political party

        参加某一政党

  • n.
    • 成员，会员
    • 成员组织，附属(或隶属分支)机构，分公司，子公司，分会，支部
    • [用复数] [美国英语]附属电台
    • 变形：
    • affiliated , affiliating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

affiliate [ ə'filieit, ə'filiit, -eit ]

  • n.
    • a subordinate or subsidiary associate; a person who is affiliated with another or with an organization
    • a subsidiary or subordinate organization that is affiliated with another organization

      "network affiliates"

  • v.
    • join in an affiliation

      "They affiliated themselves with the organization"

    • keep company with; hang out with

      "She affiliates with her colleagues"

      同义词：consort | associate | assort

    • join in an affiliation

      "The two colleges affiliated"
