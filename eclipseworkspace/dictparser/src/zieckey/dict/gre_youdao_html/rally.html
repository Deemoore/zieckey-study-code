rally1 ['ræli]

  • vt.
    • 重整(溃军等)；重新集结：

      • The company commander rallied his defeated and dispersed troops.

        连长把他的打了败仗的溃散部队重新召集起来。

    • 召集；团结：

      • We must rally the broad masses of the people round the Party.

        我们必须将广大人民群众团结在党的周围。

    • 重振(精神等)；恢复(元气等)：

      • They rallied their spirits and threw themselves into the battle.

        他们重振精神投入战斗。

    • 使(币值等)回升：

      • They tried to rally the pound sterling but in vain.

        他们企图恢复英镑币值，但劳而无功。

  • vi.
    • (溃军等)重新集结(或整顿)：

      • The fleeing troops rallied and prepared for a last-ditch fight.

        溃军重整旗鼓，准备背水一战。

    • 团结；聚集:

      • We should rally under the banner of socialism.

        我们应当在社会主义的旗帜下团结起来。

    • 扶助；支持，站到…一边：

      • The vast majority of the people rallied to the new government.

        大多数人民站在新政府一边。

    • (在精力等方面)恢复；复元：

      • The patient rallied after the operation.

        病人经过手术后身体复元了。

    • 【网球、羽毛球】来回对打(为争夺一分而连续往返拍击)；赛前对打(作为准备活动)：

      • They rallied for a long time at the set point of the first set.

        在打到第一盘的决胜分时，他们来回对打了半天。

    • 【商业、证券】(股票价格、币值等)回升：

      • The stock market will soon rally.

        股票行情不久即将回升。

    • 【球类】(棒球比赛中在落后情况下)连续得分
    • 参加公路车赛
  • n.
    • 重新集结；重整
    • (群众性)集会
    • (精神等方面)振作；(身体)复元
    • (汽车)赛车会[亦作rallye]
    • 【网球、羽毛球】连续对打；赛前试打。
    • (证券等)降后回升
    • 【军事】集合号
    • (爱好相同者的)联谊会，交谊会
  • 近义词: stir
  • 短语:
    • pep rally 鼓舞士气的会
    • rally to the support of 同心协力来支持…
    • 变形：
    • rallies
    • rallied , rallying

rally [ 'ræli ]

  • n.
    • a large gathering of people intended to arouse enthusiasm

      同义词：mass meeting

    • the feat of mustering strength for a renewed effort

      "he singled to start a rally in the 9th inning"; " he feared the rallying of their troops for a counterattack"

      同义词：rallying

    • a marked recovery of strength or spirits during an illness
    • an automobile race run over public roads
    • (sports) an unbroken sequence of several successive strokes

      "after a short rally Connors won the point"

      同义词：exchange

  • v.
    • gather

      同义词：beat up | drum up

    • call to arms; of military personnel

      同义词：call up | mobilize | mobilise

    • gather or bring together

      同义词：muster | summon | come up | muster up

    • return to a former condition

      同义词：rebound

    • harass with persistent criticism or carping

      同义词：tease | razz | rag | cod | tantalize | tantalise | bait | taunt | twit | ride
