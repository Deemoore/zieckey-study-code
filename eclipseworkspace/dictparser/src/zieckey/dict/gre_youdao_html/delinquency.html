delinquency [di'liŋkwənsi]

  • n.
    • 懈怠；失职，玩忽职守
      • 不能如期付款，拖欠债务(或税款)
      • 逾期债款(或税款)
      • 逾期票据(或汇票)
    • 错误，缺点
    • 过错，过失；不端行为
    • 【法律】(尤指)少年犯罪;犯罪(行为);违法(行为) [参较juvenile delinquency]
    • 变形：
    • delinquencies
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

delinquency [ di'liŋkwənsi ]

  • n.
    • nonpayment of a debt when due
    • a tendency to be negligent and uncaring

      "he inherited his delinquency from his father"

      同义词：dereliction | willful neglect

    • an antisocial misdeed in violation of the law by a minor

      同义词：juvenile delinquency
