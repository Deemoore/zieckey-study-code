  • n.
    • 胡闹；恶作剧；开玩笑
  • vi.
    • 胡闹；恶作剧；开过分玩笑
  • 短语:
    • play pranks
      • 戏弄，开玩笑
      • (机器等)出毛病，运转反常

prank2 [præŋk]

  • vt.
    • 打扮：

      • She always pranks herself up with the best clothes.

        她常穿着漂亮的衣服。

    • 装饰；点缀：

      • meadows pranked with all kinds of flowers

        百花盛开的牧场

  • vi.
    • 打扮得漂亮；盛装卖弄：

      • White houses prank where once were huts.

        一幢幢的房子傲然矗立在一度只是破败陋室的地方。

    • 变形：
    • pranked , pranking
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prank [ præŋk ]

  • n.
    • acting like a clown or buffoon

      同义词：buffoonery | clowning | japery | frivolity | harlequinade

    • a ludicrous or grotesque act done for fun and amusement

      同义词：antic | joke | trick | caper | put-on

  • v.
    • dress or decorate showily or gaudily

      "Roses were pranking the lawn"

    • dress up showily

      "He pranked himself out in his best clothes"
