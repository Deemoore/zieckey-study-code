overshadow: 遮蔽

GetWord（"overshadow"） 使…相形见绌

overshadow [,əuvə'ʃædəu]

  • vt.
    • 使失色，使显得不重要，使相形见绌；夺去…的光辉；使丢面子：

      • He was an excellent player, but he was overshadowed by Jordan.

        他是一名杰出的运动员，但乔丹的才华令他黯然失色。

      • to overshadow someone entirely

        使某人大丢面子

    • 遮暗，遮蔽，使阴暗不明：

      • a village overshadowed by dense forests

        被密林遮蔽的村庄

    • 给…蒙上阴影，使阴郁，使郁闷：

      • The whole country was overshadowed at the news of President Lincoln's death.

        林肯总统去世的消息给全国蒙上了阴影。

    • [古语]庇护；保护
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

overshadow [ ,əuvə'ʃædəu ]

  • v.
    • exceed in importance; outweigh

      "This problem overshadows our lives right now"

      同义词：eclipse

    • make appear small by comparison

      同义词：shadow | dwarf

    • cast a shadow upon

      "The tall tree overshadowed the house"; " The tragedy overshadowed the couple's happiness"
