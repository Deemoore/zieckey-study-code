    • 逆，反对，从反面，反面地

    • 反对的论点，反对的理由；反对的立场；争执者；反对者；反对票 [参较pro1]

con2 [kɔn]

  • vt.
    • 学习；研究；精读，熟读
    • 默记，谙记
    • 变形：
    • conned , conning

con3 [kɔn]

  • n. [俚语](负责河流或林业管理的)官员，自然保护官员

con4 [kɔn]

  • adj.
    • [美国口语]欺骗的；骗得信任的
  • vt.
    • 诈骗，哄骗；勾引；劝说，说服：

      • I'll write a letter to the manager and see if I can con him into okaying it.

        我将给经理写封信，看看是否能说服他同意这件事。

    • 【戏剧】纪念(某一角色)
    • 【海军】(向舵手发出特定指令而使船只)对准航线
  • n.
    • 电话骗子的诡计(指以合法主办者的名义出售债券，后将认购款据为己有的骗人花招)
    • (Star Tred， 美国科学幻想电视连续剧)《星球旅行》迷聚会
    • 变形：
    • conned , conning

con6 [kɔn]

con [ kɔn ]

  • n.
    • an argument opposed to a proposal
    • a person serving a sentence in a jail or prison

      同义词：convict | inmate | jail bird | jailbird | gaolbird | yard bird | yardbird

    • a swindle in which you cheat at gambling or persuade a person to buy worthless property

      同义词：bunco | bunco game | bunko | bunko game | confidence trick | confidence game | con game | gyp | hustle | sting | flimflam

  • v.
    • deprive of by deceit

      同义词：victimize | swindle | rook | goldbrick | nobble | diddle | bunco | defraud | scam | mulct | gyp | gip | hornswoggle | short-change

    • commit to memory; learn by heart

      同义词：memorize | memorise | learn

  • adv. on the negative side

    "much was written pro and con"

    同义词：in opposition
