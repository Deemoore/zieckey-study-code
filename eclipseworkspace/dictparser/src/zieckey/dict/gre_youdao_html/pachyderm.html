pachyderm ['pækidə:m]

  • n.
    • 【动物】(非反刍、有蹄的)厚皮动物(如大象、犀牛等)
    • 脸皮厚的人，麻木不仁的人，迟钝的人

pachyderm [ 'pækidə:m ]

  • n. any of various nonruminant hoofed mammals having very thick skin: elephant; rhinoceros; hippopotamus
