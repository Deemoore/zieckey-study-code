opprobrium [ə'prəubriəm]

  • n.
    • 不光彩，不名誉，耻辱
    • 不名誉的事(或人)；非难(或责骂)的对象
    • 带来耻辱的事，受辱的原由
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

opprobrium [ ə'prəubriəm ]

  • n.
    • state of disgrace resulting from public abuse

      同义词：obloquy

    • a state of extreme dishonor

      "the name was a by-word of scorn and opprobrium throughout the city"

      同义词：infamy

以上来源于: WordNet
