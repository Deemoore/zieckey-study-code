recess1 [ri'ses; 'ri:ses]

  • n.
    • (墙壁等的)凹进之处；壁龛
    • 深处，幽深之处
    • 休息；休会；休假
    • 【解剖学】隐窝
  • vt.
    • 把…放在隐蔽处：

      • to recess a window

        把窗开在墙壁的凹进处

      • recessed lighting

        隐蔽式照明

    • 使凹进；使有凹处：

      • to recess a wall

        在墙壁上建造一个凹入部分

    • 使(会议等)休会；暂停：

      • The trial was recessed by the presiding judge.

        审判长宣布审讯暂时中止。

  • vi.
    • [美国英语] 休会；休息；休假：

      • The conference will recess till tomorrow morning.

        会议暂时休会，明天上午继续开会。

  • 短语:
    • in the inmost(或secret)recesses of the heart 在内心深处

recess2 [ri'ses]

  • n.
    • 【工程】凹口，凹座，切口
    • 【地质学】凹部
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

recess [ ri'ses, 'ri:ses ]

  • n.
    • a state of abeyance or suspended business

      同义词：deferral

    • a small concavity

      同义词：recession | niche | corner

    • an arm off of a larger body of water (often between rocky headlands)

      同义词：inlet

    • an enclosure that is set back or indented

      同义词：niche

    • a pause from doing something (as work)

      同义词：respite | break | time out

  • v.
    • put into a recess

      "recess lights"

    • make a recess in

      "recess the piece of wood"

    • close at the end of a session

      同义词：adjourn | break up

以上来源于: WordNet
