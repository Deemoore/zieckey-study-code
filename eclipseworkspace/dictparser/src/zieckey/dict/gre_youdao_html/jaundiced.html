jaundice ['dʒɔ:ndis]

  • n.
    • 【病理学】黄疸;黄疸病(俗指肝炎)[亦作 icterus,参较 physiologic jaundice]
    • (由妒忌引起的)猜疑；偏见；厌恶
  • vt.
    • 使患黄疸病
    • 使妒忌；使有偏见(或不满)：

      • Her social position jaundiced his view of things.

        她的社会地位使她对事情有偏见。

  • 短语:
    • a jaundiced eye (或 view) 有偏见的眼光，挑剔的眼光
    • 变形：
    • jaundiced , jaundicing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

jaundiced [ 'dʒɔ:ndist ]

  • adj.
    • affected by jaundice which causes yellowing of skin etc

      同义词：icteric | yellow

    • showing or affected by prejudice or envy or distaste

      "looked with a jaundiced eye on the growth of regimentation"; " takes a jaundiced view of societies and clubs"
