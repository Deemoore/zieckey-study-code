perceive [pə'si:v]

  • vt.
    • 察觉；发觉；感知：

      • The students perceived that his behavior had changed.

        同学们发觉他的行为有些变化。

    • 领会；理解；认识：

      • He has now perceived his error.

        他现在认识到了自己的错误。

    • 把…看做；认为：

      • He is perceived as a religious man.

        众人认为他是个教徒。

    • [废语]得到，收到：

      • I could perceive nothing at all from her.

        我从她那里根本得不到什么。

  • vi.
    • 感知；感觉；察觉
    • 认识到，意识到；理解
  • 近义词: discern
    • 变形：
    • perceived , perceiving
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

perceive [ pə'si:v ]

  • v.
    • to become aware of through the senses

      "I could perceive the ship coming over the horizon"

      同义词：comprehend

    • become conscious of

      "She finally perceived the futility of her protest"
