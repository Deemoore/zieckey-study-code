emerge [i'mə:dʒ]

  • vi.
    • 冒出，露出(from):

      • The sun emerges from behind the clouds.

        太阳从云层里露出。

      • a ship emerging from fog

        从雾中露出的一艘船

    • 涌现，(由隐没处、暗处等)出现，出来；(由水中、液体中)浮现，呈出:

      • a ghost emerging from the grave

        似乎从坟墓里冒出的鬼魂

      • to emerge from water

        从水里露出

    • 显露，(事实、意见等)被知晓，暴露，显现出来，(问题等)冒出，(问题、困难等)发生，产生(结果):

      • The truth emerged that the driver of the car had been drunk.

        真相暴露了这个汽车司机是酒后开车。

      • It emerged from the message that all was well.

        从获知的消息来看，一切都很好。

    • 形成，出现，发生，发展，兴起；进化而成，演化:

      • A strong breed emerged.

        一个优良品种形成了。

    • (从困苦、逆境、贫穷、微贱等中)挣脱出来，从…中脱颖而出，露头，出头(from):

      • to emerge from a depression

        从萧条中摆脱出来

      • Many distinguished men have emerged from slums.

        很多名人都出身于贫民区。

    • 变形：
    • emerged , emerging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

emerge [ i'mə:dʒ ]

  • v.
    • come out into view, as from concealment

      "Suddenly, the proprietor emerged from his office"

    • come out of

      同义词：issue | come out | come forth | go forth | egress

    • become known or apparent

      "Some nice results emerged from the study"

    • come up to the surface of or rise

      "He felt new emotions emerge"

    • happen or occur as a result of something

      同义词：come forth
