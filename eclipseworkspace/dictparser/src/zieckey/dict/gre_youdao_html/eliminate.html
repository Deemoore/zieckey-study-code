eliminate [i'limineit]

  • vt.
    • 除掉，除去，消除，排除，根除，摆脱，消灭：

      • to eliminate hunger

        消灭饥饿现象

      • to eliminate risks

        排除危险

    • 不加以考虑；拒绝；摒弃；删去；省略；忽略：

      • We have eliminated all statistical tables, which are of interest only to the specialist.

        我们删去了全部统计表格，因只有专家才对这些表格感兴趣。

    • 【体育】(比赛中)淘汰，刷掉：

      • The team was eliminated.

        该队被淘汰了。

    • [口语]干掉，杀死：

      • to eliminate the enemy

        干掉敌人

    • 【代数】消去(未知量)
    • 【生理学】(从体内)排除(废料)；排泄
    • 【化学】消除，消去
  • 近义词: exclude
    • 变形：
    • eliminated , eliminating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

eliminate [ i'limineit ]

  • v.
    • terminate or take out

      "Let's eliminate the course on Akkadian hieroglyphics"

      同义词：get rid of | do away with

    • do away with

      同义词：obviate | rid of

    • kill in large numbers

      同义词：annihilate | extinguish | eradicate | wipe out | decimate | carry off

    • dismiss from consideration or a contest

      "This possibility can be eliminated from our consideration"

      同义词：rule out | winnow out | reject

    • eliminate from the body

      同义词：excrete | egest | pass

    • remove from a contest or race

      "The cyclist has eliminated all the competitors in the race"

    • remove (an unknown variable) from two or more equations
