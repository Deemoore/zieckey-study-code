monologue ['mɔnəlɔɡ]

  • n.
    • 滔滔不绝的讲话；长篇大论
    • 【戏剧】独白；独白词；独角戏；独角戏剧本
    • 单独朗诵
    • 独白体诗[亦作monolog]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

monologue [ 'mɔnəlɔɡ ]

  • n.
    • speech you make to yourself

      同义词：soliloquy

    • a long utterance by one person (especially one that prevents others from participating in the conversation)
    • a (usually long) dramatic speech by a single actor

以上来源于: WordNet
