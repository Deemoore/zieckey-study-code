profundity [prəu'fʌndəti]

  • n.
    • 深度；深处；(尤指)深渊
    • 深奥，深刻，深沉，深厚
    • [常用作复数]深奥的事物，深刻的思想，含意深刻的话
    • 变形：
    • pries
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

profundity [ prəu'fʌndəti ]

  • n.
    • wisdom that is recondite and abstruse and profound

      同义词：reconditeness | abstruseness | abstrusity | profoundness

    • intellectual depth; penetrating knowledge; keen insight; etc

      同义词：profoundness

    • the intellectual ability to penetrate deeply into ideas

      同义词：astuteness | profoundness | depth | deepness

    • the quality of being physically deep

      "the profundity of the mine was almost a mile"

      同义词：deepness | profoundness

以上来源于: WordNet
