induce [in'dju:s; in'du:s]

  • vt.
    • 引诱，诱使：

      • These attractions induce people to travel in that country.

        这些诱人之处吸引人们到那个国家去旅游。

      • People are always induced by advertisements to buy things they don't really need.

        人们总是受广告的引诱，买一些自己并不真正需要的东西。

    • 劝，说服：

      • He tries to induce me to stay.

        他试图劝说我留下。

      • Nothing shall induce me to go.

        没有什么可以说服我去的。

    • 引起，招致，导致：

      • illness induced by overwork

        工作过度引起的疾病

      • to induce vomiting with an emetic

        服用催吐剂引起呕吐

    • 【物理学】感应；感生
    • 【医学】用人工方法引导；诱导：

      • to induce abortion

        人工流产

    • 【逻辑学】归纳出
    • 【心理学】诱发，引出
    • 【棒球】击败
  • 近义词: persuade
    • 变形：
    • induced , inducing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

induce [ in'dju:s, in'du:s ]

  • v.
    • cause to arise

      "induce a crisis"

      同义词：bring on

    • cause to do; cause to act in a specified manner

      "The ads induced me to buy a VCR"

      同义词：stimulate | cause | have | get | make

    • cause to occur rapidly

      同义词：stimulate | rush | hasten

    • reason or establish by induction
    • produce electric current by electrostatic or magnetic processes

      同义词：induct

以上来源于: WordNet
