premiere [pri'miə, prə'mjer]

基本释义 同近义词

  • adj. 首位的；初次的；女主角的
  • vi. 初次公演；首次露面
  • vt. 初次公演；初次上演
  • n. 初次的演出；女主角

[过去式premiered  过去分词premiered  现在分词premiering  ]

  • adj. 首位的；初次的；女主角的

    • first-time

  • n. 初次的演出；女主角

    • leading actress , chief actress

premiere [pri'miə; prə'mjer]

  • n.
    • (戏剧等的)主角女演员
    • =premiere danseuse
    • [复数][澳大利亚英语]体育俱乐部对抗赛冠军
  • adj.
    • 突出的；杰出的
    • =premier
  • vt.
    • 初次演出：

      • Her film was premiered in Cannes Festival.

        她的影片在戛纳电影节首次放映。

  • vi.
    • 首次上演；(名演员等)首次登台：

      • The new film is premiering now.

        新电影现在正在上演。

    • 首次出现：

      • The women's magazine is scheduled to premiere tomorrow.

        这份妇女杂志预计明天首次出版。

    • 变形：
    • premiered , premiering
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

premiere [ pri'miə, prə'mjer ]

  • n. the first public performance of a play or movie
  • v.
    • be performed for the first time

      "We premiered the opera of the young composer and it was a critical success"

      同义词：premier

    • perform a work for the first time

      同义词：premier

  • adj. preceding all others in time

    "the premiere showing"

    同义词：premier
