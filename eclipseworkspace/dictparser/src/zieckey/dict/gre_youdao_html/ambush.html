ambush ['æmbuʃ]

  • n.
    • 伏击
    • 埋伏，埋伏下来等待出击
      • [总称]伏兵，伏击者
      • 伏击点，设伏地点；打埋伏的地方
  • vt.
    • 埋伏，埋伏下，隐蔽(准备伏击)，部署(士兵)进行伏击：

      • to ambush the troops in the heavy woods on either side of the road

        把部队埋伏在公路两旁的密林里

    • 伏击：

      • to ambush the occupation troops

        伏击占领军

  • vi.
    • 伏击；打埋伏
  • 短语:
    • attack from ambush 伏兵发动进攻
    • be attacked from (an) ambush 受到伏击
    • be (或 hide,lie) in ambush 埋伏着(等待敌人)，打埋伏
    • fall (或 run) into an ambush 中埋伏，遭遇埋伏
    • lay (或 make, set, construct) an ambush (for) 设下埋伏(等待…)，对…设埋伏
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ambush [ 'æmbuʃ ]

  • n. the act of concealing yourself and lying in wait to attack by surprise

    同义词：ambuscade | lying in wait | trap

  • v.
    • wait in hiding to attack

      同义词：scupper | bushwhack | waylay | lurk | ambuscade | lie in wait

    • hunt (quarry) by stalking and ambushing

      同义词：still-hunt
