ornate [ɔ:'neit]

  • adj.
    • [有时亦含贬义]装饰华丽的，过分装饰的
    • (文体)华美的，绚丽的；矫揉造作的
    • [古语]装饰的，点缀的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ornate [ ɔ:'neit ]

  • adj.
    • rich in decorative detail

      同义词：embellished | ornamented

    • marked by elaborate rhetoric and elaborated with decorative details

      "ornate rhetoric taught out of the rule of Plato"

      同义词：flowery

以上来源于: WordNet
