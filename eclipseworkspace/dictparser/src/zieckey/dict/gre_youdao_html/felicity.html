  • n.
    • 幸福，极乐；幸运
    • 造福之物，好运气
    • (措词等的)恰当，恰如其分，贴切巧妙
    • 恰当的词句；巧妙的思想
    • 吉事，幸事
    • [古语]幸运
    • 变形：
    • felicities

felicity [ fi'lisiti ]

  • n.
    • pleasing and appropriate manner or style (especially manner or style of expression)

      同义词：felicitousness

    • state of well-being characterized by emotions ranging from contentment to intense joy

      同义词：happiness
