appreciable [ə'pri:ʃiəbl]

  • adj. (大得)可看到(或察觉到、充分感觉到)的，相当可观的；可估计的，可估量的
  • 近义词: perceptible

appreciable [ ə'pri:ʃiəbl ]

  • adj. enough to be estimated or measured

    "appreciable amounts of noxious wastes are dumped into the harbor"
