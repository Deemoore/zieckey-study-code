rejuvenate [ri'dʒu:vineit]

  • vt.
    • 使返老还童；使恢复青春；使恢复活力：

      • He tried to rejuvenate himself by performing physical exercises each day.

        他试图每天进行体育锻炼以使自己返老还童。

      • The fresh mountain air will rejuvenate the middle-aged women.

        山中的新鲜空气将使中年妇女恢复青春。

    • 更新；使改观：

      • I have got my car rejuvenated.

        我已请人将我的汽车修整一新了。

    • 【地质学】使回春；使更生
  • vi.
    • 返老还童；恢复活力；复原
    • 变形：
    • rejuvenated , rejuvenating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

rejuvenate [ ri'dʒu:vineit ]

  • v.
    • cause (a stream or river) to erode, as by an uplift of the land
    • develop youthful topographical features

      "the land rejuvenated"

    • make younger or more youthful

      "The contact with his grandchildren rejuvenated him"

    • return to life; get or give new life or energy

      同义词：regenerate | restore

    • become young again

      "The old man rejuvenated when he became a grandfather"

以上来源于: WordNet
