gullible [ 'ɡʌləbl ]

  • adj.
    • naive and easily deceived or tricked

      "at that early age she had been gullible and in love"

      同义词：fleeceable | green

    • easily tricked because of being too trusting

      "gullible tourists taken in by the shell game"
