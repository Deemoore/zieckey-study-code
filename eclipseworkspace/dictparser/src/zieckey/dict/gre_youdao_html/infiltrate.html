infiltrate ['infiltreit; in'fil-]

  • vt.
    • (使)透过；(使)浸入；(使)浸润 (常与into，through连用)：

      • to infiltrate the tissue with a local anaesthetic

        用局部麻醉剂浸入组织

      • The water is infiltrating the gravel bed.

        水正渗透进碎石底层。

      • 【军事】(军队)渗透；侵袭：

        • an infiltrating column

          渗透纵队

      • 使(人员、思想等)渗入，混入：

        • Our men have been infiltrated into the enemy.

          我们的人已打入敌人内部。

  • vi.
    • 渗透，渗入(常与into连用)：

      • The blood has infiltrated into the tissue.

        血液已渗入了组织中。

    • (军队、思想、人员等的)渗透(常与into连用)：

      • Spies have infiltrated into our organization.

        间谍已侵入了我们的组织。

  • n.
    • 渗透物；渗透
    • 浸润物
    • 变形：
    • infiltrated , infiltrating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

infiltrate [ 'infiltreit, in'fil- ]

  • v.
    • cause (a liquid) to enter by penetrating the interstices
    • enter a group or organization in order to spy on the members

      "The student organization was infiltrated by a traitor"

      同义词：penetrate

    • pass into or through by filtering or permeating

      "the substance infiltrated the material"

    • pass through an enemy line; in a military conflict

      同义词：pass through
