expenditure [iks'penditʃə]

  • n.
    • (金钱、时间等的)花费，支出，消费；耗费，消耗；用光
    • (金钱的)消费额；(时间的)消耗量；支出额，开支额；经费，费用
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

expenditure [ iks'penditʃə ]

  • n.
    • money paid out

      同义词：outgo | outlay

    • the act of spending money for goods or services

      同义词：expending

    • the act of consuming something

      同义词：consumption | using up
