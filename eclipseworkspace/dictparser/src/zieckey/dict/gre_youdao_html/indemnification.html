indemnification [in,demnifi'keiʃən]

  • n.
    • 保护，保障
    • 免罚，赦免
    • 赔偿，补偿
    • 赔偿物，赔偿金；保障物
  • 近义词: reparation
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

indemnification [ in,demnifi'keiʃən ]

  • n.
    • a sum of money paid in compensation for loss or injury

      同义词：damages | amends | indemnity | restitution | redress

    • an act of compensation for actual loss or damage or for trouble and annoyance
