immerse [i'mə:s]

  • vt.
    • 使浸没，把…浸入：

      • He is immersed in the swimming pool.

        他完全沉进了水中。

      • Immerse the cloth in the dye for twenty minutes.

        把布在染液里浸20分钟。

    • 使沉浸于；使专心于(常与in连用)：

      • He immersed himself in study during the vacation.

        他在假期里埋头学习。

    • 使深陷于；使卷入：

      • to immerse oneself among the masses

        深入群众

      • His brother was immersed in deep debts.

        他的弟弟负债累累。

    • 把…浸入；把…嵌入：

      • shells immersed in the sand

        埋在沙里的贝壳

    • 【宗教】给…施浸礼
    • 变形：
    • immersed , immersing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

immerse [ i'mə:s ]

  • v.
    • thrust or throw into

      同义词：plunge

    • engross (oneself) fully

      "He immersed himself into his studies"

      同义词：steep | engulf | plunge | engross | absorb | soak up

    • enclose or envelop completely, as if by swallowing

      同义词：swallow | swallow up | bury | eat up

    • cause to be immersed

      同义词：plunge
