occult ['ɔkʌlt; ɔ'kʌlt; ə-]

  • adj.
    • 超出普通知识的，难理解的
    • 神秘的，玄妙的；超自然的
    • 秘密的，不公开的，秘传的
    • 隐蔽的，看不见的
    • 玄学的
    • 【医学】隐(性)的
  • n.
    • [the occult]玄学;神秘学(或仪式)
  • vt.
    • 掩蔽，遮蔽：

      • He's occulted with an aureole.

        他被人为地神化了。

    • 【天文学】遮蔽(星球等)，使成食
  • vi.
    • 被隐蔽，被遮蔽
    • 【天文学】(星球)被食，被遮蔽
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

occult [ 'ɔkʌlt, ɔ'kʌlt, ə- ]

  • n.
    • supernatural forces and events and beings collectively

      同义词：supernatural

    • supernatural practices and techniques

      "he is a student of the occult"

      同义词：occult arts

  • v.
    • cause an eclipse of (a celestial body) by intervention

      "Planets and stars often are occulted by other celestial bodies"

      同义词：eclipse

    • become concealed or hidden from view or have its light extinguished

      "The beam of light occults every so often"

    • hide from view

      "The lids were occulting her eyes"

  • adj.
    • hidden and difficult to see

      "an occult fracture"; " occult blood in the stool"

    • having an import not apparent to the senses nor obvious to the intelligence; beyond ordinary understanding

      "occult lore"

      同义词：mysterious | mystic | mystical | secret | orphic

以上来源于: WordNet
