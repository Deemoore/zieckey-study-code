expostulate [ik'spɔstjuleit]

  • vi.
    • 规劝，告诫，劝戒，忠告，劝告(with):

      • Her mother expostulated with her about the evils of gambling.

        她母亲就赌博的害处规劝她。

  • vt.
    • 检查，审查
    • 讨论
  • 近义词: object
    • 变形：
    • expostulated , expostulating
