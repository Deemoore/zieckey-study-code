fault [fɔ:lt]

  • n.
    • 过失，失误，欠缺；疏忽漏做(某事)
    • 缺点，毛病，瑕疵；缺陷，不足
    • 恶行，不端行为；过错，违法行为，错误
    • (对失误或错误所负的)责任
    • 【电学】(电路的)故障；停电事故
    • 【地质学、采矿】断层
    • 【狩猎】(猎犬追捕猎物时)失去嗅迹
    • 【手球、网球等】
      • 发球失误
      • 误发的球
    • [废语]短缺，缺乏，不足，需要
  • vt.
    • 挑剔，找…的缺点，责难，责备；批评；非难：

      • a performance which could not be faulted

        无可挑剔的演出

    • 做错，在…中出错：

      • The acrobat deliberately faulted the performance once to make it look difficult.

        表演者故意把表演做错一次，以显得表演很难。

    • 【地质学】在…产生断层
  • vi.
    • 出差错，犯错误：

      • Her tongue stammered and faulted with rage.

        她讲话因生气而结结巴巴并出错。

    • 【地质学】产生断层
  • 近义词: failing , weakness , foible , vice
  • 反义词: virtue
  • 短语:
    • at fault
      • (猎犬追捕猎物时)失去嗅迹
      • 有责任，有过错，有毛病，有故障
    • double fault 【网球】发球双误
    • Faults are thick where love is thin. [谚语]一朝情义淡，样样不顺眼。
    • find fault (with) 找(…的)岔，抱怨，责难，吹毛求疵
    • in fault 有责任，有过错，该受责备
    • to a fault 过分地，过头地
    • whip a fault out of someone 鞭打…使纠正缺点
    • with all faults 【商业】商品售出概不退换(不保证所售商品质量)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fault [ fɔ:lt ]

  • n.
    • responsibility for a bad situation or event

      "it was John's fault"

    • (geology) a crack in the earth's crust resulting from the displacement of one side with respect to the other

      "they built it right over a geological fault"; " he studied the faulting of the earth's crust"

      同义词：faulting | geological fault | shift | fracture | break

    • the quality of being inadequate or falling short of perfection

      "he knew his own faults much better than she did"

      同义词：demerit

    • a wrong action attributable to bad judgment or ignorance or inattention

      "I could understand his English in spite of his grammatical faults"

      同义词：mistake | error

    • an imperfection in a device or machine

      同义词：defect | flaw

    • (sports) a serve that is illegal (e.g., that lands outside the prescribed area)

      "he served too many double faults"

    • (electronics) equipment failure attributable to some defect in a circuit (loose connection or insulation failure or short circuit etc.)

      "it took much longer to find the fault than to fix it"

  • v. put or pin the blame on

    同义词：blame
