debase [di'beis]

  • vt.
    • 使(货币等)贬值；降低(硬币)的成色，降低(品质)；掺杂，掺假：

      • They debased the value of the dollar.

        他们使美元贬值。

    • 降低…的身份，有损(或降低)…的人格；贬损…的职位(或尊严)；贬低…的意义：

      • He wouldn't debase himself by quarreling with his son.

        他不想与其儿子争吵而降低身份。

      • to debase oneself for money

        为金钱而卑躬屈膝

  • 近义词: deprive , corrupt , debauch , pervert
  • 反义词: elevate , improve
    • 变形：
    • debased , debasing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

debase [ di'beis ]

  • v.
    • corrupt morally or by intemperance or sensuality

      同义词：corrupt | pervert | subvert | demoralize | demoralise | debauch | profane | vitiate | deprave | misdirect

    • lower in value by increasing the base-metal content

      同义词：alloy

    • corrupt, debase, or make impure by adding a foreign or inferior substance; often by replacing valuable ingredients with inferior ones

      同义词：adulterate | stretch | dilute
