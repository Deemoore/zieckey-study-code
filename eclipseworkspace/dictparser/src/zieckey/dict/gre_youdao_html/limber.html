limber1 ['limbə]

  • adj.
    • 易弯曲的；柔软的；柔韧的；灵活的
    • 可塑的；思想灵活的
  • vt.
    • 使柔软 (常与 up 连用)：

      • You should limber up your wits before the test.

        在测验之前你该动动脑筋。

  • vi.
    • 变得柔软；使自己柔软；(赛前)做准备活动 (常与 up 连用)：

      • The players limbered up before the game.

        选手们在赛前做准备活动。

limber2 ['limbə]

  • n.
    • 【军事】(野炮拖车或弹药车的)前车
  • vt.
    • 把(火炮等)系于前车(准备拖车)
  • vi.
    • 连接前车和火炮 (常与 up 连用)；上架：

      • The colonel ordered the soldiers to limber up.

        上校命令士兵们把火炮系在前车上。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

limber [ 'limbə ]

  • n. a two-wheeled horse-drawn vehicle used to pull a field gun or caisson
  • v.
    • attach the limber

      "limber a cannon"

      同义词：limber up

    • cause to become limber

      "The violist limbered her wrists before the concert"

  • adj.
    • (used of e.g. personality traits) readily adaptable

      "a limber imagination"

      同义词：supple

    • (used of artifacts) easily bent
    • (used of persons' bodies) capable of moving or bending freely

      同义词：supple
