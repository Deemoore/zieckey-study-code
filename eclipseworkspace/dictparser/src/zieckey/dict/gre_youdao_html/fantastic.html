fantastic [fæn'tæstik]

  • adj.
    • 想象中的；虚幻的；虚构的，不真实的
    • 奇异的，古怪的，荒诞离奇的
    • 异想天开的；幻想的，想入非非的；胡思乱想的
    • 难以置信的；不可思议的；极大的
    • [口语]极好的，极出色的[亦作 Fantastical]
  • n.
    • [古语](行为、衣着)古怪的人
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fantastic [ fæn'tæstik ]

  • adj.
    • ludicrously odd

      "fantastic Halloween costumes"

      同义词：antic | fantastical | grotesque

    • extraordinarily good; used especially as intensifiers

      "a fantastic trip to the Orient"; " the film was fantastic!"

      同义词：grand | howling(a) | marvelous | marvellous | rattling(a) | terrific | tremendous | wonderful | wondrous

    • extravagantly fanciful and unrealistic; foolish

      "a fantastic idea of his own importance"

      同义词：wild

    • existing in fancy only

      "fantastic figures with bulbous heads the circumference of a bushel"

      同义词：fantastical

    • of the highest quality

      同义词：ace | A-one | crack | first-rate | super | tiptop | topnotch | tops(p)

    • extravagantly fanciful in design, construction, appearance

      "Gaudi's fantastic architecture"

    • exceedingly or unbelievably great

      "the bomb did fantastic damage"; " Samson is supposed to have had fantastic strength"

      同义词：phenomenal

以上来源于: WordNet
