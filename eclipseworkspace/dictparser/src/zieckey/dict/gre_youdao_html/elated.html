elate [i'leit]

  • vt.
    • 使兴奋，使精神振奋；使得意洋洋；使兴高采烈，使欢欣鼓舞：

      • news to elate the hearer

        使听众欢欣鼓舞的消息

  • adj.
    • [诗歌用语]兴奋的；兴高采烈的，欢欣鼓舞的；得意洋洋的
    • 变形：
    • elated , elating

elated [ i'leitid ]

  • adj.
    • exultantly proud and joyful; in high spirits

      "the elated winner"; " felt elated and excited"

    • full of high-spirited delight

      同义词：gleeful | joyful | jubilant
