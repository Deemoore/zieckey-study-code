oracular [ɔ'rækjulə; ɔ:-]

  • adj.
    • 神谕的；似神谕的
    • 圣言的，哲人的；天启似的
    • 玄妙深奥的，晦涩的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

oracular [ ɔ'rækjulə, ɔ:- ]

  • adj.
    • of or relating to an oracle

      "able by oracular means to expose a witch"

    • obscurely prophetic

      "an oracular message"

      同义词：Delphic

    • resembling an oracle in obscurity of thought

      "the oracular sayings of Victorian poets"

      同义词：enigmatic
