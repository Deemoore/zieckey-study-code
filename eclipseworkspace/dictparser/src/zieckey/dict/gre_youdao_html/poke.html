poke1 [pəuk]

  • vt.
    • (用棍棒、手指等)触，碰；戳；捅：

      • Be careful not to poke someone in the eye with your umbrella.

        注意别把伞戳着别人的眼睛。

    • 用…戳(或捅)； 把…指(或伸)向：

      • Never poke a finger or scissors into an electric socket.

        千万不要把手指或剪刀插进电源插座。

    • 拨弄，搅动：

      • Father poked the fire with a poker.

        爸爸用通条把火捅得更旺。

    • [美国俚语]
      • 赶(牛、羊等)
      • 激发；引起：

        • All he had ever done was poke a team or explore the trail or push cattle along.

          他所做的事就是激励队伍向前、探索道路或者驱赶畜群。

      • [口语]用拳打，打出(一拳)
      • 【板球】击(球)：

        • I made a suggestive remark and she poked me in the jaw.

          我只是建议，她朝我下巴打了一拳。

    • [口语]使困居：

      • I didn’t want to stay poked up in the village.

        我不想困在农村里。

    • [俚语]同女子性交
  • vi.
    • 戳；刺；捅；拨弄(at):

      • The old man poked among the waste materials with a stick.

        老人在废料堆中东翻西戳。

    • (头等)伸出，翻弄：

      • I saw his head poking through the window.

        我看见了他的头探出了窗外。

    • 探听;刺探(有时与about 或 around 连用)：

      • They went everywhere, and poked into everything.

        他们到处都去，什么事都打听。

    • 突出：

      • The sun poked over the horizon.

        太阳从地平线上升起。

    • 探索地走；逛；闲荡；进展缓慢(常与 along连用)：

      • Produc-tion pokes along.

        生产进展缓慢。

    • 猛打，猛击；【板球】击球：

      • The boxer kept poking at him but never hit him.

        拳击手伸出拳头要打他，但总也没打着。

    • 干涉，介入：

      • They often poked into his private affairs.

        他们经常干涉他的私事。

    • (不讲次序、好奇地)寻找；调查；检验(某物)：

      • Mother went into the attic where she poked about among old boxes and trunks.

        母亲走上顶楼，在旧箱子里东翻西找。

    • 蛰居在毫无生气的地方：

      • She doesn’t want to poke around in that city any longer.

        她不想再呆在这所死气沉沉的城市里。

    • 延伸，挺伸：

      • They all saw to it that the road poked down closer to the remote city.

        他们都看到公路延伸到远方的城市。

    • [俚语]同女子性交
  • n.
    • 戳；刺；捅；拨；触
    • 朝前撑起的阔边女帽；(女帽)朝前撑起的阔边[亦作 poke bonnet]
    • (套在牛、猪等牲口上以防越栏的)触地颈扼
    • =slow poke
    • 蠢得让人讨厌的人
    • =pokeweed
    • (甲状腺肿)颈部肿大；肝蛭感染引起的疾病；羊身上因肝蛭感染引起的肿大
    • 【计算机】置数，存数；改变内存值的命令
    • [口语]一拳；【板球】一击
    • [俚语] 衣领，领子
    • [美国俚语](美国西部的)骑马牧人，牛仔
    • [俚语]性交；性交的女方
    • [俚语]力量；马力

poke [ pəuk ]

  • n.
    • tall coarse perennial American herb having small white flowers followed by blackish-red berries on long drooping racemes; young fleshy stems are edible; berries and root are poisonous

      同义词：pigeon berry | garget | scoke | Phytolacca americana

    • someone who takes more time than necessary; someone who lags behind

      同义词：dawdler | drone | laggard | lagger | trailer

    • a bag made of paper or plastic for holding customer's purchases

      同义词：sack | paper bag | carrier bag

    • a sharp hand gesture (resembling a blow)

      同义词：jab | jabbing | poking | thrust | thrusting

    • (boxing) a blow with the fist

      同义词：punch | clout | lick | biff

  • v.
    • poke or thrust abruptly

      同义词：jab | prod | stab | dig

    • search or inquire in a meddlesome way

      同义词：intrude | horn in | pry | nose

    • stir by poking

      "poke the embers in the fireplace"

    • hit hard with the hand, fist, or some heavy instrument

      同义词：thump | pound

    • make a hole by poking
