dual ['dju:əl]

  • adj.
    • 二元的；两体的；两层的；两的，双的
    • 由两人(或两件、两部分)组成的；双倍的；双重的；两重的
    • 有两重(或双重)特点的
    • 【语法学】双数的(古英语、希腊语等名词、动词等除有单复数变化外，还有双数变化)
  • n.
    • 【语法学】
      • 双数
      • 双数词(或短语)
    • 对偶的形式
    • [复数] (卡车的)双轮胎
  • vt.
    • [英国英语]把(道路)改为双车道(或复式车行道)：

      • The road was already dualled.

        那条路已改成了双车道。

    • 变形：
    • dualled , dualling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dual [ 'dju:əl ]

  • adj.
    • consisting of or involving two parts or components usually in pairs

      "dual controls for pilot and copilot"

      同义词：double | duple

    • having more than one decidedly dissimilar aspects or qualities

      "a double (or dual) role for an actor"

      同义词：double | twofold | two-fold | treble | threefold | three-fold

    • a grammatical number category referring to two items or units as opposed to one item (singular) or more than two items (plural)

      "ancient Greek had the dual form but it has merged with the plural form in modern Greek"
