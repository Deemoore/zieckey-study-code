athletics [æθ'letiks]

  • n.
      • 体育运动，运动，竞技，比赛
      • [英国英语]田径运动(包括棒球、篮球)
    • [用作单数]体育(课)
    • 运动技巧；运动(或竞技)规则
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

athletics [ æθ'letiks ]

  • n.
    • an active diversion requiring physical exertion and competition

      同义词：sport

    • a contest between athletes

      同义词：athletic contest | athletic competition

    • participation in sports events as an extracurricular activity
