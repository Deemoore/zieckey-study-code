cinch [sintʃ]

  • n.
    • (系住马鞍的)肚带
    • [口语]紧握，抓牢
    • [俚语]有把握的事情；容易做的事情；必然发生的事情
  • vt.
    • 给(马)系紧肚带；紧紧地捆上：

      • He cinched the belt of his knapsack around his shoulders.

        他把背包带紧紧地拴在肩膀上。

    • [俚语]确实把握住；确保；弄清楚：

      • Ability and hard work cinched his success.

        才干与努力保证了他的成功。

  • vi.
    • 捆紧马肚带：

      • to cinch up

        束紧腹带

  • 短语:
    • dead cinch [美国英语]确定无疑的事
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cinch [ sintʃ ]

  • n.
    • any undertaking that is easy to do

      同义词：breeze | picnic | snap | duck soup | child's play | pushover | walkover | piece of cake

    • stable gear consisting of a band around a horse's belly that holds the saddle in place

      同义词：girth

    • a form of all fours in which the players bid for the privilege of naming trumps
  • v.
    • tie a cinch around

      "cinch horses"

      同义词：girth

    • make sure of
    • get a grip on; get mastery of
