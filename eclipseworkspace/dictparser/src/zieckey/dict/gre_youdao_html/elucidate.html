elucidate [ i'lu:si,deit ]

  • v.
    • make clear and (more) comprehensible

      同义词：clarify | clear up

    • make free from confusion or ambiguity; make clear

      同义词：clear | clear up | shed light on | crystallize | crystallise | crystalize | crystalise | straighten out | sort out | enlighten | illuminate
