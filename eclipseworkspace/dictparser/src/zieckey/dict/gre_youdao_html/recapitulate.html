recapitulate [,ri:kæpitjuleit]

  • vi.,vt. 扼要重述；扼要说明；概括：

    • He recapitulated the resolution demanding better conditions which had been passed in the conference.

      他简单扼要地说明了会议中通过的改善环境的决议。

  • 近义词: repeat
    • 变形：
    • recapitulated , recapitulating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

recapitulate [ ,ri:kæpitjuleit ]

  • v.
    • summarize briefly

      "Let's recapitulate the main ideas"

      同义词：recap

    • repeat stages of evolutionary development during the embryonic phase of life
    • repeat an earlier theme of a composition

      同义词：reprise | reprize | repeat
