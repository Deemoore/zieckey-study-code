appal [ə'pɔ:l]

  • vt.
    • 使惊愕，使惊骇，使震惊，使丧胆，惊吓：

      • They were appalled by the damage from the fire.

        他们对火灾所造成的损失感到震惊。

      • I am appalled at his mistakes.

        我对他的错误感到惊愕。

  • vi.
    • 减弱，衰退
    • 失味，走味
    • 变得苍白
    • (因过多过滥而)令人发腻
    • 变形：
    • appalled , appalling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

appal [ ə'pɔ:l ]

  • v.
    • strike with disgust or revulsion

      同义词：shock | offend | scandalize | scandalise | appall | outrage

    • fill with apprehension or alarm; cause to be unpleasantly surprised

      同义词：dismay | alarm | appall | horrify

以上来源于: WordNet
