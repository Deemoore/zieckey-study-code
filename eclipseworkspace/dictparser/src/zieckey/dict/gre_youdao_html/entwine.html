entwine [in'twain]

  • vt.
    • 使缠绕，与…缠绕在一起，盘绕；编织：

      • Roses and honeysuckle entwine the little cottage.

        玫瑰与忍冬盘绕着小屋。

      • to entwine a garland

        编花环

    • 使紧密地结合；使交织，使交错；使纠缠：

      • They walked along with their fingers entwined.

        他们手拉着手往前走。

      • His fate was closely entwined with her.

        他和她的命运紧密相连。

    • [古语]拥抱
  • vi.
    • 纠缠在一起；缠绕[亦作 intwine]
    • 变形：
    • entwined , entwining
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

entwine [ in'twain ]

  • v.
    • tie or link together

      同义词：knit

    • spin or twist together so as to form a cord

      同义词：intertwine | twine | enlace | interlace | lace
