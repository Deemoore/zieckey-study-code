limpid ['limpid]

  • adj.
    • (水、空气等)清澈的；透明的
    • 平静的；无忧无虑的
    • (文体等)清晰的，明朗的，畅达的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

limpid [ 'limpid ]

  • adj.
    • clear and bright

      "limpid blue eyes"

      同义词：liquid

    • transmitting light; able to be seen through with clarity

      "could see the sand on the bottom of the limpid pool"

      同义词：crystalline | crystal clear | lucid | pellucid | transparent

    • (of language) transparently clear; easily understandable

      "writes in a limpid style"

      同义词：lucid | luculent | pellucid | crystal clear | perspicuous

以上来源于: WordNet
