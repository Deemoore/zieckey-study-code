lean1 [li:n]

  • vi.
    • 倾身；屈身；倾斜；倾向：

      • The flagpole leans to the south.

        旗杆向南倾斜。

    • 倚；靠；凭依：

      • The girl leaned on the desk.

        女孩倚靠在书桌上。

    • 依靠；依赖于：

      • She leans on her husband for advice.

        她靠丈夫出主意。

    • 倾向于(某种观点、意见或态度等)(与to 或 toward连用)：

      • In his political opinions he leans toward the Labour Party.

        他的政见倾向于工党。

  • vt.
    • 使倾斜；使倚靠；把…靠在某物上：

      • to lean one's head back

        将头后仰

      • to lean the ladder against the wall

        把梯子靠在墙上

  • n.
    • 倾向；倾斜；倾斜度
  • 短语:
    • on the lean 倾斜着

lean2 [li:n]

  • adj.
    • (人或动物)瘦的；精瘦的；少肉的
    • 无脂肪的；脂肪少的
    • 贫乏的；贫瘠的；歉收的；收益差的；无利可图的
    • 简洁的；尖锐的；犀利的；直率的
    • 质量低劣的
    • 【印刷】细体的
    • (油漆等)颜料多而油少的
    • (公司、企业等降低成本而)更高效的，更具竞争力的
  • n.
    • [the lean]瘦肉
    • 收益少的事务
    • 【材料学】贫料
  • vt.
    • 使变瘦；使瘦弱：

      • The teacher was leaned out by his long illness.

        长年生病使这位老师变得消瘦。

  • vi.
    • [废语]变瘦
  • 反义词: fat , fleshy
    • 变形：
    • leaner
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

lean [ li:n ]

  • n. the property possessed by a line or surface that departs from the vertical

    同义词：tilt | list | inclination | leaning

  • v.
    • to incline or bend from a vertical position

      "She leaned over the banister"

      同义词：tilt | tip | slant | angle

    • cause to lean or incline

      "He leaned his rifle against the wall"

    • have a tendency or disposition to do or be something; be inclined

      同义词：tend | be given | incline | run

    • rely on for support

      "We can lean on this man"

    • cause to lean to the side

      同义词：list

  • adj.
    • lacking excess flesh

      "Yon Cassius has a lean and hungry look"

      同义词：thin

    • lacking in mineral content or combustible material

      "lean ore"; " lean fuel"

    • containing little excess

      "a lean budget"

      同义词：skimpy

    • not profitable or prosperous

      "a lean year"
