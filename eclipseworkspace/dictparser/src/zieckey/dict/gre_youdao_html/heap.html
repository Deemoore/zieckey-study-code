heap [hi:p]

  • n.
    • (一)堆，堆积
    • [口语]
      • 群
      • 大量，许多
    • [口语]邋遢女人，
    • [美国俚语](破旧)汽车(或飞机)；旧机器
  • vt.
    • 把…堆成一堆，使成堆；堆积，堆起(常与 up， on或together连用):

      • He heaped the diamonds up and said they were all his.

        他把那些钻石堆成一堆，然后说那些钻石全是他的。

      • to heap stones on the road

        在路上用石子堆成堆

    • 聚集,集思(常与 up 或 together连用):

      • The best interest she likes is to heap up dollars.

        她的最大嗜好就是攒钱。

    • 大量给予，滥给；拼命添加(常与 on， upon 连用):

      • You have no right to heap insults on anybody.

        他没有权力侮辱任何一个人。

      • to heap work on someone

        把工作大量压在某人身上

    • 使充满，装满，使充溢:

      • His bag was heaped with tissues.

        他的包中装了满满一包的卫生纸。

      • to heap a bowl with soup

        盛上满满一碗汤

  • vi.
    • 堆成堆，堆积起来:

      • Sands heaped everywhere on the beach.

        海滩上到处是沙堆。

  • adv.
    • [复数][口语]很，非常
  • 短语:
    • be (或knocked) all of a heap [口语]被弄得昏头昏脑，被弄得惊慌失措，吓成一团
    • bottom of the heap [口语]失败者；输家
    • heaps of [口语]大量的，无数的，许多的
    • strike (或 knock) someone all of a heap 使某人惊慌失措，让某人大吃一惊，把某人搞得晕头转向
    • top of the heap [口语]获胜者；赢得
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

heap [ hi:p ]

  • n.
    • a collection of objects laid on top of each other

      同义词：pile | mound | agglomerate | cumulation | cumulus

    • (often followed by `of') a large number or amount or extent

      同义词：batch | deal | flock | good deal | great deal | hatful | lot | mass | mess | mickle | mint | muckle | peck | pile | plenty | pot | quite a little | raft | sight | slew | spate | stack | tidy sum | wad | whole lot | whole slew

    • a car that is old and unreliable

      同义词：bus | jalopy

  • v.
    • bestow in large quantities

      "He heaped him with work"; " She heaped scorn upon him"

    • arrange in stacks

      "heap firewood around the fireplace"

      同义词：stack | pile

    • fill to overflow

      "heap the platter with potatoes"
