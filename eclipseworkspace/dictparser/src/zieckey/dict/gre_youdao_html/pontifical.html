pontifical [pɔn'tifikəl]

  • adj.
    • 大祭司的；主教的；高级教士的
    • (弥撒等)由主教级教士主持的
    • 教皇的
    • 傲慢武断的；自负的
    • 威严的；壮观的
  • n.
    • [复数] 主教法衣与标志
    • (西派教会的)主教祭礼典，主教仪典
    • 主教仪典书
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

pontifical [ pɔn'tifikəl ]

  • n. the vestments and other insignia of a pontiff (especially a bishop)
  • adj.
    • proceeding from or ordered by or subject to a pope or the papacy regarded as the successor of the Apostles

      同义词：papal | apostolic | apostolical

    • denoting or governed by or relating to a bishop or bishops

      同义词：episcopal

    • puffed up with vanity

      "pseudo-scientific gobbledygook and pontifical hooey"

      同义词：grandiloquent | overblown | pompous | portentous

以上来源于: WordNet
