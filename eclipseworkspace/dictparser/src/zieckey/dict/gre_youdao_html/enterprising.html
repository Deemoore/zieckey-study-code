enterprising ['entəpraiziŋ]

  • adj. 有事业心的，有进取心的；充满首创精神的，富于创业精神的；有魅力的，有胆量的；富于想像力的
  • 近义词: ambitious
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

enterprising [ 'entəpraiziŋ ]

  • adj. marked by imagination, initiative, and readiness to undertake new projects

    "an enterprising foreign policy"; " an enterprising young man likely to go far"
