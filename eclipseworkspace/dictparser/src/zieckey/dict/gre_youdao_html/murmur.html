murmur ['mə:mə]

  • n.
    • (低沉连续的)沙沙声；潺潺声；低语；细语
    • 怨言；牢骚；嘀咕
    • 【病理学】(心脏等的)杂音 [亦作heart murmur]
    • 【语言学】弱元音；低语元音 [亦作murmur vowel]
  • vi.
    • 发低沉连续的声音；沙沙地响：

      • The stream is murmuring.

        溪水潺潺。

    • 小声说，私语：

      • He murmured under his breath.

        他喃喃自语。

    • 低声抱怨；嘀嘀咕咕(与against或at连用)：

      • The workers murmured against the treatment they received.

        工人们抱怨所受的待遇。

  • vt.
    • 低声说：

      • George murmured words of love in her ear.

        乔治在她耳边低声说着情话。

    • 低声抱怨(与that连用)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

murmur [ 'mə:mə ]

  • n.
    • a low continuous indistinct sound; often accompanied by movement of the lips without the production of articulate speech

      同义词：mutter | muttering | murmuring | murmuration | mussitation

    • a schwa that is incidental to the pronunciation of a consonant

      同义词：murmur vowel

    • an abnormal sound of the heart; sometimes a sign of abnormal function of the heart valves

      同义词：heart murmur | cardiac murmur

    • a complaint uttered in a low and indistinct tone

      同义词：grumble | grumbling | murmuring | mutter | muttering

  • v.
    • speak softly or indistinctly

      "She murmured softly to the baby in her arms"

    • make complaining remarks or noises under one's breath

      同义词：mutter | grumble | croak | gnarl

以上来源于: WordNet
