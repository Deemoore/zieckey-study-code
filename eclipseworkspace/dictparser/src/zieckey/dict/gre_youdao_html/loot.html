loot1 [lu:t]

  • n.
    • 掠夺物；战利品
    • 掠夺，劫掠，抢劫，洗劫
    • 赃物
    • [总称]礼物
    • [俚语]金钱；值钱货
  • vt.
    • 洗劫(城市等)：

      • The gangsters looted 3 banks in a week.

        歹徒们在一周之内抢劫了三家银行。

    • 掠夺，抢劫(物品)：

      • They looted all the belongings of the passengers.

        他们抢劫了乘客们的所有财物。

    • (用欺诈等手段)掠夺，劫掠：

      • to loot the public treasury

        劫掠政府金库

  • vi.
    • 进行掠夺，抢劫，劫夺
  • 近义词: spoil

loot2 [lu:t]

  • n. [美军俚语]陆军中尉[lieutenant的缩略变体]

loot [ lu:t ]

  • n.
    • goods or money obtained illegally

      同义词：booty | pillage | plunder | prize | swag | dirty money

    • informal terms for money

      同义词：boodle | bread | cabbage | clams | dinero | dough | gelt | kale | lettuce | lolly | lucre | moolah | pelf | scratch | shekels | simoleons | sugar | wampum

  • v.
    • take illegally; of intellectual property

      同义词：plunder

    • steal goods; take as spoils

      "During the earthquake people looted the stores that were deserted by their owners"

      同义词：plunder | despoil | reave | strip | rifle | ransack | pillage | foray
