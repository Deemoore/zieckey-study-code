herd1 [hə:d]

  • n.
    • 兽群；(尤指)牧群，牛群，羊群，猪群
    • [口语]人群，群体，集团；群众，民众，百姓
    • [the herd]普通人，贱民；乌合之众
    • 大量，多数
  • vi.
    • 聚在一起；成群地移动：

      • Wild geese herd to the south in autumn every year.

        每年秋天大雁都成群结队地飞往南方。

    • 结交，交往(与with连用)：

      • You should not herd with the man as him.

        你不应和他那样的人来往。

  • 近义词: group
  • 短语:
    • ride herd on
      • [美国英语]
      • 严密控制；管束
      • 骑马看管牛群等

herd2 [hə:d]

  • n.
    • 牧人
    • [方言]养羊
    • [美国西部方言]放牧工作
  • vt.
    • 牧放(牛、羊等)；照管(畜群、孩子等)：

      • What,am I going to herd your children this afternoon?

        什么，今天下午让我去照管你的孩子们?

      • to herd sheep

        放羊

    • 把…放入群，使入群：

      • How do you herd her with us working?

        你怎么让她和我们一起工作?

      • to be herded into the group company

        加入集团公司

    • 把…集中在一起；把…赶在一起：

      • They were separately herded into 2 rooms to lock up.

        他们分别被集中关押在两个房间里。

      • He herded us together on the ground.

        他让我们在操场上集中起来。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

herd [ hə:d ]

  • n.
    • a group of cattle or sheep or other domestic mammals all of the same kind that are herded by humans
    • a group of wild mammals of one species that remain together: antelope or elephants or seals or whales or zebra
    • a crowd especially of ordinary or undistinguished persons or things

      "the children resembled a fairy herd"

      同义词：ruck

  • v.
    • cause to herd, drive, or crowd together

      "We herded the children into a spare classroom"

      同义词：crowd

    • move together, like a herd
    • keep, move, or drive animals

      "Who will be herding the cattle when the cowboy dies?"

以上来源于: WordNet
