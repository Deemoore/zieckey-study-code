appealing [ə'pi:liŋ]

  • adj.
    • 有吸引力的，有感染力的，吸引人的，引人入胜的，动人的
    • 恳求的；哀诉的，哀恳的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

appealing [ ə'pi:liŋ ]

  • adj.
    • able to attract interest or draw favorable attention

      "He added an appealing and memorable figure to popular American mythology"; " an appealing sense of humor"; " the idea of having enough money to retire at fifty is very appealing"

    • (of characters in literature or drama) evoking empathic or sympathetic feelings

      同义词：sympathetic | likeable | likable

    • expressing earnest entreaty

      "the appealing and frightened look worn by an injured dog"

      同义词：imploring | importunate | pleading

以上来源于: WordNet
