lounge [laundʒ]

  • vi.
    • (懒散地)倚，坐，躺：

      • The child lounged in an armchair.

        孩子懒洋洋地倚在扶手椅里。

    • 混(时间)，懒洋洋地打发时光：

      • They are lounging away their working hours.

        他们在消磨工作时间。

    • 闲荡,闲逛(常与 along, around, at, off等连用)：

      • He enjoys lounging at street corners.

        他爱在街头闲荡。

  • vt.
    • 虚度(光阴)；吊儿郎当地混日子：

      • The children lounged the whole morning away in the park.

        孩子们在公园里消磨了整个上午。

  • n.
    • (旅馆、剧场等处的)休息处，休息室；吸烟室
    • [主英国英语]酒吧间
    • [主英国英语]起居室
    • 公共休息室
    • 躺椅；安乐椅；沙发
    • 休闲音乐[亦作 lounge music]
    • [罕用语]懒散的步子；懒洋洋的步态
    • [古语]闲荡(的时间)；懒散
    • 变形：
    • lounged , lounging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

lounge [ laundʒ ]

  • n.
    • an upholstered seat for more than one person

      同义词：sofa | couch

    • a room (as in a hotel or airport) with seating where people can wait

      同义词：waiting room | waiting area

  • v.
    • sit or recline comfortably
    • be about

      同义词：loiter | footle | lollygag | loaf | lallygag | hang around | mess about | tarry | linger | lurk | mill about | mill around
