    • 【兽医学】(马等的)蹄裂

cleft2 [kleft]

  • v.
    • cleave的过去式和过去分词
  • adj.
    • 劈开的；裂开的；分开的；分裂的，分离的
    • 【植物学】(叶子等)半裂的，尖裂的，深裂的

cleft [ kleft ]

  • n.
    • a split or indentation in something (as the palate or chin)
    • a long narrow opening

      同义词：crack | crevice | fissure | scissure

  • adj.
    • used of hooves

      同义词：cloven | bisulcate

    • having one or more incisions reaching nearly to the midrib

      同义词：dissected
