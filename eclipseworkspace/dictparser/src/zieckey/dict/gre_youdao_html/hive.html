hive1 [haiv]

  • n.
    • 蜂巢；蜂箱，蜂房
    • 蜂团，蜂群
    • 熙熙攘攘的人群
    • 蜂巢状物
    • 熙熙攘攘的场所，喧闹繁忙的地区
  • vt.
    • 使(蜜蜂)入蜂箱
    • 贮存(密)于蜂箱中
    • 贮存，把…存起备用(常与up连用)：

      • If you need some money, please let me know, I've hived up a sum of money.

        如果你需要用钱时请告诉我，我存了一笔钱。

  • vi.
    • (蜜蜂)进入蜂箱；(蜜蜂)生活在蜂箱中
    • (像蜜蜂般)聚居，密集群居：

      • They were hiving in the fields after earthquake.

        地震之后，他们聚居在旷野上。

    • 藏身；隐居(常与 up连用)
    • 变形：
    • hived , hiving

  • n. [用复数]【病理学】荨麻疹

hive [ haiv ]

  • n.
    • a teeming multitude
    • a man-made receptacle that houses a swarm of bees

      同义词：beehive

    • a structure that provides a natural habitation for bees; as in a hollow tree

      同义词：beehive

  • v.
    • store, like bees

      "bees hive honey and pollen"; " He hived lots of information"

    • move together in a hive or as if in a hive
    • gather into a hive

      "The beekeeper hived the swarm"
