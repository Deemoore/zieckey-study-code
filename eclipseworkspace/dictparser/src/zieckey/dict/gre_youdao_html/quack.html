quack1 [kwæk]

  • n.
    • 鸭叫声；(类似鸭叫的)嘎嘎声
  • vi.
    • (鸭子)嘎嘎地叫；发出类似鸭叫的声音：

      • The old duck quacked and quacked so I hardly slept a wink last night.

        老鸭子呱呱直叫，吵得我昨晚几乎一夜没合眼。

    • 大声讲没意思的蠢话；叽里呱啦：

      • He quacked for a long time, but I could not understand at all.

        他叽里呱啦地说了半天，但我一点也听不懂。

quack2 [kwæk]

  • n.
    • 庸医，江湖医生
    • 骗子，冒充内行者
  • adj.
    • 江湖医生的，谎称能治病的
    • 假冒的，胡吹的
  • vi.
    • 当冒牌医生，用骗术行医
    • 胡吹，招摇撞骗
  • vt.
    • 以假药或假医术行骗
    • 吹嘘(疗效等)，对…作夸大宣传
  • 近义词: charlatan , mountebank impostor , faker

quack3 [kwæk]

  • n. = quack grass

quack [ kwæk ]

  • n.
    • an untrained person who pretends to be a physician and who dispenses medical advice
    • the harsh sound of a duck
  • v.
    • utter quacking noises

      "The ducks quacked"

    • act as a medical quack or a charlatan
  • adj. medically unqualified

    "a quack doctor"
