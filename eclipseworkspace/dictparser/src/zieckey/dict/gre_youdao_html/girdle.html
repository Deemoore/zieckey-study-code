girdle1 ['ɡə:dl]

  • n.
    • (系在腰间的)腰带，饰带
    • 环形物，环绕物，围绕物，似带状物
    • (女子的)紧身褡
    • 【宝石】(宝石的)腰棱翻光面，抱角
      • (环状剥去树干一圈皮后形成的)环形带
      • 【植物学】成带现象
    • 【解剖学】肢带骨
  • vt.
    • 围绕，包围；绕…而行：

      • a village girdled with green fields

        绿色田野环绕的村庄

    • 在…周围绕行：

      • a satellite girdling the moon

        绕月球飞行的人造卫星

    • 用带束，用带缠
    • 将树皮剥掉一圈，环剥(树皮等)
    • 变形：
    • girdled , girdling

girdle2 ['ɡə:dl]

  • n. [英国英语](烘饼用的)平底浅锅，圆形铁板

girdle [ 'ɡə:dl ]

  • n.
    • an encircling or ringlike structure
    • a band of material around the waist that strengthens a skirt or trousers

      同义词：cincture | sash | waistband | waistcloth

    • a woman's close-fitting foundation garment

      同义词：corset | stays

  • v.
    • cut a girdle around so as to kill by interrupting the circulation of water and nutrients

      "girdle the plant"

      同义词：deaden

    • put a girdle on or around

      同义词：gird
