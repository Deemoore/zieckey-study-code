heterodoxy ['hetərəu,dɔksi]

  • n.
    • 信奉异教；离经叛道，持非正统见解
    • 异教，异端，异端邪说，非正统见解
    • 变形：
    • heterodoxies

heterodoxy [ 'hetərəu,dɔksi ]

  • n.
    • any opinions or doctrines at variance with the official or orthodox position

      同义词：unorthodoxy | heresy
