demote [,di:'məut]

  • vt. [美国英语]使降级，使降职，降低…的地位(promote 之对)：

    • As punishment,the corporal was demoted to private.

      作为惩罚，这下士被降为士兵。

    • The boss demoted the careless waiter to busboy.

      老板把那个粗心大意的餐厅侍者降为侍者助手。

    • 变形：
    • demoted , demoting
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

demote [ ,di:'məut ]

  • v. assign to a lower position; reduce in rank

    "She was demoted because she always speaks up"

    同义词：bump | relegate | break | kick downstairs

以上来源于: WordNet
