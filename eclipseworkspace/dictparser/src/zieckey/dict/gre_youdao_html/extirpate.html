extirpate ['ekstə:peit]

  • vt.
    • 拔除，连根拔起 (树、杂草等)：

      • to extirpate an unwanted hair

        拔除不要的毛发

    • 消灭，灭绝，铲除，根除 (恶习、疾病、物种等)；破除(异端邪说、迷信等)：

      • to extirpate the smoking habit

        戒除吸烟习惯

      • to extirpate disease and poverty

        根除疾病和贫穷

    • 【外科学】摘除(肿瘤等)：

      • to extirpate a tumour

        摘除肿瘤

  • 近义词: exterminate
    • 变形：
    • extirpated , extirpating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

extirpate [ 'ekstə:peit ]

  • v.
    • destroy completely, as if down to the roots

      同义词：uproot | eradicate | root out | exterminate

    • pull up by or as if by the roots

      同义词：uproot | deracinate | root out

    • surgically remove (an organ)
