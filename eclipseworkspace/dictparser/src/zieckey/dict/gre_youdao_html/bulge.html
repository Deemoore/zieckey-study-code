bulge [bʌldʒ]

  • n.
    • 膨胀，肿胀；鼓起，隆起，突出
      • 凸出部分，隆起物
      • 【军事】突出部
    • 【船舶学】船腹；防雷护体
    • [美国口语](体积、数目、销售、价格、股价、股份等的)暴增，暴涨，上涨，突然(或暂时)增大(或增多、增长)
    • [美国口语]优势，优越
    • [美国俚语](身体上)易发胖的部位(如臀部、肚子)
  • vi.
    • 膨胀；凸出；鼓起来：

      • His stomach bulged out over his belt.

        他的肚子在皮带上凸出来。

    • 装满，塞满：

      • The briefcase bulged with papers.

        公文包装满了文件。

      • The Christmas stocking bulged with goodies.

        圣诞节袜子里塞满了好吃的东西。

  • vt.
    • 使膨胀，使凸出：

      • Cookies bulged his box.

        糕饼把他的盒子塞得满满的。

  • 近义词: projection
  • 短语:
    • have (或 get) the bulge on 对…占优势，胜过
    • 变形：
    • bulged , bulging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

bulge [ bʌldʒ ]

  • n. something that bulges out or is protuberant or projects from its surroundings

    "the gun in his pocket made an obvious bulge"

    同义词：bump | hump | gibbosity | gibbousness | jut | prominence | protuberance | protrusion | extrusion | excrescence

  • v.
    • swell or protrude outwards

      "His stomach bulged after the huge meal"

      同义词：pouch | protrude

    • bulge out; form a bulge outward, or be so full as to appear to bulge

      同义词：bag

    • bulge outward

      同义词：start | protrude | pop | pop out | bulge out | bug out | come out

    • cause to bulge or swell outwards

      同义词：bulk
