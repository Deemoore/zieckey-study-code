pry1 [prai]

  • n.
    • 杠杆；撬具
    • 杠杆作用
  • vt.
    • (用杠杆等)撬；撬起；撬动：

      • We use an iron bar to pry open the box.

        我们用铁棍撬开箱子。

    • 费力得到：

      • to pry money from a miser

        好不容易从守财奴那里得到钱

    • 变形：
    • pries
    • pried , prying

pry2 [prai]

  • vi.
    • 窥探；盯着看；打听；探问：

      • I don't want them prying into my affairs.

        我不愿意他们打听我的私事。

  • n.
    • 窥探；打听
    • 过于好奇的人；爱打听别人事情的人
  • 短语:
    • Paul pry 见Paul
    • 变形：
    • pries
    • pried , prying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

pry [ prai ]

  • n. a heavy iron lever with one end forged into a wedge

    同义词：crowbar | wrecking bar | pry bar

  • v.
    • to move or force, especially in an effort to get something open

      "Raccoons managed to pry the lid off the garbage pail"

      同义词：prise | prize | lever | jimmy

    • be nosey

      "Don't pry into my personal matters!"

    • search or inquire in a meddlesome way

      同义词：intrude | horn in | nose | poke

    • make an uninvited or presumptuous inquiry

      同义词：prise
