nab [næb]

  • abbr.
    • no-alcoholic beer 或 non-alcoholic beer 无酒精啤酒
  • vt.
    • (当场)捉住；逮捕：

      • He was nabbed in the act of stealing.

        他偷东西时当场被捉住了。

    • 猛然抓住：

      • to nab exorbitant profits

        攫取暴利

    • 偷；抢：

      • He nabbed away the book from my hand.

        他从我手中把书抢走了。

  • n.
    • [俚语]抓人者；警官
    • 变形：
    • nabbed , nabbing

    • tag the base runner to get him out
    • take into custody

      "the police nabbed the suspected criminals"
