convict [kən'vikt]

  • vt.
    • 证明…有罪；宣判…有罪；定罪：

      • He was convicted of murder.

        他被判为杀人罪。

    • 使认罪，使服罪：

      • to convict someone of his offence

        使某人认识所犯的罪行

  • n.
    • 罪犯
    • 囚犯，服刑的人
  • adj.
    • [古语]被定罪的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

convict [ kən'vikt ]

  • n.
    • a person serving a sentence in a jail or prison

      同义词：con | inmate | jail bird | jailbird | gaolbird | yard bird | yardbird

    • a person who has been convicted of a criminal offence
  • v. find or declare guilty

    "The man was convicted of fraud and sentenced"

以上来源于: WordNet
