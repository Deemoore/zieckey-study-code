  • adj.
    • 格言的；充满格言的
    • 公理的；(不言)自明的
    • 公理似的
    • 【逻辑学】基于逻辑公理之上的

axiomatic [ ,æksiəu'mætik ]

  • adj.
    • evident without proof or argument

      "an axiomatic truth"

      同义词：self-evident | taken for granted(p)

    • containing aphorisms or maxims

      "axiomatic wisdom"

      同义词：aphoristic

    • of or relating to or derived from axioms

      "axiomatic physics"

      同义词：axiomatical | postulational
