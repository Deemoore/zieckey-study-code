dispel [dis'pel]

  • vt.
    • 驱除，驱赶，驱散(云、雾等)，赶跑：

      • The sun dispelled the dense fog.

        太阳驱散了浓雾。

    • 消除，消释；使消散，使溃散；减轻：

      • That fine performance dispelled any doubts about his abilities.

        那场精彩表演消除了对他能力的任何疑虑。

      • to dispell one's fears

        消除某人的恐惧

  • vi.
    • 消散，消融；散开
  • 近义词: scatter
    • 变形：
    • dispelled , dispelling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dispel [ dis'pel ]

  • v.
    • force to go away; used both with concrete and metaphoric meanings

      "dispel doubts"

      同义词：chase away | drive out | turn back | drive away | drive off | run off

    • to cause to separate and go in different directions

      同义词：disperse | dissipate | break up | scatter
