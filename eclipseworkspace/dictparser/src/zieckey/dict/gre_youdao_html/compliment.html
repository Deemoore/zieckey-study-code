compliment ['kɔmplimənt]

  • n.
    • 恭维话，赞美辞，颂辞
    • 谦恭有礼，敬意
    • [复数]致敬，问候；祝贺
    • [古语]谢礼，慰劳品；赏金，小账
  • vt.
    • 祝贺，向…致贺辞；向…致意，问候；赞美；恭维：

      • Her guests complimented her on her cooking.

        客人们对她的烹饪技艺大加赞扬。

    • 向(某人)敬赠礼物，送礼以表敬意
  • 短语:
    • angle for compliments 故意让人说赞扬话
    • compliment someone into 用恭维话使某人…
    • compliment someone on 称赞某人的…
    • compliment someone out of something 恭维某人以诈取某物
    • compliments of the season 恭贺佳节，节日的祝贺[圣诞节、新年祝贺用语]
    • fish for compliments =angle for compliments
    • pay (或give, make, present, send) one’s compliments to
      • 向…致意，向…问候
      • 赞扬，恭维
    • left-handed compliment 言不由衷(或挖苦人)的恭维话
    • return the (或 a) compliment
      • 回答某人的问候
      • 照样回敬(或报复)
    • two-edged compliment 模棱两可的恭维话，侮辱性的恭维
    • with someone’s compliments 某某敬赠(作者、出版者把新版书赠人时的套语)[亦作with the compliments of someone]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

compliment [ 'kɔmplimənt ]

  • n. a remark (or act) expressing praise and admiration
  • v.
    • say something to someone that expresses praise

      "He complimented her on her last physics paper"

      同义词：congratulate

    • express respect or esteem for
