embezzlement

  • n. embezzle的变形

embezzle [im'bezl]

  • vt.
    • 贪污，盗窃，盗用，擅用，挪用(公款)；侵吞(公物等)；监守自盗：

      • The clerk was charged with embezzling $3 million from the bank where he worked.

        这个职员被指控从他工作的银行盗窃了3百万美元。

    • [废语]挥霍，浪费
    • 变形：
    • embezzled , embezzling

embezzlement [ im'bezlmənt ]

  • n. the fraudulent appropriation of funds or property entrusted to your care but actually owned by someone else

    同义词：peculation | defalcation | misapplication | misappropriation
