plane1 [plein]

  • n. 【植物】悬铃木属(Platanus)树(=plane tree)

plane2 [plein]

  • adj.
    • 平的，平坦的；水平的
    • 【数学】位于平面上的；有关平面的
    • 平面图的
  • n.
    • 面，平面
    • (桌面等的)扁平物
    • 水平面；表面
    • 水平；程度；阶段；级
    • airplane的简略形式
    • 机翼；翼面；升力面
  • 近义词: level
  • 短语:
    • on the same plane as 和…同一水准(程度)

plane3 [plein]

  • n.
    • 刨，刨子
    • (瓦工的)镘
  • vt.
    • 刨平，刨光：

      • The carpenter planed a table smooth.

        木匠把桌面刨光了。

    • 刨掉(与off或away连用)：

      • He planed away the irregularities on a surface.

        他把表面上不平的地方刨掉了。

    • [古语]弄平，使平滑：

      • to plane the way

        把路弄平

  • vi.
    • 刨：

      • Poplar planes easily.

        杨木易刨。

    • 刨平
    • 在水平线上延伸：

      • The mellow farmlands plane to the water edge.

        肥沃的田地一直延伸到水边。

    • 变形：
    • planed , planing

plane4 [plein]

  • vi.
    • 翱翔；滑行：

      • A jet planed in from the south.

        一架喷气机从南面飞来。

    • (水上飞机、滑艇等)在水面滑行；在水面掠过：

      • These craft,when reaching a certain speed,plane on the flat after sections of their hull.

        当达到一定速度时，这类船以船身后部平坦部分滑行。

    • 乘飞机旅行：

      • She planed out of Beijing.

        她乘飞机离开了北京。

    • 变形：
    • planed , planing

plane [ plein ]

  • n.
    • an aircraft that has a fixed wing and is powered by propellers or jets

      "the flight was delayed due to trouble with the airplane"

      同义词：airplane | aeroplane

    • (mathematics) an unbounded two-dimensional shape

      "we will refer to the plane of the graph as the X-Y plane"; " any line joining two points on a plane lies wholly on that plane"

      同义词：sheet

    • a level of existence or development

      "he lived on a worldly plane"

    • a power tool for smoothing or shaping wood

      同义词：planer | planing machine

    • a carpenter's hand tool with an adjustable blade for smoothing or shaping wood

      "the cabinetmaker used a plane for the finish work"

      同义词：carpenter's plane | woodworking plane

  • v.
    • cut or remove with or as if with a plane

      同义词：shave

    • travel on the surface of water

      同义词：skim

    • make even or smooth, with or as with a carpenter's plane

      "plane the top of the door"

  • adj. having a surface without slope, tilt in which no part is higher or lower than another

    "a plane surface"

    同义词：flat | level
