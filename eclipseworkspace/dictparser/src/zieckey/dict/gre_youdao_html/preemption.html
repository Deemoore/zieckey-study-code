preemption [pri'empʃən; ,pri:-]

  • n.
    • 优先购买权；(尤指)政府赋予个人的公地优先购买权
    • 强制收购权
    • 先买，先占；抢先得到
    • 预先阻止；先发制人
    • 代替，取代；取消
    • 霸占；夺取
    • 【桥牌】先发制人叫牌 [亦作 pre-emption]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

preemption [ pri'empʃən, ,pri:- ]

  • n.
    • the judicial principle asserting the supremacy of federal over state legislation on the same subject

      同义词：pre-emption

    • the right of a government to seize or appropriate something (as property)

      同义词：pre-emption

    • the right to purchase something in advance of others

      同义词：pre-emption

    • a prior appropriation of something

      "the preemption of bandwidth by commercial interests"

      同义词：pre-emption
