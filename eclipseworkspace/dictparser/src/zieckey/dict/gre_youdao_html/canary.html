canary [kə'nεəri]

  • n.
    • =canary yellow
    • 加那利白葡萄酒
    • 加那利舞(一种步调轻快的舞蹈)
    • [俚语]告密者
    • [美国口语]年轻女人，姑娘
    • 【鸟类】卡纳利岛丝雀,白玉鸟 (Serinus canaria) =canary bird
  • adj.
    • 淡黄色的
    • 变形：
    • canaries
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

canary [ kə'nεəri ]

  • n.
    • someone acting as an informer or decoy for the police

      同义词：fink | snitch | snitcher | stoolpigeon | stool pigeon | stoolie | sneak | sneaker

    • a female singer
    • a moderate yellow with a greenish tinge

      同义词：canary yellow

    • any of several small Old World finches

      同义词：canary bird

  • adj. having the color of a canary; of a light to moderate yellow

    同义词：canary-yellow
