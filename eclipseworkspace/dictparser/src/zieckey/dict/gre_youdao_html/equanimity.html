equanimity [,i:kwə'niməti; ,e-]

  • n. (尤指紧张时的)镇静，镇定，沉着，平和，平静，宁静，安之若素，泰然
  • 近义词: composure , serenity
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

equanimity [ ,i:kwə'niməti, ,e- ]

  • n. steadiness of mind under stress

    "he accepted their problems with composure and she with equanimity"

    同义词：composure | calm | calmness
