harmonize ['hɑ:mənaiz]

  • vt.
    • 使和睦，使和谐，使一致，使融洽；使协调:

      • to harmonize one's views with the new situation

        使某人的观点与新的情况一致

    • 对照统一(类同叙述，尤指福音书)
    • 【音乐】给…配和声，给…加泛音
    • 使具和谐的艺术效果
  • vi.
    • 和谐，协调，相一致(与with连用):

      • Her stone necklace harmonized well with her dress.

        她的宝石项链与她的连衣裙十分相配。

    • 【音乐】和谐地演奏(或歌唱)；用和声演奏(或歌唱)
    • 达到和谐的艺术效果
    • 变形：
    • harmonized , harmonizing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

harmonize [ 'hɑ:mənaiz ]

  • v.
    • go together

      "The colors don't harmonize"

      同义词：harmonise | consort | accord | concord | fit in | agree

    • write a harmony for

      同义词：harmonise

    • sing or play in harmony

      同义词：harmonise

    • bring into consonance or relate harmoniously

      "harmonize the different interests"

      同义词：harmonise

    • bring into consonance or accord

      "harmonize one's goals with one's abilities"

      同义词：harmonise | reconcile

    • bring into consonance, harmony, or accord while making music or singing

      同义词：harmonise | chord
