elaborate [i'læbərət; i'læbəreit]

  • adj.
    • 精心设计(或计划、制作、装饰)的
    • (结构)复杂的；精巧的，精密的，精致的
    • 详尽的，详述的，(详尽)阐述的
    • (煞费)苦心的，费力的，刻苦的，艰苦的；辛勤的
  • vt.
    • 尽心竭力地做；详细搞出；用心作成，精心制作，苦心经营：

      • to elaborate his plans for a new engine

        精心设计一种新机器

    • 详述，详尽阐述；推敲；发挥；发展：

      • The expert elaborates his theory from his own reason.

        这位专家详尽阐述他的学说。

    • 【生理学】加工；从简单成分合成(复杂有机物)；使(体内食物或简单成分)转变成(能吸收、能排泄的)复杂有机物
  • vi.
    • 详述，详细说明(计划、观点、理论等)，(详尽)阐述；详尽计划(常与 on或upon 连用)：

      • to elaborate upon a theme or an idea

        对某一主题或想法作详细说明

    • 变得复杂
    • 变形：
    • elaborated , elaborating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

elaborate [ i'læbərət, i'læbəreit ]

  • v.
    • add details, as to an account or idea; clarify the meaning of and discourse in a learned way, usually in writing

      "She elaborated on the main ideas in her dissertation"

      同义词：lucubrate | expatiate | exposit | enlarge | flesh out | expand | expound | dilate

    • produce from basic elements or sources; change into a more developed product

      "The bee elaborates honey"

    • make more complex, intricate, or richer

      同义词：complicate | refine | rarify

    • work out in detail

      "elaborate a plan"

      同义词：work out

  • adj.
    • marked by complexity and richness of detail

      "an elaborate lace pattern"

      同义词：luxuriant

    • developed or executed with care and in minute detail

      "the elaborate register of the inhabitants prevented tax evasion"; " the carefully elaborated theme"

      同义词：detailed | elaborated

以上来源于: WordNet
