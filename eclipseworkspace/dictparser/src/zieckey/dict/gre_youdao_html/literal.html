literal ['litərəl]

  • adj.
    • (关于)字母的；文字(上)的；用字母(或文字)表达的
    • 字面上的；逐字的；完全按照原文的
    • 照字面的；原义的；非比喻的；非引申义的
    • 刻板的；只讲实际的；缺乏想像力的
    • 如实的；求实的；乏味的；不夸张的
    • 实实在在的；确确实实的；非常精确的[用作强调]
    • 文学的
  • n.
    • 【印刷】排字错误，错排；文字上的错误
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

literal [ 'litərəl ]

  • n. a mistake in printed matter resulting from mechanical failures of some kind

    同义词：misprint | erratum | typographical error | typo | literal error

  • adj.
    • being or reflecting the essential or genuine character of something

      "a literal solitude like a desert"

      同义词：actual | genuine | real

    • without interpretation or embellishment

      "a literal depiction of the scene before him"

    • limited to the explicit meaning of a word or text

      "a literal translation"

    • avoiding embellishment or exaggeration (used for emphasis)

      "it's the literal truth"

以上来源于: WordNet
