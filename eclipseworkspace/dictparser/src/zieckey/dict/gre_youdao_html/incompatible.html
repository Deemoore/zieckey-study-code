incompatible [,inkəm'pætəbl]

  • adj.
    • 不相容的；不协调的；不一致的；合不来的
    • 【医学】(药物)配伍禁忌的；(血型等)配合禁忌的，不能配合的
    • 【逻辑学】(命题)不能同时成立的
    • 【数学】(方程式等)不相容的，互斥的
    • (职位、封地等)不能同时享有的
    • (植物)不可嫁接的；不能自花受精的
  • n.
    • [常用复数]互不相容的人(或事物)
    • [复数]【医学】配伍禁忌的药物；不能配合的血型(或机体组织)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incompatible [ ,inkəm'pætəbl ]

  • adj.
    • not compatible

      "incompatible personalities"; " incompatible colors"

    • used especially of drugs or muscles that counteract or neutralize each other's effect

      同义词：antagonistic

    • not suitable to your tastes or needs

      同义词：uncongenial

    • incapable of being used with or connected to other devices or components without modification
    • of words so related that one contrasts with the other

      同义词：contrastive

    • not easy to combine harmoniously

      同义词：ill-sorted | mismated | unsuited

    • not compatible with other facts

      同义词：discrepant

    • not in keeping with what is correct or proper

      同义词：inappropriate | out or keeping(p) | unfitting

    • used especially of solids or solutions; incapable of blending into a stable homogeneous mixture

以上来源于: WordNet
