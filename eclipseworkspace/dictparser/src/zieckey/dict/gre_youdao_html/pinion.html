pinion1 ['pinjən]

  • n. 【机械学】小齿轮；副齿轮；齿杆

pinion2 ['pinjən]

  • n.
    • 鸟翅膀的尖端部分(包括腕骨、掌骨和指骨)
    • (鸟的)翅膀
    • 羽毛；翮
    • 昆虫翅膀的前缘
  • vt.
    • 缚住(鸟的)翼；剪断(鸟的)翅膀
    • 缚住(双翅或双臂)
    • 绑住…的双臂；把…绑住
    • 把…铐住
    • 束缚：

      • to be pinioned to one's bad habits

        被恶习所束缚住

pinion3 [pi:'njən]

  • n. ＝piñon

pinion [ 'pinjən ]

  • n.
    • a gear with a small number of teeth designed to mesh with a larger wheel or rack
    • any of the larger wing or tail feathers of a bird

      同义词：flight feather | quill | quill feather

    • wing of a bird

      同义词：pennon

  • v.
    • bind the arms of

      同义词：shackle

    • cut the wings off (of birds)
