motley ['mɔtli]

  • adj.
    • 杂色的，五颜六色的
    • 穿杂色衣服的
    • 混杂的，成分杂乱的
  • n.
    • 杂色呢；杂色布
    • 杂色衣，(小丑穿的)杂色服装；小丑装束
    • 丑角，小丑
    • 混杂的人群；混杂物；成分杂乱的东西
  • vt.
    • 使成杂色；使杂乱
  • 短语:
    • put on (或wear)the motley 扮演丑角，插科打诨
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

motley [ 'mɔtli ]

  • n.
    • a collection containing a variety of sorts of things

      同义词：assortment | mixture | mixed bag | miscellany | miscellanea | variety | salmagundi | smorgasbord | potpourri

    • a garment made of motley (especially a court jester's costume)
    • a multicolored woolen fabric woven of mixed threads in 14th to 17th century England
  • v.
    • make something more diverse and varied

      同义词：vary | variegate

    • make motley; color with different colors

      同义词：parti-color

  • adj.
    • consisting of a haphazard assortment of different kinds (even to the point of incongruity)

      "a motley crew"

      同义词：assorted | miscellaneous | mixed | sundry(a)

    • having sections or patches colored differently and usually brightly

      "a jester dressed in motley"

      同义词：calico | multicolor | multi-color | multicolour | multi-colour | multicolored | multi-colored | multicoloured | multi-coloured | painted | particolored | particoloured | piebald | pied | varicolored | varicoloured
