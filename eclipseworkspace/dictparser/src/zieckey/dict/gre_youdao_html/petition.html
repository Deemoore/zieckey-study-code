petition [pi'tiʃən]

  • n.
    • 请愿；申请；请求
    • 请愿书
    • 祈求；祈祷；祷文
    • 请求(或恳请)的东西
    • 【法律】申请书；陈情书；诉状
  • vt.
    • 向…请愿；请求；要求：

      • They petitioned the government to reduce taxes.

        他们向政府要求减税。

    • 祈求；恳求：

      • The students in the village petitioned the authority for a new playground.

        村里的学生恳求当局修建一个新操场。

  • vi.
    • 请愿；要求；请求：

      • The people petitioned to be allowed to return to their home village.

        这些人请愿，要求允许他们返回到自己的家乡。

  • 近义词: appeal
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

petition [ pi'tiʃən ]

  • n.
    • a formal message requesting something that is submitted to an authority

      同义词：request | postulation

    • reverent petition to a deity

      同义词：prayer | orison

  • v. write a petition for something to somebody; request formally and in writing
