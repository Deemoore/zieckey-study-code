reflect [ri'flekt]

  • vt.
    • 反射(声、光、热等)：

      • The high wall reflects sounds.

        这面高墙将声音反射回来。

    • 反映，反照；映出：

      • The smooth surface of the lake reflected the bright full moon.

        平静的湖面反映出明亮的圆月。

    • 给…带来，博得；蒙受(名誉或耻辱等)：

      • His behaviour reflects dishonour on his parents.

        他的行为有损于他父母的声誉。

      • The team's victory reflected credit on the coach.

        球队的胜利给教练带来了荣誉。

    • 显示，表明，反映：

      • His actions reflect his thoughts.

        他的行为反映他的思想。

    • 考虑；想到；认识到；悟及(that)[后跟从句或用于插入语]：

      • She reflected that there were still a lot of poor people living in the mountainous districts.

        她考虑到在山区还住着很多穷苦的人。“I shall not live here any more，”

      • he reflected.

        他想了一想说：“我不能再在这里住下去。”

    • 折转；反折[常用作过去分词]：

      • The trees reflected in the water are dancing.

        水中倒映的树影婆娑起舞。

  • vi.
    • 反射出(声、光、热等)，折回：

      • The sunlight reflected from the surface of the lake.

        阳光由湖面反射过来。

    • (声、光、热等)反射回来(back):

      • The sound reflected back in one tenth of a second.

        声音在十分之一秒以后反射回来。

    • 映出，被映出：

      • The mountain reflecting in the mirror of the lake looks very beautiful.

        湖面上映现出的山影十分美丽。

    • 认真思考，对…深思熟虑；沉思反省(on或upon):

      • I have reflected upon his suggestion.

        我已经认真思考了他的建议。

    • 带来耻辱；指摘，责备；损及(on 或upon):

      • What you said yesterday reflected on her honour.

        你昨天讲的话使她的名誉受到损害。

    • 使…受到影响；产生…影响(on或upon):

      • Your conduct will seriously reflect upon your future.

        你的行为将严重影响你的未来。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

reflect [ ri'flekt ]

  • v.
    • manifest or bring back

      "This action reflects his true beliefs"

    • to throw or bend back or reflect (from a surface)

      "A mirror in the sun can reflect light into a person's eyes"; " Sound is reflected well in this auditorium"

      同义词：reverberate

    • reflect deeply on a subject

      同义词：chew over | think over | meditate | ponder | excogitate | contemplate | muse | mull | mull over | ruminate | speculate

    • be bright by reflecting or casting light

      "Drive carefully--the wet road reflects"

      同义词：shine

    • give evidence of a certain behavior

      "His lack of interest in the project reflects badly on him"

    • give evidence of the quality of

      "The mess in his dorm room reflects on the student"
