arduous ['ɑ:djuəs]

  • adj.
    • 困难的，艰难的，艰巨的，费力的
    • 勤奋的，努力的，辛勤的；使劲的
    • 险峻的，陡峭的，难攀登的
    • 难以忍受的；严重的
  • 近义词: hard
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

arduous [ 'ɑ:djuəs ]

  • adj.
    • characterized by toilsome effort to the point of exhaustion; especially physical effort

      "worked their arduous way up the mining valley"

      同义词：backbreaking | grueling | gruelling | hard | heavy | laborious | operose | punishing | toilsome

    • taxing to the utmost; testing powers of endurance

      "your willingness after these six arduous days to remain here"

      同义词：straining | strenuous

    • difficult to accomplish; demanding considerable mental effort and skill

      "the arduous work of preparing a dictionary"

以上来源于: WordNet
