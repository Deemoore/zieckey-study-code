nettle ['netl]

  • n.
    • 【植物】荨麻属(Urtica)植物
    • 类似荨麻的植物
    • 令人恼火(或烦恼)的事物
  • vt.
    • 用荨麻刺；用荨麻鞭打：

      • to nettle the fugitive slaves

        用荨麻鞭打逃跑的奴隶

    • 使恼火，使烦恼，惹怒：

      • A joke nettled him.

        一句笑话，他就生气了。

  • 近义词: irritate
  • 短语:
    • grasp the nettle 迎着艰险上；大胆抓棘手问题
    • 变形：
    • nettled , nettling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

nettle [ 'netl ]

  • n. any of numerous plants having stinging hairs that cause skin irritation on contact (especially of the genus Urtica or family Urticaceae)
  • v.
    • sting with or as with nettles and cause a stinging pain or sensation

      同义词：urticate

    • cause annoyance in; disturb, especially by minor irritations

      同义词：annoy | rag | get to | bother | get at | irritate | rile | nark | gravel | vex | chafe | devil
