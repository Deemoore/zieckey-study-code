curb [kə:b]

  • n.
    • (街道或人行道的)路缘(俗称马路牙子)[英国英语亦作kerb]
    • (井边的)井栏
    • [美国英语](以前证券交易所的)场外证券市场[亦称作 curb market,英国英语亦作kerb market,kerbstone market,参较American Stock Exchange]
    • 马嚼子；马衔索
    • 【建筑工程】缘饰，边饰
    • 阻止物，抑制物；控制，限制
    • 【兽医学】(马后腿上可引起跛足的)飞节肿大
  • vt.
    • 抑止，拘束抑制；约束：

      • I could not curb my anger.

        我按捺不住我的愤怒。

    • 给(马)装马嚼子；勒住(马)
    • 给…加路缘；在…加井栏[英国英语亦作kerb]
  • 近义词: restrain
  • 短语:
    • put(或 place) a curb on 约束；控制，抑制
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

curb [ kə:b ]

  • n.
    • an edge between a sidewalk and a roadway consisting of a line of curbstones (usually forming part of a gutter)

      同义词：curbing | kerb

    • a horse's bit with an attached chain or strap to check the horse

      同义词：curb bit

    • a stock exchange in New York

      同义词：American Stock Exchange | AMEX

    • the act of restraining power or action or limiting excess

      同义词：bridle | check

  • v.
    • lessen the intensity of; temper; hold in restraint; hold or keep within limits

      同义词：control | hold in | hold | contain | check | moderate

    • to put down by force or authority

      同义词：suppress | stamp down | inhibit | subdue | conquer

    • keep to the curb

      "curb your dogs"

    • place restrictions on

      同义词：restrict | curtail | cut back
