mould1 [məuld]

  • n.
    • 模型，模子，铸模，铸型，模板
    • 【印刷】铸字盒，铸模，纸型
    • 模子铸成的形状
    • 动物的体形、形状
    • 框架；型芯
    • 模式
    • 模制品；铸成物；由模子制成的果冻(或布丁)
    • (人的)性格，气质，类型
    • (贝壳化石凸出面在泥土中留下的)压痕
    • 【建筑学】线脚
    • [废语]模范
  • vt.
    • 用模子制作；浇铸：

      • These huge presses mould the car bodies.

        这些巨大的压床模压出汽车车身。

      • a figure of a man moulded out of clay

        用模子压出的黏土人

    • 使形成，把…塑造成，塑造
    • 对…的形成施加影响：

      • His character has been moulded more by his experiences in life than by his education.

        在他性格的形成上，他所受的教育不如他的生活经历所起的影响大。

    • 与…的轮廓吻合，与…轮廓相符：

      • Her wet dress was moulded to her body.

        她的湿衣服紧贴在身上。

    • 用雕刻(或线脚)装饰
    • 【冶金学】铸…的模；用(砂)造型
  • 短语:
    • be cast in a different mould 具有完全不同的性格(或脾气)
    • be cast in mould 具有…性格
    • be cast in the same mould 仿佛一个模子倒出来的，性格完全相同

mould [ məuld ]

  • n.
    • loose soil rich in organic matter

      同义词：mold

    • a fungus that produces a superficial growth on various kinds of damp or decaying organic matter

      同义词：mold

    • sculpture produced by molding

      同义词：mold | molding | moulding | modeling | clay sculpture

    • container into which liquid is poured to create a given shape when it hardens

      同义词：mold | cast

  • v.
    • form in clay, wax, etc

      同义词：model | mold

    • form by pouring (e.g., wax or hot metal) into a cast or mold

      同义词：cast | mold

    • make something, usually for a specific function

      同义词：shape | form | work | mold | forge
