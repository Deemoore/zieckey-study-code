offset [,ɔf'set; ,ɔ:-; 'ɔfset; 'ɔ:-]

  • vt.
    • 抵消；补偿：

      • The gains offset the losses.

        收支相抵。

      • In basketball, he offsets his small size by his cleverness and speed.

        他打篮球时的灵巧和速度弥补了他身材的矮小。

    • (为了比较的目的而)把…并列(或并置)，衬托出：

      • to offset beauty against ugliness

        把美和丑放在一起(以突出美)

    • 为(管道等)装支管
    • 【印刷】
      • 把…制成胶印版
      • 用胶印法印刷
    • 【建筑业】在(墙内)筑壁阶
    • 【机械学】偏置
  • vi.
    • 形成分支；长出分枝
    • 【印刷】制胶印版
    • 【机械学】装支管，形成支管
  • n.
    • 【经济学】抵消；补偿
    • 开端；出发
    • 短匍匐茎，短匍匐枝
    • 分支，支脉；支族，旁系(亲属)
    • 【机械学】
      • (管道等的)支管，分管
      • 偏置，偏距，偏移
    • (直线、平面、物体等的)偏出部分
    • [亦称作 offset line]【测量学】偏置测线
    • 停止，休止
    • 衬托物
    • 【地质学】(断层的)水平错距；断错山脊
    • 【电学】支线，分支导线
    • 【建筑业】壁阶
    • 【印刷】胶印；胶印版印刷(品)
    • 【造船】型值
  • adj.
    • 抵消的；补偿的
    • 开端的
    • 分支的；分枝的
    • 断错的
    • 偏(离中)心的；偏置的
    • 【印刷】(适合于)胶印版印刷的
    • 变形：
    • offset , offsetting

offset [ ,ɔf'set, ,ɔ:-, 'ɔfset, 'ɔ:- ]

  • n.
    • the time at which something is supposed to begin

      同义词：beginning | commencement | first | outset | get-go | start | kickoff | starting time | showtime

    • a compensating equivalent

      同义词：counterbalance

    • a horizontal branch from the base of plant that produces new plants from buds at its tips

      同义词：stolon | runner

    • a natural consequence of development

      同义词：outgrowth | branch | offshoot

    • a plate makes an inked impression on a rubber-blanketed cylinder, which in turn transfers it to the paper

      同义词：offset printing

    • structure where a wall or building narrows abruptly

      同义词：set-back | setoff

  • v.
    • compensate for or counterbalance

      "offset deposits and withdrawals"

      同义词：countervail

    • make up for

      "His skills offset his opponent's superior strength"

      同义词：cancel | set off

    • cause (printed matter) to transfer or smear onto another surface
    • create an offset in

      "offset a wall"

    • produce by offset printing

      "offset the conference proceedings"
