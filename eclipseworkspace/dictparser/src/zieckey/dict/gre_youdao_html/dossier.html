  • n.
    • (有关某人或某事的)档案，卷宗(尤指包括详细情况的)材料汇编，全套文件
    • (作者创作)笔记汇编，笔记材料
    • 变形：
    • dossiers

dossier [ 'dɔsiei, dəu'sjei ]

  • n. a collection of papers containing detailed information about a particular person or subject (usually a person's record)
