cripple ['kripl]

  • n.
    • 跛子，瘸子；残废人；跛畜，残废牲畜
    • [美国方言]沼泽地
    • 残缺的东西；有缺陷的事物
  • vt.
    • 使跛，使成跛子：

      • He was crippled in the war.

        他在战争中成了跛子。

    • 使受伤致残；使受损；使丧失活动能力；削弱：

      • The factory was entirely crippled by the strike.

        罢工使这个工厂完全瘫痪了。

  • adj.
    • 跛的，拐的；伤残的
    • 【木工】=jack1
  • 近义词: maim
    • 变形：
    • crippled , crippling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cripple [ 'kripl ]

  • n. someone who is unable to walk normally because of an injury or disability to the legs or back
  • v.
    • deprive of strength or efficiency; make useless or worthless

      "This measure crippled our efforts"

      同义词：stultify

    • deprive of the use of a limb, especially a leg

      "The accident has crippled her for life"

      同义词：lame
