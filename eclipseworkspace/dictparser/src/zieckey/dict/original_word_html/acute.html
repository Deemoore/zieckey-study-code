acute [ə'kju:t]

  • adj.
    • 尖的，锐的，锐利的
    • 尖锐的，敏锐的
    • 敏感的，灵敏的
    • (疼痛、妒忌等)剧烈的，激烈的，强烈的，厉害的
    • 严重的；紧要关头的；危急的；极困难的
    • 短时间的
    • 尖声的，高音的，(音调)刺耳的
    • 【语言学】尖(重)音的，锐音的(如法语中e字母上加＇符号变成é)
    • 【医学】(疾病)急性的；治疗急性病的
    • 【植物学】急尖的
    • 【几何学】锐(角)的
  • n.
    • 【语言学】尖重音，尖音符
  • 近义词: critical , crucial
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

acute [ ə'kju:t ]

  • n. a mark (') placed above a vowel to indicate pronunciation

    同义词：acute accent | ague

  • adj.
    • having or experiencing a rapid onset and short but severe course

      "acute appendicitis"; " the acute phase of the illness"; " acute patients"

    • extremely sharp or intense

      "acute pain"; " felt acute annoyance"

      同义词：intense

    • having or demonstrating ability to recognize or draw fine distinctions

      "an acute observer of politics and politicians"

      同义词：discriminating | incisive | keen | knifelike | penetrating | penetrative | piercing | sharp

    • of an angle; less than 90 degrees
    • ending in a sharp point

      同义词：acuate | sharp | needlelike

    • of critical importance and consequence

      "an acute (or critical) lack of research funds"

以上来源于: WordNet
