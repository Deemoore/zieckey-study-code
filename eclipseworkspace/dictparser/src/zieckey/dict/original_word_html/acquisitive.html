acquisitive [ə'kwizitiv]

  • adj.
    • (尤指对物质财富、钱财等)渴望得到的，想获得的，孜孜以求的，迫切求取的；贪得的，贪婪的；
    • 能够获得并保存的
  • 近义词: greedy
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

acquisitive [ ə'kwizitiv ]

  • adj. eager to acquire and possess things especially material possessions or ideas

    "an acquisitive mind"; " an acquisitive society in which the craving for material things seems never satisfied"

以上来源于: WordNet
