ambidext(e)rous [,æmbi'dekstrəs]

  • adj.
    • 两手同利的，左右手都善用的，左右手同样灵活的
    • 非常灵巧的，非常熟练的，多才多艺的，多种才能的
    • (如散文和诗歌)两方面皆能的
    • 搞两面派的，两面讨好的，怀二心的；欺诈的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ambidextrous [ ,æmbi'dekstrəs ]

  • adj.
    • equally skillful with each hand

      "an ambidextrous surgeon"

      同义词：two-handed

    • marked by deliberate deceptiveness especially by pretending one set of feelings and acting under the influence of another

      同义词：deceitful | double-dealing | duplicitous | Janus-faced | two-faced | double-faced | double-tongued

以上来源于: WordNet
