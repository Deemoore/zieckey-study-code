annul [ə'nʌl]

  • vt.
    • 废除，废止，取消(法律、协议、合同、规定、用法等)：

      • to annul a contract

        废除契约

    • 使…无效，宣告…无效，宣告作废：

      • to annul a marriage

        宣告婚约无效

    • 抹去；使消失；冲掉：

      • to annul all unpleasant memories of his early childhood

        抹掉童年的一切不愉快的记忆

    • [美国英语]使(火车、航班、社交活动等)暂停一天(或一次)，取消(列车等的)运行
  • 近义词: abolish
    • 变形：
    • annulled , annuling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

annul [ ə'nʌl ]

  • v.
    • declare invalid

      "The contract was annulled"

      同义词：invalidate | quash | void | avoid | nullify

    • annul by recalling or rescinding

      同义词：revoke | lift | countermand | reverse | repeal | overturn | rescind | vacate
