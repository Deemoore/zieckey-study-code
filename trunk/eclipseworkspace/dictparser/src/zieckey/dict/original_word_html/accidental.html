accidental [,æksi'dentəl]

  • adj.
    • 意外的，偶然的，偶发的，无意中的，偶然碰到的，出乎意料的
    • 附属的，附带的；伴随的；非主要的，次要的
    • 随机的
    • 【音乐】(升、降、还原)临时记号的，临时符的
  • n.
    • 非主要特性，非本质属性，次要方面
    • 【音乐】
      • (升、降、还原)临时记号，临时符
      • 临时音调，由临时符表示的音调
    • [复数]【绘画】(以明暗构成的画面上的)突出光线；偶补光，次要光
  • 近义词: fortuitous , casual , incidental , adventitious
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

accidental [ ,æksi'dentəl ]

  • n. a musical notation that makes a note sharp or flat or natural although that is not part of the key signature
  • adj.
    • associated by chance and not an integral part

      "poetry is something to which words are the accidental, not by any means the essential form"

      同义词：adventitious

    • occurring or appearing or singled out by chance

      "their accidental meeting led to a renewal of their friendship"

      同义词：casual | chance(a)

    • without intention (especially resulting from heedless action)

      "accidental poisoning"; " an accidental shooting"

      同义词：inadvertent

以上来源于: WordNet
