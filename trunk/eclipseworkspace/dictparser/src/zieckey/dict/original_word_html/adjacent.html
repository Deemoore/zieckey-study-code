adjacent [ə'dʒeisənt]

  • adj.
    • 接近的，邻近的，毗邻的，毗连的(to)
    • 前后接连的，(前或后)连接着的
    • 【数学】邻接的，邻近的
  • n.
    • 【几何学】(三角形的)邻边
  • 近义词: adjoining , contiguous , tangent , neighbo
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

adjacent [ ə'dʒeisənt ]

  • adj.
    • nearest in space or position; immediately adjoining without intervening space

      "had adjacent rooms"

      同义词：next | side by side(p)

    • having a common boundary or edge; touching

      同义词：abutting | adjoining | conterminous | contiguous | neighboring(a)

    • near or close to but not necessarily touching

      "lands adjacent to the mountains"; " New York and adjacent cities"

以上来源于: WordNet
