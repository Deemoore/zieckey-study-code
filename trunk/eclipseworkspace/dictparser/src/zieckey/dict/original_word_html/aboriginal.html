aboriginal [,æbə'ridʒənəl]

  • adj.
    • (据文献记载)最早就存在的，最早就有的；当地一向就有的
    • 初步的，原始的；(尤指澳大利亚)土著的
    • 土著居民的，具有土著人特征的
    • [A-]澳洲土著居民的
  • n.
    • 土生动物(或植物)
    • 土著居民；[常作A-] 澳大利亚土著居民
    • (某国的)公民
  • 近义词: native
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

aboriginal [ ,æbə'ridʒənəl ]

  • n. a dark-skinned member of a race of people living in Australia when Europeans arrived

    同义词：Aborigine | Abo | native Australian | Australian Aborigine

  • adj.
    • of or pertaining to members of the indigenous people of Australia
    • characteristic of or relating to people inhabiting a region from the beginning

      "the aboriginal peoples of Australia"

      同义词：native

    • having existed from the beginning; in an earliest or original stage or state

      "aboriginal forests"

      同义词：primal | primeval | primaeval | primordial
