admonish [əd'mɔniʃ]

  • vt.
    • 警告；提醒：

      • to admonish him about his obligations

        提醒他应尽的义务

      • to admonish someone not to drive too fast

        警告某人不可把车开得太快

    • 告诫：

      • He was admonished against doing wrong.

        他被告诫不要干坏事。

    • (温和地)责备，轻责：

      • The teacher admonished the child for coming late to school.

        那个孩子迟到，教师批评了他。

      • The teacher admonished her about excessive noise.

        教师责怪她太闹。

    • 忠告，劝告，力劝，规劝；激励：

      • The teacher admonished the students against smoking.

        教师力劝学生不要抽烟。

  • 近义词: advise
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

admonish [ əd'mɔniʃ ]

  • v.
    • admonish or counsel in terms of someone's behavior

      同义词：warn | discourage | monish

    • warn strongly; put on guard

      同义词：caution | monish

    • take to task

      "He admonished the child for his bad behavior"

      同义词：reprove
