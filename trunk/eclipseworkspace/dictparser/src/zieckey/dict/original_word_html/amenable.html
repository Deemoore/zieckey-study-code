amenable [ə'mi:nəbl]

  • adj.
    • 对…负有责任(或义务)的，(应)负责的，有服从的义务的，应服从的
    • 易受控制(或影响)的；易做出响应的；肯接受劝告的，服从启导的；(愿)服从的，服理的
    • 经得起检验(或考验、考查)的 (to)
  • 近义词: obedient
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

amenable [ ə'mi:nəbl ]

  • adj.
    • disposed or willing to comply

      "someone amenable to persuasion"

      同义词：conformable

    • readily reacting to suggestions and influences

      同义词：responsive | tractable

    • open to being acted upon in a certain way

      "an amenable hospitalization should not result in untimely death"; " the tumor was not amenable to surgical treatment"

    • liable to answer to a higher authority

      "the president is amenable to the constitutional court"

以上来源于: WordNet
