accentuate [æk'sentjueit]

  • vt.
    • 重读，以重音读出：

      • to accentuate the first syllable of “into”

        重读“into”一词的第一个音节

    • 加重音符号于：

      • Accentuate the word “accent”on the first syllable.

        在“accent”一词的第一个音节上加重音符号。

    • 强调，加重，注重；着重指出；使更突出，使更明显：

      • Her dimpled cheeks accentuated her smile.

        她那带酒窝的双颊更显得笑容的妩媚。

    • 使恶化，使严重：

      • The problem is accentuated by a shortage of water and electricity.

        缺乏水电使问题愈加严重了。

    • 变形：
    • accentuated , accentuating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

accentuate [ æk'sentjueit ]

  • v.
    • to stress, single out as important

      同义词：stress | emphasize | emphasise | punctuate | accent

    • put stress on; utter with an accent

      同义词：stress | accent
