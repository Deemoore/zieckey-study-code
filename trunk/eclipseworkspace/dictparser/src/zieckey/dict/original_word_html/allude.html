allude [ə'lju:d]

  • vi.
    • 随时(或间接)提到；略为一提；暗指，暗讽，影射(to):

      • The story alluded to a mystery in her past.

        故事影射她过去生活中的一个秘密。

      • The letter alludes to something now forgotten.

        这封信略为提到某些现已被忘记了的事情。

    • (泛泛)提到，提及，说起，谈到(to)：

      • Her husband's death made her very sad; don't allude to it when you meet her.

        她丈夫之死使她很伤心；当你见到她时不要提起此事。

      • She often alluded to her poverty.

        她常常谈起她的贫穷。

    • 变形：
    • alluded , alluding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

allude [ ə'lju:d ]

  • v. make a more or less disguised reference to

    "He alluded to the problem but did not mention it"

    同义词：touch | advert
