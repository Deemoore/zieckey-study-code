pliant ['plaiənt]

  • adj.
    • 易弯的；柔韧的
    • 能适应的；适合多种用途的；灵活的
    • 柔顺的，顺从的；易受影响的
  • 近义词: pliable
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

pliant [ 'plaiənt ]

  • adj.
    • capable of being influenced or formed

      "a pliant nature"

      同义词：plastic

    • capable of being shaped or bent or drawn out

      "pliant molten glass"

      同义词：ductile | malleable | pliable | tensile | tractile

    • able to adjust readily to different conditions

      同义词：elastic | flexible | pliable

    • capable of being bent or flexed or twisted without breaking

      "a pliant young tree"

      同义词：bendable | pliable

以上来源于: WordNet
