      • 扰乱，滋扰，扰动
      • 混乱，骚乱，骚扰；动荡；动乱
      • 引起骚动的事，扰乱物，干扰物；烦恼事
    • 心神不定，心烦意乱；苦恼，烦恼；困惑；忧虑；焦急；恐慌
    • 【精神病学】困扰；错乱；精神病态
    • 【法律】(对别人财产、权利如采光、换气等权利的)侵犯；(对治安的)妨害，骚扰
    • (身体等)失调
    • 【地质学】干扰，地壳变动
    • 【气象学】扰动
    • 【无线电】干扰

disturbance [ di'stə:bəns ]

  • n.
    • activity that is an intrusion or interruption

      "he looked around for the source of the disturbance"; " there was a disturbance of neural function"

      同义词：perturbation

    • an unhappy and worried mental state

      "there was too much anger and disturbance"

      同义词：perturbation | upset

    • a disorderly outburst or tumult

      "they were amazed by the furious disturbance they had caused"

      同义词：disruption | commotion | flutter | hurly burly | to-do | hoo-ha | hoo-hah | kerfuffle

    • a noisy fight

      同义词：affray | fray | ruffle

    • the act of disturbing something or someone; setting something in motion
    • (psychiatry) a psychological disorder of thought or emotion; a more neutral term than mental illness

      同义词：mental disorder | mental disturbance | psychological disorder | folie

    • electrical or acoustic activity that can disturb communication

      同义词：noise | interference
