hypnotic [hip'nɔtik]

  • adj.
    • 催眠的；催眠术的
    • 易受催眠的
    • (易)引起睡眠的；(药物)安眠的
    • 处于受催眠状态中的
  • n.
    • 安眠药；催眠方法
    • (易)受催眠者

hypnotic [ hip'nɔtik ]

  • n. a drug that induces sleep

    同义词：soporific

  • adj.
    • of or relating to hypnosis
    • attracting and holding interest as if by a spell

      "read the bedtime story in a hypnotic voice"

      同义词：mesmeric | mesmerizing | spellbinding
