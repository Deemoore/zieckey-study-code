encompass [in'kʌmpəs]

  • vt.
    • 围绕，环绕；围住；包围：

      • They built a moat to encompass the castle.

        他们在城堡周围修了一条护城河。

    • 包封，封入，包裹：

      • The folds of a great cloak encompassed her person.

        大斗篷的褶层包裹着她的身体。

    • 包括，包含，包罗万象：

      • a work that encompasses the entire range of the world's religious beliefs

        一部包括世界一切宗教信仰的著作

    • 造成，促使，促成；完成，实现：

      • to encompass its destruction

        促成它的毁灭

    • [废语]用狡计击败；以机智胜过
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

encompass [ in'kʌmpəs ]

  • v. include in scope; include as part of something broader; have as one's sphere or territory

    "This group encompasses a wide range of people from different backgrounds"

    同义词：embrace | comprehend | cover

以上来源于: WordNet
