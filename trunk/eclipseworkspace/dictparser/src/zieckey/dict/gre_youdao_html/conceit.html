conceit [kən'si:t]

  • n.
    • 自高自大，骄傲自满，自负
    • 想法，意见，私见
    • 幻想；异想天开，狂想，怪念
    • 牵强附会的比喻
    • 不实用的花哨物品
  • vt.
    • [废语]幻想，想像
    • [古语]喜欢，对…有好感
  • 近义词: pride
  • 短语:
    • full of conceit 自高自大，十分自负
    • in one's own conceit [古语]自以为，在自己看来
    • out of conceit with [古语]不再喜欢(某人或某物)，厌弃(某人或某物)
    • put someone out of conceit with [古语]使某人不再满意(或喜欢)…
    • take the conceit out of 使…不再自负，挫…的骄气
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

conceit [ kən'si:t ]

  • n.
    • feelings of excessive pride

      同义词：amour propre | self-love | vanity

    • an elaborate poetic image or a far-fetched comparison of very dissimilar things
    • a witty or ingenious turn of phrase

      "he could always come up with some inspired off-the-wall conceit"

    • an artistic device or effect

      "the architect's brilliant conceit was to build the house around the tree"

    • the trait of being unduly vain and conceited

      同义词：conceitedness | vanity
