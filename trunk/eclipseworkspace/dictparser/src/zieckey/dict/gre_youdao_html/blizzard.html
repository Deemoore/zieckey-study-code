blizzard ['blizəd]

  • n.
      • 暴风雪，雪暴，大风雪
      • 暴风雨
      • 大风沙
      • (伴随有雪花和酷寒的)强烈风暴
      • 暴风雪似的一阵；猛攻，猛击；闪击
      • 射击，一阵射击
  • vi.
    • 下暴风雪

  • n.
    • a storm with widespread snowfall accompanied by strong winds

      同义词：snowstorm

    • a series of unexpected and unpleasant occurrences

      "a blizzard of lawsuits"
