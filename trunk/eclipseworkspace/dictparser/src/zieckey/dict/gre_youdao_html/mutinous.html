mutinous ['mju:tinəs]

  • adj.
    • 叛变的；反抗的；有意造反的；参与反叛的
    • 骚动的；哗变的
    • 难控制的；不守秩序的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

mutinous [ 'mju:tinəs ]

  • adj.
    • disposed to or in a state of mutiny

      "the men became mutinous and insubordinate"

    • consisting of or characterized by or inciting to mutiny

      "mutinous acts"; " mutinous thoughts"; " a mutinous speech"
