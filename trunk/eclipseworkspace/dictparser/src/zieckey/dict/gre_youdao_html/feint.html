feint [feint]

  • n.
    • 佯装，假装，假象，伪装
    • (如拳击、击剑、战争等中的)佯攻，虚击，声东击西
  • vi.,vt.
    • 佯攻，虚击，声东击西：

      • The boxer feinted with his right hand and struck with his left hand.

        这个拳击手用右手佯攻而出左手击打。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

feint [ feint ]

  • n. any distracting or deceptive maneuver (as a mock attack)
  • v. deceive by a mock action

    "The midfielder feinted to shoot"
