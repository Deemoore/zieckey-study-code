poise1 [pɔiz]

  • n.
    • 平衡；均衡；平稳
    • 沉着；泰然自若；自信；镇定
    • 安静；沉静；安详；平静，宁静
    • (身体或头部的)姿态，姿势
    • 徘徊；盘旋
    • 砝码；秤锤
    • [罕用语]悬而不决；犹豫
    • (动作或变化的)暂停，中止
    • [废语]沉重
    • [废语]重要性，严重性
  • vt.
    • 使平衡；使保持均衡：

      • The girl poised the glass on the edge of the table.

        小姑娘把杯子稳稳地放在桌子边上。

    • [poise oneself]使悬着[通常与被动语态或反身代词连用]：

      • Masses of ice are poised at one moment and the next come crashing down.

        大块大块的冰空悬了一会儿，然后哗啦掉了下来。

    • 平稳地拿，稳稳地举：

      • He walked along with a water jug poised on his head.

        他头顶着水罐稳稳地向前走去。

    • 使(头部等)摆出某种姿态：

      • to poise one's head forward

        使头向前倾

    • 权衡，考虑：

      • to poise a question

        权衡一个问题

    • 使做好准备，使振作：

      • to poise the troops for the battle

        使军队作好战斗准备

    • [罕用语]称…重量
    • [废语](以镇重物)使变稳定
  • vi.
    • 平衡；被拿稳：

      • The diver poised on the edge of the high board.

        跳水者在高台边上站稳。

    • 悬起，悬着：

      • A dancer poised in midair.

        一位舞蹈演员滞空平衡。

    • (鸟等)盘旋：

      • A bee is poising over the flowers.

        蜜蜂在花上飞来飞去。

    • 做好准备：

      • We knew that they were poising for the encounter.

        我们知道他们正在为这次冲突做准备。

  • 近义词: tact
    • 变形：
    • poised , poising

  • n. 【物理学】泊(动粘度单位)

poise [ pɔiz ]

  • n.
    • a cgs unit of dynamic viscosity equal to one dyne-second per square centimeter; the viscosity of a fluid in which a force of one dyne per square centimeter maintains a velocity of 1 centimeter per second
    • a state of being balanced in a stable equilibrium
    • great coolness and composure under strain

      同义词：aplomb | assuredness | cool | sang-froid

  • v.
    • be motionless, in suspension

      "The bird poised for a few moments before it attacked"

    • prepare (oneself) for something unpleasant or difficult

      同义词：brace

    • cause to be balanced or suspended
    • hold or carry in equilibrium

      同义词：balance

以上来源于: WordNet
