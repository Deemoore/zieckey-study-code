incorporate1 [in'kɔ:pəreit; in'kɔ:pərət]

  • vt.
    • 包含；吸收；加入：

      • to be incorporated a member of the union

        被吸收为工会会员

      • New ideas will be incorporated into the book.

        新思想将被收入书中。

    • 合并；使并入：

      • The computer company will be incorporated with another.

        该计算机公司将于另一家合并。

    • 使组成(有限)公司；使成为社团
    • 使混合：

      • a chemical substance incorporated with another

        与另一种化学物质混合的化学物质

    • 使具体化；体现：

      • His book incorporates his military thinking.

        他的书体现了他的军事思想。

    • [英国英语]承认(学生等)的同等学位
  • vi.
    • 包含；吸收
    • 合并；混合：

      • The factory incorporates with others.

        这家工厂与别家合并了。

    • 组成公司；结成社团
  • adj.
    • = incorporated
    • [古语]具体化的
    • 变形：
    • incorporated , incorporating

incorporate2 [in'kɔ:pərət]

  • adj. = incorporeal
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incorporate [ in'kɔ:pəreit, in'kɔ:pərət ]

  • v.
    • make into a whole or make part of a whole

      "She incorporated his suggestions into her proposal"

      同义词：integrate

    • include or contain; have as a component

      同义词：contain | comprise

    • form a corporation
    • unite or merge with something already in existence

      "incorporate this document with those pertaining to the same case"

  • adj. formed or united into a whole

    同义词：incorporated | integrated | merged | unified

以上来源于: WordNet
