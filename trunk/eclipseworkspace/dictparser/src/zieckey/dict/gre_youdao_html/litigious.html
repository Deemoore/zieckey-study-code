litigious [ li'tidʒəs ]

  • adj.
    • of or relating to litigation
    • inclined or showing an inclination to dispute or disagree, even to engage in law suits

      "a litigious and acrimonious spirit"

      同义词：contentious | combative | disputatious | disputative
