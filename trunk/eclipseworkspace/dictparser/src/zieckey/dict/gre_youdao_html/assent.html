assent [ə'sent]

  • vi.
    • 同意，对(意见、计划等)表示同意，赞同，赞成(to):

      • The king has assented to the new law.

        国王已同意新法律。

    • [古语]妥协；让步；让与：

      • Assenting to this demands.

        对这个要求让步。

  • n.
    • (对建议等表示的)同意，赞成，赞同
    • 答应，允许，准许，认可；批准
    • 顺从，依从，屈从(to)
  • 近义词: consent
  • 短语:
    • by common assent 一致默许；一致同意
    • with one assent 一致(同意或通过)；无异议地
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

assent [ ə'sent ]

  • n. agreement with a statement or proposal to do something

    "he gave his assent eagerly"

    同义词：acquiescence

  • v. to agree or express agreement

    "The Maestro assented to the request for an encore"

    同义词：accede | acquiesce
