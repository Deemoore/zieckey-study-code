lance [lɑ:ns; læns]

  • n.
    • 长矛
    • 矛状器具；捕鲸矛；捕鲸枪
    • ＝lancer
    • 【冶金学】喷枪
    • 【医学】(外科用)柳叶刀；双刃小刀
    • [复数] 枪骑兵；长矛轻骑兵
    • 【军事】撞杆(旧时装填大炮的弹药杆)
  • vt.
    • 用柳叶刀切开：

      • to lance an abscess on the gum

        切开牙龈上的脓肿

    • 用矛刺穿
    • 投掷
  • vi.
    • 急速前进
    • 【历史】持枪矛与某人进行马上比武(中世纪武士的比武方式)
  • 短语:
    • break a lance with someone for something 为某事与某人交锋；和某人比赛
    • carry a lance for someone 为某人进行辩护
    • 变形：
    • lanced , lancing

Lance [læns]

    • 兰斯(男子名)
    • 【军械】“长矛”导弹(地对地导弹)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

lance [ lɑ:ns, læns ]

  • n.
    • a long pointed rod used as a weapon

      同义词：spear | shaft

    • an implement with a shaft and barbed point used for catching fish

      同义词：spear | gig | fizgig | fishgig

    • a surgical knife with a pointed double-edged blade; used for punctures and small incisions

      同义词：lancet

  • v.
    • move quickly, as if by cutting one's way

      "Planes lanced towards the shore"

    • pierce with a lance, as in a knights' fight
    • open by piercing with a lancet

      "lance a boil"
