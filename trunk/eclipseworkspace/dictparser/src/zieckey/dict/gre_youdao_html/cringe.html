cringe [krindʒ]

  • vi.
    • 畏缩；蜷缩：

      • The dog cringed at the sight of a snake.

        那条狗看见了一条蛇便畏缩不前。

    • 奉承；谄媚；卑躬屈膝：

      • They cringed before the queen.

        他们在女王面前卑躬屈膝。

    • 变形：
    • cringed , cringing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cringe [ krindʒ ]

  • v.
    • draw back, as with fear or pain

      同义词：flinch | squinch | funk | shrink | wince | recoil | quail

    • show submission or fear

      同义词：fawn | crawl | creep | cower | grovel
