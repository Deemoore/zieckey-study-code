authorize ['ɔ:θəraiz]

  • vt.
    • (官方)批准；认可，许可，允许，准许，核定，使合法化：

      • The city authorized a housing project.

        该市批准了一项住房建筑规划。

      • Congress authorized the new tax on tobacco.

        国会批准对烟草征收新税。

    • 授权；委任；委托：

      • to authorize an employee to sign purchase orders

        授权雇员签订货单

      • He confidentially authorized me to act for him while he is abroad.

        他信任地委托我在他出国期间代行他的事务。

    • 使有(正当)理由，证明…是正当的；为…提供根据，成为…的根据；辩护
    • 由权威的典籍 (或用法)确定：

      • an arrangement long authorized by etiquette books

        由权威的礼仪书籍长久以来确定下来的一种办法

  • 近义词: commission , accredit , license
    • 变形：
    • authorized , authorizing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

authorize [ 'ɔ:θəraiz ]

  • v.
    • grant authorization or clearance for

      "The rock star never authorized this slanderous biography"

      同义词：authorise | pass | clear

    • give or delegate power or authority to

      "She authorized her assistant to sign the papers"

      同义词：empower | authorise
