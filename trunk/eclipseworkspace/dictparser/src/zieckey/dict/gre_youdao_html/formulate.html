formulate ['fɔ:mjuleit]

  • vt.
    • 使公式化；用公式表示；把…化成公式
    • 系统(或确切)地阐述(或提出)(理论、计划等)：

      • to formulate a new theory

        系统地阐述一种新理论

    • 构想出(计划、方法等)；规划(制度等)
    • 制定…的配方；按配方制造(或配制)
  • 短语:
    • formulate and advance 出台；亮相
    • 变形：
    • formulated , formulating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

formulate [ 'fɔ:mjuleit ]

  • v.
    • elaborate, as of theories and hypotheses

      同义词：explicate | develop

    • come up with (an idea, plan, explanation, theory, or principle) after a mental effort

      同义词：invent | contrive | devise | excogitate | forge

    • put into words or an expression

      "He formulated his concerns to the board of trustees"

      同义词：give voice | word | phrase | articulate

    • prepare according to a formula

以上来源于: WordNet
