honk [hɔŋk]

  • n.
    • 鹅叫声
    • 鹅叫似的声音；鼻鼾声
    • 汽车喇叭声
  • vi.
    • (鹅)叫：

      • The goose honked flying into the sky.

        那只大雁鸣叫着飞入了高空。

    • 发出鹅叫似的声音；(汽车喇叭)鸣响，(汽车或司机)鸣喇叭：

      • He honked to hurry me up down the stairs.

        他鸣汽车喇叭催我赶紧下楼。

    • [英国俚语]呕吐
    • [美国俚语](意识到)性的冲动
  • vt.
    • 鸣(汽车喇叭)：

      • No need to honk the horn under such a traffic block.

        在这种塞车的情况下，你再按喇叭也无济于事。

    • 鸣汽车喇叭让…避开(常与off连用)：

      • to honk the crowd off

        按汽车喇叭让人群让开

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

honk [ hɔŋk ]

  • n. the cry of a goose (or any sound resembling this)
  • v.
    • make a loud noise

      同义词：blare | beep | claxon | toot

    • use the horn of a car

      同义词：claxon

    • cry like a goose

      "The geese were honking"

      同义词：cronk

    • eject the contents of the stomach through the mouth

      同义词：vomit | vomit up | purge | cast | sick | cat | be sick | disgorge | regorge | retch | puke | barf | spew | spue | chuck | upchuck | regurgitate | throw up
