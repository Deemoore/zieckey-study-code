hasten ['heisən]

  • vt.
    • 催促；使赶快，使赶紧:

      • Father hastened our going.

        父亲催促我们快去。

      • to hasten germination

        催芽

    • 加速，加紧:

      • They hastened the development of a new attacker.

        他们加紧了新型攻击机的研制。

  • vi.
    • 赶快，赶紧；急忙:

      • He hastened home after reading the information in the beeper.

        看完传呼机上的信息后，他急忙回家了。

      • I hasten to tell you that your car has been found.

        我急着要告诉你的是你的汽车找到啦。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

hasten [ 'heisən ]

  • v.
    • act or move at high speed

      同义词：rush | hurry | look sharp | festinate

    • step on it

      同义词：rush | hotfoot | hie | speed | race | pelt along | rush along | cannonball along | bucket along | belt along

    • speed up the progress of; facilitate

      同义词：expedite

    • cause to occur rapidly

      同义词：induce | stimulate | rush
