phrase [freiz]

  • n.
    • 措词；用语；说法；话语
    • 简短的警句；简洁的语句
    • (舞蹈)连续动作的一小段；舞句(式)
    • 【语法学】短语；片语；习语；词组
    • 【音乐】短句；乐节
    • [复数]空话；废话
  • vt.,vi.
    • 用话表示；用短语描述；用语言表达：

      • He phrased his criticisms in careful terms.

        他的批评用词十分谨慎。

    • 【音乐】把…分成短句：

      • She will phrase a fugue of Bach's in these days.

        这几天她将把巴赫的赋格曲分成旋律分句。

  • 短语:
    • a turn of phrase 表述方式；措辞
    • coin a phrase
      • 编造(或杜撰)一个词语
      • [戏谑语]套用一种说法
    • sans phrase 直截了当地，干脆
    • set phrase
      • 固定词组
      • 客套话
    • turn a phrase 善于辞令
    • 变形：
    • phrased , phrasing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

phrase [ freiz ]

  • n.
    • an expression consisting of one or more words forming a grammatical constituent of a sentence
    • a short musical passage

      同义词：musical phrase

    • an expression whose meanings cannot be inferred from the meanings of the words that make it up

      同义词：idiom | idiomatic expression | phrasal idiom | set phrase

    • dance movements that are linked in a single choreographic sequence
  • v.
    • put into words or an expression

      同义词：give voice | formulate | word | articulate

    • divide, combine, or mark into phrases

      "phrase a musical passage"

以上来源于: WordNet
