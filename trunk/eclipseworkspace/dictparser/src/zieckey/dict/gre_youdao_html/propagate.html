propagate ['prɔpəɡeit]

  • vt.
    • 繁殖；增殖：

      • The weeds propagate themselves rapidly.

        杂草繁殖极快。

    • 繁衍，使繁殖：

      • Human beings propagate their species by sexual reproduction,

        人类是通过有性生殖来繁衍同类的。

    • 遗传(特征等)：

      • Scientists were hoping to propagate the best qualities of both types of sheep.

        科学家希望使这两种绵羊的最佳特性遗传给下一代。

    • 传播；宣传；传送：

      • to propagate and carry out the policies

        宣传和落实这些政策

    • 【物理学】(声波、电磁辐射等)传播，传导：

      • to propagate radio waves over a great distance

        远距离传送无线电波

  • vi.
    • (动物、植物)繁殖；增殖：

      • Most plants propagate by seed.

        大多数植物靠种子繁殖。

    • 蔓延；扩展：

      • to cause the flame to propagate along the dry sticks

        使火焰沿着枯枝蔓延

    • 变形：
    • propagated , propagating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

propagate [ 'prɔpəɡeit ]

  • v.
    • transmit from one generation to the next

      "propagate these characteristics"

    • travel through the air

      "sound and light propagate in this medium"

    • transmit

      "propagate sound or light through air"

    • become distributed or widespread

      同义词：spread

    • transmit or cause to broaden or spread

      "This great civilization was propagated throughout the land"

    • cause to become widely known

      同义词：circulate | circularize | circularise | distribute | disseminate | broadcast | spread | diffuse | disperse | pass around

    • cause to propagate, as by grafting or layering
    • multiply sexually or asexually
