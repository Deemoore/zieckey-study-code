    • 【军事】 (兵营或工事的) 环形防线；防御带

perimeter [ pə'rimitə ]

  • n.
    • the boundary line or the area immediately inside the boundary

      同义词：margin | border

    • a line enclosing a plane areas
    • the size of something as given by the distance around it

      同义词：circumference
