imaginary [i'mædʒinəri]

  • adj.
    • 想像中的；非真实的；幻想的；假想的；虚构的
    • 【数学】虚数的
    • [废语]想像的
  • n.
    • = imaginary number
    • 变形：
    • imaginaries
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

imaginary [ i'mædʒinəri ]

  • n. (mathematics) a number of the form a+bi where a and b are real numbers and i is the square root of -1

    同义词：complex number | complex quantity | imaginary number

  • adj. not based on fact; dubious

    "a small child's imaginary friends"

    同义词：fanciful | imagined | notional

以上来源于: WordNet
