crease1 [kri:s]

  • n.
    • 折痕；皱褶
    • (尤指脸上的)皱纹
    • (男裤的)折缝
    • (冰球、曲棍球的)球门区
    • 【板球】
      • =bowling crease
      • =popping crease
    • [美国俚语](女性的)性器官，阴门，阴道
  • vt.
    • 使起折痕；使有折缝；弄皱：

      • A frown creased his forehead.

        眉头一皱使他的前额出现很多皱纹。

    • (枪弹)擦伤：

      • The bullet merely creased his shoulder.

        子弹仅擦破他肩部的皮肤。

  • vi.
    • 变皱；起折痕：

      • Artificial silk creases more easily than natural silk.

        人造丝比天然丝更容易起皱。

    • 变形：
    • creased , creasing

crease3 [kri:s]

  • vt. [英国俚语]使其大笑(常与with连用)：

    • His joke really creased me (with laughter).

      他开的玩笑真使我捧腹大笑。

    • 变形：
    • creased , creasing

crease [ kri:s ]

  • n.
    • an angular or rounded shape made by folding

      "a crease in his trousers"

      同义词：fold | plication | flexure | crimp | bend

    • a slight depression in the smoothness of a surface

      同义词：wrinkle | furrow | crinkle | seam | line

    • a Malayan dagger with a wavy blade

      同义词：kris | creese

  • v.
    • make wrinkles or creases into a smooth surface

      同义词：wrinkle | ruckle | crinkle | scrunch | scrunch up | crisp

    • make wrinkled or creased

      同义词：furrow | wrinkle

    • scrape gently

      同义词：graze | rake

    • become wrinkled or crumpled or creased

      同义词：rumple | crumple | wrinkle | crinkle
