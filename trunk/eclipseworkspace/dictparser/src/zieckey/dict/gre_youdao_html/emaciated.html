emaciated [i'meisieitid]

  • vt.
    • emaciate的变形
  • adj.
    • 憔悴的，消瘦的，瘦弱的

emaciate [i'meisieit]

  • vt.
    • 使憔悴，使瘦弱，使消瘦；使衰弱:

      • A long illness had emaciated the invalid.

        久病使这病人消瘦了。

  • vi.
    • 变消瘦，消瘦下去，衰弱下去
    • 变形：
    • emaciated , emaciating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

emaciated [ i'meisieitid ]

  • adj. very thin especially from disease or hunger or cold

    "emaciated bony hands"

    同义词：bony | cadaverous | gaunt | haggard | pinched | skeletal | wasted
