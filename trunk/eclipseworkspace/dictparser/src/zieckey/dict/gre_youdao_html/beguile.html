beguile [bi'ɡail]

  • vt.
    • 欺骗，诱骗：

      • to beguile someone into doing something

        诱骗某人做某事

      • She beguiled me into lending her money.

        她诱骗我把钱借给她。

      • The girl was beguiled into thinking she could become a famous singer.

        这女孩被诱骗，误以为她能成为一名著名歌唱家。

    • 向…骗取，向…诈取，诈骗，欺诈，骗取(某人)的(of或out of)：

      • to be beguiled of money

        被骗了钱

      • She beguiled him out of his money.

        她骗取了他的钱。

    • 使(时间等)过得愉快，使愉快地度过(时间)；轻松地消磨(时间)：

      • I beguiled the long afternoon with a good book.

        我读一本好书把长长的下午消磨了过去。

    • 迷住，使着迷，使陶醉；使高兴，使欣喜；给…消遣(或娱乐)：

      • Beijing has a multitude of attractions to beguile the foreign tourist.

        北京有许多能迷住外国旅游者的景物。

      • Beguiled by the warm day,the children fell to daydreaming.

        为暖和的日子所陶醉，孩子们做白日梦。

  • 近义词: amuse , deceive , lure
    • 变形：
    • beguiled , beguiling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

beguile [ bi'ɡail ]

  • v.
    • influence by slyness

      同义词：juggle | hoodwink

    • attract; cause to be enamored

      同义词：capture | enamour | trance | catch | becharm | enamor | captivate | charm | fascinate | bewitch | entrance | enchant
