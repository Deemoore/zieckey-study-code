laminate ['læmineit; -nit]

  • vt.
    • 把(金属或塑料)碾压成薄片，制成薄片
    • 把…切成薄片
    • 层合；用薄片叠成
    • 用薄片覆盖；包以薄膜
    • 【材料学】使成叠层(状)
  • vi.
    • 分成薄片 —adj＝laminated [亦作laminous]
  • n.
    • 薄片制品；层压制件
    • 【材料学】叠层组合材,层积材[亦作laminated composite, laminated material]
    • 变形：
    • laminated , laminating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

laminate [ 'læmineit, -nit ]

  • n. a sheet of material made by bonding two or more sheets or layers
  • v.
    • create laminate by putting together several thin sheets of a material
    • press or beat (metals) into thin sheets
    • cover with a thin sheet

      "laminate the table"

    • split (wood) into thin sheets
