hector ['hektə]

  • n.
    • 威吓者，虚张声势的人；恃强凌弱的人
  • vt.
    • 威吓，欺凌；戏弄:

      • Why do you allow yourself to be hectored by him?

        你怎么能任他欺负你呢?

      • Don't hector my old firend.

        别戏弄我的老朋友。

  • vi.
    • 威胁恐吓，恃强凌弱；戏弄他人:

      • Pay no attention to him, he's hectoring upon you.

        别理他，他在戏弄你呢。

  • n. (Greek mythology) a mythical Trojan who was killed by Achilles during the Trojan War
  • v. be bossy towards
