cornucopia [,kɔ:nju'kəupjə]

  • n.
    • 【希腊神话】哺乳宙斯的羊角
    • (象征丰收的)丰饶角，丰收角
    • 丰富，丰饶
    • 圆锥形容器
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cornucopia [ ,kɔ:nju'kəupjə ]

  • n.
    • a goat's horn filled with grain and flowers and fruit symbolizing prosperity

      同义词：horn of plenty

    • the property of being extremely abundant

      同义词：profusion | profuseness | richness
