lease1 [li:s]

  • n.
    • 租契，租约
    • 租借的财产；租借物，租赁物
    • 租期，租借期限
    • 租赁权，租借权
  • vt.
    • 出租，租让：

      • He leased his shop to a friend.

        他把商店租给了一个朋友。

    • 租得；租有：

      • We leased an apartment from the school.

        我们从学校租到了一套公寓住房。

  • vi.
    • 出租：

      • The land leases at a monthly rental of $500.

        这块地以每月500美元出租。

  • 近义词: hire
  • 短语:
    • a lease of life 寿命
    • by (或 on) lease 以租借方式
    • new lease of(或[美国英语]on) life
      • (病愈或烦恼消除后的)重生；精神焕发；富于希望的新生；更加愉快的生活
      • (事物的)重新活跃；(由于情况改善或东西修补之后而)完整如新
    • hold on (或 by) lease 租借
    • put out to lease 出租
    • take (或 get) a new (或 fresh) lease of life
      • 在经历危险或困境后重获成功的机缘
      • 修理一新(尚能重新使用)
    • take lease on 租用
    • 变形：
    • leased , leasing

lease [ li:s ]

  • n.
    • property that is leased or rented out or let

      同义词：rental | letting

    • a contract granting use or occupation of property during a specified time for a specified payment
    • the period of time during which a contract conveying property to a person is in effect

      同义词：term of a contract

  • v.
    • let for money

      同义词：rent

    • hold under a lease or rental agreement; of goods and services

      同义词：rent | hire | charter

    • grant use or occupation of under a term of contract

      同义词：let | rent

    • engage for service under a term of contract

      同义词：rent | hire | charter | engage | take
