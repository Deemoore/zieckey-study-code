forge1 [fɔ:dʒ]

  • n.
    • 锻铁炉，熔炉
    • 铁匠工场，铁匠铺
    • 锻造车间
  • vt.
    • 锻造(金属)，打(铁)，使成形；锻造，铸造：

      • The smith forged the horseshoe with great skill.

        那铁匠非常熟练地锻造马蹄铁。

    • 做出，制订；生产：

      • to forge a scheme

        设计计谋

    • 仿造，伪造(尤指支票等)：

      • to forge a check

        伪造支票

    • 捏造，编造(谎言、故事等)
  • vi.
    • 锻造，当锻工，做铁匠
    • 犯伪造罪；伪造
    • (马狂奔、行走时)前蹄铁与后蹄铁相撞击
    • 变形：
    • forged , forging

forge2 [fɔ:dʒ]

  • vt., vi.
    • 稳步前进，(似乎迎着困难地)徐缓推进；(船只)顺势(或顺流)前进(常与ahead连用)：

      • The ship forged ahead under a favourable wind.

        那只船乘风前进。

    • 突然加速前进；渐渐追过，领先(常与ahead连用)：

      • At last the horse forged ahead of the rest and won the race.

        最后这匹马超过了其他马，赢得了比赛。

    • 变形：
    • forged , forging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

forge [ fɔ:dʒ ]

  • n.
    • furnace consisting of a special hearth where metal is heated before shaping
    • a workplace where metal is worked by heating and hammering

      同义词：smithy

  • v.
    • create by hammering

      "forge a pair of tongues"

      同义词：hammer

    • make a copy of with the intent to deceive

      "She forged a Green Card"

      同义词：fake | counterfeit

    • come up with (an idea, plan, explanation, theory, or principle) after a mental effort

      同义词：invent | contrive | devise | excogitate | formulate

    • move ahead steadily

      "He forged ahead"

    • move or act with a sudden increase in speed or energy

      同义词：spurt | spirt

    • make something, usually for a specific function

      同义词：shape | form | work | mold | mould

    • make out of components (often in an improvising manner)

      同义词：fashion

以上来源于: WordNet
