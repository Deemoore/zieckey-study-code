exaggerate [iɡ'zædʒəreit]

  • vt.
    • 夸大，夸张，把…讲得过分，对…言过其实：

      • to exaggerate the difficulties of a situation

        夸大处境的困难

    • 使(失常地)增加，使增大，使扩大；使过大；使逾常；过分强调；使更显眼：

      • Those shoes exaggerate the size of his feet.

        那双鞋使他的双脚显得过大。

  • vi.
    • 夸大，夸张，夸大其词，(如在文章或演说中)使用夸张手法：

      • a person who is always exaggerating

        一个老是言过其实的人

    • 变形：
    • exaggerated , exaggerating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

exaggerate [ iɡ'zædʒəreit ]

  • v.
    • to enlarge beyond bounds or the truth

      "tended to romanticize and exaggerate this `gracious Old South' imagery"

      同义词：overstate | overdraw | hyperbolize | hyerbolise | magnify | amplify

    • do something to an excessive degree

      同义词：overdo
