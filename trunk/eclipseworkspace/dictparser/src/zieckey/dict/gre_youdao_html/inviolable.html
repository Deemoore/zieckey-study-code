inviolable [in'vaiələbl]

  • adj. 不可侵犯的；神圣的，不可亵渎的；不可违背的；无法破坏的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

inviolable [ in'vaiələbl ]

  • adj.
    • incapable of being transgressed or dishonored

      "the person of the king is inviolable"; " an inviolable oath"

    • able to withstand attack

      "fortifications that made the frontier inviolable"

      同义词：impregnable | secure | strong | unassailable | unattackable

    • must be kept sacred

      同义词：inviolate | sacrosanct

    • not capable of being violated or infringed

      同义词：absolute | infrangible

以上来源于: WordNet
