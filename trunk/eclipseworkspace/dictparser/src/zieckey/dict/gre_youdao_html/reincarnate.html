reincarnate [ri:'inkɑ:neit; ,ri:in'k-]

  • vt.
    • 赋予(灵魂)新的肉体；使转生[常用被动语态]：

      • He believed he might be reincarnated as a woman.

        他相信他死后可能转世变为妇人。

  • adj.
    • 转生的
    • 变形：
    • reincarnated , reincarnating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

reincarnate [ ri:'inkɑ:neit, ,ri:in'k- ]

  • v.
    • be born anew in another body after death

      同义词：transmigrate

    • cause to appear in a new form

      "the old product was reincarnated to appeal to a younger market"

      同义词：renew

  • adj. having a new body
