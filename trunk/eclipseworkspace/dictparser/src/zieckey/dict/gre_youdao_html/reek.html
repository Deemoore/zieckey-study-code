reek [ri:k]

  • n.
    • 汽；水蒸气；雾
    • 臭气，臭味
    • [苏格兰英语]烟
  • vi.
    • 散发臭气；具有强烈的气味(或气息等)(常与of，with连用)：

      • His breath reeked of garlic.

        他满口蒜味。

    • 充满(某种极不愉快的东西)(常与of，with连用):

      • The conference room reeked of tobacco smoke.

        会议室里弥漫着烟草的臭味。

      • He was a murderer whose hands reeked with blood.

        他是一个双手沾满鲜血的杀人犯。

    • 冒水汽(或雾气等)；冒烟
  • vt.
    • (用烟等)熏
    • 散发(水汽、烟等)；发出…气味；显示出：

      • His manner reeks high position with high pay.

        他的气派表现得好像是个高官厚禄的人物。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

reek [ ri:k ]

  • n. a distinctive odor that is offensively unpleasant

    同义词：malodor | malodour | stench | stink | fetor | foetor | mephitis

  • v.
    • have an element suggestive (of something)

      同义词：smack

    • smell badly and offensively

      "The building reeks of smoke"

      同义词：stink

    • be wet with sweat or blood, as of one's face

      同义词：fume

    • give off smoke, fumes, warm vapour, steam, etc.

      "Marshes reeking in the sun"
