abuse [ə'bju:z; ə'bju:s]

  • vt.
    • 滥用；妄用；误用：

      • to abuse a privilege

        滥用特权

      • to abuse one's authority

        滥用权力

    • [常用被动语态]虐待，酷待，伤害，凌辱：

      • the abused children

        被虐待儿童

      • to abuse one's eyesight

        损害视力

      • to abuse a horse

        虐待一匹马

    • 辱骂，口出恶言，恶语伤人；毁谤：

      • to abuse each other

        相互辱骂

    • [abuse oneself]对…进行手淫
    • [古语]强奸；猥亵
    • [废语]欺骗，诓骗；把…引入歧途
  • n.
    • 滥用，妄用；误用
    • (对麻醉毒品、兴奋剂或刺激物等的)非法服用，过度服用
    • 虐待，酷待；凌辱；伤害
    • 辱骂，恶言伤人；毁谤
    • 恶习，陋习，弊端，弊病
    • [古语]强奸；猥亵
    • [废语]欺骗，诓骗
  • 近义词: wrong
    • 变形：
    • abused , abusing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

abuse [ ə'bju:z, ə'bju:s ]

  • n.
    • cruel or inhumane treatment

      同义词：maltreatment | ill-treatment | ill-usage

    • a rude expression intended to offend or hurt

      "when a student made a stupid mistake he spared them no abuse"

      同义词：insult | revilement | contumely | vilification

    • improper or excessive use

      同义词：misuse

  • v.
    • treat badly

      "This boss abuses his workers"

      同义词：mistreat | maltreat | ill-use | step | ill-treat

    • change the inherent purpose or function of something

      "Don't abuse the system"

      同义词：pervert | misuse

    • use foul or abusive language towards

      "The actress abused the policeman who gave her a parking ticket"

      同义词：clapperclaw | blackguard | shout

    • use wrongly or improperly or excessively

      "Her husband often abuses alcohol"; " while she was pregnant, she abused drugs"
