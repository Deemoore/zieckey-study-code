homely ['həumli]

  • adj.
    • 像家一样的，舒适的，友好的，亲切的
    • 适合家庭(生活)的；家常的，简单的；平常的，普通的
    • 无装饰的，素色的
    • 朴实无华的，不做作的
    • 原始的，无饰的
    • 类似的，相像的
    • [美国英语]相貌平常的，不漂亮的
    • [古语]熟悉的，亲切友好的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

homely [ 'həumli ]

  • adj.
    • lacking in physical beauty or proportion

      "a homely child"; " several of the buildings were downright homely"

      同义词：plain

    • having a feeling of home; cozy and comfortable

      "the homely everyday atmosphere"

      同义词：homelike | homey | homy

    • plain and unpretentious

      "homely truths"; " letters to his son full of homely advice"; " homely fare"

    • without artificial refinement or elegance

      "plain homely furniture"; " homely manners"

以上来源于: WordNet
