loom1 [lu:m]

  • n.
    • (手工或动力操作的)织机；织布机
    • [the loom]织造(术)
    • 桨柄，橹柄
    • 【电学】绝缘线束
    • 【航空学】翼肋腹部
  • vt.
    • 用织机织

loom2 [lu:m]

  • vi.
    • 隐现，隐约出现，隐约可见：

      • A flagpole loomed up through the mist.

        在薄雾中隐隐可见一根旗杆。

    • (忧虑、危险、战争等)阴森森地逼近；赫然耸现：

      • A life-and-death struggle is looming ahead.

        一场你死我活的斗争迫在眉睫。

  • n.
    • 朦胧呈现的形象；隐约出现的巨大的影子
    • [亦作 looming] 【气象学】上现蜃景
  • 短语:
    • loom large 赫然耸现；显得突出(或严重)

    • = guillemot

loom [ lu:m ]

  • n. a textile machine for weaving yarn into a textile
  • v.
    • come into view indistinctly, often threateningly

      "Another air plane loomed into the sky"

    • appear very large or occupy a commanding position

      "Large shadows loomed on the canyon wall"

      同义词：tower | predominate | hulk

    • hang over, as of something threatening, dark, or menacing

      同义词：brood | hover | bulk large
