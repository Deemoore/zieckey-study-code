  • n.
    • 愚蠢；笨拙；无能；不称职
    • 不适当；不合适
    • 愚蠢的言行；不适当的举止(或谈吐)

ineptitude [ i'neptitju:d ]

  • n.
    • unskillfulness resulting from a lack of training

      同义词：awkwardness | clumsiness | ineptness | maladroitness | slowness

    • having no qualities that would render it valuable or useful

      同义词：worthlessness
