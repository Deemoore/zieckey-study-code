conceited [ kən'si:tid ]

  • adj. characteristic of false pride; having an exaggerated sense of self-importance

    "a conceited fool"; " an attitude of self-conceited arrogance"

    同义词：egotistic | egotistical | self-conceited | swollen | swollen-headed | vain
