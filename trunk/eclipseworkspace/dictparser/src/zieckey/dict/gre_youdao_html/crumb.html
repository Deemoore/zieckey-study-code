crumb [krʌm]

  • n.
    • 面包屑；糕饼屑
    • 少量，少许，点滴
    • [复数](糕点的)装饰配料面包心
    • [俚语]讨厌的人；无价值的人
    • ＝crumb rubber
  • vt.
    • 在…上裹上面包屑
    • 捏碎，捣碎
    • 除去(面包屑)：

      • The waiter crumbed the table.

        服务员清除桌上的面包屑。

  • 短语:
    • to a crumb 精细地；精确地
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

crumb [ krʌm ]

  • n.
    • a very small quantity of something

      "he gave only a crumb of information about his plans"; " there were few crumbs of comfort in the report"

    • a person who is deemed to be despicable or contemptible

      同义词：rotter | dirty dog | rat | skunk | stinker | stinkpot | bum | puke | lowlife | scum bag | so-and-so | git

    • small piece of e.g. bread or cake
  • v.
    • coat with bread crumbs

      "crumb a cutlet"

    • break into crumbs
    • remove crumbs from

      "crumb the table"
