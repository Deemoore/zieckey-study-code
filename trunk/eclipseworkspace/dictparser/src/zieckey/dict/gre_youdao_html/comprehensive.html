comprehensive [,kɔmpri'hensiv]

  • adj.
    • 广泛的；综合的；包含多的，无所不包的，全面的
    • 领悟的；有理解力的，能充分理解的
    • 【保险业】总体担保的
  • n.
    • [常用复数]综合测验,专业综合考试[全称comprehensive examination]
    • 综合学校，综合性中学
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

comprehensive [ ,kɔmpri'hensiv ]

  • n. an intensive examination testing a student's proficiency in some special field of knowledge

    同义词：comprehensive examination | comp

  • adj.
    • including all or everything

      "comprehensive coverage"; " a comprehensive history of the revolution"; " a comprehensive survey"; " a comprehensive education"

    • broad in scope

      "a comprehensive survey of world affairs"
