nonchalant [ 'nɔnʃələnt, ,nɔnʃə'lɑ:nt ]

  • adj. marked by blithe unconcern

    "drove his car with nonchalant abandon"; " was polite in a teasing nonchalant manner"

    同义词：casual | insouciant
