  • adj. 首要必备的
  • n. 先决条件

prerequisite ['pri:'rekwizit]

  • adj.
    • (尤指先决条件)必须先具备的，先决条件的，必要的
  • n.
    • 先决条件；前提；必备的事物；必要条件
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prerequisite [ 'pri:'rekwizit ]

  • n. something that is required in advance

    "Latin was a prerequisite for admission"

    同义词：requirement

  • adj. required as a prior condition or course of study
