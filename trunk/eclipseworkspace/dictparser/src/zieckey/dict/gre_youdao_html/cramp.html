cramp1 [kræmp]

  • n.
    • (肌肉)痉挛，痛性痉挛
    • [常用复数] 急性腹痛，绞痛
    • (经期)腹痛；(分娩时)阵痛
    • [用复数] 月经；月经痛，痛经
    • =writer's cramp
  • vt.
    • 使痉挛；使绞痛[常用被动语态]：

      • Gout is cramping his limbs.

        痛风使他的手脚抽筋。

cramp2 [kræmp]

  • n.
    • 夹子,夹钳(=cramp iron)
    • 铁钩，铁箍；马铁
    • 约束物，束缚物
    • 约束，束缚(的状态或部分)
  • vt.
    • 用夹钳夹紧
    • 约束，束缚；限制；阻碍；上镣：

      • All these things cramped his progress.

        这一切妨碍了他的进步。

    • 使(汽车前轮)转动方向：

      • to cramp the wheels into the curb when parked

        停车时使前轮转向路边

  • adj.
    • 难以理解的；字迹潦草难辨认的
    • 狭窄的，紧缩的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cramp [ kræmp ]

  • n.
    • a painful and involuntary muscular contraction

      同义词：spasm | muscle spasm

    • a clamp for holding pieces of wood together while they are glued
    • a strip of metal with ends bent at right angles; used to hold masonry together

      同义词：cramp iron

  • v.
    • secure with a cramp

      "cramp the wood"

    • prevent the progress or free movement of

      同义词：hamper | halter | strangle
