bug [ bʌɡ ]

  • n.
    • general term for any insect or similar creeping or crawling invertebrate
    • a fault or defect in a system or machine

      同义词：glitch

    • a small hidden microphone; for listening secretly
    • insects with sucking mouthparts and forewings thickened and leathery at the base; usually show incomplete metamorphosis

      同义词：hemipterous insect | hemipteran | hemipteron

    • a minute life form (especially a disease-causing bacterium); the term is not in technical use

      同义词：microbe | germ

  • v.
    • annoy persistently

      同义词：tease | badger | pester | beleaguer

    • tap a telephone or telegraph wire to get information

      "Is this hotel room bugged?"

      同义词：wiretap | tap | intercept

臭虫

臭虫是异翅目臭虫科昆虫，它们约有75种。它们分布于全世界，是分布最广泛的人类寄生虫。吸食人血和温血动物的血液，在它们吸食人血的同时，还可能会将病菌或病毒传播给我们。体扁宽，长4∼5公釐(少於0.2吋)，红褐色，翅退化呈鳞状，有臭腺，分泌物有特殊的气味。
