primitive ['primitiv]

  • adj.
    • 初期的；古老的
    • 远古的；原始的；上古的
    • 粗糙的；简单的；未开化的；(画)风格朴实无华的
    • 非派生的；基本的；原来的；公理的；自明的
    • 【生物学】原生的；原始的
    • 【地质学】原始的；初生的
    • 【数学】本原的；原始的
    • 朴素的；自然的
    • 自学出来的；由自学的艺术家创作出来的
    • 【语言学】(词或语言)原始的；词根的，非派生的
  • n.
    • 原始人；原始事物
    • (尤指使用文字以前的)原始期艺术家及其作品；原始派艺术家
    • 风格质朴的艺术家及其作品；自学而成的艺术家
    • 文艺复兴前的艺术家及其作品
    • 【数学】本原，原始；基本式
    • 【语言学】原词；根词；原始(语)；原素
    • 纯朴的人
    • [P-]始初循道会派教徒
    • [P-] =Primitive Methodist

primitive [ 'primitiv ]

  • n.
    • a person who belongs to an early stage of civilization

      同义词：primitive person

    • a mathematical expression from which another expression is derived
    • a word serving as the basis for inflected or derived forms

      "`pick' is the primitive from which `picket' is derived"

  • adj.
    • belonging to an early stage of technical development; characterized by simplicity and (often) crudeness

      "primitive movies of the 1890s"; " primitive living conditions in the Appalachian mountains"

      同义词：crude | rude

    • little evolved from or characteristic of an earlier ancestral type

      "primitive mammals"; " the okapi is a short-necked primitive cousin of the giraffe"

      同义词：archaic

    • used of preliterate or tribal or nonindustrial societies

      "primitive societies"

    • of or created by one without formal training; simple or naive in style

      "primitive art such as that by Grandma Moses is often colorful and striking"

      同义词：naive
