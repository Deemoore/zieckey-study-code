penetrating ['penitreitiŋ]

  • vt.
    • penetrate的变形
  • adj.
    • 穿透的；贯穿的；渗透的
    • 尖刺的；刺鼻的；尖声的
    • 伸入很深的；进入很远的
    • (伤口等)深的；贯通伤口
    • 敏锐的；深刻的；透彻的
    • 有洞察力的;了解透彻的 [亦作 penetrative ／'penitrətiv／, penetrant]

penetrate ['penitreit]

  • vt.
    • 穿过；穿透；透过：

      • The nail easily penetrated the soft wood.

        钉子很容易地就穿透了软木材。

    • 刺入，刺透：

      • The knife penetrated enemy's stomach.

        刀子刺穿了敌人的肚子。

    • 弥漫于；扩散于：

      • The foul odor penetrated the entire house.

        难闻的臭气弥漫于整个房间。

    • 伸入，进入，打入：

      • The army penetrated the territory of enemy.

        部队进入敌人领土。

    • 渗透，浸入：

      • The heavy rain has penetrated the roof of my house.

        大雨渗透了我家的房顶。

    • 洞察，看破，看穿，识破：

      • This employee has penetrated his boss' s meaning.

        这位雇员已经看穿了他老板的意思。

    • (思想、感情等) 深入于；打动：

      • penetrated with discontent

        深为不满

    • 掌握；理解：

      • Were you able to penetrate the author's symbolism?

        你能理解作者的象征主义吗?

    • (性交时)把阴茎插入…的阴道
    • [美国俚语] (偷偷地或未经许可地) 破门而入
  • vi.
    • 穿入；穿透：

      • The nail will not penetrate.

        这枚钉子钉不进去。

    • 刺入，戳入，刺透：

      • The bayonet penetrates through the target.

        刺刀戳入靶子。

    • 延伸，进入，打进：

      • He has penetrated into high society.

        他已经踏进上层社会。

    • 洞察，看破，看穿，识破：

      • insight that penetrates to the very heart of the problem

        看穿问题的洞察力

    • 渗透，透过：

      • Rain has penetrated right through his coat.

        雨水湿透了他的衣衫。

    • (声音等)响亮，传很远：

      • Her voice doesn't penetrate.

        她的声音不太响亮。

    • 打动人心；(思想、感情等)深入人心：

      • Women's movement has not really penetrated here.

        妇女运动在这里还没有真正深入人心。

    • 被理解：

      • I heard what he said, but it didn't penetrate.

        我听他所言，但不明白说些什么。

    • 变形：
    • penetrated , penetrating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

penetrating [ 'penitreitiŋ ]

  • adj.
    • having or demonstrating ability to recognize or draw fine distinctions

      "penetrating insight"

      同义词：acute | discriminating | incisive | keen | knifelike | penetrative | piercing | sharp

    • tending to penetrate; having the power of entering or piercing

      "a cold penetrating wind"; " a penetrating odor"

      同义词：penetrative
