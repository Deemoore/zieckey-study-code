marrow1 ['mærəu]

  • n.
    • 髓，骨髓；脊髓；最深的地方
    • 精髓，精华；实质
    • 活力；生气，力量
    • (供食用的) 动物骨髓；精美食物；滋养品；山珍海味
    • ＝vegetable marrow
  • 短语:
    • chilled to the marrow 寒冷彻骨
    • suck someone to the very marrow 对某人敲骨吸髓
    • to the marrow (of one's bones)
      • 透入骨髓
      • 彻头彻尾地，十足地

marrow2 ['mærəu]

  • n.
    • 同伴，伙伴，伴侣；配偶
    • 配对物，配对的一个
    • 地位相等的人；同辈
    • 敌手
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

marrow [ 'mærəu ]

  • n.
    • the fatty network of connective tissue that fills the cavities of bones

      同义词：bone marrow

    • any of various squash plants grown for their elongated fruit with smooth dark green skin and whitish flesh

      同义词：marrow squash | vegetable marrow

    • very tender and very nutritious tissue from marrowbones

      同义词：bone marrow

    • large elongated squash with creamy to deep green skins

      同义词：vegetable marrow

    • the choicest or most essential or most vital part of some idea or experience

      同义词：kernel | substance | core | center | essence | gist | heart | heart and soul | inwardness | meat | nub | pith | sum | nitty-gritty
