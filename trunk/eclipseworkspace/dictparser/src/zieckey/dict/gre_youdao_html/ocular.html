ocular [ 'ɔkjulə ]

  • n. combination of lenses at the viewing end of optical instruments

    同义词：eyepiece

  • adj.
    • of or relating to or resembling the eye

      "ocular muscles"; " an ocular organ"; " ocular diseases"; " an ocular spot is a pigmented organ or part believed to be sensitive to light"

      同义词：optic | optical | opthalmic

    • relating to or using sight

      "ocular inspection"

      同义词：optic | optical | visual

    • able to be seen

      "be sure of it"; " give me the ocular proof"

      同义词：visual
