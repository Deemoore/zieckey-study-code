cascade [kæs'keid]

  • n.
    • 小瀑布(尤指连续数级瀑布中的一级)
    • 瀑布状的东西；一连串；一系列的装置
    • 上情逐级下达(的会议)；信息由上级层层向下级传达(的会议)
    • 【电工学】级联，串联
    • 【化学】阶式蒸发器
    • 【电子学】
      • 雪崩
      • 雪崩效应;汤森德雪崩[亦作 Townsend avalanche]
  • vi.
    • 瀑布般落下:

      • The price of a television set cascaded to 320 pounds last week.

        电视机价格上周暴跌到每台320镑。

    • 【电工学】串联
    • 传递信息，传达信息
  • vt.
    • 使像瀑布般落下
    • 【电工学】使串联
    • 传递(信息)，传达(信息)
    • 变形：
    • cascaded , cascading
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cascade [ kæs'keid ]

  • n.
    • a small waterfall or series of small waterfalls
    • a succession of stages or operations or processes or units

      "progressing in severity as though a cascade of genetic damage was occurring"; " separation of isotopes by a cascade of processes"

    • a sudden downpour (as of tears or sparks etc) likened to a rain shower

      "a sudden cascade of sparks"

      同义词：shower

  • v.
    • rush down in big quantities, like a cascade

      同义词：cascade down

    • arrange (open windows) on a computer desktop so that they overlap each other, with the title bars visible

以上来源于: WordNet
