ardent ['ɑ:dənt]

  • adj.
    • 热情的，热心的；热切的；热烈的；激动的；(感情)强烈的
    • 虔诚的，忠诚的，忠实的
    • 炽热的，白热的；高热的；燃烧的
    • 发光的
    • (欲望等)炽热如焚的
  • 近义词: passionate
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ardent [ 'ɑ:dənt ]

  • adj.
    • characterized by intense emotion

      "ardent love"; " an ardent lover"

      同义词：burning(a) | fervent | fervid | fiery | impassioned | perfervid | torrid

    • characterized by strong enthusiasm

      "ardent revolutionaries"

      同义词：warm

    • glowing or shining like fire

      "from rank to rank she darts her ardent eyes"; " frightened by his ardent burning eyes"
