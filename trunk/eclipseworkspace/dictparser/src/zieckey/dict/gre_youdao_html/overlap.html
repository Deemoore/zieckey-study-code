overlap [,əuvə'læp; 'əuvəlæp]

  • vt.
    • 与…互搭，与…重叠，部分叠盖住：

      • Two old trees overlapped their upper branches.

        那两棵古树的上端分枝相互交搭复生。

    • 盖过，伸展越过(某物)：

      • The ends of cloth overlap the bed.

        床罩布的各端都从床边垂下。

    • 与…部分一致(或巧合)；与…有共性；与…部分同时发生：

      • to overlap the truth

        与事实部分巧合

      • The two lives overlapped each other.

        那两种生物有一段时间相互间具有共性。

  • vi.
    • 交搭；重叠：

      • the tiles that overlap

        叠搭的瓦片

    • 部分一致(或巧合)；部分相同：

      • Sometimes physics and chemistry overlap.

        有时很难区分出究竟是物理学还是化学。

  • n.
    • 交搭；重叠；部分相同(或同时发生)
    • 交搭处；重叠处
    • 重叠部分
    • 交搭量(或程度)；重叠量(或程度)
    • (帆船比赛中的)并排
    • 【地质学】超覆
    • 变形：
    • overlapped , overlapping
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

overlap [ ,əuvə'læp, 'əuvəlæp ]

  • n.
    • a representation of common ground between theories or phenomena

      "there was no overlap between their proposals"

      同义词：convergence | intersection

    • the property of partial coincidence in time
    • a flap that lies over another part

      同义词：lap

  • v.
    • coincide partially or wholly

      "Our vacations overlap"

    • extend over and cover a part of

      "The roofs of the houses overlap in this crowded city"
