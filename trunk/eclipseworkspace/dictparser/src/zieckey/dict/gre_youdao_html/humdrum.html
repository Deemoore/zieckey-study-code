humdrum ['hʌmdrʌm]

  • adj.
    • 平常的，平凡的；单调的，无聊的，乏味的
  • n.
    • 平凡；单调，无聊
    • 凡人
    • 无聊的人，令人厌烦的人
    • 无聊的工作(或生活等)
  • vi.
    • 单调乏味地进行
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

humdrum [ 'hʌmdrʌm ]

  • n. the quality of wearisome constancy, routine, and lack of variety

    "he was sick of the humdrum of his fellow prisoners"

    同义词：monotony | sameness

  • adj.
    • not challenging; dull and lacking excitement

      同义词：commonplace | prosaic | unglamorous | unglamourous

    • tediously repetitious or lacking in variety

      "a humdrum existence"; " all work and no play"

      同义词：monotonous

以上来源于: WordNet
