addled ['ædld]

  • vt.
    • addle的变形
  • adj.
    • ＝ addle

addle ['ædl]

  • adj.
    • (头脑)糊涂的，昏乱的；愚蠢的[现多用于复合词]
    • 腐臭的，腐败的，腐坏的；变质的
  • vt.
    • 使糊涂，使昏乱：

      • He has addled his head with reading and writing all day long.

        他整天读书写字，头都昏了。

    • 使腐坏，使腐臭变质
    • [英国方言]谋(生)，(通过劳动)挣(钱)
  • vi.
    • (蛋等)腐臭，腐坏，变质：

      • Not one of these eggs ever addled.

        这些蛋没一个坏的。

    • 变糊涂；变昏乱；成为混乱，混淆：

      • The object is to addle and not to elucidate.

        其目的是为了混淆而不是为了阐明。

    • 变形：
    • addled , addling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

addled [ 'ædld ]

  • adj.
    • (of eggs) no longer edible

      "an addled egg"

    • confused and vague; used especially of thinking

      "your addled little brain"

      同义词：befuddled | muddled | muzzy | woolly | wooly | woolly-headed | wooly-minded
