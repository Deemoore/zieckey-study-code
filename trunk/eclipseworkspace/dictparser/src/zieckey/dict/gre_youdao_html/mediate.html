mediate ['mi:dieit]

  • vi.
    • 调解;调停;斡旋(between, in)：

      • They mediated a settlement between labour and management.

        他们通过调停解决了劳资纠纷。

      • Father mediated in the quarrel between the two girls.

        父亲调解两个女孩之间的争吵。

    • 居于中间地位；介于中间
    • 【心理学】(在观念、意向等与其结果之间)起中介作用
  • vt.
    • 调停；调解：

      • to mediate an agreement

        经调解达成协议

      • The government must mediate the struggle for water resources.

        政府必须通过调解解决对水资源的争夺。

    • 传播知识；传递消息；转交礼物
    • 【心理学】通过起中介作用促成(某种结果)
  • adj.
    • 间接的；有间接关系的
    • 调停的
    • 居间的
    • 【心理学】中介的，间接的
  • 近义词: interpose
    • 变形：
    • mediated , mediat ing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

mediate [ 'mi:dieit ]

  • v.
    • act between parties with a view to reconciling differences

      "He mediated a settlement"

      同义词：intercede | intermediate | liaise | arbitrate

    • occupy an intermediate or middle position or form a connecting link or stage between two others

      "mediate between the old and the new"

  • adj.
    • acting through or dependent on an intervening agency

      "the disease spread by mediate as well as direct contact"

    • being neither at the beginning nor at the end in a series

      "in a mediate position"

      同义词：in-between | middle

以上来源于: WordNet
