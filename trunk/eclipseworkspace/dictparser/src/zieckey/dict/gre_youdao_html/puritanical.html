puritanical [,pjuəri'tænikəl]

  • adj.
    • [P-]清教徒的，清教徒主义的
    • 宗教(或道德)上极端拘谨的 [亦作 puritanic ／-ik／]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

puritanical [ ,pjuəri'tænikəl ]

  • adj.
    • of or relating to Puritans or Puritanism
    • exaggeratedly proper

      同义词：priggish | prim | prissy | prudish | square-toed | straitlaced | strait-laced | straightlaced | straight-laced | tight-laced | victorian

    • morally rigorous and strict

      "she was anything but puritanical in her behavior"

      同义词：blue(a) | puritan | puritanic
