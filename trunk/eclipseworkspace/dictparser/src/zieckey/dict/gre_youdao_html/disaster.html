disaster [,di'zɑ:stə]

  • n.
    • 大不幸，大难，大祸，灾难，灾害，天灾
    • 祸患；成事不足败事有余的人
      • 彻底(或完全)的失败，惨败；事故，故障
      • 彻底(或完全)失败的事情
    • [废语](星向)凶位，凶向
  • 近义词: calamity , catastrophe , cataclysm
  • 短语:
    • a disaster waiting to happen [讽刺语]临头的大灾祸；被忽略的潜在危险(或问题)
    • a walking disaster 或 a disaster on two legs [口语]经常出差错的人；混乱无序的人
    • court(或invite) disaster 招致灾难，惹祸
    • walk with disaster 与灾祸打交道，招致灾难
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

disaster [ ,di'zɑ:stə ]

  • n.
    • a state of extreme (usually irremediable) ruin and misfortune

      "his policies were a disaster"

      同义词：catastrophe

    • an event resulting in great loss and misfortune

      "the earthquake was a disaster"

      同义词：calamity | catastrophe | tragedy | cataclysm

    • an act that has disastrous consequences
