eruption [i'rʌpʃən]

  • n.
    • (熔岩从火山的)喷出，爆发
    • (熔岩、水、蒸汽等的)喷发
    • 喷出物(如火山熔岩、尘埃或蒸汽等)
    • (如感情或战争的)爆发，迸发，突发
    • 【病理学】
      • 发疹
      • 疹
    • 【牙科学】(牙齿等的)长出，萌出
  • 短语:
    • eruption of stars 演员(或明星)大会演
    • eruption of teeth 出牙，生齿
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

eruption [ i'rʌpʃən ]

  • n.
    • the sudden occurrence of a violent discharge of steam and volcanic material

      同义词：volcanic eruption

    • symptom consisting of a breaking out and becoming visible
    • (of volcanos) pouring out fumes of lava (or a deposit so formed)

      同义词：eructation | extravasation

    • a sudden violent spontaneous occurrence (usually of some undesirable condition)

      同义词：outbreak | irruption

    • a sudden very loud noise

      同义词：bang | clap | blast | bam

    • the emergence of a tooth as it breaks through the gum

以上来源于: WordNet
