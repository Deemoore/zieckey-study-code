decamp [di'kæmp]

  • vi.
    • 撤营，拔营：

      • They decamped before rain began.

        他们在下雨前撤营。

    • 逃跑，逃亡，潜逃，秘密(或匆忙、突然)地逃走，溜走：

      • The band of thieves decamped in the night.

        窃贼团伙星夜潜逃了。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

decamp [ di'kæmp ]

  • v.
    • leave a camp

      "The hikers decamped before dawn"

      同义词：break camp

    • run away; usually includes taking something or somebody along

      同义词：abscond | bolt | absquatulate | run off | go off | make off

    • leave suddenly

      "She persuaded him to decamp"

      同义词：skip | vamoose

以上来源于: WordNet
