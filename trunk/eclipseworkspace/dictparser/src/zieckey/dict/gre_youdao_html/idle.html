idle ['aidl]

  • adj.
    • 空闲的；不忙的；闲散的；无活动的
    • 不在工作的，失业的
    • 懒惰的；懒散的；无所事事的
    • 闲置的；未利用的
    • 无价值的；无意义的
    • 徒劳的；无效的；无用的
    • 无根据的；无理由的
    • 【机械学】空转的；慢转的；怠速的
    • 【电子学】空载的；无功的
    • 不符合军人身份的
  • vt.
    • 虚度；浪费(常与away连用):

      • Don't idle away your time.

        别浪费光阴。

      • to idle away the afternoon

        虚度一下午

    • 让(发动机等)空转：

      • I waited in the car while idling the engine.

        我在汽车里待着而让发动机空转着。

      • to idle down the motor

        让发动机空转

    • 使空闲，使没事干：

      • The strike idled many workers.

        罢工使许多工人没事干。

  • vi.
    • 闲逛；无所事事(常与away连用)：

      • Mother asked me to stop idling about.

        妈妈叫我不要再闲混。

      • to idle along the avenue

        沿大街闲逛

    • (发动机等)空转：

      • The driver left the car idling away.

        那个司机让汽车发动机空转着。[1915～1920]

  • n.
    • (发动机的)空转；怠速
  • 短语:
    • An idle youth, a needy age. [谚语]少壮不努力，老大徒伤悲。
    • Idle brain is the devil's shop. [谚语]懒惰是罪恶的渊源。
    • 变形：
    • idler , idlest
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

idle [ 'aidl ]

  • n. the state of an engine or other mechanism that is idling

    "the car engine was running at idle"

  • v.
    • run disconnected or idle

      同义词：tick over

    • be idle; exist in a changeless situation

      同义词：laze | slug | stagnate

  • adj.
    • not in action or at work

      "an idle laborer"; " idle drifters"; " the idle rich"; " an idle mind"

    • without a basis in reason or fact

      "idle fears"

      同义词：baseless | groundless | unfounded | unwarranted | wild

    • not in active use

      "the machinery sat idle during the strike"; " idle hands"

      同义词：unused

    • silly or trivial

      "idle pleasure"; " light idle chatter"

      同义词：light

    • lacking a sense of restraint or responsibility

      "idle talk"

      同义词：loose

    • not yielding a return

      "idle funds"

      同义词：dead

    • not having a job

      "idle carpenters"

      同义词：jobless | out of work
