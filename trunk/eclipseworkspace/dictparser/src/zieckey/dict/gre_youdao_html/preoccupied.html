preoccupied [pri:'ɔkjupaid]

  • adj.
    • 被抢先占据的；被抢先占有的
    • 全神贯注的；出神的
    • 【生物学】(种或属的名称)不能再以新义使用的
  • vt.
    • preoccupy的变形

preoccupy [pri:'ɔkjupai]

  • vt.
    • 使对…全神贯注；使专心于；使出神：

      • Something seems to be preoccupying her at the moment.

        她此刻若有所思。

    • 抢先占有；抢先取得：

      • The front seats have been preoccupied.

        前排座位已给别人先占了。

    • [废语]使有偏见
    • 变形：
    • preoccupied , preoccupying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

preoccupied [ pri:'ɔkjupaid ]

  • adj.
    • deeply absorbed in thought

      "a preoccupied frown"

      同义词：bemused | deep in thought(p) | lost(p)

    • having or showing excessive or compulsive concern with something

      "got no help from his wife who was preoccupied with the children"

      同义词：haunted | obsessed | taken up(p)
