divulge [dai'vʌldʒ; di-]

  • vt.
    • 泄漏(秘密等)，揭露(隐私、本不为人知的事等)：

      • The boy promised not to divulge the whereabouts of the hiding place.

        那男孩许诺不泄露隐藏地的所在。

    • [古语]宣布，公布，昭示；宣扬；发表
    • 变形：
    • divulged , divulging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

divulge [ dai'vʌldʒ, di- ]

  • v. make known to the public information that was previously known only to a few people or that was meant to be kept a secret

    同义词：unwrap | disclose | let on | bring out | reveal | discover | expose | impart | break | give away | let out

以上来源于: WordNet
