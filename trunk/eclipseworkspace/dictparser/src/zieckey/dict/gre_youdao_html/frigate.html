  • n.

    • [英国英语](排水量在三千吨以下的)护卫舰，护航舰

frigate [ 'friɡit ]

  • n.
    • a medium size square-rigged warship of the 18th and 19th centuries
    • a United States warship larger than a destroyer and smaller than a cruiser
