liquidate ['likwideit]

  • vt.
    • 清偿，了结(债务、损失费等)：

      • to liquidate a debt

        清偿债务

      • to liquidate a mortgage

        清偿抵押借款

    • 清理(账目)；确定(负责或损失的)数额；清算(破产的公司等)：

      • They have decided to liquidate the corporation by the end of the next month.

        他们已经决定在下月底之前对公司进行清理。

    • 将(资产等)变换成现金，变卖：

      • He was compelled to liquidate his securities.

        他被迫将债券变换成现金。

    • 肃清：消灭；杀戮；清洗：

      • Innocents were brutally liquidated by thousands.

        数以千计的无辜者遭到残忍地杀戮。

    • 消除：取消；废除：

      • It is possible to liquidate their doubts.

        消除他们的疑虑是有可能的。

      • to liquidate a partnership

        取消合股关系

    • [古语]阐明；弄清楚
  • vi.
    • 清理(债务、资产、账目等)；清算；偿清
    • (公司等)停业清理
    • 变形：
    • liquidated , liquidating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

liquidate [ 'likwideit ]

  • v.
    • get rid of (someone who may be a threat) by killing

      "The mafia liquidated the informer"

      同义词：neutralize | neutralise | waste | knock off | do in

    • eliminate by paying off (debts)

      同义词：pay off

    • convert into cash

      "I had to liquidate my holdings to pay off my ex-husband"

    • settle the affairs of by determining the debts and applying the assets to pay them off

      "liquidate a company"
