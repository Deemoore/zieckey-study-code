rafter [ 'rɑ:ftə, 'ræf- ]

  • n.
    • one of several parallel sloping beams that support a roof

      同义词：balk | baulk

    • someone who travels by raft

      同义词：raftsman | raftman

  • v. provide (a ceiling) with rafters
