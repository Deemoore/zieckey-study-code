hoop1 [hu:p]

  • n.
    • (箍桶用的)箍；环；圈
    • (儿童滚着玩的)铁环；(杂技演员等使用的)大木圈
    • (大炮等的)环带；(车轮的)辋圈
    • (旧时扩张裙子用的)圈环
    • =hoop skirt
    • =hoop petticoat
    • 【建筑学】(柱子的)箍环，(钢筋混凝土柱主钢筋四边的)环钢筋
    • 【槌球】弓形小门
    • [美国英语]【篮球】
      • 篮；篮圈
      • 得分
    • 环状物
    • 戒指；耳环
    • (刺绣用的)撑环，绷子
  • adj.
    • 【篮球】篮圈的
  • vt.
    • 箍；用箍把…围住：

      • to hoop a barrel

        箍桶

    • 环绕，包围；拥抱：

      • a village hooped by hills

        四面环山的村庄

    • 【篮球】投(篮)；得(分)
  • vi.
    • 成环形
  • 短语:
    • go through the hoop (或hoops) 受磨炼；受苦难
    • jump through a hoop [美国口语]俯首帖耳，百般讨好
    • put someone through the hoop (或hoops)
      • [口语]
      • 使人经受磨炼
      • 责问某人
      • 苦苦思索
    • roll one's hoop
      • 顺利向前
      • 稳妥办事
      • [美国俚语]只管自己的事

hoop [ hu:p ]

  • n.
    • a light curved skeleton to spread out a skirt
    • a rigid circular band of metal or wood or other material used for holding or fastening or hanging or pulling

      "there was still a rusty iron hoop for tying a horse"

      同义词：ring

    • a small arch used as croquet equipment

      同义词：wicket

    • horizontal circular metal hoop supporting a net through which players try to throw the basketball

      同义词：basket | basketball hoop

  • v. bind or fasten with a hoop

    "hoop vats"
