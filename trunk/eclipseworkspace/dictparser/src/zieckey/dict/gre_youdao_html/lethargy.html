lethargy ['leθədʒi]

  • n.
    • 昏睡；嗜睡症
    • 冷漠；冷淡；懒散；无精打采；无生气
  • 近义词: languor , lassitude
    • 变形：
    • lethargies
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

lethargy [ 'leθədʒi ]

  • n.
    • a state of comatose torpor (as found in sleeping sickness)

      同义词：lassitude | sluggishness

    • weakness characterized by a lack of vitality or energy

      同义词：inanition | lassitude | slackness

    • inactivity; showing an unusual lack of energy

      同义词：languor | sluggishness | phlegm | flatness
