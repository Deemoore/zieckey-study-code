liquor ['likə]

  • n.
    • (蒸馏而成的)酒(如白兰地、威士忌或朗姆酒等)，酒精饮料，烈性酒
    • 液体；汁水；煮汁；煎汁
    • 【药物学】溶液；液剂
    • (工业用)溶液；乳汁液
  • vt.
    • [口语]使喝醉(常与up连用)：

      • He was liquored up by his colleagues.

        他被同事灌醉了。

    • (在各种工业生产过程中)将…浸泡在溶液中
    • 给(鞋子、皮革等)上油
  • vi.
    • (大量地)喝酒；狂饮(常与up连用)：

      • They liquored up at the feast.

        他们在筵席上狂饮。

  • 短语:
    • carry one's liquor like a gentleman 毫无醉意，酒量很大
    • have (或take) a liquor (或liquor up) [俚语]喝杯酒提神
    • hold one's liquor 保持不醉
    • in liquor 带有醉意，喝醉
    • red liquor [美国俚语]烈酒(尤指威士忌)
    • run liquor 贩运私酒
    • the worse for liquor ([苏格兰英语]the worse of liquor) 带有醉意，喝醉
    • under the influence of liquor 微醉
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

liquor [ 'likə ]

  • n.
    • an alcoholic beverage that is distilled rather than fermented

      同义词：spirits | booze | hard drink | hard liquor | John Barleycorn | strong drink

    • a liquid substance that is a solution (or emulsion or suspension) used or obtained in an industrial process

      "waste liquors"

    • the liquid in which vegetables or meat have be cooked

      同义词：pot liquor | pot likker
