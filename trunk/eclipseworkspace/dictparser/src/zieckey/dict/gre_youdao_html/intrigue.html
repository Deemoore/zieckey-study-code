intrigue [in'tri:ɡ; 'in-]

  • n.
    • 阴谋，诡计；密谋，策划
    • 私通
    • (戏剧、小说等错综复杂的)情节；吸引力
  • vi.
    • 策划阴谋；施诡计；密谋(常与against连用):

      • to intrigue against one's friends

        阴谋陷害朋友

      • to intrigue with each other

        互相勾结

    • 私通(常与with连用):

      • to intrigue with a woman

        与女人私通

  • vt.
    • 用诡计取得：

      • He was intrigued out of power.

        他被人施诡计搞下了台。

    • 引起…的兴趣；使着迷：

      • The film intrigued all of us.

        这部电影引起了我们大家的兴趣。

    • 使好奇；使迷惑；使困惑：

      • The natives were intrigued with the colour of our skin.

        当地人对我们的肤色感到好奇。

    • [古语]哄骗；欺骗
  • 近义词: plot
    • 变形：
    • intrigued , intriguing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

intrigue [ in'tri:ɡ, 'in- ]

  • n.
    • a crafty and involved plot to achieve your (usually sinister) ends

      同义词：machination

    • a clandestine love affair
  • v.
    • cause to be interested or curious

      同义词：fascinate

    • form intrigues (for) in an underhand manner

      同义词：scheme | connive
