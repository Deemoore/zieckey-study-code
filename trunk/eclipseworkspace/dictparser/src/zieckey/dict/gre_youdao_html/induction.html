induction [in'dʌkʃən]

  • n.
    • 就任；就职
    • 成为会员；[美国英语]征召(入伍)
    • 入门，首次经验
    • 引诱，诱导
    • 【电学】(电磁)感应;感应现象[参较 electromagnetic induction, electrostatic induction]
    • 【生物学】诱导
    • 【医学】人工引导；诱导
    • 【逻辑学、数学】归纳法;归纳[亦作 mathematical induction]
    • 【机械学】(内燃机)进气，吸气
    • [古语]前言，导语，序幕

induction [ in'dʌkʃən ]

  • n.
    • a formal entry into an organization or position or office

      "he was ordered to report for induction into the army"

      同义词：initiation | installation

    • an electrical phenomenon whereby an electromotive force (EMF) is generated in a closed circuit by a change in the flow of current

      同义词：inductance

    • reasoning from detailed facts to general principles

      同义词：generalization | generalisation | inductive reasoning

    • stimulation that calls up (draws forth) a particular class of behaviors

      同义词：evocation | elicitation

    • the act of bringing about something (especially at an early time)

      "the induction of an anesthetic state"

    • an act that sets in motion some course of events

      同义词：trigger | initiation

归纳法

归纳法或归纳推理，有时叫做归纳逻辑，是论证的前提支持结论但不确保结论的推理过程。它把特性或关系归结到基于对特殊的代表(token)的有限观察的类型；或公式表达基于对反复再现的现象的模式(pattern)的有限观察的规律。例如，使用归纳法在如下特殊的命题中:冰是冷的。 在击打球杆的时候弹子球移动。 推断出普遍的命题如:所有冰都是冷的。或: 在太阳下没有冰。 对于所有动作，都有相同和相反的重做动作。
