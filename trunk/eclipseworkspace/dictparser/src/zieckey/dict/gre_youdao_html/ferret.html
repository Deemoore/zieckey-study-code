ferret1 ['ferit]

  • n. (棉、毛、丝混合制成的)细带；丝棉(意大利名称)[亦作ferreting]

ferret2 ['ferit]

  • n.
    • 【动物】雪貂(Mustela属)
    • 【动物】白鼬[美国西部的一种珍贵的黑足鼬鼠(Muslela nigripes),亦称作black-footed ferret]
    • [Ferret]电子侦察卫星
  • vt.
    • 用雪貂把(猎物等)逐出：用雪貂猎取；把…逐出：

      • to ferret out enemies

        把敌人驱赶出来

      • to ferret rabbits

        用雪貂猎取野兔

    • 搜寻，搜索；侦察出，搜出，查获(秘密罪犯等)(常与out连用)：

      • He ferreted out quite a lot of information.

        他侦察到了相当多的情报。

    • 使折磨，使痛苦：

      • His problems ferreted him day and night.

        他的困难使他日夜不能安宁。

  • vi.
    • 用雪貂狩猎：

      • to go ferreting

        带雪貂去狩猎

    • 搜查，搜索：

      • The policeman ferreted around all over the house.

        警察在房子四周搜索。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ferret [ 'ferit ]

  • n.
    • musteline mammal of prairie regions of United States; nearly extinct

      同义词：black-footed ferret | Mustela nigripes

    • domesticated albino variety of the European polecat bred for hunting rats and rabbits
  • v.
    • hound or harry relentlessly
    • hunt with ferrets
    • search and discover through persistent investigation

      "She ferreted out the truth"

      同义词：ferret out
