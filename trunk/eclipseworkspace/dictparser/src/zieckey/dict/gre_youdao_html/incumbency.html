incumbency [in'kʌmbənsi]

  • n.
    • 任职；任期；现任职位(或职权)
    • 职责；责任；义务
    • 压，上覆，覆盖；凭倚，倚靠
    • 上覆物；倚靠物
    • 变形：
    • incumbencies
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incumbency [ in'kʌmbənsi ]

  • n.
    • the term during which some position is held

      同义词：tenure | term of office

    • a duty that is incumbent upon you
    • the office of an incumbent
