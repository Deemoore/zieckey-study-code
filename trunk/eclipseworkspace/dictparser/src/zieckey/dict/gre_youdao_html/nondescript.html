nondescript ['nɔndi,skript]

  • adj.
    • 难以辨认的，难以明确的
    • 难以归类的，难以形容的
  • n.
    • 无特征的人(或事物)；难以形容(或归类)的人(或事物)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

nondescript [ 'nɔndi,skript ]

  • n. a person is not easily classified and not very interesting
  • adj. lacking distinct or individual characteristics; dull and uninteresting

    "women dressed in nondescript clothes"; " a nondescript novel"

    同义词：characterless
