emergency [i'mə:dʒənsi]

  • n.
    • 突然事件，意外事故，不测事件；紧急情况，非常时刻
    • 紧急需要
    • 急症(病人)
    • [新西兰英语]【体育】预备队员，替补队员
  • adj.
    • 紧急情况下出现(或使用)的；应紧急需要的，应急的，紧急的，备用的
  • 近义词: exigency , contingency , crisis , strait
  • 短语:
    • in an emergency 在紧急关头，在非常时候，遇到紧急情况[亦作 in case of emergency]
    • rise to the emergency 有应变之才；能应变，能够应付紧急事变[亦作 rise to the occasion]
    • 变形：
    • emergencies
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

emergency [ i'mə:dʒənsi ]

  • n.
    • a sudden unforeseen crisis (usually involving danger) that requires immediate action

      "he never knew what to do in an emergency"

      同义词：exigency | pinch

    • a state in which martial law applies

      "the governor declared a state of emergency"

    • a brake operated by hand; usually operates by mechanical linkage

      同义词：hand brake | emergency brake | parking brake
