crude [kru:d]

  • adj.
    • 生的，天然的，未加工的；未提炼的
    • (意见等)不成熟的，初步的；肤浅的，幼稚的；简陋的
    • 粗糙的；生硬的；不完善的
    • 粗野的，缺乏教养的
    • 露骨的，不加掩饰的；赤裸裸的
  • n.
    • [美国英语](未加工的)天然物质； (尤指)原油 (= crude oil)
    • 变形：
    • cruder , crudest
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

crude [ kru:d ]

  • n. a dark oil consisting mainly of hydrocarbons

    同义词：petroleum | crude oil | rock oil | fossil oil

  • adj.
    • not carefully or expertly made

      "managed to make a crude splint"; " a crude cabin of logs with bark still on them"

      同义词：rough

    • conspicuously and tastelessly indecent

      "a crude joke"; " crude behavior"

      同义词：coarse | earthy | gross | vulgar

    • not refined or processed

      "crude oil"

      同义词：unrefined | unprocessed

    • belonging to an early stage of technical development; characterized by simplicity and (often) crudeness

      "the crude weapons and rude agricultural implements of early man"

      同义词：primitive | rude

    • devoid of any qualifications or disguise or adornment

      "the crude facts"

      同义词：blunt | stark(a)

    • not processed or subjected to analysis

      "only the crude vital statistics"

      同义词：raw
