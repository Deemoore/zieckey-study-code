lapse [læps]

  • n.
    • (时间的)流逝，间隔；逝去的时间
    • 失言，口误；笔误；记错
    • 过失，错失，小错，小疵；失检
    • (尤指气温、气压的)跌落，下降
    • 放弃信仰；违背教义
    • 废止，中断
    • 【法律】(由于未能履行义务或行使权力而导致权利或特权的)失效，终止，丧失
    • (因继承人死亡而导致的)遗产继承权失效
    • (由于未按时交纳保险金而引起的)保险中止
  • vi.
    • (时间)流逝：

      • Two weeks have lapsed since they arrived in Paris.

        自他们抵达巴黎以来，已过去了两个星期了。

    • 滑入；陷入(某种状态)：

      • The driver lapsed into unconsciousness after the car accident.

        车祸之后，司机陷入昏迷状态。

      • He tried to speak English all the day,but gradually he lapsed into his native tongue.

        他试图整天说英语，可是渐渐地他又讲起本国语了。

    • 倒退；退步；没达到(标准、期望等)：

      • The audience were disappointed in the comedian because he lapsed from his formerly high standard of performance.

        观众对这位喜剧演员感到失望，因为他的表演没有过去水平高了。

    • 终止，结束：

      • a subscription that has lapsed

        (报纸、杂志等的)订阅期已截止

    • 【法律】(权利、所有权、契约等的)失效；丧失；消灭：

      • Remind me to renew the driver's license.It will lapse next month.

        提醒我更换驾驶执照，它下个月就失效了。

    • 放弃信仰，背离正道：

      • to lapse from good manners

        行为变坏

  • vt.
    • 废止，使失效；丧失：

      • The company has lapsed the right to the use of the land.

        这家公司已经丧失了这块土地的使用权。

  • 短语:
    • lapse of attention 一时疏忽
    • lapse from virtue 堕落，道德败坏
    • lapse of memory 记错；记忆力衰退
    • lapse of the pen 笔误
    • lapse of time 一段时间；时光的流逝
    • lapse of the tongue 口误；失言
    • with the lapse of time 随着时间的推移；渐渐
    • 变形：
    • lapsed , lapsing

LAPSE

  • abbr. long-term ambulatory physiological surveillance equipment 【医学】(供医院监护病房使用的)非卧床病人长期生理监护设备

lapse [ læps ]

  • n.
    • a mistake resulting from inattention

      同义词：oversight

    • a break or intermission in the occurrence of something

      "a lapse of three weeks between letters"

    • a failure to maintain a higher state

      同义词：backsliding | lapsing | relapse | relapsing | reversion | reverting

  • v.
    • pass into a specified state or condition

      同义词：sink | pass

    • end, at least for a long time

      "The correspondence lapsed"

    • drop to a lower level, as in one's morals or standards

      同义词：backslide

    • go back to bad behavior

      同义词：relapse | recidivate | regress | retrogress | fall back

    • let slip

      "He lapsed his membership"

    • pass by

      "three years elapsed"

      同义词：elapse | pass | slip by | glide by | slip away | go by | slide by | go along
