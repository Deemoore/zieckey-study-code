babble ['bæbl]

  • vi.
    • (婴孩)咿呀学语，牙牙学语：

      • The infant babbled(away 或 on) for half an hour.

        这婴儿连续半个小时咿呀学语。

    • (成人)模糊不清地说：

      • The patient babbled of home.

        这个病人模糊不清地说要回家。

    • 说蠢话，胡说；喋喋不休，唠叨：

      • Mother always babbles about trifles.

        母亲总是唠叨些琐事。

      • to babble without stopping

        不停地胡说

    • 吐露(或脱口泄露)秘密；多嘴：

      • to babble out the secret

        泄露秘密

      • Don't babble.

        别多嘴。

    • (流水发)潺潺声(常与away,on,along连用):

      • a babbling stream

        流水潺潺的小溪

      • The little brook babbled away just behind our tent.

        紧挨着我们帐篷后面的小溪水声潺潺。

  • vt.
    • 咿呀学语，模糊不清地说：

      • She babbled a few words to him.

        她跟他咿咿呀呀地说了几句话。

    • 唠唠叨叨地说，喋喋不休地讲；不断地表示：

      • He babbled his thanks in a great hurry.

        他急忙连声道谢。

    • (因愚蠢或不加思考)泄漏，脱口泄露，随口说出；愚蠢地说出：

      • He babbled the secret (out) to his lover.

        他把这个秘密向情人说(出去)了。

      • to babble a secret

        失口泄密

    • 以潺潺声发出：

      • The fountain babbled its familiar music.

        泉水发出惯常的潺潺声。

  • n.
    • 婴儿咿呀学语声，儿语
    • 模糊不清的话
    • 蠢话；胡言乱语，胡话，空话
    • 嘈杂的说话声，讲话的嘈杂声，杂音
    • (流水)潺潺声
    • 【电信学】(多路感应的)复杂失真(或畸变)，集扰[参较 cross talk]
  • 短语:
    • Much babbling is not without offence. [谚语]言多必失。
    • 变形：
    • babbled , babbling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

babble [ 'bæbl ]

  • n. gibberish resembling the sounds of a baby

    同义词：babbling | lallation

  • v.
    • utter meaningless sounds, like a baby, or utter in an incoherent way
    • to talk foolishly

      "The two women babbled and crooned at the baby"

      同义词：blather | smatter | blether | blither

    • flow in an irregular current with a bubbling noise

      同义词：ripple | guggle | burble | bubble | gurgle

    • divulge confidential information or secrets

      同义词：spill the beans | let the cat out of the bag | talk | tattle | blab | peach | sing | babble out | blab out

以上来源于: WordNet
