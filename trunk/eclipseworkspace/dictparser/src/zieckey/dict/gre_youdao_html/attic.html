attic [ 'ætik ]

  • n.
    • floor consisting of open space at the top of a house just below roof; often used for storage

      同义词：loft | garret

    • the dialect of Ancient Greek spoken and written in Attica and Athens

      同义词：Classical Greek

    • informal terms for a human head

      同义词：bean | bonce | noodle | noggin | dome

    • (architecture) a low wall at the top of the entablature; hides the roof
  • adj. of or relating to Attica or its inhabitants or to the dialect spoken in Athens in classical times

以上来源于: WordNet
