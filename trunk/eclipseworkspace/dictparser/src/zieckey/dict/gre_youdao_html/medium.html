medium ['mi:diəm; -djəm]

  • n.
    • 中间物；中等；适中；中庸
    • 媒质；传导体；媒介物
    • 工具；手段；方法
    • 生活条件；生存空间
    • 环境
    • [常作 media] [有时用作单数]宣传工具；传播媒介；新闻媒介
    • 【细菌学】培养基 [亦作 culture medium]
    • (有机物标本的)防腐剂
    • (颜料的)溶剂
    • (艺术创作用的)材料；(艺术的)表现方式
    • (居中斡旋的)中间人
    • [复数] mediums 灵媒；巫师；(搞迷信活动的)关亡人
    • 中号印刷纸(通常为18×23英寸的印刷用纸)
    • 【化学】过滤物质(如滤纸)
    • (舞台上用的)灯光滤光片
    • [用作复数] [英国英语] 【证券】中期金边证券；中期优等证券
  • adj.
    • 中间的；中等的；适中的
    • 肉烧得中等熟度的；肉烧得适中的
  • 短语:
    • by (或through) the medium (of) 借助于；以…为媒介；通过…手段
    • happy medium 中庸之道 [参见 strike the happy medium]
    • news media 新闻媒介；新闻传播工具
    • strike a (或the) happy medium 采取中庸之道；采取折中办法
    • 变形：
    • mediums , media

medium [ 'mi:diəm, -djəm ]

  • n.
    • a means or instrumentality for storing or communicating information
    • the surrounding environment

      "fish require an aqueous medium"

    • an intervening substance through which signals can travel as a means for communication
    • (bacteriology) a nutrient substance (solid or liquid) that is used to cultivate micro-organisms

      同义词：culture medium

    • an intervening substance through which something is achieved

      "the dissolving medium is called a solvent"

    • a liquid with which pigment is mixed by a painter
    • (biology) a substance in which specimens are preserved or displayed
    • a state that is intermediate between extremes; a middle position

      "a happy medium"

    • someone who serves as an intermediary between the living and the dead

      "he consulted several mediums"

      同义词：spiritualist | sensitive

    • (usually plural) transmissions that are disseminated widely to the public

      同义词：mass medium

    • an occupation for which you are especially well suited

      同义词：metier

  • adj.
    • around the middle of a scale of evaluation of physical measures

      "medium bombers"

      同义词：average | intermediate

    • (meat) cooked until there is just a little pink meat inside
