hawker1 ['hɔ:kə]

  • n. (尤指沿街叫卖的)小贩，叫卖商

hawker2 ['hɔ:kə]

  • n.
    • 驯养猎鹰的人；放鹰行猎者
    • 飞行中捕获猎物的飞虫(或鸟类)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

hawker [ 'hɔ:kə ]

  • n.
    • someone who travels about selling his wares (as on the streets or at carnivals)

      同义词：peddler | pedlar | packman | pitchman

    • a person who breeds and trains hawks and who follows the sport of falconry

      同义词：falconer

以上来源于: WordNet
