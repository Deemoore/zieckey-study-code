dissent [di'sent]

  • vi.
    • 意见分歧，(信仰、意见、感情等)不同；不同意，不一致；持异议，争执，唱反调(常与from连用)：

      • Three of the justices dissented from the majority decision.

        三名法官不同意大多数人的裁决。

      • She enjoys dissenting from us.

        她乐于保留与我们的不同意见。

    • 【宗教】(对国教、正教或其教义)表示不顺从，不信奉(英国)国教、正教或其教义或权威(from):

      • to dissent from the doctrines of an established church

        不遵从国教的教义

  • n.
    • 意见(或感情)的不同(或不一致、分歧)
    • [英国英语]【宗教】不顺从国教(或正教)
    • 【法律】(合议庭法官对多数法官作出的判决所提出的)不同意见，异议(=dissenting opinion)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dissent [ di'sent ]

  • n.
    • (law) the difference of one judge's opinion from that of the majority

      "he expressed his dissent in a contrary opinion"

    • a difference of opinion
    • the act of protesting; a public (often organized) manifestation of dissent

      同义词：protest | objection

  • v.
    • withhold assent

      "Several Republicans dissented"

    • express opposition through action or words

      "dissent to the laws of the country"

      同义词：protest | resist

    • be of different opinions

      同义词：disagree | differ | take issue
