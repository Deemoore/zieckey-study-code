adhere [əd'hiə]

  • vi.
    • 粘附，附着，胶着(to):

      • Wet clothes adhere to the skin.

        湿衣服贴身子。

      • The mud adhered to her shoes.

        泥粘附在她的鞋上。

    • 遵守，坚持；固执；墨守(to):

      • We adhered to our original plan of swimming in spite of the bad weather.

        尽管天气恶劣，我们仍然坚持按预定计划游泳。

      • to adhere to a plan

        按计划行事

    • 依附，追随，支持，拥护，忠于(思想、领导人等) (to):

      • to adhere to a leader

        追随某个领导人

      • to adhere to a party

        忠于某个党

    • [废语]前后一致
  • vt.
    • 使粘附：

      • Glue will adhere the tiles to the wallboard.

        胶水会把瓷砖粘在墙板上。

    • 变形：
    • adhered , adhering
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

adhere [ əd'hiə ]

  • v.
    • be compatible or in accordance with

      "You must adhere to the rules"

    • follow through or carry out a plan without deviation

      "They adhered to their plan"

    • come or be in close contact with; stick or hold together and resist separation

      同义词：cling | cleave | stick | cohere

    • be a devoted follower or supporter

      "The residents of this village adhered to Catholicism"

      同义词：stick

    • be loyal to

      同义词：stand by | stick by | stick

    • stick to firmly

      "Will this wallpaper adhere to the wall?"

      同义词：hold fast | bond | bind | stick | stick to
