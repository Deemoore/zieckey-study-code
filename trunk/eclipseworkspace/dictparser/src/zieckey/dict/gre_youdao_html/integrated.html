integrated ['intiɡreitid]

  • adj.
    • 完整的；整体的；完全的；综合的
    • (各组成部分)互相协调的；和谐的
    • [主美国英语]取消(种族)隔离的；平等对待的[参较 segregated]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

integrated [ 'intiɡreitid ]

  • adj.
    • formed or united into a whole

      同义词：incorporate | incorporated | merged | unified

    • formed into a whole or introduced into another entity

      "a more closely integrated economic and political system"; " an integrated Europe"

    • not segregated; designated as available to all races or groups

      "integrated schools"

    • resembling a living organism in organization or development

      "society as an integrated whole"

      同义词：structured

    • caused to combine or unite

      同义词：amalgamated | intermingled | mixed

以上来源于: WordNet
