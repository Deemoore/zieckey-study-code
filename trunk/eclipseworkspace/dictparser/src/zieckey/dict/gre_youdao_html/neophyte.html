neophyte ['ni:əufait]

  • n.
    • 新入教者；新受洗者
    • 新皈依者
    • 【罗马天主教】新受职的神父；见习修道士
    • 初学者，新手
    • [美国英语](兄弟会等中)已结束誓约期但尚未入会者
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

neophyte [ 'ni:əufait ]

  • n.
    • a plant that is found in an area where it had not been recorded previously
    • any new participant in some activity

      同义词：newcomer | fledgling | fledgeling | starter | freshman | newbie | entrant

    • a new convert being taught the principles of Christianity by a catechist

      同义词：catechumen

以上来源于: WordNet
