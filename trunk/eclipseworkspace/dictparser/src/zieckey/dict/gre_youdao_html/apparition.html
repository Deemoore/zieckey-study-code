apparition [,æpə'riʃən]

  • n.
    • 出乎意料(或离奇)地出现的东西；(尤指)幻象，幻影，怪影，妖怪，幽灵，鬼
    • 特异景象
    • (非凡的人或事、特异景象等的)出现，显露，显形
    • 【天文学】(彗星、行星等隐没后的)初现；出现期
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

apparition [ ,æpə'riʃən ]

  • n.
    • a ghostly appearing figure

      "we were unprepared for the apparition that confronted us"

      同义词：phantom | phantasm | phantasma | fantasm | specter | spectre

    • the appearance of a ghostlike figure

      "I was recalled to the present by the apparition of a frightening specter"

    • something existing in perception only

      "a ghostly apparition at midnight"

      同义词：phantom | phantasm | phantasma | fantasm | shadow

    • an act of appearing or becoming visible unexpectedly

      "natives were amazed at the apparition of this white stranger"

以上来源于: WordNet
