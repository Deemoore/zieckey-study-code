illustrious [i'lʌstriəs]

  • adj.
    • 卓越的，杰出的，著名的
    • (事迹等)辉煌的，灿烂的
    • [古语]发光的；有光泽的；明亮的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

illustrious [ i'lʌstriəs ]

  • adj.
    • widely known and esteemed

      "an illustrious judge"

      同义词：celebrated | famed | far-famed | famous | notable | noted | renowned

    • having or conferring glory

      "an illustrious achievement"

    • having or worthy of pride

      同义词：glorious | redoubtable | respected
