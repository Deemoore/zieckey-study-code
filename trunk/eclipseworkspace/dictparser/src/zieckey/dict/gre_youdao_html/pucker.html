pucker ['pʌkə]

  • vt.
    • 折叠；使起皱；使缩拢：

      • Worry puckered his brows.

        忧虑使他蹙额皱眉。

  • vi.
    • 起皱纹，缩拢：

      • Her little mouth puckered up and tears filled her eyes.

        她噘起小嘴，眼泪汪汪。

    • 折成褶：

      • The dress fitted badly and puckered at the waist.

        这件连衣裙不合身，腰部很皱。

  • n.
    • 皱纹；皱褶；襞
    • [古语]激动；不安；困惑
    • [美国俚语]双唇，嘴
  • 短语:
    • in a pucker [口语]慌张；紧张；激动
    • in puckers 起皱
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

pucker [ 'pʌkə ]

  • n. an irregular fold in an otherwise even surface (as in cloth)

    同义词：ruck

  • v.
    • to gather something into small wrinkles or folds

      "She puckered her lips"

      同义词：rumple | cockle | crumple | knit

    • draw fabric together and sew it tightly

      同义词：gather | tuck

    • become wrinkled or drawn together

      "her lips puckered"

      同义词：ruck | ruck up
