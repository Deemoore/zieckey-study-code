denounce [di'nauns]

  • vt.
    • 谴责，痛斥，指责，公然抨击：

      • She denounced his drinking.

        她指责他饮酒。

      • to denounce a politician as morally corrupt

        指责一政客道德败坏

      • The council denounced the new law.

        该委员会谴责新法律。

    • (如向警方或法庭)揭发，告发，控告，控诉：

      • She was denounced as a thief.

        她被告发为贼。

      • to denounce a person to the authorities

        向当局告发某人

      • to denounce someone to the police

        向警方告发某人

    • 正式通知废除(协定、契约、条约等)，声明…无效：

      • to denounce a treaty, pact, agreement, or the like

        通知废止条约、契约、协议等

    • [古语]宣布，宣称(尤指某事不详、邪恶)：

      • She denounced vengeance against him.

        她扬言要向他报仇。

    • [废语]预示[亦作denunciate]
    • 变形：
    • denounced , denouncing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

denounce [ di'nauns ]

  • v.
    • speak out against

      "He denounced the Nazis"

    • to accuse or condemn or openly or formally or brand as disgraceful

      "He denounced the government action"

      同义词：stigmatize | stigmatise | brand | mark

    • announce the termination of, as of treaties
    • give away information about somebody

      同义词：tell on | betray | give away | rat | grass | shit | shop | snitch | stag
