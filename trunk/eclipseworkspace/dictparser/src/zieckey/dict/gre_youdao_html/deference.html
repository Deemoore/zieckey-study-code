deference ['defərəns]

  • n.
    • 遵从，听从，依从，服从，顺从
    • 敬意，尊敬，敬重
  • 短语:
    • in deference to 遵从，服从，顺从，有鉴于，考虑
    • out of deference to 遵从，有鉴于，考虑
    • pay(或 show) deference to 对…表示敬意，尊重
    • with all deference to 尽管对…十分尊重(表示不同意见时的客气话)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

deference [ 'defərəns ]

  • n.
    • a courteous expression (by word or deed) of esteem or regard

      "his deference to her wishes was very flattering"

      同义词：respect

    • courteous regard for people's feelings

      "in deference to your wishes"

      同义词：respect | respectfulness

    • a disposition or tendency to yield to the will of others

      同义词：complaisance | compliance | compliancy | obligingness

以上来源于: WordNet
