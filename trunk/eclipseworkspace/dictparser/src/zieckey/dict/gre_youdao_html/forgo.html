forgo [fɔ:'ɡəu]

  • vt.
    • 弃绝，摒绝，放弃，抛弃；作罢，戒除；节制，抑制
    • [古语]离开；经过
    • [古语]忽略，疏忽[亦作 forego]
  • 近义词: relinquish
    • 变形：
    • forgowent , forgone , forgoing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

forgo [ fɔ:'ɡəu ]

  • v.
    • do without or cease to hold or adhere to

      同义词：waive | relinquish | foreswear | dispense with

    • be earlier in time; go back further

      同义词：predate | precede | forego | antecede | antedate

    • lose or lose the right to by some error, offense, or crime

      同义词：forfeit | give up | throw overboard | waive

    • refrain from consuming

      "You will have to forgo alcohol"

      同义词：give up

以上来源于: WordNet
