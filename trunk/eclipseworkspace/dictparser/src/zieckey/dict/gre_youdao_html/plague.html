plague [pleiɡ]

  • n.
    • 灾害；天灾；祸害
    • 瘟疫;鼠疫;流行性传染病 [参较 bubonic plague, pneumonic plague, septicemic plague]
    • 【圣经】上帝的惩罚；天谴
    • [口语]讨厌的东西(或人)；烦恼，苦恼
  • vt.
    • 传染瘟疫：

      • diseases that plague mankind

        传染人类的各种疾病

    • 使得灾祸：

      • Famine plagued a score of nations.

        二十来个国家遭受饥荒。

    • 使苦恼；折磨；烦扰：

      • He's been plaguing me with silly questions all day!

        他整天老是拿愚蠢的问题来烦我!

  • 近义词: annoy
  • 短语:
    • avoid someone (或something) like (或 as)the plague 极力回避某人(或某物)
    • black plague(或 death) 鼠疫，黑死病
    • Plague on him (或it等)! 遭瘟!该死!
    • plague the life out of someone 极力取笑(或折磨)某人
    • What the (或 a) plague! 怎么啦!究竟是怎么回事!
    • 变形：
    • plagued , plaguing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

plague [ pleiɡ ]

  • n.
    • a serious (sometimes fatal) infection of rodents caused by Yersinia pestis and accidentally transmitted to humans by the bite of a flea that has bitten an infected animal

      同义词：pestilence | pest | pestis

    • any epidemic disease with a high death rate

      同义词：pestilence | pest

    • a swarm of insects that attack plants

      "a plague of grasshoppers"

      同义词：infestation

    • any large scale calamity (especially when thought to be sent by God)
    • an annoyance

      "those children are a damn plague"

  • v.
    • cause to suffer a blight

      同义词：blight

    • annoy continually or chronically

      同义词：harass | hassle | harry | chivy | chivvy | chevy | chevvy | beset | molest | provoke
