fluctuate ['flʌktjueit]

  • vi.
    • 涨落，起伏，波动：

      • fluctuating prices

        波动的价格

      • The temperature fluctuates from day to day.

        温度天天在变化。

    • (状态、位置等)不断变化，动摇不定，不稳定：

      • He fluctuated between hopes and fears.

        他在希望和恐惧中徘徊。

  • vt.
    • 使波动；使涨落；使起伏；使不稳定；使变动
  • 近义词: swing
    • 变形：
    • fluctuated , fluctuating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fluctuate [ 'flʌktjueit ]

  • v.
    • cause to fluctuate or move in a wavelike pattern
    • move or sway in a rising and falling or wavelike pattern

      同义词：vacillate | waver

    • be unstable

      "The stock market fluctuates"
