compress [kəm'pres; 'kɔmpres]

  • vt.
    • 压紧；压缩；压榨：

      • Wood cuttings and sawdust can be compressed into boards.

        木头的碎渣和锯末可以压成木板。

    • 把…打成包，压捆：

      • Cotton has been compressed into bales.

        棉花已捆压成包。

  • n.
    • 【医学】压布，敷布
    • [美国英语](棉花等的)打包机；压缩机
  • 近义词: contract
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

compress [ kəm'pres, 'kɔmpres ]

  • n. a cloth pad or dressing (with or without medication) applied firmly to some part of the body (to relieve discomfort or reduce fever)
  • v.
    • make more compact by or as if by pressing

      "compress the data"

      同义词：compact | pack together

    • squeeze or press together

      "she compressed her lips"

      同义词：constrict | squeeze | compact | contract | press
