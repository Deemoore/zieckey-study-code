cavil ['kævil]

  • vi.
    • 挑剔，找岔，吹毛求疵 (常与 at， about 连用) ：

      • Although his composition was flawless, the teacher was always cavilling at his handwriting.

        虽然他的作文写得完美无瑕，但老师对他的书法总是百般挑剔。

  • vt.
    • [罕用语] 对…吹毛求疵
  • n.
    • 吹毛求疵；苛责；挑剔
    • 变形：
    • caviled , cavilled , caviling或-illing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cavil [ 'kævil ]

  • n. an evasion of the point of an argument by raising irrelevant distinctions or objections

    同义词：quibble | quiddity

  • v. raise trivial objections

    同义词：carp | chicane
