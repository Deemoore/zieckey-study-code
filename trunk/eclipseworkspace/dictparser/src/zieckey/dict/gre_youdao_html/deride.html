deride [di'raid]

  • vt. 取笑，嘲笑，愚弄，嘲弄：

    • They derided the new music.

      他们嘲笑新乐曲。

    • The children derided her for her fear of the snake.

      孩子们因她怕蛇而嘲笑她。

  • 近义词: ridicule
    • 变形：
    • derided , deriding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

deride [ di'raid ]

  • v. treat or speak of with contempt

    "He derided his student's attempt to solve the biggest problem in mathematics"

以上来源于: WordNet
