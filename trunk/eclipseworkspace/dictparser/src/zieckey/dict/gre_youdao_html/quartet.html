quartet, quartette [kwɔ:'tet]

  • n.
    • 四人一组；四件一套
    • 【音乐】
      • 四重唱；四重奏(曲)
      • 四重唱(或四重奏)演出小组
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

quartet [ kwɔ:'tet ]

  • n.
    • the cardinal number that is the sum of three and one

      同义词：four | 4 | IV | tetrad | quatern | quaternion | quaternary | quaternity | quadruplet | foursome | Little Joe

    • four performers or singers who perform together

      同义词：quartette

    • a set of four similar things considered as a unit

      同义词：quartette | quadruplet | quadruple

    • four people considered as a unit

      "he joined a barbershop quartet"

      同义词：quartette | foursome

    • a musical composition for four performers
