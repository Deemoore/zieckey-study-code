discourse ['diskɔ:s; dis'kɔ:s]

  • n.
    • 交谈，谈话；话语；言语
    • 演讲，演说；讲道；讲课；学术讲演
    • 论文；论述；论说；(口头或书面对某问题进行的)正式讨论
    • 【语言学】语段；话语，言谈；语篇，篇章
    • [废语]交谈能力；熟悉社交
    • [古语]推理；推理能力(或过程)
  • vi.
    • 谈话，交谈，谈论；交换意见：

      • They discoursed at the gate for a while.

        他们在大门口交谈了一会儿。

    • 讲述，论述；演讲；著述(on，upon):

      • The professor discoursed at length on how students from different language backgrounds make the same kinds of mistakes in the use of articles.

        该教授详尽讲述了来自不同语言背景的学生，怎样使用冠词时都犯同类的错误。

  • vt.
    • 说，说出，讲，讲述
    • 演奏出；发出(乐声或声音)：

      • to discourse musical sounds

        发出乐声

  • 近义词: speak
    • 变形：
    • discoursed , discoursing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

discourse [ 'diskɔ:s, dis'kɔ:s ]

  • n.
    • extended verbal expression in speech or writing
    • an address of a religious nature (usually delivered during a church service)

      同义词：sermon | preaching

    • an extended communication (often interactive) dealing with some particular topic

      同义词：discussion | treatment

  • v.
    • to consider or examine in speech or writing

      同义词：talk about | discuss

    • carry on a conversation

      同义词：converse

    • talk at length and formally about a topic

      同义词：hold forth | dissertate
