moult [ məult ]

  • n. periodic shedding of the cuticle in arthropods or the outer skin in reptiles

    同义词：molt | molting | moulting | ecdysis

  • v. cast off hair, skin, horn, or feathers

    同义词：shed | molt | exuviate | slough
