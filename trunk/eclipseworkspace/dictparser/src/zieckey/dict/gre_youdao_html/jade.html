jade [ dʒeid ]

  • n.
    • a semiprecious gemstone that takes a high polish; is usually green but sometimes whitish; consists of jadeite or nephrite

      同义词：jadestone

    • a woman adulterer

      同义词：adulteress | fornicatress | hussy | loose woman | slut | strumpet | trollop

    • a light green color varying from bluish green to yellowish green

      同义词：jade green

    • an old or over-worked horse

      同义词：hack | nag | plug

  • v.
    • get tired of something or somebody

      同义词：tire | pall | weary | fatigue

    • exhaust or get tired through overuse or great strain or stress

      同义词：tire | wear upon | tire out | wear | weary | wear out | outwear | wear down | fag out | fag | fatigue

  • adj. of something having the color of jade; especially varying from bluish green to yellowish green

    同义词：jade-green
