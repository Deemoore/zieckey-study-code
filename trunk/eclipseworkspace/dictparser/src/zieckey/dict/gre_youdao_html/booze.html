booze [bu:z]

    • [口语]
  • vi.
    • 狂饮，暴饮，痛饮，过量饮酒，贪杯：

      • They went out boozing late last night.

        他们昨晚出去狂饮至深夜。

  • n.
      • 酒
      • [美国英语]烈酒，威士忌酒
      • 狂饮，痛饮，暴饮
      • 酒宴
  • 短语:
    • be (或go)on the booze 痛饮，狂饮
    • booze it up 嗜酒成性
    • have a booze =be on the booze
    • 变形：
    • boozed , boozing

booze [ bu:z ]

  • n. an alcoholic beverage that is distilled rather than fermented

    同义词：liquor | spirits | hard drink | hard liquor | John Barleycorn | strong drink

  • v. consume alcohol

    同义词：drink | fuddle
