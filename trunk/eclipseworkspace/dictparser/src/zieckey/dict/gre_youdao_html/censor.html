censor ['sensə]

  • n.
    • (新闻、出版物、戏剧、电影、广播、电视节目等的)审查官员
    • (战时邮件等的)检查员；【军事】保密检查员
    • (英国某些大学的)学监
    • (古罗马负责调查人口、检查公共道德的)监察官
    • 【精神分析学】对潜意识的稽查
    • [废语]喜欢挑剔的人，爱吹毛求疵的人
  • vt.
    • 审查，检查(新闻、出版物、戏剧、电影、广播、电视节目等)：

      • All the letters from the war area were censored.

        所有从战区寄来的信件都经过了检查。

    • 修改；删掉：

      • The obscene part of the film should be censored.

        影片中的淫秽部分应予删除。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

censor [ 'sensə ]

  • n.
    • someone who censures or condemns
    • a person who is authorized to read publications or correspondence or to watch theatrical performances and suppress in whole or in part anything considered obscene or politically unacceptable
  • v.
    • forbid the public distribution of ( a movie or a newspaper)

      同义词：ban

    • subject to political, religious, or moral censorship

      "This magazine is censored by the government"
