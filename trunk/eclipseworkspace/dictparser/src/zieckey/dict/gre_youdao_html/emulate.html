emulate ['emjuleit; 'emjulit]

  • vt.
    • 努力赶上(或超过)；尤指竭力仿效(或模仿)以便赶上(或超过)：

      • to emulate one's father as a concert violinist

        作为一名能在音乐会上演奏的小提琴手胜过某人的父亲

    • (成功地)与…竞争，与…竞赛，与…相匹敌；(有着某种程度成功的)赛得过，比得上：

      • Some smaller cities now emulate the major capitals in their cultural offerings.

        某些小城市在提供文化设施方面如今比得上大都市。

    • 模仿，仿效：

      • a management system that has been emulated nationwide

        全国竞相仿效的管理体制

    • 【计算机】仿真，(使用仿真器)仿效(另一电脑系统)，模仿
  • adj.
    • [废语]好胜的；竞争心强的；有野心的 (= emulous)
    • 变形：
    • emulated , emulating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

emulate [ 'emjuleit, 'emjulit ]

  • v.
    • strive to equal or match, especially by imitating
    • imitate the function of (another system), as by modifying the hardware or the software
    • compete with successfully; approach or reach equality with

      "This artist's drawings cannot emulate his water colors"
