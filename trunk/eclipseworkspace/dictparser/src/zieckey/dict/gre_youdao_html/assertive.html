assertive [ə'sə:tiv]

  • adj.
    • 肯定的，断定的，断言的，武断的；过分自信的；有冲劲的；固执己见的
    • (酒等)有独特(或浓郁)香味的；有特殊风味的[亦作 assertative]
  • 近义词: aggressive
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

assertive [ ə'sə:tiv ]

  • adj.
    • inclined to bold and confident assertion; aggressively self-assured

      "an energetic assertive boy who was always ready to argue"; " pointing directly at a listener is an assertive act"

    • confidently aggressive

      "too assertive as a salesman"; " his self-assertive and unflagging energy"

      同义词：self-asserting | self-assertive
