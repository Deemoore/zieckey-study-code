gravity ['ɡræviti]

  • n.
    • 【物理学】重力，引力；万有引力，地球引力；比重；重力加速度 ( =acceleration of gravity)
    • 重，重量；具有重量
    • (泛指)吸引力，引力
    • 庄严，庄重，严肃；肃穆；危险(性)，威胁；凶兆，不祥，不吉
    • (形势等的)严重性，重要性；认真(或严肃)的态度
    • 【音乐】(音调的)低沉，低抑
    • [美国英语]格拉弗蒂(美国石油协会制定的)比重
  • adj.
    • 受重力(或引力)作用的；利用重力操作的
    • 变形：
    • gravities

gravity [ 'ɡræviti ]

  • n.
    • (physics) the force of attraction between all masses in the universe; especially the attraction of the earth's mass for bodies near its surface

      "the more remote the body the less the gravity"

      同义词：gravitation | gravitational attraction | gravitational force

    • a manner that is serious and solemn

      同义词：graveness | sobriety | soberness | somberness | sombreness

    • a solemn and dignified feeling

      同义词：solemnity
