joust [dʒaust]

  • n.
    • (手执长矛的骑士间的)比武；格斗
    • 比赛；竞争
  • vi.
    • 手执长矛骑马比武
    • 参加比赛；竞争[亦作 Just]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

joust [ dʒaust ]

  • n. a combat between two mounted knights tilting against each other with blunted lances

    同义词：tilt

  • v. joust against somebody in a tournament by fighting on horseback
