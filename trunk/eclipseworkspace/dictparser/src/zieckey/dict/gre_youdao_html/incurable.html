incurable [in'kjuərəbl]

  • adj.
    • 医治不好的；无法治愈的；不可救药的
    • 不能矫正的；无可救药的
  • n.
    • 患不治之症的病人
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incurable [ in'kjuərəbl ]

  • n. a person whose disease is incurable
  • adj.
    • incapable of being cured

      "an incurable disease"; " an incurable addiction to smoking"

    • unalterable in disposition or habits

      "an incurable optimist"

以上来源于: WordNet
