attest [ə'test]

  • vt.
    • 宣称…为真实(或真正)的；(证人)连署(或宣誓)以证明(遗嘱、行为、事件等)是真的；证明；证实；为…作证：

      • to attest the truth of a statement

        证明所述属实

    • (事物)作为…的证据(或证明)，(事物等)是…的证据，为…作证；论证；表明：

      • This attests her industry.

        这表明她的勤奋。

      • The man’s ability was attested by his rapid promotion.

        此人的能力由他升迁之快得到证实。

    • 使发誓，使宣誓，使立誓
    • [英国英语]使(新兵)宣誓入军籍
    • 证实(词语等)的使用
  • vi.
    • 证明，用书面形式证明；表明；证实；为…作证( to ):

      • to attest to the reliability of an employee

        表明一名雇员的可靠性

      • His success attests to his ability.

        他的成功证实了他的能力。

    • [英国英语](新兵)宣誓入军籍
  • n.
    • 证明，证实
    • 证据；证词；宣誓
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

attest [ ə'test ]

  • v.
    • provide evidence for; stand as proof of; show by one's behavior, attitude, or external attributes

      "His high fever attested to his illness"

      同义词：certify | manifest | demonstrate | evidence

    • authenticate, affirm to be true, genuine, or correct, as in an official capacity

      "I attest this signature"

    • give testimony in a court of law

      同义词：testify | take the stand | bear witness

    • establish or verify the usage of

      "This word is not attested until 1993"

以上来源于: WordNet
