madrigal ['mædriɡəl]

  • n.
    • 抒情短诗；情歌
    • (无伴奏的)重唱歌曲
    • 牧歌；歌曲；无伴奏的合唱歌曲
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

madrigal [ 'mædriɡəl ]

  • n. an unaccompanied partsong for 2 or 3 voices; follows a strict poetic form
  • v. sing madrigals

    "The group was madrigaling beautifully"
