kneel [ni:l]

  • vi.
    • (双腿或单腿)跪下(常与down连用):

      • to kneel down to pray

        跪下祈祷

    • 跪着：

      • to kneel in prayer

        跪着祷告

  • n.
    • 跪
    • 变形：
    • knelt , kneeling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

kneel [ ni:l ]

  • n. supporting yourself on your knees

    同义词：kneeling

  • v. rest one's weight on one's knees

    "In church you have to kneel during parts of the service"
