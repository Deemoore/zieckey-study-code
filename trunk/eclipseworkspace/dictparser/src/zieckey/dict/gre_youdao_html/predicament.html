    • =category
    • 情况，境况
    • 可断定的事物；(事物的)属性；[复数](亚里士多德逻辑学中的)十大范畴

predicament [ pri'dikəmənt ]

  • n. a situation from which extrication is difficult especially an unpleasant or trying one

    "finds himself in a most awkward predicament"

    同义词：quandary | plight

以上来源于: WordNet
