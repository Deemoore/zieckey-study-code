prostrate ['prɔstreit]

  • adj.
    • 俯卧的，卧倒的
    • 平卧的，卧倒在地的
    • 被征服的；降伏的；屈服的
    • (表示尊敬或顺从等)拜倒的；匍匐的
    • 衰竭的；筋疲力尽的；沮丧的
    • 【植物学】蔓延的；匍匐的，爬地的
  • vt.
    • 使倒匐；使平卧；弄倒；(风)吹倒，吹翻：

      • The trees were prostrated by the gales yesterday.

        昨天大风刮倒了树木。

    • 使匍匐；使屈从；使屈服：

      • They prostrated themselves before the king.

        他们拜倒在国王的脚下。

    • 使衰竭；使疲惫：

      • The competitors were prostrated by the heat.

        竞赛者们由于天气炎热而力不从心。

  • vi.
    • 俯卧；平卧
    • 俯伏，拜倒；屈服
  • 近义词: prone
    • 变形：
    • prostrated , prostrating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prostrate [ 'prɔstreit ]

  • v.
    • get into a prostrate position, as in submission

      同义词：bow down

    • render helpless or defenseless

      "They prostrated the enemy"

    • throw down flat, as on the ground

      "She prostrated herself with frustration"

  • adj.
    • stretched out and lying at full length along the ground

      同义词：flat

    • lying face downward

      同义词：prone
