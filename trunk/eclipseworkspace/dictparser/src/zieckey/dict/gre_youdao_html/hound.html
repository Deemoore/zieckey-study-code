hound1 [haund]

  • n.
    • 猎犬；(尤指)猎狐犬
    • [贬义]狗
    • [口语]卑鄙的人
    • [the hounds][英国英语]猎狐犬群，狐𤟥群
    • [the Hound][美国英语]“灰犬”长途公共汽车
    • (“犬兔赛跑”游戏中的)扮犬者
    • [口语]有…嗜好的人，…迷；收集成癖的人
    • [美国俚语](大学)新生
    • [美国俚语]伟人
    • [用复数][美国俚语]脚
  • vt.
    • (用猎犬)追猎(或追逐)；紧追，追踪；嗾使(猎犬)追逐(与at连用)：

      • I hounded a criminal to San Francisco.

        我追踪一名罪犯，一直追到旧金山。

      • to hound a dog at a fox

        嗾犬追逐狐狸

    • 追逼；烦扰；迫害:

      • He was hounded to the cliff.

        他被追逼到了悬崖边上。

      • He said he had been cruelly hounded in the concenration camp.

        他说他在集中营曾受到残酷的迫害。

    • 催促；嗾使，煽动(与on连用)：

      • Mam hounds me to get up early.

        妈妈叫我早点儿起床。

      • They were hounded on the foreign policy of the government.

        他们受到煽动攻击政府的对外政策。

  • 近义词: bait
  • 短语:
    • follow the hounds 骑马纵犬打猎
    • hold with the hare and hunt with the hounds 耍两面手法，两面讨好
    • hounds of law 缉捕吏

hound [ haund ]

  • n.
    • any of several breeds of dog used for hunting typically having large drooping ears

      同义词：hound dog

    • someone who is morally reprehensible

      同义词：cad | bounder | blackguard | dog | heel

  • v. pursue or chase relentlessly

    "the detectives hounded the suspect until they found the him"

    同义词：hunt | trace
