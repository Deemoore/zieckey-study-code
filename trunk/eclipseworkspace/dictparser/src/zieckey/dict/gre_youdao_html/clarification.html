clarification

  • n. clarify的变形

clarify ['klærifai]

  • vt.
    • 澄清；说明，阐明，讲清楚:

      • Can you clarify this long sentence?

        你能把这个长句子解释清楚吗?

      • He issued a statement to clarify the situation.

        他发表了一项声明以澄清形势。

    • 使净化，使(液体等)澄清:

      • You can clarify the water by adding a little alum.

        你在水里加一点明矾就能使水净化。

    • 使(头脑、神智等)清醒:

      • The cool autumn wind clarified my mind.

        凉爽的秋风使我的头脑清醒过来。

  • vi.
    • (液体)变得澄清，净化:

      • The cloudy solution clarified quickly.

        混浊的溶液很快就澄清了。

    • 变得清楚；清醒；

      • He left the matter to clarify gradually by itself.

        他任凭此事逐渐自行大白于天下。

      • His mind suddenly clarified.

        他的脑子突然清醒过来。

    • 变形：
    • clarified , clarifying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

  • n.
    • an interpretation that removes obstacles to understanding

      "the professor's clarification helped her to understand the textbook"

      同义词：elucidation | illumination

    • the act of removing solid particles from a liquid

      同义词：clearing
