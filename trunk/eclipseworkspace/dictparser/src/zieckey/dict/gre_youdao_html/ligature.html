ligature ['liɡə,tʃuə; -tʃə]

  • n.
    • 绑扎；结扎
    • 带子；绳索；绷带
    • 【外科学】缚线，结扎线，缝合线
    • 纽带；维系物；联结物
    • 【音乐】
      • 连线
      • 连音，集结音符
      • 簧片卡子
    • 【印刷】
      • 连字(如æ)
      • 连字弧线
  • vt.
    • 绑扎；结扎；联结：

      • to ligature the blood vessels

        结扎血管

    • 变形：
    • ligatured , ligaturing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

ligature [ 'liɡə,tʃuə, -tʃə ]

  • n.
    • (music) a group of notes connected by a slur
    • character consisting of two or more letters combined into one
    • a metal band used to attach a reed to the mouthpiece of a clarinet or saxophone
    • thread used by surgeons to bind a vessel (as to constrict the flow of blood)
    • something used to tie or bind

      同义词：binder

    • the act of tying or binding things together

      同义词：tying
