crinkle ['kriŋkl]

  • vi.,vt.
    • (使)起皱；(使)卷曲；(使)成波浪形：

      • Some paper is crinkled.

        有些纸被弄得起了皱纹。

    • (使)作沙沙声；(使)发瑟瑟声：

      • Paper crinkles when it is crushed.

        纸受压时瑟瑟做响。

  • n.
    • 皱；皱纹；卷曲，波纹
    • 沙沙声，瑟瑟声
    • 变形：
    • crinkled , crinkling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

crinkle [ 'kriŋkl ]

  • n. a slight depression in the smoothness of a surface

    同义词：wrinkle | furrow | crease | seam | line

  • v.
    • make wrinkles or creases into a smooth surface

      同义词：wrinkle | ruckle | crease | scrunch | scrunch up | crisp

    • become wrinkled or crumpled or creased

      同义词：rumple | crumple | wrinkle | crease
