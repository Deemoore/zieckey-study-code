outstrip [,aut'strip]

  • vt.
    • 超过；优于，胜过：

      • to outstrip in history

        擅长历史学

    • (在跑步中)跑在…的前面，追过
    • (在比赛或竞争中)胜过，领先于
    • [废语]经过(某地)
    • 变形：
    • outstripped , outstripping
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

outstrip [ ,aut'strip ]

  • v.
    • be or do something to a greater degree

      同义词：surpass | outmatch | outgo | exceed | outdo | surmount | outperform

    • go far ahead of

      同义词：outdistance | distance
