conscript [kən'skript]

  • vt.
    • 征召…(服兵役)，征募；强制征召(入伍)：

      • Even many young students were conscripted into the army.

        甚至很多年轻的学生也被征召到陆军中服兵役。

    • 征用(劳力、物资等)：

      • They proposed to conscript both capital and labour.

        他们打算把资金和劳力一起征用。

  • n.
    • 征召入伍的士兵
  • adj.
    • 应征入伍的；征召的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

conscript [ kən'skript ]

  • n. someone who is drafted into military service

    同义词：draftee | inductee

  • v. enroll into service compulsorily

    "The men were conscripted"
