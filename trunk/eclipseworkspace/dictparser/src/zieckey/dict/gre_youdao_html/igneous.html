igneous [ 'iɡniəs ]

  • adj.
    • produced under conditions involving intense heat

      "igneous rock is rock formed by solidification from a molten state"; " especially from molten magma"; " igneous fusion is fusion by heat alone"

      同义词：pyrogenic | pyrogenous

    • produced by the action of fire or intense heat

      "rocks formed by igneous agents"

      同义词：eruptive

    • like or suggestive of fire

      "an igneous desert atmosphere"

      同义词：fiery

以上来源于: WordNet
