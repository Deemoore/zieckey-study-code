pedigree ['pediɡri:]

  • n.
    • 家谱；谱系
    • (家畜的)种，纯种；品种形成
    • 家系，血统；门第
    • (语言的)起源，词源；(事物的)起源和历史
    • (有名望的)祖先
    • [美国俚语](警察局的)犯罪档案
  • adj.
    • 纯种的，有纯种系谱证明的
  • vt.
    • 建立纯种系谱而繁育
    • 为(动物)取得纯种系谱而证明
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

pedigree [ 'pediɡri: ]

  • n.
    • the descendants of one individual

      同义词：lineage | line | line of descent | descent | bloodline | blood line | blood | ancestry | origin | parentage | stemma | stock

    • line of descent of a purebred animal
    • ancestry of a purebred animal

      同义词：bloodline

  • adj. having a list of ancestors as proof of being a purebred animal

    同义词：pedigreed | pureblood | pureblooded | thoroughbred
