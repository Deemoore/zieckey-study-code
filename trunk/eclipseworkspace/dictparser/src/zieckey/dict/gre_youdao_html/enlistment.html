enlistment [in'listmənt]

  • n.
      • 征募，兵籍登记
      • 应募，应募入伍
    • 服(兵)役期
    • 谋求帮助 (或赞助、支持)的行为
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

enlistment [ in'listmənt ]

  • n.
    • a period of time spent in military service

      同义词：hitch | term of enlistment | tour of duty | duty tour | tour

    • the act of enlisting (as in a military service)
