  • n.
    • 【地理学】纬度
    • 纬度地区
    • 宽容度；活动余地；(言语、行动等的)回旋余地
    • =Astronomical Latitude,celestial latitude
    • [古语]范围[参较 Longitude]
  • 短语:
    • be allowed some latitude 被允许有些自由
    • understand something in its proper latitude 十分了解某事
    • wide latitude 广泛的行动自由

latitude [ 'lætitju:d ]

  • n.
    • the angular distance between an imaginary line around a heavenly body parallel to its equator and the equator itself
    • freedom from normal restraints in conduct

      "allowed his children considerable latitude in how they spent their money"

    • an imaginary line around the Earth parallel to the equator

      同义词：line of latitude | parallel of latitude | parallel

    • scope for freedom of e.g. action or thought; freedom from restriction
