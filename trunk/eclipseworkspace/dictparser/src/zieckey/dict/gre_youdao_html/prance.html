prance [prɑ:ns; præns]

  • vi.
    • (马)后足立地腾跃：

      • The horses pranced in the circus ring.

        马匹在马戏场上腾跳。

    • 乘马傲然前进；快活地走；欢跃：

      • They were prancing about to the music.

        他们跟着音乐欢蹦乱跳。

    • (人)跃马前进；乘腾跃之马：

      • The herdsmen pranced on the grasslands.

        牧民在草原上策马前进。

    • 昂首阔步；神气活现地走：

      • pranced out of the room in high dudgeon

        怒气冲冲地昂着头走出了房间

  • vt.
    • 使(马)腾跃
  • n.
    • 昂首阔步；欢跃
    • (马的)腾跃
    • 欢跃的舞蹈动作
    • 变形：
    • pranced , prancing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prance [ prɑ:ns, præns ]

  • n. a proud stiff pompous gait

    同义词：strut | swagger

  • v.
    • to walk with a lofty proud gait, often in an attempt to impress others

      同义词：tittup | swagger | ruffle | strut | sashay | cock

    • spring forward on the hind legs
    • cause (a horse) to bound spring forward
    • ride a horse such that it springs and bounds forward
