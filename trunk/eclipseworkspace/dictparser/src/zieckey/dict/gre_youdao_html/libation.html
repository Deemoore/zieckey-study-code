libation [lai'beiʃən]

  • n.
    • 奠酒(祭神仪式)，祭酒
    • 祭奠用的酒
    • [戏谑语]饮酒；(醉人的)酒
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

libation [ lai'beiʃən ]

  • n.
    • (facetious) a serving of an alcoholic beverage
    • a serving (of wine) poured out in honor of a deity
    • the act of pouring a liquid offering (especially wine) as a religious ceremony

以上来源于: WordNet
