impartial [ im'pɑ:ʃəl ]

  • adj.
    • showing lack of favoritism

      "the cold neutrality of an impartial judge"

      同义词：fair

    • free from undue bias or preconceived opinions

      "the impartial eye of a scientist"

      同义词：unprejudiced
