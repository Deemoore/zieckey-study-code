deviate ['di:vieit; 'di:viət]

  • vi.
    • (从航向、道路、通路)偏离，背离(正路、路线、方向、标准、主义等) (from):

      • The plane deviated from its normal flight path.

        飞机偏离了正常航线。

      • The witness deviated from the truth.

        证人偏离了事实真相。

    • (从行动方向、一般的规范)越轨，脱离常轨，越出正轨，出偏差(from):

      • Don't deviated from the rules.

        不要违反规定。

      • He never deviates from his regular habits.

        他从不改变自己通常的习惯。

    • 扯开，离题(from):

      • He deviated from the main theme.

        他偏离了主题。

  • vt.
    • 使偏离，使背离，脱离(常轨)(from):

      • He deviated her from the right path.

        他使她不走正道。

  • adj.
    • (行为等)脱离常轨的，越轨的，偏常的，异常的，不正常的；离经叛道的
  • n.
    • 脱离常轨的人(或物)
    • 不正常者，偏常者，(尤指)性变态者，性欲倒错者；离经叛道者
    • 【统计学】偏差，离差
  • 近义词: swerve , veer , diverge , digress
    • 变形：
    • deviated , deviating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

deviate [ 'di:vieit, 'di:viət ]

  • n. a person whose behavior deviates from what is acceptable especially in sexual behavior

    同义词：pervert | deviant | degenerate

  • v.
    • be at variance with; be out of line with

      同义词：vary | diverge | depart

    • turn aside; turn away from

      同义词：divert

    • cause to turn away from a previous or expected course

      "The river was deviated to prevent flooding"

  • adj. markedly different from an accepted norm

    同义词：aberrant | deviant
