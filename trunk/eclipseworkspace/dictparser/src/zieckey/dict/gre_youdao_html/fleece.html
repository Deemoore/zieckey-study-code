fleece ['fli:s]

  • n.
    • (羊或类似动物身上的)毛，羊毛
    • 从一只羊身上一次剪下的羊毛量
    • 羊毛状(覆盖)物(如羊毛状的头发)
    • 【纺织业】(用作衬里的)绒头织物
    • (绒头织物的)绒毛或绒面
  • vt.
    • 从(羊等)身上剪毛，剪下(羊等)的毛
    • 骗取…的钱财，诈取，骗取，抢夺，掠夺；剥削：

      • to fleece a man of his money

        骗取某人的钱财

    • (如用羊毛)盖满，(用羊毛状东西)点缀，使有斑点：

      • a sky fleeced with clouds

        白云朵朵的天空

    • 变形：
    • fleeced , fleecing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fleece [ 'fli:s ]

  • n.
    • the wool of a sheep or similar animal
    • tanned skin of a sheep with the fleece left on; used for clothing

      同义词：sheepskin

    • a soft bulky fabric with deep pile; used chiefly for clothing
    • outer coat of especially sheep and yaks

      同义词：wool

  • v.
    • rip off; ask an unreasonable price

      同义词：overcharge | soak | surcharge | gazump | plume | pluck | rob | hook

    • shear the wool from

      同义词：shear
