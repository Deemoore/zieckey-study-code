insert [in'sə:t; 'insə:t]

  • vt.
    • 插入；嵌入：

      • to insert a bookmark into a book

        把书签插入书中

    • 插(话)；添写，添加：

      • to insert a word in a line

        在一行中插入一个字

    • 登载，刊登(广告等)：

      • to insert an advertisement in a newspaper

        在报纸上登广告

    • (缝纫中)镶，补：

      • to insert lace on a cuff of a sleeve

        在袖口镶花边

    • 把(人造卫星等)射入(轨道)：

      • to insert a satellite into orbit

        把人造卫星射入轨道

  • vi.
    • 【解剖学】(肌肉等)附着
  • n.
    • 插入物；嵌入物
    • 插页；(夹在邮寄的书报刊中的)散页广告，插入广告；广告插页
    • (电影中的)插入镜头；插入画面
    • 镶嵌物
  • 近义词: introduce
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

insert [ in'sə:t, 'insə:t ]

  • n.
    • a folded section placed between the leaves of another publication
    • an artifact that is inserted or is to be inserted

      同义词：inset

    • (broadcasting) a local announcement inserted into a network program

      同义词：cut-in

    • (film) a still picture that is introduced and that interrupts the action of a film

      同义词：cut-in

  • v.
    • put or introduce into something

      "insert a picture into the text"

      同义词：infix | enter | introduce

    • introduce

      同义词：enclose | inclose | stick in | put in | introduce

    • fit snugly into

      "insert your ticket into the slot"

      同义词：tuck

    • insert casually

      同义词：slip in | stick in | sneak in

以上来源于: WordNet
