forbearing [fɔ:'bεəriŋ]

  • vt.
    • forbear1的变形
  • adj.
    • 宽容的，忍耐的；有耐心的

forbear1 ['fɔ:'bεə]

  • vt.
    • 克制，自制，抑制，节制，避免(或停止)(做、说等)：

      • He forbears to mention the matter again.

        他克制自己，不再提及此事。

    • [古语、方言]忍耐，忍受，容忍
    • [废语]避开；丧失
  • vi.
    • 克制，自制；避免：

      • to forbear from asking question

        避免发问

    • 容忍，忍耐；抑制
  • 短语:
    • bear and forbear [古语]一忍再忍
    • 变形：
    • forbore , forborne , forbearing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

forbearing [ fɔ:'bεəriŋ ]

  • adj. showing patient and unruffled self-control and restraint under adversity; slow to retaliate or express resentment

    "seemly and forbearing...yet strong enough to resist aggression"

    同义词：longanimous
