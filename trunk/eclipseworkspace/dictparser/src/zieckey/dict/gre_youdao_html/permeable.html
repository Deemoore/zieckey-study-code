permeable ['pə:miəbl]

  • adj.
    • 可渗透的；具渗透性的(尤指液体)
    • 不密封的；可穿过的；可透过的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

permeable [ 'pə:miəbl ]

  • adj. allowing fluids or gases to pass or diffuse through

    "permeable membranes"; " rock that is permeable by water"
