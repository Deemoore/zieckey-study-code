cede [si:d]

  • vt.
    • 放弃(权利)；割让；让步：

      • This island was ceded to Spain more than one hundred years ago.

        这个岛屿在一百多年以前就割让给西班牙了。

    • 转让；移交：

      • They have ceded their business to Brown Brothers.

        他们已将他们的商行转让给布朗兄弟公司。

    • 变形：
    • ceded , ceding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cede [ si:d ]

  • v.
    • give over; surrender or relinquish to the physical control of another

      同义词：concede | yield | grant

    • relinquish possession or control over

      同义词：surrender | deliver | give up
