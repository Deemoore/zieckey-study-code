impudent ['impjudənt]

  • adj.
    • 厚颜无耻的；冒失的，放肆的；无礼的
    • [废语]无耻的，不谦恭的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

impudent [ 'impjudənt ]

  • adj.
    • marked by casual disrespect

      "the student was kept in for impudent behavior"

      同义词：insolent | snotty-nosed | flip

    • improperly forward or bold

      "an impudent boy given to insulting strangers"

      同义词：fresh | impertinent | overbold | smart | saucy | sassy | wise

以上来源于: WordNet
