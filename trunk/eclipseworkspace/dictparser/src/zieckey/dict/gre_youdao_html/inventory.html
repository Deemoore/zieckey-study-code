inventory ['invəntəri; -tɔ:ri]

  • n.
    • 存货清单；盘存报表；财产目录
    • 库存，盘存，存货；盘存物品；存货总值
    • 细账；细目表；详细目录
    • 存货清单(或财产目录)的编制
    • 自然资源目录
    • 总结；概述
    • 个人品德(或技能)的鉴定记录
  • vt.
    • 编制…的目录；开列…的清单；盘存，清查：

      • The store inventories its stock once a month.

        该商店每月盘点一次存货。

    • 总结，概述：

      • a book that inventories the uses of the infinitives

        一本总结了不定式用法的书

  • vi.
    • 对清单上存货的估价，具有清单所示价值
  • 近义词: list
  • 短语:
    • take inventory
      • 清查存货；存货盘存
      • 调查(自然资源)
      • 评价；作鉴定
    • 变形：
    • inventories
    • inventoried , inventorying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

inventory [ 'invəntəri, -tɔ:ri ]

  • n.
    • a detailed list of all the items in stock

      同义词：stock list

    • the merchandise that a shop has on hand

      "they carried a vast inventory of hardware"; " they stopped selling in exact sizes in order to reduce inventory"

      同义词：stock

    • (accounting) the value of a firm's current assets including raw materials and work in progress and finished goods
    • a collection of resources

      同义词：armory | armoury

    • making an itemized list of merchandise or supplies on hand

      "an inventory may be necessary to see if anything is missing"; " they held an inventory every month"

      同义词：inventorying | stocktaking | stock-taking

  • v. make or include in an itemized record or report

    同义词：take stock | stock-take
