  • n.
    • 拨给；划归
    • 分配；配给，配置，分派，派定
    • 部署；定位置
    • 配给物，分配物；配给量，分配额；拨给数量；划拨的款项(或经费)；(经费、收入等的)分配法
    • [英国英语]【会计】分配
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

allocation [ ,æləu'keiʃən ]

  • n.
    • a share set aside for a specific purpose

      同义词：allotment

    • the act of distributing by allotting or apportioning; distribution according to a plan

      同义词：allotment | apportionment | apportioning | parceling | parcelling | assignation

    • (computer science) the assignment of particular areas of a magnetic disk to particular data or instructions

      同义词：storage allocation
