fractious ['frækʃəs]

  • adj.
    • 易(激)怒的，暴躁的，好争吵的，乖戾的
    • 难以驾驭的；不守规矩的；执拗的，倔强的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fractious [ 'frækʃəs ]

  • adj.
    • stubbornly resistant to authority or control

      "a fractious animal that would not submit to the harness"

      同义词：refractory | recalcitrant

    • easily irritated or annoyed

      "an incorrigibly fractious young man"

      同义词：cranky | irritable | nettlesome | peevish | peckish | pettish | petulant | scratchy | testy | tetchy | techy

    • unpredictably difficult in operation; likely to be troublesome

      "rockets were much too fractious to be tested near thickly populated areas"; " fractious components of a communication system"

以上来源于: WordNet
