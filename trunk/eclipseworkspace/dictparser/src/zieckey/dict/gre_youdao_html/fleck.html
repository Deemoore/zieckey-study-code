fleck [flek]

  • n.
    • (皮肤的)斑，斑点，雀斑，斑纹，(色、光的)斑点
    • 小片，薄片；粒子，微粒
  • vt.
    • 使…有斑点，使…有斑纹；点缀：

      • The bird's breast is flecked with brown.

        这只鸟胸前有棕色斑点。

      • a sky flecked with clouds

        布满点点云彩的天空

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fleck [ flek ]

  • n.
    • a small fragment of something broken off from the whole

      同义词：bit | chip | flake | scrap

    • a small contrasting part of something

      "a fleck of red"

      同义词：spot | speckle | dapple | patch | maculation

  • v. make a spot or mark onto

    同义词：spot | blob | blot
