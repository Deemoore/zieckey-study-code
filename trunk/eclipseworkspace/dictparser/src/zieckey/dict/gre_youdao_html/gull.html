gull [ ɡʌl ]

  • n.
    • a person who is gullible and easy to take advantage of

      同义词：chump | fool | mark | patsy | fall guy | sucker | soft touch | mug

    • mostly white aquatic bird having long pointed wings and short legs

      同义词：seagull | sea gull

  • v.
    • make a fool or dupe of

      同义词：fool | befool

    • fool or hoax

      同义词：dupe | slang | befool | cod | fool | put on | take in | put one over | put one across
