delineate [di'linieit]

  • vt.
    • 画出(或勾画出)…的轮廓，描绘出…的外廓，描出…的外形：

      • The teacher delineated China on the blackboard with a coloured chalk.

        老师用彩色粉笔在黑板上勾勒出中国的边界。

    • 描述，叙述：

      • In his speech he delineated the country plan with great care.

        他在讲话中精心描述了该国规划。

    • 刻画，描写：

      • The author delineated many characters in his story.

        作者在其故事中刻画了许多人物。

    • 变形：
    • delineated , delineating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

delineate [ di'linieit ]

  • v.
    • show the form or outline of

      同义词：define

    • determine the essential quality of

      同义词：specify | define | delimit | delimitate

    • trace the shape of

      同义词：limn | outline

    • make a mark or lines on a surface

      同义词：trace | draw | line | describe

    • describe in vivid detail
  • adj. represented accurately or precisely

    同义词：delineated | represented

以上来源于: WordNet
