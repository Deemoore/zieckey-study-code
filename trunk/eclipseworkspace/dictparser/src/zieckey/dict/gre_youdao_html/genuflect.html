genuflect ['dʒenju:flekt]

  • vi.
    • 屈膝，(宗教仪式中)跪拜：

      • to genuflect to the altar

        对着祭坛跪拜

    • 屈从，服从，顺从，卑躬屈膝
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

genuflect [ 'dʒenju:flekt ]

  • v.
    • bend the knees and bow in church or before a religious superior or image
    • bend the knees and bow in a servile manner

      同义词：scrape | kowtow
