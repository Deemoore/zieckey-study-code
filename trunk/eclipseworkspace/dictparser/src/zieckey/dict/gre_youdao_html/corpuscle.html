corpuscle ['kɔ:pʌsl]

  • n.
    • 【解剖学】细胞，(尤指)血球
    • 【生理学】小体
    • 【物理化学】微粒 [亦作 corpuscule]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

corpuscle [ 'kɔ:pʌsl ]

  • n.
    • (nontechnical usage) a tiny piece of anything

      同义词：atom | molecule | particle | mote | speck

    • either of two types of cells (erythrocytes and leukocytes) and sometimes including platelets

      同义词：blood cell | blood corpuscle
