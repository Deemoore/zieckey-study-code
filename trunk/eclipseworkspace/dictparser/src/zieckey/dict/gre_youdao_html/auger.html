auger ['ɔ:ɡə]

  • n.
    • 【木工】(钻木用的)螺旋钻，木螺钻
    • (钻地用的)土螺钻，地螺钻(＝ earth auger)
  • vt.
    • 用钻子钻洞于

auger [ 'ɔ:ɡə ]

  • n.
    • a long flexible steel coil for dislodging stoppages in curved pipes

      同义词：plumber's snake

    • hand tool for boring holes
