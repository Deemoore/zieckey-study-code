luxuriant [lʌɡ'zjuəriənt; -'ʒuə-]

  • adj.
    • 茂盛的，繁茂的；茂密的
    • (土地等)丰饶的；富饶的；肥沃的
    • 丰富的；过多的
    • (文体，装饰等)绚丽的；华丽的；华美的；奢华的
  • 近义词: profuse
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

luxuriant [ lʌɡ'zjuəriənt, -'ʒuə- ]

  • adj.
    • marked by complexity and richness of detail

      同义词：elaborate

    • displaying luxury and furnishing gratification to the senses

      同义词：epicurean | luxurious | sybaritic | voluptuary | voluptuous

    • produced or growing in extreme abundance

      同义词：exuberant | lush | profuse | riotous
