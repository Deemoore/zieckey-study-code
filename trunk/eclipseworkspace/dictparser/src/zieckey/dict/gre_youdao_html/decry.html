decry [di'krai]

  • vt.
    • 诋毁；贬低；公然非难，(公开)谴责，(正式)责难；(强烈)反对：

      • He decried the lack of support for the arts in this country.

        他谴责在这个国家里对艺术缺乏支持。

      • The king decried the lack of support he received from the people.

        国王公然非难他受人民支持不足。

    • 公开对(货币)贬值
    • 变形：
    • decried , decrying
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

decry [ di'krai ]

  • v. express strong disapproval of

    同义词：condemn | reprobate | objurgate | excoriate

以上来源于: WordNet
