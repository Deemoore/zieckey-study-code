excoriate [ek'skɔ:rieit]

  • vt.
    • 严厉指责，痛斥，痛骂，非难：

      • Suzane was excoriated for her mistakes.

        苏珊因犯了错误而被指责。

    • 擦破，抓破，擦伤…的皮肤等；剥去(皮)；磨损：

      • Her palms were excoriated by the hard labour of shoveling.

        她的手掌因繁重的挖土劳动而磨破了皮。

    • 变形：
    • excoriated , excoriating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

excoriate [ ek'skɔ:rieit ]

  • v.
    • express strong disapproval of

      同义词：condemn | reprobate | decry | objurgate

    • tear or wear off the skin or make sore by abrading

      同义词：chafe
