dislodge [dis'lɔdʒ]

  • vt.
    • (从特定位置上)强行取出(或去除、移开、移动)(from):

      • They needed a bulldozer to dislodge the rock.

        他们需要一台推土机来挖出岩石。

      • to dislodge a stone with one's foot

        用脚踢开石头

    • 把…赶出住处(或占有处、躲藏处)，从守地(或躲藏处、有利地形等)把…逐出(或驱逐出、逐退)(from):

      • We dislodged the enemy troops from their positions.

        我们把敌军逐出阵地。

  • vi.
    • 离开住处(或原处、躲藏处)(from):

      • We dislodged from our old house.

        我们搬出了旧房。

    • 变形：
    • dislodged , dislodging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dislodge [ dis'lɔdʒ ]

  • v.
    • remove or force out from a position

      "The dentist dislodged the piece of food that had been stuck under my gums"

      同义词：free

    • change place or direction

      同义词：shift | reposition

    • remove or force from a position of dwelling previously occupied

      "The new employee dislodged her by moving into her office space"

      同义词：displace | bump
