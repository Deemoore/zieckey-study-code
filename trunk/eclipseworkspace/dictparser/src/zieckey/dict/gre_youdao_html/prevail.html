prevail [pri'veil; pri:-]

  • vi.
    • 胜，胜过；优胜；获胜：

      • Justice has prevailed,the guilty man has been punished.

        正义已经获胜，有罪的人受到了惩罚。

    • 生效；有效：

      • The temptation to exploit consumers usually prevails unless it is curbed.

        剥削消费者的诱惑除非加以限制，否则就会得逞。

    • 占优势：

      • Truth will prevail.

        真理必胜。

    • 流行，盛行；普遍：

      • The use of horese for ploughing still prevails among the poorer farmers.

        用马耕作的现象在贫苦农民中仍然十分盛行。

    • (核战中)获胜
  • 近义词: persuade
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prevail [ pri'veil, pri:- ]

  • v.
    • be larger in number, quantity, power, status or importance

      同义词：predominate | dominate | rule | reign

    • be valid, applicable, or true

      同义词：hold | obtain

    • continue to exist

      同义词：persist | die hard | run | endure

    • prove superior

      "The champion prevailed, though it was a hard fight"

      同义词：triumph

    • use persuasion successfully

      "He prevailed upon her to visit his parents"

以上来源于: WordNet
