prompt [prɔmpt]

  • adj.
    • 敏捷的；迅速的；干脆的
    • 立刻行动的；果断的
    • 【商业】(付款)即时的，即期的，即付的；(货物)当场交的
    • [英国英语](演戏剧等中的)提词员的，提白员的；提词(用)的
    • 【计算机】提示信息的
  • adv.
    • [口语]准时，正(指时间)
  • vt.
    • 促使，推进；激励；鼓舞；怂恿：

      • What prompted her to be so generous?

        是什么原因使得她如此大方呢?

    • 激起，唤起(思想感情)，引起：

      • Her guestion was prompted by her worries about future.

        她提的那个问题是因为她对前途十分忧虑。

    • 鼓励对方继续说；为讲者提示：

      • to prompt a witness in court

        在法庭上提示证人

    • 给演员提词，提白：

      • The actor needed to be prompted frequently.

        那演员经常需要提词。

  • n.
    • 【商业】交割日期，付款期限；付款期限约定
    • (对演员的)提白；提词
    • 催促；激励；怂恿；提醒
    • = prompt-note
    • 【计算机】提示(屏幕上显示用户进一步操作的单词或符号)
  • 近义词: quick
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prompt [ prɔmpt ]

  • n.
    • a cue given to a performer (usually the beginning of the next line to be spoken)

      "the audience could hear his prompting"

      同义词：prompting

    • (computer science) a symbol that appears on the computer screen to indicate that the computer is ready to receive a command

      同义词：command prompt

  • v.
    • give an incentive for action

      同义词：motivate | actuate | propel | move | incite

    • serve as the inciting cause of

      "She prompted me to call my relatives"

      同义词：inspire | instigate

    • assist (somebody acting or reciting) by suggesting the next words of something forgotten or imperfectly learned

      同义词：remind | cue

  • adj.
    • according to schedule or without delay

      "a prompt reply"

      同义词：on time

    • characterized by speed and efficiency

      同义词：expeditious | timesaving

    • quick in apprehending or reacting

      "a prompt (or ready) response"; " a prompt smile"

    • performed with little or no delay

      "prompt obedience"

      同义词：immediate | quick | straightaway

以上来源于: WordNet
