recoup [ri'ku:p]

  • vt.
    • 弥补；补偿 ：

      • They are trying to recoup themselves for their losses.

        他们正在设法挽回他们的损失。

    • 重获；恢复：

      • He didn't recoup his health until recently.

        他最近才恢复健康。

    • 付还；偿还：

      • I will recoup you for any money you spend.

        你所用掉的钱我将如数付还。

    • 【法律】扣除；扣留(部分应付款)：

      • to recoup the cost of construction

        扣除建筑工程费用

  • vi.
    • 补偿损失：

      • It is impossible for me to recoup.

        叫我来弥补损失是不可能的。

    • 【法律】请示扣除，请求扣留
  • n.
    • 补偿；付还；扣除
  • 近义词: recover
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

recoup [ ri'ku:p ]

  • v.
    • reimburse or compensate (someone), as for a loss

      同义词：reimburse

    • regain or make up for

      同义词：recover | recuperate

    • retain and refrain from disbursing; of payments

      同义词：withhold | deduct

以上来源于: WordNet
