quixotic [kwik'sɔtik]

  • adj.
    • [常作 Q-](堂)吉诃德的；(堂)吉诃德式的；愚侠的
    • 空想的；不切实际的[亦作 Quixotical]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

quixotic [ kwik'sɔtik ]

  • adj. not sensible about practical matters; unrealistic

    "as quixotic as a restoration of medieval knighthood"

    同义词：romantic | wild-eyed

以上来源于: WordNet
