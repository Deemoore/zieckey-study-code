foment ['fəument]

  • vt.
    • 惹(麻烦)；煽动；挑拨：

      • to foment a riot

        煽动暴乱

    • 【医学】热敷，热罨；用药液、温水敷(患部)；用药液纱布或温湿布罨(患部)
  • 近义词: incite
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

foment [ 'fəument ]

  • v.
    • try to stir up public opinion

      同义词：agitate | stir up

    • bathe with warm water or medicated lotions

      "His legs should be fomented"
