hypocrisy [hi'pɔkrisi]

  • n.
    • 假装，矫饰；虚伪
    • 虚假的虔诚；伪善(行为)
    • 变形：
    • hypocrisies
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

hypocrisy [ hi'pɔkrisi ]

  • n.
    • an expression of agreement that is not supported by real conviction

      同义词：lip service

    • insincerity by virtue of pretending to have qualities or beliefs that you do not really have
