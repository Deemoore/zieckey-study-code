extrapolate [ek'stræpəleit]

  • vt.
    • 推断，推延，推定，推知，推测：

      • to extrapolate public opinion from the public's known reactions to other issues

        从公众对其他问题的已知反应来推测民意

    • 【统计学】外推，外插
    • 【数学】外推，用外推法求，推定诸元
  • vi.
    • 进行推断；进行外推，外推：

      • to extrapolate from today's information

        从今天的信息对未来进行推断

    • 变形：
    • extrapolated , extrapolating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

extrapolate [ ek'stræpəleit ]

  • v.
    • draw from specific cases for more general cases

      同义词：generalize | generalise | infer

    • estimate the value of

      同义词：interpolate

    • gain knowledge of (an area not known or experienced) by extrapolating
