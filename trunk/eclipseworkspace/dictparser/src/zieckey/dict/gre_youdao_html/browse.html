browse [brauz]

  • n.
    • (牲畜吃的)嫩叶，嫩枝，嫩草；青饲料
    • 放牧，吃嫩叶(或嫩枝、嫩草)
    • 浏览，粗看
  • vt.
    • (牛、鹿等牲畜)啃(吃)(嫩叶、嫩枝、嫩草等)；吃…上的草：

      • Two cows browsed the hillside.

        两头牛在山坡上吃草。

    • (牛、鹿等)放牧；用青饲料喂(牲畜)，喂(草食)
    • 随便翻阅，浏览(书刊)；随意观看：

      • I'm browsing the bookshelves for books to read.

        我正在书架上找书阅读。

    • 【计算机】(尤指在万维网上)浏览，查看网络信息：

      • to browse the Internet

        浏览互联网

      • I browsed the Web looking for interesting web sites.

        我在网上进行浏览，寻找有趣的网址。

  • vi.
    • (牛、鹿等牲畜)啃(吃)嫩叶(或嫩枝、嫩草等)，吃草，吃(on):

      • Three deers were browsing in the field.

        三只鹿正在原野上吃草。

      • Two cows were browsing in the meadow.

        两头牛正在牧场上吃草。

    • (在图书馆里)浏览书刊，随便翻阅：

      • I browsed through the library.

        我在图书馆里浏览书刊。

      • I just browsed through the paper.

        我刚才在浏览报纸。

    • (在商店里)随便看看，随意观看：

      • I browsed through the department store.

        我在百货商店里随意看看。

    • 变形：
    • browsed , browsing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

browse [ brauz ]

  • n.
    • reading superficially or at random

      同义词：browsing

    • the act of feeding by continual nibbling

      同义词：browsing

  • v.
    • shop around; not necessarily buying

      同义词：shop

    • feed as in a meadow or pasture

      同义词：crop | graze | range | pasture

    • look around casually and randomly, without seeking anything in particular

      "browse a computer directory"

      同义词：surf

    • eat lightly, try different dishes

      同义词：graze
