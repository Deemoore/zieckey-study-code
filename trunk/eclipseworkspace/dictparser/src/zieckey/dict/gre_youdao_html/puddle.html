puddle ['pʌdl]

  • n.
    • 水坑；(尤指)污水坑、洼、泥潭
    • (液体积成的)小坑；水潭
    • (粘土和沙捣成的)胶土
    • 【治金学】(金属的)熔融部分
    • (划桨激起的)漩涡
    • 冰上融水坑
    • (地下核爆炸形成的)放射性熔岩
    • [口语]撒下的一滩尿
    • (商品)分类目录
    • [俚语]混乱
  • vt.
    • 搅浑；混凝：

      • great pails of puddled mire

        大桶的淤泥

    • (把湿土和砂)捣成糊状：

      • wall constructed of straw and puddled clay

        由稻草和混合土构筑成的墙体

    • 用胶泥填塞；用泥浆填塞洞：

      • to puddle a canal

        用胶泥填沟

    • 使在…上布满水坑：

      • The meltwater puddled the flat sea ice.

        融化的冰水在平坦冰冻的海面上形成水坑。

    • 【冶金学】搅炼：

      • enabling the iron to be puddled into a bloom

        使铁熔炼成钢坯

    • 【农业】粘闭，搅糊：

      • Once soils are puddled badly, it may require several seasons to restore.

        一旦土壤板结粘闭，可能要几个季节才能得以恢复。

  • vi.
    • 搅泥浆：

      • The gravel was puddling with the mud.

        沙砾搅着泥浆。

    • 在污水(或浅水)中泼溅：

      • The children always puddle about after a rain.

        雨后孩子们常在水坑中泼水玩耍。

    • 瞎忙，瞎抓：

      • She puddled about at first one thing and another, never getting anything done.

        她东忙忙，西抓抓，一事无成。

    • 撒尿：

      • The baby alternately dozed and puddled.

        宝宝一会儿睡一会儿尿。

  • 短语:
    • Big puddle (或 Pond) [戏谑语]大池塘(大西洋的别名)
    • 变形：
    • puddled , puddling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

puddle [ 'pʌdl ]

  • n.
    • a mixture of wet clay and sand that can be used to line a pond and that is impervious to water when dry
    • a small body of standing water (rainwater) or other liquid

      "there were puddles of muddy water in the road after the rain"

      同义词：pool

    • something resembling a pool of liquid

      "his chair sat in a puddle of books and magazines"

      同义词：pool

  • v.
    • wade or dabble in a puddle

      "The ducks and geese puddled in the backyard"

    • subject to puddling or form by puddling

      "puddle iron"

    • dip into mud before planting

      "puddle young plants"

    • work a wet mixture, such as concrete or mud
    • mess around, as in a liquid or paste
    • make into a puddle

      "puddled mire"

      同义词：muddle

    • make a puddle by splashing water
    • mix up or confuse

      同义词：addle | muddle

    • eliminate urine

      同义词：make | urinate | piddle | micturate | piss | pee | pee-pee | make water | relieve oneself | take a leak | spend a penny | wee | wee-wee | pass water
