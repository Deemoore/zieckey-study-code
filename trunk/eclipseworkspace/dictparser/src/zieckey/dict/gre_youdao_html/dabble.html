dabble ['dæbl]

  • vi.
    • (尤指用手)玩水，嬉水，戏水：

      • Birds are dabbling in the water.

        鸟儿戏水。

      • Children like to dabble in water.

        孩子们喜欢玩水。

    • (如同玩水似的)拨弄：

      • to dabble with the coffee

        拨弄着咖啡

    • 草率(或轻率)地对待；涉猎，涉足，浅尝(at， in， with):

      • to dabble in literature

        涉猎文字

      • to dabble at painting

        涉猎绘画

      • to dabble with stocks

        经营一部分股票

    • (鸭)在浅水底用喙觅食：

      • to dabble amid the sedge

        (鸭)在河草丛中觅食

    • [古语]篡改(with):

      • to dabble with the text

        篡改原文

  • vt.
    • 把(手脚等)浸入水中，浸一下；用(手、脚等)嬉水：

      • to dabble one's hand in the water

        伸手玩水

    • 溅，使溅湿，洒(湿)，弄湿；沾，浸，蘸，涂抹：

      • Her shoes were dabbled with mud.

        她鞋上溅满泥浆。

      • He is dabbled in blood.

        他身上溅满了血。

    • [主美国中南部方言]漂洗掉，轻洗掉
    • 变形：
    • dabbled , dabbling
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

dabble [ 'dæbl ]

  • v.
    • dip a foot or hand briefly into a liquid
    • play in or as if in water, as of small children

      同义词：paddle | splash around

    • work with in an amateurish manner

      "She dabbles in astronomy"

      同义词：smatter | play around
