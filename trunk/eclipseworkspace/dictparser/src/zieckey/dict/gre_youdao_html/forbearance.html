forbearance [fɔ:bεərəns]

  • n.
    • 克制，自制，容忍，忍耐
    • 耐性；自制力；宽容
    • 【法律】(债权人权利的)延缓行使；(债务清偿期的)延展
  • 近义词: abstinence , tolerance , toleration , sufferance
  • 短语:
    • Forbearance is no acquittance [谚语]缓期不等于取消。
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

forbearance [ fɔ:bεərəns ]

  • n.
    • good-natured tolerance of delay or incompetence

      同义词：patience | longanimity

    • a delay in enforcing rights or claims or privileges; refraining from acting

      "his forbearance to reply was alarming"
