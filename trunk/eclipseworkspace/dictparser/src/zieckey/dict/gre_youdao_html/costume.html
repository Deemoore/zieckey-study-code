costume ['kɔstju:m; kɔ'stju:m]

  • n.
    • (某国、某阶层、某时期典型的)服装式样，装束；全套服饰
    • 化装用服装，戏装
    • 全套外衣，外套；女服，女套装
    • (某种场合穿的)成套服装
  • adj.
    • 戏装的；化妆穿戴的
  • vt.
    • 为…提供服装；给…穿衣：

      • The leading role in the film is costumed in medieval armor.

        影片中的主角穿着中古时代的甲胄。

    • 变形：
    • costumed , costuming
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

costume [ 'kɔstju:m, kɔ'stju:m ]

  • n.
    • the attire worn in a play or at a fancy dress ball

      "he won the prize for best costume"

    • unusual or period attire not characteristic of or appropriate to the time and place

      "in spite of the heat he insisted on his woolen costume"

    • the prevalent fashion of dress (including accessories and hair style as well as garments)
    • the attire characteristic of a country or a time or a social class

      "he wore his national costume"

  • v.
    • dress in a costume

      同义词：dress up

    • furnish with costumes; as for a film or play

以上来源于: WordNet
