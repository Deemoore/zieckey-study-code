impediment [im'pedimənt]

  • n.
    • 阻碍，妨碍；障碍物(常与to连用)
    • 口吃，结巴；残疾
    • [复数]-ments， menta ／-'mentə／【法律】法定婚姻的障碍；缔结合同的法定障碍
    • [古语][复数] = impedimenta
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

impediment [ im'pedimənt ]

  • n.
    • something immaterial that interferes with or delays action or progress

      同义词：hindrance | hinderance | deterrent | balk | baulk | check | handicap

    • any structure that makes progress difficult

      同义词：obstruction | obstructor | obstructer | impedimenta

以上来源于: WordNet
