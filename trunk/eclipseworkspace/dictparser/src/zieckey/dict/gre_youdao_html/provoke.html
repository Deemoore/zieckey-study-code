provoke [prəu'vəuk]

  • vt.
    • 对…挑衅；挑拨；煽动：

      • to provoke a viot

        煽动骚乱

    • 激怒；刺激：

      • I am not easily provoked , but his behaviour is intolerable!

        我这个人不易被激怒，但是他的行为让人忍无可忍!

    • 激起；引起；诱发；挑逗：

      • His remarks about her weight provoked her into telling him to shut up.

        他讲到她的体重，使得她叫他闭嘴。

    • 唤起；使发生；惹；成为…的原因：

      • Don't throw one bone to two dogs; you'll only provoke a fight.

        别把一根骨头扔给两只狗，那会引起它们打架。

  • 近义词: excite , stimulate
    • 变形：
    • provoked , provoking
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

provoke [ prəu'vəuk ]

  • v.
    • call forth (emotions, feelings, and responses)

      同义词：arouse | elicit | enkindle | kindle | evoke | fire | raise

    • evoke or provoke to appear or occur

      "Her behavior provoked a quarrel between the couple"

      同义词：evoke | call forth | kick up

    • provide the needed stimulus for

      同义词：stimulate

    • annoy continually or chronically

      同义词：harass | hassle | harry | chivy | chivvy | chevy | chevvy | beset | plague | molest
