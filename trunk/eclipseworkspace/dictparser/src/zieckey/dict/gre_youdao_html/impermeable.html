impermeable [im'pə:miəbl]

impermeable layer 不透水层；防渗层

impermeable barrier 不透水层；不透水煤柱；不渗透边界

impermeable [ im'pə:miəbl ]

  • adj. preventing especially liquids to pass or diffuse through

    "impermeable stone"; " an impermeable layer of scum"; " a coat impermeable to rain"
