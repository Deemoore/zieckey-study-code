compact1 [kəm'pækt; 'kɔmpækt]

  • adj.
    • 密集的；紧密的，致密的；严实的；坚固的
    • 挤满的，拥挤的
    • (各部分结构)致密的，小型的，小巧的，轻巧的
    • (体格)结实的；壮实的
    • 简明的；紧凑的；简练的
    • 由…组成的
    • [亦作 bicompact] 【数学】 紧(致)的
  • vt.
    • 把…压紧；束紧；压缩；使简练：

      • The snow on the pavement was compacted and has turned to ice.

        人行便道上的雪被踩实并结成了冰。

    • 由…组成；紧凑地组合成：

      • It is a mob compacted of violent elements of the underworld.

        那是一群由黑社会的凶悍分子组成的暴民。

  • vi.
    • 变得坚实：

      • The old snow has compacted into the hardness of ice.

        雪堆积日久而凝固如冰。

  • n.
    • (妇女用的)带镜小粉盒，小化妆盒
    • [亦称作 compact car] [美国英语]小型汽车
    • 【冶金学】坯块
  • 近义词: close

  • n. 条约；契约，合同；协议

compact [ kəm'pækt, 'kɔmpækt ]

  • n.
    • a small cosmetics case with a mirror; to be carried in a woman's purse

      同义词：powder compact

    • a signed written agreement between two or more parties (nations) to perform some action

      同义词：covenant | concordat

    • a small and economical car

      同义词：compact car

  • v.
    • have the property of being packable or of compacting easily

      "This powder compacts easily"

      同义词：pack

    • compress into a wad

      同义词：pack | bundle | wad

    • make more compact by or as if by pressing

      同义词：compress | pack together

    • squeeze or press together

      同义词：compress | constrict | squeeze | contract | press

  • adj.
    • closely and firmly united or packed together

      "compact soil"; " compact clusters of flowers"

    • having component parts closely crowded together

      "a compact shopping center"

      同义词：dense | thick

    • heavy and compact in form or stature

      "a wrestler of compact build"

      同义词：heavyset | stocky | thick | thickset

    • briefly giving the gist of something

      "a compact style is brief and pithy"

      同义词：compendious | succinct | summary

以上来源于: WordNet
