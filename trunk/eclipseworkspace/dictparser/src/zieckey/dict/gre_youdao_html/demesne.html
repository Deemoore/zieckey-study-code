demesne [di'mein]

  • n.
    • 【法律】(自己拥有所有权的土地的)占有，私有
    • (占有的)房地产
    • (庄园、房屋周围的)土地
    • 国土，领土
    • 地区，区域
    • 领域，范围
    • (旧时封建领主的)领地；地产
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

demesne [ di'mein ]

  • n.
    • extensive landed property (especially in the country) retained by the owner for his own use

      同义词：estate | land | landed estate | acres

    • territory over which rule or control is exercised

      同义词：domain | land
