diffusion [di'fju:ʒən]

  • n.
    • 扩散，散开；散播；弥漫
    • 【人类学、社会学】
      • (一种文化的要素特点向另一种文化的)传播，(文化、知识、消息等的)传播，散布，普及
      • (社会习俗、神话、技能等跨越文化群落的)传流，传播
    • (说话或文章等的)冗长，芜蔓，啰嗦
    • 【固体物理学】扩散；漫射
    • 【摄影术】(照片影像轮廓的)柔化
    • 【气象学】扩散；漫射
    • 【光学】漫散
    • 【电子学】扩散法
    • =diffusivity
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

diffusion [ di'fju:ʒən ]

  • n.
    • (physics) the process of diffusing; the intermingling of molecules in gases and liquids as a result of random thermal agitation
    • the spread of social institutions (and myths and skills) from one society to another
    • the property of being diffused or dispersed

      同义词：dissemination

    • the act of dispersing or diffusing something

      "the diffusion of knowledge"

      同义词：dispersion | dispersal | dissemination
