  • adj.
    • 连在一起的，联结的；连锁的
  • vt.
    • 连接，联结；连锁；把…连在一起
    • 变形：
    • concatenated , concatenating

concatenate [ kɔn'kætineit ]

  • v.
    • arranged into chained list
    • add by linking or joining so as to form a chain or series
