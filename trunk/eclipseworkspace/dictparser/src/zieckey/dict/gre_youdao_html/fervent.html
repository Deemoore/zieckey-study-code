fervent ['fə:vənt]

  • adj.
    • 热的，白热的；灼热的
    • 热烈的，满怀热情的，热心的，深表热诚的；强烈的
  • 近义词: fervid , warm , eager , earnest , zealous , impassioned , passionate
  • 反义词: apathetic
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

fervent [ 'fə:vənt ]

  • adj.
    • characterized by intense emotion

      "a fervent desire to change society"; " a fervent admirer"

      同义词：ardent | burning(a) | fervid | fiery | impassioned | perfervid | torrid

    • extremely hot

      "the fervent heat...merely communicated a genial warmth to their half-torpid systems"

      同义词：fervid
