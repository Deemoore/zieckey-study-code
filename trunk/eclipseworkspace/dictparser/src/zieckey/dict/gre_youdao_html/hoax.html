      • Don't hoax the new boy who's my nephew.

        别戏弄新来的小伙子啊，他是我外甥。

  • n.
    • 欺骗，愚弄，戏弄；恶作剧
    • 骗局，圈套

hoax [ həuks ]

  • n. something intended to deceive; deliberate trickery intended to gain an advantage

    同义词：fraud | fraudulence | dupery | humbug | put-on

  • v. subject to a playful hoax or joke

    同义词：pull someone's leg | play a joke on
