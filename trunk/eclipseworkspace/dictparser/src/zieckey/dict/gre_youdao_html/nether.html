nether [ 'neðə ]

  • adj.
    • lower

      "gnawed his nether lip"

    • dwelling beneath the surface of the earth

      "nether regions"

      同义词：chthonian | chthonic

    • located below or beneath something else

      "nether garments"

      同义词：under
