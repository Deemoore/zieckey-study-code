excise1 ['eksaiz; ek's-; ek'saiz]

  • n.
    • [亦作 excise tax] (烟、酒等的)消费税，国内货物(间接)税
    • 领许可证税，执照税
    • 赋税
    • 【英国历史】间接税务局(现并入关税与消费税局 Bureau of Customs and Excise)
  • vt.
    • 向(某人)强征消费税
    • 对(某货物)课以间接税(或消费税)
    • [英国方言、古语]对…乱要价，向…索高价
    • 变形：
    • excised , excising

excise2 [ek'saiz]

  • vt.
    • 删除，删去，勾掉(文、句、段落等)：

      • to excise a part of a book

        删去书的一部分

      • to excise a passage from the letter

        将信中一段删除

    • 【外科学】切除，切掉，切去，割掉，割去(肿瘤等)：

      • to excise the tonsils

        割去扁桃腺

    • 把…开槽，在…上开槽(或切口子)
    • 变形：
    • excised , excising
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

excise [ 'eksaiz, ek's-, ek'saiz ]

  • n. a tax that is measured by the amount of business done (not on property or income from real estate)

    同义词：excise tax

  • v.
    • remove by erasing or crossing out or as if by drawing a line

      同义词：strike | scratch | expunge

    • levy an excise tax on
    • remove by cutting

      "The surgeon excised the tumor"
