prate [preit]

  • vi.
    • 唠叨；喋喋不休地瞎聊；吹嘘：

      • Listen to him prating on about his wealth.

        听他没完没了地吹嘘自己有钱。

  • vt.
    • 瞎说；喋喋不休地瞎谈：

      • to prate nonsense

        胡说八道

  • n.
    • 唠叨；空谈；瞎扯
    • 变形：
    • prated , prating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prate [ preit ]

  • n. idle or foolish and irrelevant talk

    同义词：prattle | idle talk | blether | chin music

  • v. speak (about unimportant matters) rapidly and incessantly

    同义词：chatter | piffle | palaver | tittle-tattle | twaddle | clack | maunder | prattle | blab | gibber | tattle | blabber | gabble
