cronyism ['krəuniizəm]

  • n.
    • 友谊；交友愿望(或能力)
    • [美国英语]任用亲信，任人唯亲；袒护亲属，偏向亲友 [亦作 croneyism]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

cronyism [ 'krəuniizəm ]

  • n. favoritism shown to friends and associates (as by appointing them to positions without regard for their qualifications)

以上来源于: WordNet
