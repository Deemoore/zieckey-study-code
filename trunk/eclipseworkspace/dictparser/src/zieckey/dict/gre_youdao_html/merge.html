merge [mə:dʒ]

  • vt.
    • 使合并；使并入；使联合：

      • We can merge our two small businesses into a larger one.

        我们可以把我们的两家小商店合并为一家大商行。

      • The big company merged various small businesses.

        那家大公司兼并了多家小商行。

    • 使融合；使合为一体：

      • The colours of the evening sky slowly merged into blue.

        傍晚时天空的色彩慢慢融成一片蓝色。

      • The lights flickering ahead of her merged together.

        她前面的灯光融合到了一起。

    • 使渐渐消失：

      • to merge gradually

        逐渐消失

      • to merge imperceptibly

        不知不觉地消失

    • [废语]浸；使淹没
  • vi.
    • 合并；联合：

      • Our bank merged with theirs.

        我们的银行与他们的银行合并了。

      • The two firms have been merged.

        那两家公司已经合并。

    • 合为一体；结合；融合：

      • The two marching columns moved closer and finally merged.

        两支行进的行列靠得更近了，最终合二为一。

      • The two roads merged into one.

        那两条路汇合成一条路。

    • 渐渐变化；逐渐消失(into):

      • She merged into the crowd.

        她消失在人群中。

      • Twilight merged into total darkness.

        暮色渐渐变成漆黑。

    • [美国俚语]结婚
  • 近义词: mix
  • 短语:
    • merge together 混合起来；使混合起来
    • 变形：
    • merged , merging
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

      同义词：unify | unite

    • mix together different elements

      同义词：blend | flux | mix | conflate | commingle | immix | fuse | coalesce | meld | combine

      "We merged our resources"
