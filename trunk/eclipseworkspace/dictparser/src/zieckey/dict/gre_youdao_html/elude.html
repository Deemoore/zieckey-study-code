elude [i'lju:d]

  • vt.
    • (迅速、机敏、巧妙地)逃避，躲避，回避，规避，闪避，逃脱：

      • to elude capture

        逃避逮捕

      • The fox eluded the hunters by turning back quickly.

        狐狸急转身逃脱了猎人的追逐。

    • 避开(别人耳目)；避开(责任、困难、查问、危险等)：

      • to elude observation

        避人耳目

    • 不为…所记得(或注意、理解等)；使困惑；把…难倒：

      • Her name eludes me.

        她的名字我记不起来了。

  • 近义词: escape
    • 变形：
    • eluded , eluding
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

elude [ i'lju:d ]

  • v.
    • escape, either physically or mentally

      "The thief eluded the police"

      同义词：evade | bilk

    • be incomprehensible to; escape understanding by

      "What you are seeing in him eludes me"

      同义词：escape

    • avoid or try to avoid fulfilling, answering, or performing (duties, questions, or issues)

      同义词：hedge | fudge | evade | put off | circumvent | parry | skirt | dodge | duck | sidestep

以上来源于: WordNet
