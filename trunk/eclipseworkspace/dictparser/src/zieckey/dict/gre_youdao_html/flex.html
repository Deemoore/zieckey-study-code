flex ['fleks]

  • vt.
    • (手臂、膝关节等)屈曲；折曲，弯曲：

      • He flexed his stiff arm slowly.

        他慢慢地弯曲他那僵硬的手臂。

    • 使(肌肉)收缩
    • 折曲(地层等)
    • 【考古学】将(尸体)屈肢葬
  • vi.
    • 屈曲；折曲，弯曲；收缩
  • n.
    • 屈曲；折曲；弯曲；收缩
    • [英国英语]
      • 【电工学】(外有绝缘体的)电线，花线，皮线
      • [俚语]松紧带(如吊袜带)
  • adj.
    • 弹性工作制的
  • 短语:
    • flex one's muscles [俚语]显示力量[见 muscle]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flex [ 'fleks ]

  • n. the act of flexing

    "he gave his biceps a flex to impress the ladies"

  • v.
    • contract

      "flex a muscle"

    • exhibit the strength of

      "The victorious army flexes its invincibility"

    • form a curve

      同义词：bend

    • bend a joint

      "flex your wrists"

      同义词：bend

    • cause (a plastic object) to assume a crooked or angular form

      同义词：bend | deform | twist | turn
