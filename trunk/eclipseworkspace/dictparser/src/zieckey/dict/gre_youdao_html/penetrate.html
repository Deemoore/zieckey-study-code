penetrate ['penitreit]

  • vt.
    • 穿过；穿透；透过：

      • The nail easily penetrated the soft wood.

        钉子很容易地就穿透了软木材。

    • 刺入，刺透：

      • The knife penetrated enemy's stomach.

        刀子刺穿了敌人的肚子。

    • 弥漫于；扩散于：

      • The foul odor penetrated the entire house.

        难闻的臭气弥漫于整个房间。

    • 伸入，进入，打入：

      • The army penetrated the territory of enemy.

        部队进入敌人领土。

    • 渗透，浸入：

      • The heavy rain has penetrated the roof of my house.

        大雨渗透了我家的房顶。

    • 洞察，看破，看穿，识破：

      • This employee has penetrated his boss' s meaning.

        这位雇员已经看穿了他老板的意思。

    • (思想、感情等) 深入于；打动：

      • penetrated with discontent

        深为不满

    • 掌握；理解：

      • Were you able to penetrate the author's symbolism?

        你能理解作者的象征主义吗?

    • (性交时)把阴茎插入…的阴道
    • [美国俚语] (偷偷地或未经许可地) 破门而入
  • vi.
    • 穿入；穿透：

      • The nail will not penetrate.

        这枚钉子钉不进去。

    • 刺入，戳入，刺透：

      • The bayonet penetrates through the target.

        刺刀戳入靶子。

    • 延伸，进入，打进：

      • He has penetrated into high society.

        他已经踏进上层社会。

    • 洞察，看破，看穿，识破：

      • insight that penetrates to the very heart of the problem

        看穿问题的洞察力

    • 渗透，透过：

      • Rain has penetrated right through his coat.

        雨水湿透了他的衣衫。

    • (声音等)响亮，传很远：

      • Her voice doesn't penetrate.

        她的声音不太响亮。

    • 打动人心；(思想、感情等)深入人心：

      • Women's movement has not really penetrated here.

        妇女运动在这里还没有真正深入人心。

    • 被理解：

      • I heard what he said, but it didn't penetrate.

        我听他所言，但不明白说些什么。

    • 变形：
    • penetrated , penetrating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

penetrate [ 'penitreit ]

  • v.
    • pass into or through, often by overcoming resistance

      "The bullet penetrated her chest"

      同义词：perforate

    • come to understand

      同义词：fathom | bottom

    • become clear or enter one's consciousness or emotions

      "she was penetrated with sorrow"

      同义词：click | get through | dawn | come home | get across | sink in | fall into place

    • enter a group or organization in order to spy on the members

      同义词：infiltrate

    • make one's way deeper into or through

      "The hikers did not manage to penetrate the dense forest"

    • insert the penis into the vagina or anus of

      "Did the molester penetrate the child?"

    • spread or diffuse through

      "music penetrated the entire building"

      同义词：permeate | pervade | interpenetrate | diffuse | imbue | riddle
