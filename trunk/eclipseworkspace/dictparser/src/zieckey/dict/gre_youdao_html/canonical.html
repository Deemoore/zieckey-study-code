canonical [kə'nɔnikəl]

  • adj.
    • 教会法的；按照教会法的，以教会法规为准的
    • 有权威的；公认的；规范的，典范的；标准的
    • 属于(基督教)正典圣经的，正经的
    • 关于(天主教)教士的；关于(基督教)牧师的
    • 【音乐】轮唱的；轮唱曲的
    • 【数学】(方程式等)典型的；标准的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

canonical [ kə'nɔnikəl ]

  • adj.
    • appearing in a biblical canon

      "a canonical book of the Christian New Testament"

      同义词：canonic

    • of or relating to or required by canon law

      同义词：canonic

    • reduced to the simplest and most significant form possible without loss of generality

      "a canonical syllable pattern"

      同义词：basic | canonic

    • conforming to orthodox or recognized rules

      "the drinking of cocktails was as canonical a rite as the mixing"

      同义词：canonic | sanctioned
