notched [ nɔtʃt ]

  • adj.
    • notched like a saw with teeth pointing toward the apex

      同义词：serrate | serrated | saw-toothed | toothed

    • having an irregularly notched or toothed margin as though gnawed

      同义词：erose | jagged | jaggy | toothed
