flaccid ['flæksid]

  • adj.
    • (肌肉等)不结实的，松弛的，松软的
    • 无力的；软弱的，虚弱的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flaccid [ 'flæksid ]

  • adj.
    • lacking in strength or firmness or resilience

      "flaccid muscles"

      同义词：lax | limp | slack

    • drooping without elasticity; wanting in stiffness

      "a flaccid penis"

    • out of condition; not strong or robust; incapable of exertion or endurance

      "flaccid cheeks"

      同义词：soft | flabby
