indiscreet [,indis'kri:t]

  • adj.
    • 不谨慎的；不慎重的；轻率的
    • 不明智的；不策略的
    • 不加克制的
    • [苏格兰英语]无礼貌的

indiscreet [ ,indis'kri:t ]

  • adj. lacking discretion; injudicious

    "her behavior was indiscreet at the very best"
