garland ['ɡɑ:lənd]

  • n.
    • 花环，花冠；花彩，华饰
    • 诗歌集；文选集；戏曲集
    • 荣誉；奖品，锦标
    • 【船舶学】(系于帆杆的)索环，索眼；水手餐袋
    • 【采矿】井筒(壁上的)集水槽
    • 【滑雪】侧滑下坡
  • vt.
    • 把…做成花环(或花冠)
    • 用花环(或花冠、花彩)装饰；给…戴上花环：

      • They garlanded him with flowers.

        他们给他戴上了花环。

    • 给…荣誉
  • 短语:
    • carry (或carry away, gain, get, go away with, win) the garland 夺得锦标，夺标获胜

garland [ 'ɡɑ:lənd ]

  • n.
    • United States singer and film actress (1922-1969)

      同义词：Judy Garland

    • a city in northeastern Texas (suburb of Dallas)
    • an anthology of short literary pieces and poems and ballads etc.

      同义词：florilegium | miscellany

    • flower arrangement consisting of a circular band of foliage or flowers for ornamental purposes

      同义词：wreath | coronal | chaplet | lei

  • v. adorn with bands of flowers or leaves

    "They garlanded the statue"
