flock1 [flɔk]

  • n.
    • 畜群；兽群；鸟群(指在一起喂养或聚居的同种动物，如羊、山羊、绵羊等牲畜或飞禽)
    • 一大群人；一大批东西；一班学生；一家的子女
    • (对牧师而言)教会内的全体教徒
    • (对基督而言)全体基督教徒
    • [古语]一队人，一帮人，一伙人
  • vi.
    • 成群结队而行，群集；聚集；簇拥；蜂拥：

      • Students are flocking to such fields as genetics and molecular biology.

        大学生正在大量从事诸如遗传学和分子生物学等领域的研究。

      • Sheep usually flock together.

        羊通常是成群结队的。

  • 近义词: bevy , covey , flight , gaggle , brood , hatch , litter , shoal , school , swarm , group , company
  • 短语:
    • fire into the wrong flock [美国口语]打错目标；搞错，失误
    • flocks and herds 羊群和牛群，牧群

flock2 [flɔk]

  • n.
    • 毛束，棉束，棉丛
    • [有时作复数](填塞沙发垫、椅垫、褥垫、床垫等用的)毛屑，棉屑，布屑，废棉
    • [有时作复数](供植绒用的)短绒
    • = floc
  • vt.
    • 用毛(或棉)屑填塞(或填充)
    • 用短绒覆盖(或装饰)为…植绒
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flock [ flɔk ]

  • n.
    • a church congregation guided by a pastor
    • a group of birds
    • (often followed by `of') a large number or amount or extent

      同义词：batch | deal | good deal | great deal | hatful | heap | lot | mass | mess | mickle | mint | muckle | peck | pile | plenty | pot | quite a little | raft | sight | slew | spate | stack | tidy sum | wad | whole lot | whole slew

    • an orderly crowd

      同义词：troop

    • a group of sheep or goats

      同义词：fold

  • v.
    • move as a crowd or in a group

      "Tourists flocked to the shrine where the statue was said to have shed tears"

    • come together as in a cluster or flock

      同义词：cluster | constellate | clump
