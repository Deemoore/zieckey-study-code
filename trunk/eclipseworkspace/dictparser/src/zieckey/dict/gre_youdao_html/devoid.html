devoid [di'vɔid]

  • adj.
    • [通常作表语]完全没有的，毫无的，无…的，缺乏…的(of)
  • vt.
    • 消除(或剥去)…的某种特性(或实质)：

      • imprisonment that devoids a person of humanity

        剥夺某人人权的关押

  • 短语:
    • devoid of 缺乏…的，无…的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

devoid [ di'vɔid ]

  • adj.
    • completely lacking
    • completely wanting or lacking

      "a novel devoid of wit and inventiveness"

      同义词：barren | destitute | innocent
