  • n.

    • 【医学】 风疹(= German measles)
    • 家畜囊尾蚴病

measles [ 'mi:zlz ]

  • n. an acute and highly contagious viral disease marked by distinct red spots followed by a rash; occurs primarily in children

    同义词：rubeola | morbilli
