assimilate [ə'simileit]

  • vt.
    • 【生理学】吸收(食物)；消化：

      • What we eat is assimilated into our systems.

        人们吃的东西被吸收到身体里去。

    • 吸收(思想)：

      • We assimilated many new experience on our European trip.

        我们从欧洲之行中吸收 了许多新的经验。

    • 吸收(异种文化)，使(民族等)同化，(在习俗、观念、性格等方面)同化：

      • to assimilate the new immigrants

        使新移民同化

    • 把…比做，把…比拟，比喻(to， with):

      • to assimilate a conqueror to (或with) a robber

        把征服者比做强盗

    • 使相同，使相似，使类似(to，with):

      • Snow assimilates all objects.

        雪使万物均呈一片白色。

    • 使适应，使适合；使一致(to，with):

      • They assimilated their customs and behaviour to the new environment.

        他们改变了他们的风俗和行为以适应新环境。

    • 【语音学】 使(两个音)经历同化过程，使(语音)同化(to)
  • vi.
    • 成为相同(或相似)(to，with)
    • (食物等)被吸收；被消化：

      • Some kinds of food assimilate easily.

        某种食物易被吸收。

    • (在习俗、观念、性格等方面)被同化；适应(into，to，with):

      • The new arrivals assimilated easily and quickly.

        新来者很容易很快就适应了。

    • 【语音学】因同化而改变，被同化
  • n.
    • 被吸收物；被同化物
    • 变形：
    • assimilated , assimilating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

assimilate [ ə'simileit ]

  • v.
    • take up mentally

      同义词：absorb | ingest | take in

    • become similar to one's environment

      "Immigrants often want to assimilate quickly"

    • make similar

      "This country assimilates immigrants very quickly"

    • take (gas, light or heat) into a solution

      同义词：imbibe

    • become similar in sound

      "The nasal assimilates to the following consonant"
