endorsement [in'dɔ:smənt]

  • n.
      • (支票、汇票等)背书；票据签字
      • 签字转让
      • (商业文件、票据等的)签署
      • (文件等的)批注
      • 【保险业】(说明变更保险范围的)保险单附加条款 b)称赞(人或产品)的广告词
    • 批准，认可，赞成，同意，支持；担保
    • [主英国英语](在驾驶执照等上的)违章记录 [亦作 indorsement]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

endorsement [ in'dɔ:smənt ]

  • n.
    • a promotional statement (as found on the dust jackets of books)

      同义词：indorsement | blurb

    • a speech seconding a motion

      同义词：second | secondment | indorsement

    • formal and explicit approval

      "a Democrat usually gets the union's endorsement"

      同义词：sanction | countenance | indorsement | warrant | imprimatur

    • a signature that validates something

      "the cashier would not cash the check without an endorsement"

      同义词：indorsement

    • the act of endorsing

      "a star athlete can make a lot of money from endorsements"

      同义词：indorsement
