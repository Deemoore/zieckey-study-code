rejoicing [ri'dʒɔisiŋ]

  • vi.
    • rejoice的变形
  • n.
    • 欣喜，高兴
    • [常作复数]欢庆的场合；喜庆；欢宴

rejoice [ri'dʒɔis]

  • vi.
    • 欣喜，感到高兴(常与at，in或over连用)：

      • He often rejoices at other people's misfortunes.

        他常常对别人幸灾乐祸。

      • The ball fans rejoiced over the news of their team's victory.

        这些球迷听到他们球队胜利的消息而欢欣鼓舞。

  • vt.
    • 使…高兴，使…欣喜：

      • The admission notice he received from the university rejoiced his mother's heart.

        他收到的大学录取通知书使他的母亲心花怒放。

    • 变形：
    • rejoiced , rejoicing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

rejoicing [ ri'dʒɔisiŋ ]

  • n.
    • a feeling of great happiness
    • the utterance of sounds expressing great joy

      同义词：exultation | jubilation

  • adj. joyful and proud especially because of triumph or success

    "rejoicing crowds filled the streets on VJ Day"

    同义词：exultant | exulting | jubilant | prideful | triumphal | triumphant
