cuff1 [kʌf]

  • n.
    • 袖口
    • (裤脚的)翻边
    • (手套的)护腕
    • [复数]手铐
    • (血压计的)橡皮囊细带
    • 套箍
  • vt.
    • 为…装袖口；将裤腿翻边：

      • to cuff a pair of trousers

        给裤腿加翻边

    • 给…戴上手铐
    • [俚语]把香烟(或大麻烟)握藏在手心：

      • The student cuffed his joint so the teacher wouldn't see it.

        那名学生把大麻烟藏在手里以防被老师发现。

  • 短语:
    • off the cuff [美国俚语]即兴地，即席地，临时地；非正式地
    • on the cuff
      • 以赊账方式，赊欠地(的)
      • 免费地(的)
      • 私下；当场，即席
    • shoot one's cuffs 使衬衫袖口露在上衣袖口外面

cuff2 [kʌf]

  • vt.
    • 用巴掌打，掌击，掴
  • vi.
    • 打架，殴斗
  • n.
    • 掌击，一巴掌

cuff [ kʌf ]

  • n.
    • the lap consisting of a turned-back hem encircling the end of the sleeve or leg

      同义词：turnup

    • shackle that consists of a metal loop that can be locked around the wrist; usually used in pairs

      同义词：handcuff | handlock | manacle

  • v.
    • hit with the hand

      同义词：whomp

    • confine or restrain with or as if with manacles or handcuffs

      "The police handcuffed the suspect at the scene of the crime"

      同义词：manacle | handcuff
