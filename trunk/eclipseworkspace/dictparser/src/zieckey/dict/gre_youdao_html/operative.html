operative ['ɔpərətiv; -rei-]

  • adj.
    • 操作的；运转着的；从事生产劳动的
    • 实行中的；有效的
    • 有效果的；有效验的
    • 起作用的，产生影响的；实施的
    • 手术的
    • (词)关键的，最重要的
  • n.
    • 有经验的工人；技工
    • [美国口语]侦探；特务，间谍
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

operative [ 'ɔpərətiv, -rei- ]

  • n.
    • a person secretly employed in espionage for a government

      同义词：secret agent | intelligence officer | intelligence agent

    • someone who can be employed as a detective to collect information

      同义词：private detective | PI | private eye | private investigator | shamus | sherlock

  • adj.
    • being in force or having or exerting force

      "operative regulations"; " the major tendencies operative in the American political system"

    • of or relating to a surgical operation

      "operative surgery"

    • relating to or requiring or amenable to treatment by surgery especially as opposed to medicine

      "operative dentistry"

      同义词：surgical

    • effective; producing a desired effect

      "the operative word"

      同义词：key

    • (of e.g. a machine) performing or capable of performing

      同义词：running(a) | functional | working(a)

以上来源于: WordNet
