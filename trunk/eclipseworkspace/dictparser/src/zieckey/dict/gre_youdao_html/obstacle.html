obstacle ['ɔbstəkl]

  • n.
    • 障碍，妨碍
    • 障碍物，妨碍者
    • [英国英语](障碍赛马中的)栅栏；篱笆障碍
  • 近义词: impediment , obstruction , hindrance , barrier
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

obstacle [ 'ɔbstəkl ]

  • n.
    • something immaterial that stands in the way and must be circumvented or surmounted

      "lack of imagination is an obstacle to one's advancement"; " the poverty of a district is an obstacle to good education"

      同义词：obstruction

    • an obstruction that stands in the way (and must be removed or surmounted or circumvented)
