patent [ 'pætnt ]

  • n.
    • a document granting an inventor sole rights to an invention

      同义词：patent of invention

    • an official document granting a right or privilege

      同义词：letters patent

  • v.
    • obtain a patent for

      "Should I patent this invention?"

    • grant rights to; grant a patent for
    • make open to sight or notice

      "His behavior has patented an embarrassing fact about him"

  • adj.
    • (of a bodily tube or passageway) open; affording free passage

      "patent ductus arteriosus"

    • clearly revealed to the mind or the senses or judgment

      "patent advantages"

      同义词：apparent | evident | manifest | plain | unmistakable

百科

专利

专利（patent)是专利权的简称，从字面上讲，是指专有的利益和权利。一词来源于拉丁语Litterae patentes，意为公开的信件或公共文献，是中世纪的君主用来颁布某种特权的证明，后来指英国国王亲自签署的独占权利证书。英语“Patent”一词包括了“垄断”和“公开”两个方面的意思，与现代法律意义上的专利基本特征是吻合的。
