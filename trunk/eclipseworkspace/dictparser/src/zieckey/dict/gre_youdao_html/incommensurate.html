incommensurate ['inkə,menʃərət]

  • adj.
    • 不相称的；不适当的；不相对应的；不足的
    • 不能相比的；不能用同一标准衡量的(=incommensurable)

incommensurate [ 'inkə,menʃərət ]

  • adj. (often followed by `with') not corresponding in size or degree or extent

    "a reward incommensurate with his effort"
