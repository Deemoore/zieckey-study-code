rack1 [ræk]

  • n.
    • 挂物架；搁物架；架子
    • 文件架；病历架
    • (火车、飞机等座位上面的)行李架
    • 饲草架；牲口槽
    • 拉肢刑架
    • (身体或精神上的)巨大痛苦；痛苦的根源
    • (修理汽车用的)千斤顶
    • 【机械学】齿条；齿板；齿轨
    • 【印刷】字盘架
    • 【台球】(台球开局前的)三角形排列；(固定球位的)三角形框
    • 一对鹿角
    • 烤肉架
    • (在水中设置的)拦鱼网架
  • vt.
    • 将…放在架上：

      • He racked all the bottles of wine after dinner.

        饭后他将一瓶瓶酒都放在酒架上。

    • 将…放在拉肢刑架上施刑：

      • The traitor refused to confess and was racked at last.

        叛徒拒不招供，最后被拖到拉肢刑架上施刑。

    • 折磨，使痛苦：

      • High fever racked his whole body.

        发高烧使他浑身难受。

    • (以齿轮等)调准，定准:

      • He racked his camera and pressed down the shutter release.

        他将照相机的焦距调好，然后按下快门。

    • 榨取(尤指索取高额租金)
    • (皮革等的)上架处理
    • 【台球】(开局前)将球摆成三角形排列
    • 曲解，歪曲：

      • The author's meaning is so clear that there can be no room to rack the text.

        作者原意十分清楚，不可能对其文字进行曲解。

  • vi.
    • 扭曲；变歪斜
    • [苏格兰英语]拉伸
    • 定准，调准
  • 近义词: torment
  • 短语:
    • a rack of bones [美国口语]骨瘦如柴的人(或动物)
    • in a high rack [英国方言]身居高位的，身居要津的
    • off the rack (衣服)现成的
    • on the rack
      • 极感焦虑；心情紧张；焦虑不安；痛苦万分，(肉体上或精神上)受到极大痛苦，受到难以忍受的折磨
      • 处于非常紧张的状态中；处于持续不断的活动中；处于过度的使用中
    • put(或set)something on the rack 使受到严峻考验；使非常紧张；予以过度的使用
    • rack and manger
      • 富裕；奢侈
      • 毁灭
    • rack back [俚语]申斥
    • rack down 【摄影术】调低(画格)
    • stand the rack 受煎熬

rack [ ræk ]

  • n.
    • framework for holding objects
    • rib section of a forequarter of veal or pork or especially lamb or mutton
    • the destruction or collapse of something

      "wrack and ruin"

      同义词：wrack

    • an instrument of torture that stretches or disjoints or mutilates victims

      同义词：wheel

    • a support for displaying various articles

      "the newspapers were arranged on a rack"

      同义词：stand

    • a form of torture in which pain is inflicted by stretching the body
    • a rapid gait of a horse in which each foot strikes the ground separately

      同义词：single-foot

  • v.
    • go at a rack

      同义词：single-foot

    • stretch to the limits

      "rack one's brains"

    • put on a rack and pinion

      "rack a camera"

    • obtain by coercion or intimidation

      同义词：extort | squeeze | gouge | wring

    • run before a gale

      同义词：scud

    • fly in high wind
    • draw off from the lees

      "rack wine"

    • torment emotionally or mentally

      同义词：torment | torture | excruciate

    • work on a rack

      "rack leather"

    • seize together, as of parallel ropes of a tackle in order to prevent running through the block
    • torture on the rack
