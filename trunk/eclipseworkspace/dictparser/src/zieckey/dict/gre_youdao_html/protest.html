protest [prəu'test; 'prəu-]

  • vt.
    • [美国英语]抗议；反对；对…提出异议：

      • a large crowd protesting the war

        反对这场战争的一大群人

    • 明言；(坚决)表示；断言：

      • She protested that she had never seen the accused man before.

        她申明说她以前从未见过这个被指控的男子。

    • 【商业】拒付(票据)
    • [废语]公布，把…公诸于众
    • [废语] 严肃地保证
    • [废语]传…作证
  • vi.
    • 抗议；反对；提出异议：

      • She protested about the expense.

        她对这项支出强烈反对。

    • 明言；主张；断言：

      • You don't have to protest so much.

        你不必这样断言。

  • n.
    • 反对；抗议；异议；抗议书
    • 明言；郑重声明
    • 【法律】(公证人对汇票等的)拒付证书；船长证明书(证明船只损坏由灾难造成)；(缴付人对苛捐等的)抗议书
    • 【体育】(运动员向组织者提出的)抗议
  • adj.
    • 抗议性的；表示抗议的
  • 近义词: object
  • 短语:
    • enter (lodge 或 make) a protest (against) (对…)提出抗议(或异议)；反对
    • under protest 抗议着；持异议地；很不情愿地；极不乐意地；被迫地
    • without protest 无异议地；心甘情愿地；心平气和地
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

protest [ prəu'test, 'prəu- ]

  • n.
    • a formal and solemn declaration of objection

      "they finished the game under protest to the league president"; " the senator rose to register his protest"; " the many protestations did not stay the execution"

      同义词：protestation

    • the act of protesting; a public (often organized) manifestation of dissent

      同义词：objection | dissent

    • the act of making a strong public expression of disagreement and disapproval

      "he shouted his protests at the umpire"; " a shower of protest was heard from the rear of the hall"

  • v.
    • utter words of protest
    • express opposition through action or words

      同义词：resist | dissent

    • affirm or avow formally or solemnly

      "The suspect protested his innocence"

以上来源于: WordNet
