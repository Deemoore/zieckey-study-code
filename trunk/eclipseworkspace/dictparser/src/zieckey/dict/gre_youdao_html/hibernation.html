hibernation [,haibə'neiʃən]

  • n. hibernate的变形

hibernate ['haibəneit]

  • vi.
    • (动物等)冬眠，蛰居：

      • Black bears hibernate in winters.

        黑熊在冬季蛰居过冬。

    • (人等)避寒；过冬：

      • His family often hibernates to the South.

        他们家每到冬天都到南方去过冬。

    • 处于不活动状态；隐退 ：

      • He has hibernated for several years.

        他已经辞官退隐好多年了。

    • 潜伏不动：

      • You should hibernate before I contact with you.

        在我与你联系之前，你得潜伏不动。

    • 变形：
    • hibernated , hibernating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

hibernation [ ,haibə'neiʃən ]

  • n.
    • the torpid or resting state in which some animals pass the winter
    • cessation from or slowing of activity during the winter; especially slowing of metabolism in some animals
    • the act of retiring into inactivity

      "he emerged from his hibernation to make his first appearance in several years"

冬眠

冬眠也叫“冬蛰”。某些动物在冬季时生命活动处于极度降低的状态，是这些动物对冬季外界不良环境条件（如食物缺少、寒冷）的一种适应。熊、蝙蝠、刺猬、极地松鼠、青蛙、蛇等都有冬眠习惯。
