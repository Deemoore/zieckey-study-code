annotate ['ænəuteit]

  • vt.
    • 给(文学作品等)评注(或注解)，作注，注释：

      • Shakespeare's plays are often elaborately annotated to make them easier to understand.

        莎士比亚剧本往往注释详尽，便于理解。

      • to annotate the works of Dickens

        对狄更斯的作品作注释

  • vi.
    • 评注，作注解，注释:

      • Some people annotate as they read.

        有些人边读书边作评注。

    • 变形：
    • annotated , annotating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

annotate [ 'ænəuteit ]

  • v.
    • add explanatory notes to or supply with critical comments

      "The scholar annotated the early edition of a famous novel"

      同义词：footnote

    • provide interlinear explanations for words or phrases

      "He annotated on what his teacher had written"

      同义词：gloss | comment

以上来源于: WordNet
