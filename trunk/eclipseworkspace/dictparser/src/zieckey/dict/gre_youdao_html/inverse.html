inverse ['invə:s; in'v-; in'və:s]

  • adj.
    • 相反的；逆向的；倒转的；颠倒的
    • 【数学】反的，逆的[参较 direct]
  • n.
    • 相反；倒转；颠倒；反面
    • 相反的事物；颠倒的事物
    • 【数学】反数;倒数;逆无素[参见 inverse function]
  • vt.
    • 使倒转；使颠倒
    • 变形：
    • inversed , inversing

inverse [ 'invə:s, in'v-, in'və:s ]

  • n. something inverted in sequence or character or effect

    "when the direct approach failed he tried the inverse"

    同义词：opposite

  • adj.
    • reversed (turned backward) in order or nature or effect

      同义词：reverse

    • opposite in nature or effect or relation to another quantity

      "a term is in inverse proportion to another term if it increases (or decreases) as the other decreases (or increases)"
