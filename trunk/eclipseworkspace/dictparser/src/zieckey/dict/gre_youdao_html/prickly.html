prickly ['prikli]

  • adj.
    • 多刺的；满是针刺的
    • 针刺般痛的；刺痛的
    • (人)易生气的，易动怒的；敏感的
    • 棘手的，难办的，麻烦的
    • 变形：
    • pricklier-liest
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prickly [ 'prikli ]

  • adj.
    • very irritable

      "he became prickly and spiteful"

      同义词：bristly | splenetic | waspish

    • having or covered with protective barbs or quills or spines or thorns or setae etc.

      同义词：barbed | barbellate | briary | briery | bristled | bristly | burred | burry | setose | setaceous | spiny | thorny
