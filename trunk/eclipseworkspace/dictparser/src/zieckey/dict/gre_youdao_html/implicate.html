implicate ['implikeit]

  • vt.
    • 使卷入；使牵连其中；涉及：

      • He was implicated in a conspiracy.

        他卷入了一宗阴谋。

      • to implicate somebody in the robbery

        把某人牵连进抢劫案中

    • 暗示，暗指；含有…的意思：

      • His absence implicates disagreement.

        他的缺席意味着他不同意。

    • 影响[用于被动语态]：

      • His suggestion implicates my plan.

        他的建议影响了我的计划。

    • [罕用语]纠缠，纠结：

      • to implicate one's interest with those of others

        把自己的利益和别人的利益纠结在一起

    • 【逻辑学】蕴涵
    • 变形：
    • implicated , implicating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

implicate [ 'implikeit ]

  • v.
    • bring into intimate and incriminating connection

      "He is implicated in the scheme to defraud the government"

    • impose, involve, or imply as a necessary accompaniment or result

      同义词：entail
