guttural ['ɡʌtərəl]

  • adj.
    • 【解剖学】喉的，咽喉的
    • [口语](声音)由喉间发出的；粗哑的；喉中形成的
    • 【语音学】腭音的，喉音的
    • 严厉的，刺耳的
  • n.
    • 腭音，喉音
    • [罕用语]腭音字母；腭音符号
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

guttural [ 'ɡʌtərəl ]

  • n. a consonant articulated in the back of the mouth or throat

    同义词：guttural consonant | pharyngeal | pharyngeal consonant

  • adj.
    • like the sounds of frogs and crows

      "a guttural voice"; " acres of guttural frogs"

      同义词：croaking | croaky

    • relating to or articulated in the throat

      "the glottal stop and uvular `r' and `ch' in German `Bach' are guttural sounds"
