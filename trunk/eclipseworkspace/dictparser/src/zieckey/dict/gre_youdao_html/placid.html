placid ['plæsid]

  • adj. 平静的；安静的；温和的；宁静的
  • 近义词: calm , peaceful
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

placid [ 'plæsid ]

  • adj.
    • (of a body of water) free from disturbance by heavy waves

      "a ribbon of sand between the angry sea and the placid bay"

      同义词：quiet | still | tranquil | smooth | unruffled

    • not easily irritated

      "not everyone shared his placid temperament"; " remained placid despite the repeated delays"

      同义词：equable | even-tempered | good-tempered

    • living without undue worry; taking life easy

      "an air of placid sufficiency"

      同义词：easygoing | calm

以上来源于: WordNet
