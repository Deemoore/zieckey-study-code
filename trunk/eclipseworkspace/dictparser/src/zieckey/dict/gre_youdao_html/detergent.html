detergent [di'tə:dʒənt]

  • n.
    • (溶于水，有湿润和乳化作用的)清洗剂，洗涤剂，去垢剂
    • (用于润滑油、干洗剂等的)除垢剂，去污剂
    • (包括肥皂在内的)任何洗涤剂[参较anionic detergent,cationic detergent,synthetic detergent]
  • adj.
    • 使清洁的，使干净的，清洗的，去垢的，净化的[亦作detersive]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

  • n.
    • a surface-active chemical widely used in industry and laundering
    • a cleansing agent that differs from soap but can also emulsify oils and hold dirt in suspension
  • adj. having cleansing power
