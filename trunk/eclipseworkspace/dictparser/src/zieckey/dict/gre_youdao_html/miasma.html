miasma [mi'æzmə; mai-]

  • n.
    • 瘴气；臭气，烟雾弥漫的空气
    • 不良气氛(或影响)[亦作 miasm]
    • 变形：
    • miasmas , miasmata
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

miasma [ mi'æzmə, mai- ]

  • n.
    • an unwholesome atmosphere

      "the novel spun a miasma of death and decay"

      同义词：miasm

    • unhealthy vapors rising from the ground or other sources

      "the miasma of the marshes"; " a miasma of cigar smoke"

      同义词：miasm
