prevailing market price 现行市价

prevailing party 胜诉的一方

prevailing [pri'veiliŋ; pri:-]

  • adj.
    • 占优势的；有力的
    • 主要的；有影响的；显著的
    • 流行的，盛行的；普遍的
  • 近义词: current , prevalent
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

prevailing [ pri'veiliŋ, pri:- ]

  • adj.
    • most frequent or common

      "prevailing winds"

      同义词：predominant

    • encountered generally especially at the present time

      "the prevailing opinion was that a trade war could be averted"

      同义词：prevalent | rife
