derivative [di'rivətiv]

  • adj.
    • 被导出的，被引申出的；被诱导的；被推论出的
    • 衍生的，派生的
    • 非原始的，非原有的，次要的，第二手的；模仿别人的，缺乏独创性的
  • n.
    • 派生物；引出物；转成物
    • [亦称作derived form]【语言学】派生词
    • [亦称作differential guotient,[英国英语]differential coefficient]【数学】微商,导数[参较first derivative,second derivative]
    • 【化学】衍生物
    • 【精神分析学】诱导出[亦作derivate]
    • [用复数]金融衍生工具[1994]

derivative [ di'rivətiv ]

  • n.
    • the result of mathematical differentiation; the instantaneous change of one quantity relative to another; df(x)/dx

      同义词：derived function | differential coefficient | differential | first derivative

    • a financial instrument whose value is based on another security

      同义词：derivative instrument

    • (linguistics) a word that is derived from another word

      "`electricity' is a derivative of `electric'"

  • adj. resulting from or employing derivation

    "a derivative process"; " a highly derivative prose style"

以上来源于: WordNet
