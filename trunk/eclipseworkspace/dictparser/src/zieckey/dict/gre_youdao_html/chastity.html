    • 纯洁；端庄；高雅
    • 禁欲；节制
    • (文风等的)简洁，朴实无华

chastity [ 'tʃæstəti ]

  • n.
    • abstaining from sexual relations (as because of religious vows)

      同义词：celibacy | sexual abstention

    • morality with respect to sexual relations

      同义词：virtue | sexual morality
