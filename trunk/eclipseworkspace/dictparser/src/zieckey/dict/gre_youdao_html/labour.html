labour ['leibə]

  • n.
    • (体力或脑力)劳动；生产性活动
    • 工作，辛苦，劳累
    • 劳方，劳工
    • 工人阶级；劳动力
    • 劳动成果
    • 【医学】分娩；阵痛；剧痛
    • 【医学】分娩期；阵痛期
    • [L-]＝Labour Party
    • [复数] 世事，俗物
    • 【航海学】(船在海上的)颠簸；左右摇摆
  • vi.
    • 劳动，干活：

      • They labour assiduously for the benefit of future generations.

        他们为造福于后代而勤奋地劳动。

    • 努力(争取)；苦干：

      • to labour for the peace of the world

        为争取世界和平而努力

      • to labour at a mathematical problem

        努力解决一道数学题

    • 为…所苦恼；为…而吃苦头；为…迷惑，受…蒙蔽：

      • The couple have been labouring under housing difficulties for several years.

        这对夫妇几年来一直为住房困难所困扰。

    • 费力地缓缓前进；艰难地行进：

      • The climbers laboured up the hillside.

        爬山者吃力地缓缓爬上山坡。

    • 【航海学】(船只在海上)颠簸；左右摇摆：

      • The passengerliner laboured in the ruffled sea.

        客轮在波浪翻滚的海面上颠簸。

  • vt.
    • 过分花费精力于…；过于详尽地阐述：

      • There's no need for the manager to labour the point.

        经理没必要对这一点啰唆。

    • 使…辛勤地工作：

      • They seldom labour their wits on thankless task.

        他们很少为吃力不讨好的工作动脑筋。

    • 使…厌烦；麻烦；使…疲倦：

      • Don't labour me with trifles.

        别拿琐事烦我。

    • [古语]干活；耕作(土地等)
  • adj.
    • 劳工的；劳方的；工会的
    • [英国英语]工党的
  • 短语:
    • be in labour 在分娩中；在阵痛中
    • labour for breath 吃力地呼吸
    • labour in vain 徒劳[亦作lost labour]
    • Labour is light where love doth pay. [谚语]有爱为酬，干活轻松。
    • labour of Sisyphus 无休无止的艰苦劳动[亦作Sisyphean toil]
    • labour the point 详细说明；啰唆地说
    • to labour under a delusion (misapprehension, misunderstanding) 幻想，痴心妄想；因弄错而吃苦头；为误解所苦恼
    • surplus labour 【政治经济学】剩余劳动(力)
    • sweated labour 血汗劳动；工资低微的工人
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

labour [ 'leibə ]

  • n.
    • a social class comprising those who do manual labor or work for wages

      同义词：labor | working class | proletariat

    • concluding state of pregnancy; from the onset of labor to the birth of a child

      同义词：parturiency | labor | confinement | lying-in | travail | childbed

    • a political party formed in Great Britain in 1900; characterized by the promotion of labor's interests and the socialization of key industries

      同义词：Labour Party | Labor Party | Labor

    • productive work (especially physical work done for wages)

      同义词：labor | toil

  • v.
    • work hard

      同义词：labor | toil | fag | travail | grind | drudge | dig | moil

    • strive and make an effort to reach a goal

      同义词：tug | labor | push | drive

    • undergo the efforts of childbirth

      同义词：labor
