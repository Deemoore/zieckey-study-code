hard-nosed ['hɑ:dnəuzd]

  • adj.
    • (猎犬)嗅觉不灵敏的
    • [口语]倔强的，顽固的，固执的
    • [口语]切合实际的
    • [美国俚语]精明的，讲求实际的
  • n.
    • 重视实际的人[亦作hardnosed]

hard-nosed [ 'hɑ:dnəuzd ]

  • adj. guided by practical experience and observation rather than theory

    "a hard-nosed labor leader"

    同义词：hardheaded | practical | pragmatic
