expire [ik'spaiə; ek-]

  • vt.
    • 呼出(气体)，排出：

      • to expire air from the lungs

        从肺里呼出气

    • [古语] 发出，散发(蒸汽、气味等)
  • vi.
    • (法律、专利权、所有权等因到期而)成为无效，开始失效；(期限)终止，结束；期满，届期：

      • The lease expired.

        租借期限终止。

      • Now her passport has expired.

        现在她的护照已过期了。

    • 呼气，吐气：

      • The whales expired with a rushing sound.

        那些鲸鱼呼气时发出一种急促的声音。

    • (火等)熄灭：

      • The sparks expired in the ashes.

        火星在灰烬中熄灭。

    • 死亡，断气：

      • The old woman expired in agony.

        老妇在极度痛苦中断气。

  • 近义词: die
    • 变形：
    • expired , expiring
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

expire [ ik'spaiə, ek- ]

  • v.
    • lose validity

      "My passports expired last month"

      同义词：run out

    • pass from physical life and lose all bodily attributes and functions necessary to sustain life

      同义词：die | decease | perish | go | exit | pass away | pass | kick the bucket | cash in one's chips | buy the farm | conk | give-up the ghost | drop dead | pop off | choke | croak | snuff it

    • expel air

      同义词：exhale | breathe out
