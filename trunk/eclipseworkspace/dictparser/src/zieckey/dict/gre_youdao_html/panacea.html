    • 能解决一切问题、对付一切困难的万能措施

  • n. 帕那刻亚(古希腊医药女神)

panacea [ ,pænə'si:ə ]

  • n.
    • hypothetical remedy for all ills or diseases; once sought by the alchemists

      同义词：nostrum | catholicon | cure-all

    • (Greek mythology) the goddess of healing; daughter of Aesculapius and sister of Hygeia
