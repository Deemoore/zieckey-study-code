clumsy ['klʌmzi]

  • adj.
    • 笨拙的，笨手笨脚的，不灵便的
    • 制作粗陋的；样子不好看的
    • 笨重的
    • 愚笨的；不圆滑的
  • 近义词: awkward
    • 变形：
    • clumsier , clumsiest
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

clumsy [ 'klʌmzi ]

  • adj.
    • lacking grace in movement or posture

      "clumsy fingers"

      同义词：gawky | clunky | ungainly | unwieldy

    • not elegant or graceful in expression

      "a clumsy apology"

      同义词：awkward | cumbersome | inapt | inept | ill-chosen

    • difficult to handle or manage especially because of shape

      "clumsy wooden shoes"

      同义词：awkward | bunglesome | ungainly

    • showing lack of skill or aptitude

      "did a clumsy job"

      同义词：bungling | fumbling | incompetent

以上来源于: WordNet
