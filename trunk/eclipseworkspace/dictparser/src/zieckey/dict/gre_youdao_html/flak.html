flak [fleik]

  • n.
    • 高射炮；高射炮火
    • [美国口语](互相)指责，攻讦，批评，责备，非难
    • [美国口语]；互相批评；激烈的争论；热烈的讨论[亦作flack]
    • 雇主形象的保护(或庇护)者；(起保护雇主作用的)挡箭牌；(雇主与公众之间矛盾的)调解人，说客 [亦作flak catcher]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

flak [ fleik ]

  • n.
    • a slick spokesperson who can turn any criticism to the advantage of their employer

      同义词：flak catcher | flack catcher | flack

    • intense adverse criticism

      "don't give me any flak"

      同义词：fire | attack | flack | blast

    • artillery designed to shoot upward at airplanes

      同义词：antiaircraft | antiaircraft gun | flack | pom-pom | ack-ack | ack-ack gun
