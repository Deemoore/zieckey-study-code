detonate ['detəneit]

  • vi.
    • 猛烈爆炸，起爆，爆破，爆发，炸裂：

      • The terrorists were killed when their bomb detonated unexpectedly.

        那些恐怖分子的炸弹意外爆炸时他们被炸死了。

  • vt.
    • 引爆，起爆，使(炸弹、炸药等)爆炸：

      • A remote control device was used to detonate the bomb.

        用遥控装置引爆炸弹。

      • Workers detonated the charges that collapsed the building.

        工人们引爆了爆破大楼的炸药。

    • 引起，激起，激发，触发(连串事件)
    • 变形：
    • detonated , detonating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

detonate [ 'detəneit ]

  • v.
    • cause to burst with a violent release of energy

      同义词：explode | blow up | set off

    • burst and release energy as through a violent chemical or physical reaction

      "the bomb detonated at noon"

      同义词：explode | blow up
