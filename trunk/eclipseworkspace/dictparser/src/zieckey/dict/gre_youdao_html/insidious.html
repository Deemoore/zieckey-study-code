insidious [in'sidiəs]

  • adj.
    • 阴险的，狡诈的；诡计多端的；暗中为害的
    • 伺机欺骗的；伺机陷害的；有毒而诱人的
    • (疾病)不知不觉间加剧的；隐伏的；潜伏的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

insidious [ in'sidiəs ]

  • adj.
    • beguiling but harmful

      "insidious pleasures"

    • intended to entrap
    • working or spreading in a hidden and usually injurious way

      "glaucoma is an insidious disease"

      同义词：pernicious | subtle

以上来源于: WordNet
