humour ['hju:mə]

  • n.
    • 幽默，诙谐，滑稽
    • 幽默感
    • 幽默的文章(或言词、谈吐等)；令人发笑的场景(或事物)
    • [用复数]有趣(或滑稽)的特性
    • 脾气，气质，性情
    • 心情，心境；情绪
    • 古怪念头，想入非非；遐想
    • 【生物学】液，体液；汁液
    • [古语] 【生理学】(中世纪医学认为的)体液
    • 【病理学】(因血热等引起的)慢性(或顽固性)皮肤病
    • [废语]潮气；蒸汽
  • vt.
    • 迎合，迁就，纵容：

      • You shouldn't write such obscene things to humour their dirty interests.

        你可不应该为迎合某些人的低级趣味而写这些乌七八糟的东西。

    • 使自己适应(某事物)；顺着…的自然情势行事：

      • You must humour her pleasure to talk with her in order to win her favour.

        为了博得她的欢心，你得顺着她的话说。

  • 近义词: indulge , mood
  • 短语:
    • in(a) good humour 心情好，高兴
    • out of humour 心情不好，不高兴
    • sense of humour 幽默感
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

humour [ 'hju:mə ]

  • n.
    • a characteristic (habitual or relatively temporary) state of feeling

      同义词：temper | mood | humor

    • a message whose ingenuity or verbal skill or incongruity has the power to evoke laughter

      同义词：wit | humor | witticism | wittiness

    • (Middle Ages) one of the four fluids in the body whose balance was believed to determine your emotional and physical state

      同义词：humor

    • the liquid parts of the body

      同义词：liquid body substance | bodily fluid | body fluid | humor

    • the quality of being funny

      同义词：humor

    • the trait of appreciating (and being able to express) the humorous

      同义词：humor | sense of humor | sense of humour

  • v. put into a good mood

    同义词：humor
