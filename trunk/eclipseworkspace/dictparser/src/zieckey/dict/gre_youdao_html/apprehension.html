apprehension [,æpri'henʃən]

  • n.
    • 逮捕，拘押；捕获，拿获
    • 理解，领会，领悟，颖悟；理解力
    • 看法，意见，主张，见解
    • (对行将出现的危险、灾祸等的)忧虑，挂念，担心；畏惧，恐惧，恐怖，疑惧
  • 短语:
    • be under the apprehension(that) 惟恐，担心
    • entertain(或have) apprehension(或apprehensions) of (或for) 对…有些担心
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

apprehension [ ,æpri'henʃən ]

  • n.
    • fearful expectation or anticipation

      "the student looked around the examination room with apprehension"

      同义词：apprehensiveness | dread

    • the cognitive condition of someone who understands

      同义词：understanding | discernment | savvy

    • painful expectation

      同义词：misgiving

    • the act of apprehending (especially apprehending a criminal)

      同义词：arrest | catch | collar | pinch | taking into custody
