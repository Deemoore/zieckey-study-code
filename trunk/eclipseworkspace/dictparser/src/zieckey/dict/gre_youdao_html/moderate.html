moderate ['mɔdərət; 'mɔdəreit]

  • adj.
    • 适度的，有节制的，不过分的，不大不小的
    • 温和的，稳健的，不激进的
    • 中等的；普通的，一般的；平庸的
    • (价格)适中的，公道的，公平的
    • (颜色)中等深浅的，中等色度的
    • 有限的，不大的
  • n.
    • 温和主义者，持温和观点的人，稳健的人
    • [亦作 M-](政党中的)稳健派，温和派
  • vt.
    • 使和缓，减轻，减弱；节制：

      • She should moderate her language when children are present.

        在孩子面前，她说话应该节制。

      • His fury moderated when he learned why she had done it.

        当他知道了她那样做的原因时，他的火气就消了。

    • 主持(会议)
    • 【核物理学】使(中子)减速；慢化(反应堆)；给反应堆添加缓和剂
  • vi.
    • 变弱；变温和，变得和缓：

      • The adjustment of the interest rate will moderate.

        利率的调整将会放缓。

    • 主持会议，主持节目(或讨论)：

      • to moderate the opening ceremony of the Olympic Games

        主持奥林匹克运动会开幕仪式

    • [古语]作调解人
  • 近义词: temperate
  • 反义词: excessive , extreme
    • 变形：
    • moderated , moderating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

moderate [ 'mɔdərət, 'mɔdəreit ]

  • n. a person who takes a position in the political center

    同义词：centrist | middle of the roader | moderationist

  • v.
    • preside over

      "John moderated the discussion"

      同义词：chair | lead

    • make less fast or intense

      "moderate your speed"

    • lessen the intensity of; temper; hold in restraint; hold or keep within limits

      "moderate your alcohol intake"

      同义词：control | hold in | hold | contain | check | curb

    • make less severe or harsh

      "He moderated his tone when the students burst out in tears"

      同义词：mince | soften

    • make less strong or intense; soften

      同义词：tone down | tame

    • restrain or temper

      同义词：chasten | temper

  • adj.
    • being within reasonable or average limits; not excessive or extreme

      "moderate prices"; " a moderate income"; " a moderate fine"; " moderate demands"; " a moderate estimate"; " a moderate eater"; " moderate success"; " a kitchen of moderate size"; " the X-ray showed moderate enlargement of the heart"

    • not extreme

      "a moderate penalty"

      同义词：temperate

    • marked by avoidance of extravagance or extremes

      "moderate in his demands"

      同义词：restrained
