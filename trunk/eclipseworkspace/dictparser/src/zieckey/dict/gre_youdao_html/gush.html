gush [ɡʌʃ]

  • vi.
    • 涌出，喷出，迸出：

      • Oil gushed from the well.

        油从油井中喷出。

    • (眼泪、血液等的)突然涌流：

      • blood gushing from a wound

        伤口处涌流出的血液

    • 表现出过分的热情；感情迸发；滔滔不绝地说(或写)：

      • young mothers gushing over their babies

        滔滔不绝谈论她们孩子的年轻母亲们

  • vt.
    • 使涌出；使喷出；使迸出：

      • The gaping wound gushed out a crimson flood.

        裂开的伤口鲜血直冒。

    • 滔滔地说
  • n.
    • 涌出，喷出，迸出
    • 涌出之物；喷出之物
    • 过分的热情；感情的迸发；滔滔不绝的话(或文章)
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

gush [ ɡʌʃ ]

  • n.
    • a sudden rapid flow (as of water)

      "there was a little gush of blood"

      同义词：flush | outpouring

    • an unrestrained expression of emotion

      同义词：effusion | outburst | blowup | ebullition

  • v.
    • gush forth in a sudden stream or jet

      "water gushed forth"

      同义词：spurt | spirt | spout

    • praise enthusiastically

      同义词：rave

    • issue in a jet; come out in a jet; stream or spring forth

      同义词：jet

以上来源于: WordNet
