correlate ['kɔ:rə,leit]

  • vt.
    • 使相互关联，使相互对照：

      • It is interesting to correlate the history of the 20th century with its literature.

        把20世纪的历史与文学联系起来是很有趣的。

  • vi.
    • 关联，相关：

      • His research results correlate with yours.

        他的研究成果与你的研究成果有相互关系。

  • adj.
    • 关联的，相关的
  • n.
    • 相互关联的人(或物)
    • 变形：
    • correlated , correlating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

correlate [ 'kɔ:rə,leit ]

  • n. either of two correlated variables

    同义词：correlative

  • v.
    • to bear a reciprocal or mutual relation

      "Do these facts correlate?"

    • bring into a mutual, complementary, or reciprocal relation

      "I cannot correlate these two pieces of information"

  • adj. mutually related

    同义词：correlative | correlated
