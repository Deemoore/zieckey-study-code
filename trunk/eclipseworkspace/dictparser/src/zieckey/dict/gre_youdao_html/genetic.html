genetic [dʒi'netik]

  • adj.
    • 起源的，起因的；发生的；创造的
    • 发展的，演变的；历史上的
    • (有关)遗传学的
    • = genic [亦作 genetical]
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

genetic [ dʒi'netik ]

  • adj.
    • tending to occur among members of a family usually by heredity

      "genetically transmitted features"

      同义词：familial | hereditary | inherited | transmitted | transmissible

    • of or relating to or produced by or being a gene

      "genetic code"

      同义词：genic | genetical

    • pertaining to or referring to origin

      "genetic history reconstructs the origins of a literary work"

    • of or relating to the science of genetics

      "genetic research"

      同义词：genetical

以上来源于: WordNet
