recompense ['rekəmpens]

  • vt.
    • 酬谢；回报(某人)：

      • You will be recompensed for your kindness.

        你的好意将会得到回报。

    • 赔偿：补偿(损失等)：

      • The firm will recompense you for your traveling expenses.

        公司将偿付你的旅费。

  • vi.
    • 酬劳；赔偿：

      • He didn't try to recompense for our loss.

        对我们的损失他无意补偿。

  • n.
    • 报酬
    • 赔偿；补偿
  • 近义词: pay
    • 变形：
    • recompensed , recompensing
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

recompense [ 'rekəmpens ]

  • n.
    • payment or reward (as for service rendered)
    • the act of compensating for service or loss or injury

      同义词：compensation

  • v.
    • make amends for; pay compensation for

      同义词：compensate | repair | indemnify

    • make payment to; compensate

      同义词：compensate | remunerate
