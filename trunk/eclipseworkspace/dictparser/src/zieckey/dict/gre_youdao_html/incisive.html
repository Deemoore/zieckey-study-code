incisive [in'saisiv]

  • adj.
    • 切入的，穿透的
    • 锐利的，锋利的；敏锐的，尖锐的
    • 讽刺的，辛辣的
    • 【解剖学】切牙的；门齿的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incisive [ in'saisiv ]

  • adj.
    • having or demonstrating ability to recognize or draw fine distinctions

      "incisive comments"; " as sharp and incisive as the stroke of a fang"

      同义词：acute | discriminating | keen | knifelike | penetrating | penetrative | piercing | sharp

    • suitable for cutting or piercing

      "incisive teeth"

      同义词：piercing

以上来源于: WordNet
