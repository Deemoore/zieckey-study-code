incline [in'klain]

  • vi.
    • 倾斜，偏斜：

      • The road inclines upward.

        这条路向上倾斜。

    • 点头，弯腰，鞠躬：

      • to incline forward to hear somebody

        向前弯腰以听清某人的讲话

    • 倾向，意向；喜爱；赞同：

      • People incline to tiredness in Summer.

        夏天人们很容易疲惫。

      • to incline to traditional way

        喜欢传统方式

      • to incline to one's view

        赞成某人的观点

    • 趋向，趋势(常与to连用):

      • Prices incline to go up.

        价格有上升趋势。

    • 接近，近于：

      • purple inclining to red

        接近红色的紫色

    • 【军事】侧转前进：

      • Right incline!

        半面向右转走!

  • vt.
    • 使倾斜：

      • to incline a post against the wall

        把柱子靠墙斜立着

    • 点头，弯腰，躬身：

      • to incline one's head in greeting

        点头致意

    • 使倾向于；使有意于：

      • His attitude did not incline me to help him.

        他的态度使我不想帮他。

  • n.
    • 倾斜；倾度
    • 倾度 ；斜面
    • 【铁路】 = inclined railway b)斜面[亦作 inclined plane,incline plane]
  • 短语:
    • to incline one's ear 见 ear¹
    • 变形：
    • inclined , inclining
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incline [ in'klain ]

  • n.
    • an elevated geological formation

      同义词：slope | side

    • an inclined surface connecting two levels

      同义词：ramp

  • v.
    • have a tendency or disposition to do or be something; be inclined

      "He inclined to corpulence"

      同义词：tend | be given | lean | run

    • bend or turn (one's ear) towards a speaker in order to listen well

      "He inclined his ear to the wise old man"

    • lower or bend (the head or upper body), as in a nod or bow

      "She inclined her head to the student"

    • be at an angle

      同义词：slope | pitch

    • make receptive or willing towards an action or attitude or belief

      "Their language inclines us to believe them"

      同义词：dispose

以上来源于: WordNet
