cerebral ['seribrəl]

  • adj.
    • 脑的，大脑的
    • 重理智的；凭理智行事的；动脑筋的
    • 【语音学】翘舌的，翘舌音的，卷舌音的
  • n.
    • 【语音学】翘舌音，卷舌音，顶音

cerebral [ 'seribrəl ]

  • adj.
    • involving intelligence rather than emotions or instinct

      "a cerebral approach to the problem"; " cerebral drama"

      同义词：intellectual

    • of or relating to the cerebrum or brain

      "cerebral hemisphere"; " cerebral activity"
