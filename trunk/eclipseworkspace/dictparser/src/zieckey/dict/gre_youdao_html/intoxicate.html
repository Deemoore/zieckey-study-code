intoxicate [in'tɔksikeit; -kit; -keit]

  • vt.
    • 使醉酒；使麻醉：

      • be intoxicated from wine

        喝醉了

    • 使陶醉，使沉醉，使狂喜：

      • The beauty of the night intoxicated me.

        美丽的夜色让我沉醉。

    • 使中毒
  • vi.
    • 醉
  • adj.
    • [古语]= intoxicated
    • 变形：
    • intoxicated , intoxicating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

intoxicate [ in'tɔksikeit, -kit, -keit ]

  • v.
    • fill with high spirits; fill with optimism

      同义词：elate | lift up | uplift | pick up

    • make drunk (with alcoholic drinks)

      同义词：soak | inebriate

    • have an intoxicating effect on, of a drug
