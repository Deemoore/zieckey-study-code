loop1 [lu:p]

  • n.
    • (用线、绳、带等打成的)环；圈；环孔
    • 环状物；(文字、画等的)弯曲部分；环形部分
    • 拎环；搭环；环状饰物
    • (电车、铁路等终点的)回路；让车道
    • (盘山路的)环路；环线
    • [主英国英语]【铁路】会车线
    • [常作 the loop]【医学】(双S形)节育环；避孕环；利普斯环[亦作 Lippes loop]
    • 【解剖学】箕形指纹
    • 【航空学】翻筋斗(一种飞行特技)
    • (溜冰时单脚)打圈儿
    • 【无线电】环形天线[亦作 loop aerial]
    • 【物理学】(波)腹；回线
    • 【电学】回路；闭合电路；滞后回线
    • 【计算机】循环，循环程序，回线
    • [the Loop]卢普区(美国芝加哥闹市区)
  • vt.
    • (把绳索等)打成环状；使成圈：

      • They looped a rope around the thief.

        他们用绳子把那个贼捆了起来。

    • 以环(圈)围绕；缠：

      • to loop the wire around the post

        把导线绕在柱子上

    • 打环扣住；用环状物系住(常与 up 连用)：

      • to loop the curtain up

        将窗帘打环卷起

    • 使(导弹等)在空中作环状飞行；成弧线抛投(手榴弹等)：

      • The soldier looped a grenade into the enemy shelter trench.

        士兵将一枚手榴弹呈弧形抛进敌方掩蔽壕里。

    • 【航空学】使(飞机)翻筋斗
    • 【电学】把(导线)连成回路
  • vi.
    • 打环；成圈；形成环状：

        该河绕城镇流过。

    • 【航空学】(飞机)翻筋斗
    • (手榴弹或球等)呈弧线飞过，作环状运动

loop [ lu:p ]

  • n.
    • fastener consisting of a metal ring for lining a small hole to permit the attachment of cords or lines

      同义词：cringle | eyelet | grommet | grummet

    • anything with a round or oval shape (formed by a curve that is closed and does not intersect itself)
    • (computer science) a single execution of a set of instructions that are to be repeated

      同义词：iteration

    • an inner circle of advisors (especially under President Reagan)

      "he's no longer in the loop"

    • the basic pattern of the human fingerprint
    • a computer program that performs a series of instructions repeatedly until some specified condition is satisfied
    • the topology of a network whose components are serially connected in such a way that the last component is connected to the first component

      同义词：loop topology

    • an intrauterine device in the shape of a loop
    • a complete electrical circuit around which current flows or a signal circulates

      同义词：closed circuit

    • a flight maneuver; aircraft flies a complete circle in the vertical plane

      同义词：loop-the-loop

  • v.
    • move in loops

      "The bicycle looped around the tree"

    • make a loop in

      "loop a rope"

      同义词：intertwine

    • fly loops, perform a loop

      "the stunt pilot looped his plane"

    • wind around something in coils or loops

      同义词：coil | curl

    • fasten or join with a loop

      "He looped the watch through his belt"
