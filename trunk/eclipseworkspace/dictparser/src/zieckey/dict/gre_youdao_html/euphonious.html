euphonious [ ju:'fəuniəs ]

  • adj.
    • having a pleasant sound

      "a euphonious trill of silver laughter"

      同义词：euphonous

    • (of speech or dialect) pleasing in sound; not harsh or strident

      "her euphonious Southern speech"
