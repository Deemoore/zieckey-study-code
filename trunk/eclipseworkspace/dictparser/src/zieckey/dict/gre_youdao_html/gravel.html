gravel ['ɡrævəl]

  • n.
    • [总称]砾，沙砾，小石；砾石
    • 【病理学】尿砂；尿砂症
    • [废语]沙
  • vt.
    • 用砾石铺(路)
    • 使为难，使窘迫；使困惑；使气恼；搅扰
    • [口语]成为…生气(或激怒、恼怒等)的原因
    • [废语]使(船)搁浅在沙滩上，使(船)坐滩
  • adj.
    • (声音)粗重而沙哑的，刺耳的
  • 短语:
    • hit the gravel [俚语](为隐蔽而)卧倒
    • scratch gravel
      • [美国口语]
      • 飞奔，飞跑，疾驰
      • 为生活而奔波
    • 变形：
    • gravelled或-eled , gravelling或-eling

gravel [ 'ɡrævəl ]

  • n. rock fragments and pebbles

    同义词：crushed rock

  • v.
    • cause annoyance in; disturb, especially by minor irritations

      同义词：annoy | rag | get to | bother | get at | irritate | rile | nark | nettle | vex | chafe | devil

    • cover with gravel

      "We gravelled the driveway"

    • be a mystery or bewildering to

      同义词：perplex | vex | stick | get | puzzle | mystify | baffle | beat | pose | bewilder | flummox | stupefy | nonplus | amaze | dumbfound

  • adj. unpleasantly harsh or grating in sound

    "a gravelly voice"

    同义词：grating | gravelly | rasping | raspy | rough | scratchy
