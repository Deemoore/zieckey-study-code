regenerate [ri'dʒenəreit; ri:-]

  • vt.
    • 使(精神上)新生：

      • They were spiritually regenerated by his speech.

        他们听了他的讲话后在精神上获得了新生。

    • 改革；更新；重建：

      • It became a main force that would regenerate society.

        它成为改造社会的一个主要力量。

    • 使恢复原状；重新产生；复兴：

      • Their main task is to regenerate Chinese coal industry.

        他们的主要任务是复兴中国煤炭工业。

    • 【生物学】重新生出：

      • A lizard will regenerate its tail if it's cut off.

        蜥蜴的尾巴如被切掉，它会重新生出一条尾巴。

    • 【化学】使再生；还原：

      • The last step is to regenerate the catalyst.

        最后一步是将催化剂还原。

    • 【电工学】使再生；恢复
    • 【机械工程】使回热(或回电等)
    • 【物理学】还原；使再生
  • vi.
    • 再生；更新：

      • The earthworm can regenerate if it's cut in half.

        蚯蚓被切成两段后可以重新生长。

    • 新生；恢复：

      • We believed that the damaged countries would soon regenerate.

        我们相信受到破坏的国家将很快恢复生机。

  • adj.
    • (精神上)新生的
    • 更新的；革新的；再生的
  • n.
    • 再生物；再生部分
    • 改过自新的人
    • 变形：
    • regenerated , regenerating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

regenerate [ ri'dʒenəreit, ri:- ]

  • v.
    • reestablish on a new, usually improved, basis or make new or like new

      同义词：renew

    • amplify (an electron current) by causing part of the power in the output circuit to act upon the input circuit
    • bring, lead, or force to abandon a wrong or evil course of life, conduct, and adopt a right one

      同义词：reform | reclaim | rectify

    • return to life; get or give new life or energy

      同义词：restore | rejuvenate

    • replace (tissue or a body part) through the formation of new tissue

      "The snake regenerated its tail"

    • be formed or shaped anew
    • form or produce anew

      "regenerate hatred"

    • undergo regeneration
    • restore strength

      同义词：revitalize

  • adj. reformed spiritually or morally

    "a regenerate sinner"; " regenerate by redemption from error or decay"

以上来源于: WordNet
