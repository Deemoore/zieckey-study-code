clandestine [klæn'destin]

  • adj. 保密的，秘密的；暗中的，私下的；鬼鬼祟祟的，偷偷摸摸的
  • 近义词: secret
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

clandestine [ klæn'destin ]

  • adj. conducted with or marked by hidden aims or methods

    "clandestine intelligence operations"

    同义词：cloak-and-dagger | hole-and-corner(a) | hugger-mugger | hush-hush | secret | surreptitious | undercover | underground
