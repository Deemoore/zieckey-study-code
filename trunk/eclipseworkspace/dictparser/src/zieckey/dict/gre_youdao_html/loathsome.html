loathsome ['ləuðsəm; 'ləuθ-]

  • adj. 讨厌的；令人厌恶的；令人憎恨的；令人恶心的；令人作呕的
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

loathsome [ 'ləuðsəm, 'ləuθ- ]

  • adj.
    • causing or able to cause nausea

      同义词：nauseating | nauseous | noisome | queasy | offensive | sickening | vile

    • highly offensive; arousing aversion or disgust

      "a loathsome disease"

      同义词：disgusting | disgustful | distasteful | foul | loathly | repellent | repellant | repelling | revolting | skanky | wicked | yucky

以上来源于: WordNet
