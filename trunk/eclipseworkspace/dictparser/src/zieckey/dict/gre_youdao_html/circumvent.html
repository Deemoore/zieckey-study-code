circumvent

    • /,sə:kəm'vent/
  • vt.
    • 围绕；包围；绕行：

      • to circumvent the lake

        围绕该湖

      • Our village is circumvented by a stream.

        我们的村庄周围有一条小河围绕着。

    • (用计谋)包围；诱捕；使陷入圈套：

      • to circumvent a body of enemy troops

        智困敌军

    • 用计谋取胜，智胜；(巧妙地)避开，规避：

      • to circumvent a law

        规避一项法规

      • He circumvented their objection to the plan by having one of their own members propose it.

        他设法使他们自己的人来提出这个计划而避过了他们的反对。

以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

circumvent [ ,sə:kəm'vent ]

  • v.
    • surround so as to force to give up

      同义词：besiege | beleaguer | surround | hem in

    • beat through cleverness and wit

      同义词：outwit | overreach | outsmart | outfox | beat

    • avoid or try to avoid fulfilling, answering, or performing (duties, questions, or issues)

      同义词：hedge | fudge | evade | put off | parry | elude | skirt | dodge | duck | sidestep
