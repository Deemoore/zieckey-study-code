refinement [ri'fainmənt]

  • n.
    • (感情、趣味、举止、言谈等的)优雅，高尚，文雅
    • 文雅、高尚的感情、举止等
    • 精炼；纯化，精制
    • 改进品，改良品
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

refinement [ ri'fainmənt ]

  • n.
    • a highly developed state of perfection; having a flawless or impeccable quality

      "I admired the exquisite refinement of his prose"

      同义词：polish | culture | cultivation | finish

    • the result of improving something

      "he described a refinement of this technique"

      同义词：elaboration

    • the process of removing impurities (as from oil or metals or sugar etc.)

      同义词：refining | purification

    • a subtle difference in meaning or opinion or attitude

      同义词：nuance | nicety | shade | subtlety

    • the quality of excellence in thought and manners and taste

      "a man of intellectual refinement"

      同义词：civilization | civilisation

以上来源于: WordNet
