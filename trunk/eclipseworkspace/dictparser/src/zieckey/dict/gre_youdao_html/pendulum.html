pendulum ['pendjuləm; -dʒə-]

  • n.
    • (钟等的)摆；钟摆
    • 动摇的人(物)；犹豫不决的人
    • 吊烛架
  • 短语:
    • play pendulum 左右摇摆；处于不稳定的状态
    • swing like a pendulum 摇摆不定
    • swing of the pendulum 见 swing

  • n. an apparatus consisting of an object mounted so that it swings freely under the influence of gravity
