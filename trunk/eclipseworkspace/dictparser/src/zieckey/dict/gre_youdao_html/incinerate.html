incinerate [in'sinəreit]

  • vt.
    • 把…烧成灰烬；焚化，火化，火葬：

      • to incinerate the dead body

        将尸体火化

  • vi.
    • 烧成灰烬；体火化，火葬
    • 变形：
    • incinerated , incinerating
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

incinerate [ in'sinəreit ]

  • v.
    • become reduced to ashes

      "The paper incinerated quickly"

    • cause to undergo combustion

      同义词：burn
