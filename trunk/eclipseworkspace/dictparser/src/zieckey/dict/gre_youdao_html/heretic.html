    • 异教徒，持异端者，离经叛道者
  • adj.
    • ＝heretical
以上来源于：《21世纪大英汉词典》

heretic [ 'herətik ]

  • n.
    • a person who holds religious beliefs in conflict with the dogma of the Roman Catholic Church

      同义词：misbeliever | religious outcast

    • a person who holds unorthodox opinions in any field (not merely religion)
